== Etymology ==
Kinjal is of Sanskrit origin and means "river bank". The name is used for both genders. Bhatia is a Punjabi surname.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
Her name suggests Indian descent, she is most likely originating from Northern India.
