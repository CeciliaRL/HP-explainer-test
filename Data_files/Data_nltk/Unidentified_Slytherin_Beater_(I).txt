This individual was a wizard who played as a Beater for the Slytherin team in Quidditch. In the Slytherin versus Gryffindor Hogwart's Quidditch Cup match in 1991, he is shown to have his bat taken by Marcus Flint.


== Behind the scenes ==
He only appears in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone.
It is possible that this person is Lucian Bole.
