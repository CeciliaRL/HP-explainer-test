The Ravenclaw Wizard Duelling Champion as the title suggests, refers to the duelling champion for the Ravenclaw house. He is a selected student from the Ravenclaw house to represent the house in the Founder Duels.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
This boy attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s and was Sorted into Ravenclaw House. His Head of House was Filius Flitwick, the Charms Professor and Frog Choir conductor, and he shared a dormitory with four boys in his year in the Ravenclaw Tower.
