This individual (fl. 1975) was a wizard and werewolf
who preferred to remain anonymous. He wrote a heartrending account of his struggle against lycanthropy in the 1970s. Hairy Snout, Human Heart, the title of his book, was a classic by the 1990s.


== Appearances ==
Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (First mentioned)
The Tales of Beedle the Bard (Mentioned only)
