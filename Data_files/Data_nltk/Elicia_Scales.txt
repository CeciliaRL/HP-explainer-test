Elicia Scales was a witch who the Ministry of Magic kept tabs on while it was under Death Eater control in 1997. Her file was signed by Chief Registration Authority F. L. Wakefield.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Elicia Scales, who worked as an archivist on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince,  Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
