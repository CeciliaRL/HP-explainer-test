Joyce Haddock was a Muggle woman. A swimming examiner at Smeltings Academy, she presented an award to Dudley Dursley for swimming five metres, or one-quarter the full length of the pool in the 1990s.


== Behind the scenes ==
Her name is likely a reference to the fish Haddock.
