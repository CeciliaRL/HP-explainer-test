This girl was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was Sorted into Gryffindor House. She and other students were watching the first game of Gryffindor's Quidditch Team with Rubeus Hagrid in 1991 and celebrated with Dean and Ron after the match was over.


== Biography ==
This girl started her magical education at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1991 and was Sorted into Gryffindor House. This girl, like the other Gryffindor students, was very happy to go see the first game, and followed Rubeus Hagrid to the Quidditch field.

 	 	 	 		 		 		 			The girl with Ron and Dean going to the Quidditch field 		 	 
Also in 1991, she celebrated when the Gryffindor team was victorious in the first game. She and other Gryffindor students greeted Harry in the middle of the Quidditch field.


== Behind the scenes ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			Celebrating the Gryffindor's victory 		 	 
This individual is played by Julianne Hough in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, but was not credited.
She could be Sally-Anne Perks, a student in Harry's year whose House is unknown.
She could also be Alice Tolipan, who joined Dumbledore's Army in 1995 to oppose Dolores Umbridge.
Other possible identities could be Lavender Brown, Lily Moon, Sophie Roper, Sally Smith, or Runcorn.
