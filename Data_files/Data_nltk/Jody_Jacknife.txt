Jody Jacknife was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1960s. She was Captain of the Slytherin Quidditch team from 1960 to 1964.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Jody Jackman, an art department coordinator on every Harry Potter film, excepting Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone.
