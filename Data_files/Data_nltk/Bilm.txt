Bilm was a house-elf that worked at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1980s. They worked in the kitchens along with the other Hogwarts house-elves.


== Physical appearance ==
The elf had long, pig-like ears and a pointy, beakish nose. They wore what appeared to be a potato sack emblazoned with a small Hogwarts crest.
