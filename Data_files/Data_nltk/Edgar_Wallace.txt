Richard Horatio Edgar Wallace (1 April, 1875—10 February, 1932) was a Muggle English crime writer, journalist, novelist, screenwriter, and playwright, who wrote 175 novels, 24 plays, and numerous articles in newspapers and journals.
Edgar Wallace was also the author of The Flying Squad. Albus Dumbledore kept a copy of the Swedish translation of this book (Kokain Nästet) in his office at Hogwarts Castle in the 1990s.
