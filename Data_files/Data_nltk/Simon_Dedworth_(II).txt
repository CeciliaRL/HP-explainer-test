Simon Dedworth was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. During the 1996–1997 school year, he blocked access to areas of the school grounds that were off limits.


== Etymology ==
The name "Simon" is from Σιμων (Simon), the New Testament Greek form of the Hebrew name שִׁמְעוֹן (Shim'on) which meant "he has heard".
