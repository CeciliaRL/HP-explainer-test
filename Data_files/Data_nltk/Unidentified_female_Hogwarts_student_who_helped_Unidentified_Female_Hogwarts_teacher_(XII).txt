This witch was a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry at least during the 1997-1998 school year. She participated in the Battle of Hogwarts and helped a teacher to go to the Viaduct Courtyard. It is unknown if she survived.


== Behind the scenes ==
She could be Parvati Patil by her light brown colour of the skin.
The teacher who helped was possibly Aurora Sinistra.
