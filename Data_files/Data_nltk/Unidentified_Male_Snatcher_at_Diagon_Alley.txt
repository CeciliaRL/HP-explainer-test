This individual was a wizard and a Snatcher.


== Biography ==
In 1997, he and some fellow Snatchers patrolled Diagon Alley in their search for Muggle-borns. He and his fellow Snatchers died when the Covered Bridge were blown up by Neville Longbottom, Seamus Finnigan and other students.
