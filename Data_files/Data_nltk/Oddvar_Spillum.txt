Oddvar Spillum was a Norwegian wizard who, in 2014, coached the Norwegian National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
When his team, one of the joint favourites of the 2014 Quidditch World Cup, suffered a heavy defeat against Bulgaria (170 to 20), Oddvar Spillum had no post-match comment for reporters, apart from broken sobs.
