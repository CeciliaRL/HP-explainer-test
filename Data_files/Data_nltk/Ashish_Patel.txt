Ashish Patel was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Patel is an Indian surname, meaning headman or village chief.


== Behind the scenes ==
Based on his name, he is presumably of Indian descent.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)
