Achilles Tolliver was an American wizard who worked as an Auror for the Magical Congress of the United States of America in the 1920s.
While Jacob Kowalski was under the influence of an Entrancing Enchantment cast by Queenie Goldstein, he thought the name 'Tolliver' was amusing.


== Biography ==
In 1927, he was dating fellow Auror Tina Goldstein.
