Mrs Tuttle was a British witch and a Seer. She had at least two daughters, one of whom was Liz, who did not inherit her gift, to her disappointment.


== Biography ==
At some point in her early life, she lived in Somalia and was home-schooled in magic by her parents.
In her youth, Liz was taught astrology by her mother; after this, Liz "[would] never trust Mercury again". When Liz started her third year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, her mother forced her to take Divination as an elective.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Divination: Liz Tuttle's mother was a Seer, meaning she was naturally gifted with the Inner Eye and could receive genuine visions of the future.
Wandless magic: Accoding to Liz, her mother was capable of performing wandless magic, which her parents taught her how to do in Somalia.
