Revd. Dr Stephen Swain was a Muggle and the vicar of the St Jerome's Church, in Godric's Hollow during the 1990s.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named in honour of Stephen Swain, who worked in the Art Department in all the Harry Potter films, first as an assistant, then as a draughtsman, and finally as an assistant art director.
