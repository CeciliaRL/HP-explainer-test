This waiter served food and drinks at the Slug Club Christmas party in 1996 along with Neville Longbottom and another waiter. He offered dragon tartare to some party guests, but they passed.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is currently unknown whether he was a student or an adult hired specifically for the party.
In LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7, Neville Longbottom was the only waiter serving food and drinks.
