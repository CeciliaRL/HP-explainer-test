Roddy Doyle (b. 8 May, 1958) was an Irish Muggle author who wrote The Commitments and The Van.


== Behind the scenes ==
Roddy Doyle is an Irish novelist, dramatist, and screenwriter. J. K. Rowling has stated that Doyle's The Woman Who Walked Into Doors is her favourite novel, and that she would like to meet Doyle in person some day.
