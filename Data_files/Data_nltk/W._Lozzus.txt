W. Lozzus was an arts and culture reporter for The New York Ghost in the 1920s.
They wrote an article entitled "Black magic: how to get the most out of your every day robes" that was published in the 1 December 1926 issue of the paper. Five days later, on 6 December, the paper printed a report by them about a planned Wizard Broadway adaptation of the popular book Big Foot's Last Stand. This was followed by a report by them in 7 December's sunset edition about how casting of the play had begun and was progressing quickly.
