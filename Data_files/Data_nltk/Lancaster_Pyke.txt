Lancaster Pyke, also known as Magic Joe, was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the United States of America in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Pyke offering 4850 dragots for is arrest, which described him as "older" and stated that he was wanted for "embezzlement of spells and No-Maj murder".
