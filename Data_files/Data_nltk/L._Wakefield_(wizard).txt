L. Wakefield was a witch or wizard, numerologist, and the author of at least one book on the subject, Numerology.
Wakefield also gave their name to L. Wakefield's Broomcare Wax. It is unknown whether Wakefield was this product's inventor or simply its manufacturer.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Lauren Wakefield, a graphic artist who worked on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, and both parts of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows.
