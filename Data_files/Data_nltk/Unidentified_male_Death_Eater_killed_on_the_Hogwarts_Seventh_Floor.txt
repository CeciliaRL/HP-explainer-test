This Dark Wizard was a Death Eater who fought during the Second Wizarding War. 


== Biography ==
This wizard was presumably born in Great Britain or Ireland some time during the 20th century. At some point in his adult life, he became well-versed in the Dark Arts and joined the Death Eaters, becoming a loyal servant of Lord Voldemort. He presumably fought during the First Wizarding War with his fellow Death Eaters for Voldemort's cause. 
On 2 May 1998, This Death Eater was called by his master to fight in the Battle of Hogwarts, which he responded to and fought with his fellow Death Eaters against the defenders of Hogwarts. During the battle, he was killed by a student or member of staff on the Seventh Floor.
