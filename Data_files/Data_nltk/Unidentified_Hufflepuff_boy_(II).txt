This wizard was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
He was a great supporter of Cedric Diggory and yelled his surname when Cedric was chosen as champion in the Triwizard Tournament in 1994. Along with another student he blamed Harry Potter for being chosen as the fourth champion and for being a cheat.


== Behind the scenes ==
In the original version, it is almost impossible to hear him when he congratulates Cedric. This is fixed in the Danish version.
