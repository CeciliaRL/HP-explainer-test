Mr Krum (fl. 1977-1995) was a Bulgarian wizard, who was the father of Viktor Krum.


== Biography ==
Mr Krum travelled to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry with his wife to watch Viktor face the final task of the Triwizard Tournament in 1995.
Viktor told Harry Potter that his grandfather was killed by Gellert Grindelwald. This man could have been either Mr Krum's father or his father-in-law.


== Physical appearance ==
He was described as having dark hair and a hooked nose like his son.
