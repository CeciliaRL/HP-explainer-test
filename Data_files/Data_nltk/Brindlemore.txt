Professor Brindlemore was a witch who taught Defence Against the Dark Arts at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during at least the late 2000s and early 2010s.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Defence Against the Dark Arts: As a Professor of the subject at Hogwarts, Brindelmore was proficient in the field of teaching others how to magically defend themselves against the Dark Arts.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible that this individual is Amycus Carrow's successor.
