Lottie Turner was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 2008.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Astronomy: Lottie was known to enjoy the study of Astronomy.
Charms: Lottie was capable of producing a fully corporeal Patronus in the form of a hummingbird, an exceptional magical feat for a Hogwarts student. She was also proficient with the Shrinking Charm and Doubling Charm.
Artist: Lottie was good at painting and used to paint with her father.
