These two agents worked for the Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes, at the Accidental Magic Reversal Squad.


== History ==
In 1993 they were sent out to modify the memory of Marjorie Dursley's mind in regards to the spell that Harry Potter had inadvertently used on her.
