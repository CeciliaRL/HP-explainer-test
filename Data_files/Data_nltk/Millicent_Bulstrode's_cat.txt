This cat was a pet cat of the Slytherin student Millicent Bulstrode. Millicent had it with her as her permitted pet at Hogwarts in 1992.


== Biography ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			Hermione partially transformed into the cat after accidentally adding the cat's hair to her Polyjuice Potion 		 	 
Hermione Granger plucked a small hair of the cat from Millicent Bulstrode's robes, thinking it was hers. She added the cat's hair to the Polyjuice Potion she was brewing, which resulted in her partial animal transformation after she drank it.


== Behind the scenes ==
In LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4, Hermione did not realise she had become the cat until Ron and Harry pointed it out.
