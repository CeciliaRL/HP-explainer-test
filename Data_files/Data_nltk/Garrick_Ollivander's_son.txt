Mr Ollivander was the son of Garrick Ollivander and his wife. He had a sister who, by 2011, had died.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible that Mr Ollivander took over from his father as the owner of Ollivanders.
This individual was only mentioned on the original version of the "Mr Ollivander" article on the old Pottermore.
