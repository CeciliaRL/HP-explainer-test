This Hornbill was a pet male bird owned by Minerva McGonagall from 1992 or earlier.


== History ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			McGonagall using her bird to demonstrate Vera Verto 		 	 
In 1992, Professor McGonagall used the bird to demonstrate the transforming spell, Vera Verto, in front of her second-years' Transfiguration class.
During the 2020s, this hornbill appeared as a Foundable during the Calamity.
