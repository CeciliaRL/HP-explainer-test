Lydia Fry was a Muggle media personality. She gave an interview in the 28th February-26th March 1997 issue of My Teeth, in which she shared her latest personal tips on life and maintaining dental health. In the summer of that same year, she gave an interview in Radio Times, in which she discussed what motivated her to take up fishing.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Lydia Fry, who worked as an uncredited temporary graphic design assistant on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince,  and as an art department assistant on Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
