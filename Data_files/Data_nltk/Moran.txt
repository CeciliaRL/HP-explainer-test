Moran was an Irish witch who played as the Chaser for the Irish National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
She played alongside Connolly, Barry Ryan, Troy, Mullet, Quigley and Lynch during the 1994 Quidditch World Cup final, when they won over the Bulgarians. She, along with her teammates scored one of the points for Ireland, played well, performing moves that astonished Harry Potter and the Weasleys.


== Etymology ==
The Irish surname "Moran" means a descendant of Mórán, translated as "big one."


== Behind the scenes ==
Moran appeared in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire. It is unknown who played her, and she only makes a small appearance, as most of the match is cut from the film.
