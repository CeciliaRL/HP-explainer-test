This Minister of Magical Transportation (born c. 1949) was a wizard who headed the Department of Magical Transportation by 1996. During his school years at Hogwarts, he was a member of the Slug Club.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
This individual was born in around 1949. He attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he was made a member of the Slug Club by his Potions Master, Horace Slughorn. He graduated in 1967.


=== Adulthood ===


==== Professional life ====
This individual grew up to work at the Ministry of Magic and by December of 1996 was considered the "new" Minister of Magical Transportation.


==== Slug Club Christmas party ====
On 20 December, 1996, this individual attended a Christmas party at Hogwarts Castle, held by his former professor, Horace Slughorn. Slughorn was delighted to see this individual and quickly dismissed Harry Potter upon seeing him.
