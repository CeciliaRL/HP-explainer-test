This individual was an American witch who worked as an Executioner for Magical Congress in 1926.


== Biography ==
Along with Bernadette and another wizard assisted in the execution of Newton Scamander and Porpentina Goldstein after being sentenced to Death by Gellert Grindelwald disguised as Percival Graves for violating the International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy. The execution was stopped when Newt and Tina were rescued by a Swooping Evil.


== Behind the scenes ==
This Executioner was portrayed by Elizabeth Moynihan in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
