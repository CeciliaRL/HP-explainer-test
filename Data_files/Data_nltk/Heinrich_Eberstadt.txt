Heinrich Eberstadt was a Swiss wizard who was a delegate to the International Confederation of Wizards.


== Biography ==
He participated in an emergency meeting alongside fellow members of the Confederation in New York in 1926 to discuss the potential exposure of the wizarding world.
President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America, Seraphina Picquery, rebuked his criticisms, noting that she would not be lectured by the man who let Gellert Grindelwald slip through his fingers.


== Behind the scenes ==
Heinrich Eberstadt is portrayed by Martin Oelbermann in the film inspired by Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
