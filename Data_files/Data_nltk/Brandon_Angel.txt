Brandon Angel was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He once signed up for Quidditch training at 7pm on Tuesday on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the bulletin board in the Gryffindor Common Room.


== Etymology ==
Brandon is from a surname which was derived from a place name meaning "hill covered with broom" in Old English.
