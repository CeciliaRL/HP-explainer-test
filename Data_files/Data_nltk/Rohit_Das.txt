Rohit Das was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
The name Rohit is Sanskrit and means "red" or "first rays of the sun". Das is an Indian surname, meaning "servant of God".


== Behind the scenes ==
Given his name, he is most likely of Indian descent.
