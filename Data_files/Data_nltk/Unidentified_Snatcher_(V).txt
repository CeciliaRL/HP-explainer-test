This individual was a wizard who became an Snatcher during the Second Wizarding War. It is unknown if he was killed or imprisoned in Azkaban later after the end of it in 1998.


== Behind the scenes ==
The snatcher was played by Sam Parham in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1.
