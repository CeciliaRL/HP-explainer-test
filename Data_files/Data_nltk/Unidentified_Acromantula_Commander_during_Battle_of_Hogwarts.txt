This Unidentified Acromantula was a member of the Acromantula colony in the Forbidden Forest, on Hogwarts  grounds. 


== Biography ==
On 2 May 1998, after Lord Voldemort and his Death Eaters drove the colony out of their habitat by taking over the forest as their base for operations, this Acromantula took control of the colony and led them into fighting in the Battle of Hogwarts, where they attacked both the defenders of Hogwarts and Death Eaters alike.
