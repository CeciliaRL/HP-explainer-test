Addison Fawley was the son of Grim Fawley and Penelope Fawley, who were Aurors. He had a sister, Melody Fawley.


== Biography ==
In 2015,, Penelope went missing and a subsequent investigation overseen by the council of which Gareth Greengrass was a member, was called off. Grim began searching for her, leading Constance Pickering to suspect he had unleashed the Calamity through the use of the Foundables Spell.


== Personality ==
Addison was said to have inherited his mother Penelope's stubbornness, but she thought he was like Grim.
