Josef Wronski (pl. Józef Wroński) was a Polish wizard who played for the Grodzisk Goblins, as Seeker. Regarded as arguably the world's most innovative Seeker, Wronski invented the famed Wronski Feint.
The Josef Wronski Award for Excellent Pitch Skills was named after him.
As of 2014, Josef Wronski did not play for the Grodzisk Goblins any more.
