Simon Wilkinson was a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He used a purple-and-red marbled notebook in his third year.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is a tribute to Simon Wilkinson, who worked as a propman on all eight Harry Potter films.
