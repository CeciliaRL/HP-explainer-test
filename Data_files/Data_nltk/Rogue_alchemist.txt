This individual was a wizard who wore a purple cloak with a goatee, that operated in Great Britain during the late 20th century.


== Biography ==
At one point, he began scamming other wizards by alleging to be an alchemist that had cracked the code of panacea; a remedy that was supposed to cure any ailment. Rather than doing what it was supposed to, however, all his fake panacea did was give several patients boils.
