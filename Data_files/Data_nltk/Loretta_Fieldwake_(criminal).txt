Loretta Fieldwake was an  American wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In or around 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Fieldwake, stating that she was wanted for No-Maj & Elf murder and "bearing wand, most menacing and extremely dangerous." A reward of 2500 Dragots was offered for information leading to her capture.
