This caroler was a goblin and a member of the choir in Hogsmeade in 1993 during the Christmas holidays. During the singing he was pushed rudely by an angry Harry Potter under his Invisibility cloak.


== Behind the scenes ==
This goblin was portrayed by Binde Johal in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, who also portrayed the tall goblin in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
