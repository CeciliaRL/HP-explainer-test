This individual was a Death Eater who fought at the Battle of Hogwarts. He attacked the students inside the castle but was stunned by Harry Potter and fell into one of the stands and hit his head.


== Behind the scenes ==
This Death Eater individual is possibly the one that was stunned by Harry in the Grand Staircase Tower, with some misinformation.
