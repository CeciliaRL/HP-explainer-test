Albert Gibbering Stump, O.M. (unknown class), (b. 1662) was a wizard whose portrait (dated 1700, when Albert was 38) hung at Hogwarts Castle during the 1990s.


== Biography ==
Albert was part of the prominent wizarding Stump family and, as such, was most likely related to Grogan Stump, Google Stump and Tobias Stump.
