Miss Boyle was a American No-Maj woman who was part of the upper echelons of New York society in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
She was one of the attendees of the fundraising dinner for the re-election of Senator Henry Shaw in December 1926. When the function was interrupted by Credence Barebone's Obscurus's attack on the Senator, Miss Boyle was at a loss as to what to do as chaos ensued. She later was quoted in a report on the incident published in The New York Star.
