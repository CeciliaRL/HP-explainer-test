This individual was a Ministry of Magic official. On 2 September, 1997, he passed by Harry Potter (disguised as Albert Runcorn) while under his Invisibility Cloak on Level One of the Ministry of Magic. The wizard was murmuring instructions to a floating quill scribbling on a trail of parchment.


== Behind the scenes ==
His quill was possibly a Self-Writing Quill or a Quick-Quotes Quill.
