R. Amorim was a reporter for The New York Ghost in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In the 7 December sunset edition, they co-wrote a report with M. Carneirus and C. Williamsum about top Aurors being called to New York City Hall to investigate the death of Henry Shaw Junior, which was published on the front page.
