These individuals were the Muggle parents of a child who was a witch or wizard. They lived in the early 21st century.


== Biography ==
At one point in between 1996 and 1997, they had a child. When their child was young, they were taken on holidays. Their child soon began to cause strange things to happen, but when they told their parents, they laughed and thought they had to be seeing things.
At the age of eleven, their child found out about their magical heritage by Rubeus Hagrid, and began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
