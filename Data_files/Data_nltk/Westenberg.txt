Mr Westenberg (d. 1997) was an English wizard who lived in the north of England.


== Biography ==
He and his wife were supporters of the Order of the Phoenix and had, on occasion, provided shelter for its members. He and his wife were found murdered in their cellar in the summer of 1997.
