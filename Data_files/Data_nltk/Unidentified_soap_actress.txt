This blonde-haired actress starred on a British soap during the 1990s. 


== History ==
She wrote an article, entitled "My Secret Shame," which was published on page 5 of the 2 August 1995 issue of the Daily Express. This article was teased on the front page of the newspaper with a photo by Chris Lunney of the actress wearing dark sunglasses and fending off cameras.
