This individual was a wizard and helper of Gilderoy Lockhart's in the early 1990s. On 19 August, 1992, this individual accompanied Lockhart into his book signing at Flourish and Blotts, presenting Harry Potter with Lockhart's entire collected works (free of charge) on Lockhart's word.
