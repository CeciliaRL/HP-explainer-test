Glenda Chittock (b. 1964) was a British witch who was host of the Wizarding Wireless Network programme, Witching Hour. Being host of this enormously famous radio programme, she is featured on a Chocolate Frog Card.


== Behind the scenes ==
If Glenda attended Hogwarts, her first year would have been the Marauders' fifth year.
