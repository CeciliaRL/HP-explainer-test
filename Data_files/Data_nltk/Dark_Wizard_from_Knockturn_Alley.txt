This Dark Wizard was stolen from by Mundungus Fletcher and duelled Jacob's sibling in the 1988–1989 school year.


== Biography ==
During the 1988–1989 school year, Mundungus Fletcher asked Jacob's sibling to duel this wizard, who, according to him, cheated him, in Knockturn Alley in exchange for the jinxed Ever-Bashing Boomerangs. Jacob's sibling did so, and after the duel, the wizard threatened to crush Fletcher if he stole from him again.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Dark Arts: This wizard was proficient with dark magic, being a Dark Wizard.
Duelling: This wizard was an accomplished duellist who was skilled with martial magic.
