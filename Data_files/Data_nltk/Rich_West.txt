Rich West was a Muggle musician. He released at least one album, Driving It Home, which was available for sale at Record Shop on Charing Cross Road in London.


== Behind the scenes ==
The name of this character is apparently a tribute to Rich West, who worked as the art director/show set designer for The Wizarding World of Harry Potter theme park in Orlando, Florida from 2012 to 2014.


== Appearances ==
The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (name appears on album cover)
