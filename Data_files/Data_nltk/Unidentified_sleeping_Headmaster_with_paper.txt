This wizard was appointed Headmaster of Hogwarts at some unknown time. After his death, his portrait was hung in the Headmaster's office, in order to give advice to the current head.


== Behind the scenes ==
In Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film), this headmaster's portrait is found nearly at ground level to the left of the Headmaster's desk. At The Making of Harry Potter his portrait is near the top of the wall above that of Fytherley Undercliffe.
