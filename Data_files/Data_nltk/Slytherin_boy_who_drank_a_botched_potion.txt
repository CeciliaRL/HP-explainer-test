This wizard was a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1980s who was sorted into Slytherin. He had Potions class with Jacob's sibling.


== Biography ==
This individual started attending Hogwarts on 1 September 1984 and was sorted into Slytherin House.
The individual frequently drank various botched potions in Potions classes, taught by Professor Severus Snape.


== Behind the scenes ==
Sometimes this character is replaced by Barnaby Lee in class segments.
