Heathcote Barbary (b. 1974) was a wizard and the rhythm guitarist for the popular wizarding band the Weird Sisters. On 25 December 1994, he performed at the Yule Ball along with the rest of his band at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.


== Behind the scenes ==
Barbary was portrayed by Jason Buckle in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.
If he attended Hogwarts, he would have been in school between 1985 and 1992, which means he would have been in his seventh year when Harry Potter was in his first year.
