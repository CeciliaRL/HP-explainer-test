Babajide Akingbade was an African wizard and Uagadou graduate who succeeded Albus Dumbledore as the Supreme Mugwump of the International Confederation of Wizards in 1995, when the latter was voted out by British Ministry wizards after he made a speech announcing Lord Voldemort's return.


== Etymology ==
Babajide came from a Yoruba words "baba" meaning "father" and "dziade" meaning "raise from the dead". Babajide means "father has come to life again" or "father has returned".
Akingbade is a Yoruba surname meaning "strong/brave one who wears the crown".
