Gordon Horton was a wizard who played as the Keeper for the Quidditch team the Chudley Cannons.


== Biography ==
He was attacked and injured by a magical beast at the Chudley Cannons' training grounds. He was sent to St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries to be treated by a Healer. Omar Abasi concluded that he had a mild concussion from his fall.
It was later discovered that he had been attacked by a Hippogriff nesting with its young in the nearby Old Bell Tower.
