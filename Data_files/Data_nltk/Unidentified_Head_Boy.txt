This student was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the late 20th century. He was appointed the Head Boy.


== Biography ==
This wizard began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1979. In his Seventh year, he was appointed the Head Boy. He dated the Head Girl, which might have been an unusual event.


== Possessions ==
Head Boy badge: He was awarded one of these special badges when he was made Head Boy at Hogwarts, for which he would have worn during his formal duties.
