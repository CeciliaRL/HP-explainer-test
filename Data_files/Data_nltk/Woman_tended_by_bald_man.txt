This individual was a witch who fought during the Battle of Hogwarts in 1998. She was tended by a bald teacher. It is unknown if she survived the battle.


== Behind the scenes ==
The bald teacher could be her husband.
She was played by an unknown actress in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
