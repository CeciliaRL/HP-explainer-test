Valmai Morgan was a Welsh witch who played as the Chaser for the Welsh all-female Quidditch team, the Holyhead Harpies.
She was a Chaser for the team who replaced Wilda Griffiths on the team, after the latter left for a 1000 Galleon fee to join Puddlemere United.
Valmai started on a high note, scoring ten goals on her first game.
