== Etymology ==
Aamir is a male given name of Indian origin, meaning "full", "populous" and "prosperous". Also used to refer to a prince, or ruler.  
The meaning of 'Loonat' is unknown.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
Based on his name, it can be presumed he is of Indian descent.
