This individual was a wizard who possibly lived at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. In 1995 he insulted the Fat Lady, calling her fat and ugly.


== Behind the scenes ==
If he had a portrait at Hogwarts he may have died prior to 1995 as many portraits hung at Hogwarts, depicting deceased individuals at that time.
