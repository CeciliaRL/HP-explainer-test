Ashley Sanders was a witch and a former Captain for the Gryffindor Quidditch team.
By the early 1990s, there was a plaque on the mantlepiece at Gryffindor Tower listing her as a former Gryffindor Quidditch Captain.


== Behind the scenes ==
Ashley Sanders is an artist for PlayStation Home and this naming is a tribute to her.
