Nimbostratus was an employee of Gringotts Wizarding Bank who was apparently responsible for handling transactions. This individual's entry on one particular form was crossed out for an unknown reason.


== Etymology ==
"Nimbostratus" is a term used to refer to low, grey, usually dark clouds that produce rain, snow, and other forms of precipitation.


== Behind the scenes ==
As Gringotts hired both Goblins and wizards as staff, it is unknown what species this individual was.
