This individual was a male giant who fought in the Battle of Hogwarts for Lord Voldemort and his Death Eaters. He was seen in a courtyard close to Greenhouse One in which he slept. He held a blue ice cream, which Ronald Weasley and Hermione Granger took in order to make an Exploding Potion.
