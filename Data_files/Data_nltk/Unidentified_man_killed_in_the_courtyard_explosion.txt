This individual was a wizard who fought during the Battle of Hogwarts in 1998. He was one of the defenders who were stationed by the checkpoint towers entrance to the Viaduct Courtyard. When the towers were attacked he ran into the courtyard but was killed by either an explosion or the heat from it.
