Martin MacFusty was a Scottish wizard who looked after Hebridean Black dragons alongside his family.


== Behind the scenes ==
If he was educated, he most likely went to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry as the family was based in Scotland which Hogwarts resided in.
