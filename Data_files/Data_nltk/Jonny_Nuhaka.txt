Jonny Nuhaka was an Australian wizard who played as the Keeper for the Australian National Quidditch team during the four-hundred and twenty-second Quidditch World Cup, in 1994.
During the 1996–1997 school year, Nuhaka's Chocolate Frog Card could be won by beating David Nolton at a Quidditch challenge.


== Etymology ==
Nuhaka is the Māori name for a small settlement in New Zealand's Hawke's Bay Region. It is also the name of a river in the same area.
