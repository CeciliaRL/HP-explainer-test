Rachael Codnor was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
During the 1996–1997 school year, she was usually seen in the Paved Courtyard, challenging other students to games of Wizard Skittles.


== Etymology ==
"Rachael" is from the Hebrew name רָחֵל (Rachel) meaning "ewe".


== Behind the scenes ==
This particular student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
