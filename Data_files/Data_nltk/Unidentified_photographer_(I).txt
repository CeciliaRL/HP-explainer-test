This individual was an American No-Maj who worked as photographer who interviewed a witness who saw a building get destroyed by an Obscurus in New York in 1926.


== Behind the scenes ==
The photographer was portrayed by Matthew Sim in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
