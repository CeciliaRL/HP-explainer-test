This girl was a witch and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. She shared a dormitory with Ginny Wealsey.


== Biography ==


=== Sorting and early years ===
She attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, likely beginning in 1992, and was Sorted into Gryffindor House.


=== Fourth year ===
In her fourth year at Hogwarts, she was awoken one morning a day or two after the first trip to Hogsmeade of the 1995-1996 school year by Ron Weasley attempting to enter the Gryffindor Girls' Dormitory. However, because the staircase had been bewitched to make a loud noise when a boy tried to climb them, it alerted her and her friend to his presence.
