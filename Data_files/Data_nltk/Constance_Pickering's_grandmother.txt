This witch was the grandmother of Constance Pickering.


== Biography ==
She attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in her youth and was a member of Hufflepuff house.
When Constance was ten years old, her grandmother came over for Christmas. She had a badger brooch, that Constance pilfered to play with. In punishment, Constance spent the rest of Christmas locked in the attic.


== Possessions ==
Badger brooch: She owned a brooch in the shape of a badger, the mascot of Hufflepuff House.
