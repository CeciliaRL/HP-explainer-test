Mrs Stevens was a resident in Northumberland. She witnessed an explosion on a Saturday morning while chatting with Ms Ross and a Muggle. She was mentioned by her friend when he was interviewed by (unknown to him) a delegation from the British Ministry of Magic.
The cause of the explosion was later revealed to be a Erumpent horn that had been dropped by a wizard. It's unknown if Mrs Stevens, if not secretly a witch herself, knew enough about the incident that she had to be Obliviated, although the friend who mentioned her was.
