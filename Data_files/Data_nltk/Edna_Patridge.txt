Edna Patridge was a witch and Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
She could often be found walking around in the Tapestry Corridor during the 1996–1997 school year.


== Ety​mology ==
The name "Edna" means "pleasure" in Hebrew.
