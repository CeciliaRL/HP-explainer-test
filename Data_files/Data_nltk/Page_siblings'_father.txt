Mr Page was a Muggle who married a witch and had three children. They had a a daughter and two sons, squib Reggie and wizard Daniel.
He was involved in illicit situations, and he involved his elder son in such activities. They were eventually caught by Muggle police, and his wife tried to use her magic to help the two escape. She frightened the police and violated the International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy in doing so.
Her efforts didn't help, and she was sentenced to Azkaban while he and his son were taken to a Muggle prison. The incident was put in a Muggle newspaper under the heading, "The Bloody Pages". His younger son, Daniel was put under the guardianship of his daughter.
