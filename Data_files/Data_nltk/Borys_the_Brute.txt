Borys the Brute was a  hypnotist in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
He was part of the Circus Arcanus, a travelling freak show, which billed him as "perfidious" and warned people to "take heed of his artful deception."


== Behind the scenes ==
Considering that the Circus Arcanus was magical, it seems likely that Borys could perform a magical form of hypnosis, possibly similar to that of Wampus cats.
