Aki Sugiyama was a witch and Hufflepuff student who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
She was seen wandering around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Aki is a Japanese female given name which can mean "autumn," "bright," or "sparkle" depending on the character used to write it.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student was only featured in the console version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
