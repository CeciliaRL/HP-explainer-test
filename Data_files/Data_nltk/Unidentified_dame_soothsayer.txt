This dame was a woman who worked as a fortune teller. During her lifetime, she claimed to people that she could tell them their future, for a nickel.


== History ==
In her lifetime, she told people their future. At some point, she worked at a fair and used a crystal ball, and wore a veil. Jacob Kowalski gave her a nickel and she told him his future, but she "missed out on quite a bit" according to him.
