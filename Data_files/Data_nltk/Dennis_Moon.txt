Dennis Moon was a wizard from New Zealand who played as a Chaser for the 2014 New Zealand National Quidditch team. During the first round match of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, against Bulgaria, Moon was sent off by referee Georgios Xenakis. New Zealand lost the match 410 - 170. His manager Charlie Baverstock proclaimed himself 'madder than a bloke who's been locked in a box of Fwoopers' after the sending off.
