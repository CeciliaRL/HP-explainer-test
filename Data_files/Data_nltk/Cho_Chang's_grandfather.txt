Chang was a wizard and Auror who lived in the early 20th century.


== Biography ==
In 1927, he was one of the Aurors led by Theseus Scamander who were summoned to break-up Gellert Grindelwald's rally at the Lestrange Mausoleum in Paris, France.
Chang was Cho Chang's paternal grandfather.


== Behind the scenes ==
He is played by Simon Wan in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.
