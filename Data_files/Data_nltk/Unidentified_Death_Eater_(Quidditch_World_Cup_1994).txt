This Death Eater was a Dark Wizard and a servant of Lord Voldemort.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
He eventually joined the Death Eaters and probably fought in the First Wizarding War. He successfully managed to avoid imprisonment in Azkaban.


=== 1994 ===
In 1994, he attacked the Quidditch World Cup alongside other Death Eaters, however fled the scene when the Dark Mark was conjured in the sky by Bartemius Crouch Junior.
