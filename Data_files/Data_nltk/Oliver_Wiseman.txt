Oliver Wiseman (b. 1954) was a Detective Inspector for the Westminster Anti-Terrorism Unit. He investigated the collapse of the Millennium Bridge in 1996, but refused to give The Guardian any statement about it.


== Behind the scenes ==
The naming is a tribute to the real-life Oliver Wiseman, who served as the assistant to director David Heyman in the final three films of the Harry Potter series.
