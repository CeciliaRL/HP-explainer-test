Mr Goshawk (fl. 1915-1920) was the husband of Mrs Goshawk, and the father of the nine Goshawk sisters, including Diadema, Romilda, Tangwystl and Miranda Goshawk.
This individual had several siblings and, through their marriages, many nieces and nephews.
