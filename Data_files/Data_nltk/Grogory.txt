Saint Grogory was a saint after whom St. Grogory's Primary School was named.


== Behind the scenes ==
St. Grogory is possibly a reference to the real St Gregory: Pope Gregory I. He is considered the patron saint of, among other things, students and teachers, making him a prime candidate to name a school after.
