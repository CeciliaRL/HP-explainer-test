Ellerby was a wizard or witch who worked along with Able Spudmore and founded the Black Forest Broomstick Company Ellerby and Spudmore. They released the Tinderblast in 1940 and then the Swiftstick in 1952.


== Behind the scenes ==
Ellerby's full name and gender are never revealed, and they are only referenced indirectly as the namesake of Ellerby and Spudmore, a broomstick company that is only mentioned in Quidditch Through the Ages.
