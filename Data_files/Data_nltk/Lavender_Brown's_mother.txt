Mrs Brown was a pure-blood witch, wife of Mr Brown and mother of Lavender. In 1993, she and her husband sent a letter to Lavender, informing her that her pet rabbit Binky had been killed by a fox.
Prior to that school year, either Mrs Brown or her husband signed a Hogsmeade permission form for Lavender.
On 2 May, 1998, her daughter died during the Battle of Hogwarts when she was attacked by Fenrir Greyback.


== Behind the scenes ==
Lavender's parents are only mentioned in the French version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
