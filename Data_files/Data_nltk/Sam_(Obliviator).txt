Sam was an American wizard who worked as an Obliviator for the Magical Congress of the United States of America in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In 1926, Sam was tasked with wiping the memory of the No-Maj Jacob Kowalski after he entered the MACUSA headquarters, but was intercepted by Queenie Goldstein. Queenie, a Legilimens, was able to read Sam's mind and see that he was in relationships with two women at the same time, fellow MACUSA employees Ruby and Cecily. Queenie threatened to reveal his infidelity and was thus able to convince him not to Obliviate Jacob.


== Physical appearance ==
Sam had brown hair and wore a navy blue blazer with the MACUSA logo on the right side and a white button-down shirt.


== Behind the scenes ==
Sam was portrayed by Matthew Wilson in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
