Shanice Higgins was a Jamaican witch who played as the Seeker for the 2014 Jamaican National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
During the fourth match of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, USA versus Jamaica, she was beaten to the Snitch by US Seeker Darius Smackhammer, resulting in a narrow victory for the United States.
