This individual served as the Head of Magical Co-operation in the Transylvanian wizarding government.


== History ==
Percy Weasley, during the course of his duties as personal assistant to Bartemius Crouch Snr, Head of the British Department of International Magical Co-operation, had a meeting scheduled with this individual in early 1995 to persuade him to sign the International Ban on Duelling.
