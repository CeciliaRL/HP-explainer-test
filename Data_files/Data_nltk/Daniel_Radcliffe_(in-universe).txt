Daniel Radcliffe (fl. 1997) was a Muggle actor who had a large part in Equus, a play that was staged sometime in the late 1990s. There was a poster mentioning him at Luchino Caffe in the summer of 1997.


== Behind the scenes ==
This mention is an obvious reference to Daniel Radcliffe, the actor who played Harry Potter in all the Harry Potter films and who also had a part in the 2007 revival of Equus. It was put on the set as a little inside joke.
