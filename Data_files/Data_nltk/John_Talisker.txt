John Talisker was a British wizard who was a Captain for the Scottish National Quidditch team and a SOS Task Force member in the 2010s.
During an international Quidditch friendly against the Irish National Quidditch team, he learned that the entire Irish team were left-handed, which inspired a tactical change for Scotland.
