This waitress was a female Muggle employee at Treats Café, a café in Surbiton Station in 1996. She flirted with Harry Potter during the summer of 1996 and came close to discovering the wizarding world.


== Biography ==
 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			Harry Potter reads the Daily Prophet in the café 		 	 
She flirted with Harry Potter in Treats Café, at Surbiton Station, at some point in the summer of 1996. Harry was a regular at the café in which she worked as he found taking trains kept his mind off things and would often spend nights there. She almost became aware of the existence of the Wizarding world when Harry allowed her to see a copy of the Daily Prophet with moving images, but she brushed off her suspicions due to working late.


== Behind the scenes ==
The waitress was portrayed by Elarica Gallacher in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
The waitress was not in the book, nor was the entire scene at Treats Café.
She was also included in LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7 although the restaraunt Treats does not appear, she appears sitting by Harry putting lipstick on her lips. She is a playable character only in the consoles versions.
