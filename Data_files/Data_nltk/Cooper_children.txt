The children of Mr Cooper became a target of their uncle, Buckley Cooper, who wanted to get revenge for the cursed broomstick he had been given by their father. In a letter to the Daily Prophet's advice column, Buckley asked whether he would be able to get away with kidnapping the children and transforming them into vampire ferrets, and was told that he would not.
