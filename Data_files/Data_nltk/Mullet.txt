Mullet was an Irish witch who played as the Chaser for the Irish National Quidditch team during the 1994 Quidditch World Cup. She played during the 1994 Quidditch World Cup final against Bulgaria, which the Irish team won. She won her side a penalty when Bulgarian Keeper, Lev Zograf fouled her by cobbing.


== Etymology ==
A "mullet" is a hairstyle and a kind of fish.
