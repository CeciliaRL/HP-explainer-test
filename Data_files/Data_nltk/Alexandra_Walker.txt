Alexandra Walker makes headlines 		 	 
Alexandra Walker was a witch. She was charged with attempted robbery of Ollivanders in the early summer of 1996; this was recorded in the Daily Prophet.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Alexandra Walker, an art director on Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, as well as the two films preceding it.
