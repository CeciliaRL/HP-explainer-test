This boy was an American No-Maj who lived in New York in the early 20th century.


== History ==
In 1926, the boy went to the Second Salem Church, where poor children were fed in exchange for distributing leaflets. While he was waiting in line for soup, another boy asked New Salem Philanthropic Society leader Mary Lou Barebone if the birthmark on his face was a "witch's mark," to which she replied that it was not.


== Behind the scenes ==
The boy was portrayed by Leo Heller in the film Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
