Reiko Shibazaki was a witch and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Reiko is a female given name of Japanese origin. 麗 (rei) means "lovely" combined with the popular suffix 子 (ko) "child".


== Behind the scenes ==
Given her name, it can be presumed that she is of Japanese descent.
