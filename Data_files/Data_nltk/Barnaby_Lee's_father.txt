Barnaby Lee's father was a Dark Wizard who was a Death Eater during the First Wizarding War.


== Biography ==
He once owned a pet Kneazle, which Lord Voldemort drowned. One time he and his son were exterminating Pixies at their house, and he yelled at Barnaby for crying. Mr Lee also always used to say he ought to get his son some brains for his birthday, clearly showing him as a rather cruel and abusive father.
Mr. Lee was also acquainted with Horace Slughorn.
