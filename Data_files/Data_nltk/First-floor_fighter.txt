The first-floor fighter was a Dark Wizard who fought for the Death Eaters during the Battle of Hogwarts in the First-floor corridor, and later in the Onslaught at the Viaduct where he charged the castle with the giants.


== Biography ==
Born sometime in or prior to 1981, to a pure-blood or half-blood family, the individual was trained somehow in the magical arts. Eventually, he decided to join Lord Voldemort and the Death Eaters as one of his loyal servants.
On 2 May, 1998, he was called to arms by the Death Eaters to participate in Voldemort's attempt to capture Hogwarts School. He was seen fighting in the First-floor corridor and later at the Viaduct.
During the battle, he was hit with an offensive spell by Ronald Weasley, that either killed or incapacitated him.


== Physical appearance ==
He was a thickset man with wavy black hair, a pale face with a pointy nose, and wore black robes. He wore a silvery glove.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Dark Arts: To have been accepted into the ranks of the Death Eaters, he must have been a skilled and accomplished Dark Wizard and with dark magic. He was most likely capable of casting the Unforgivable Curses, along with other dark charms and spells. 
Duelling: In the Battle of Hogwarts, he fought in the First-floor corridor, and survived to go onto the Onslaught at the Viaduct, showing him to be a competent duellist with a considerable degree of skill in martial magic.
