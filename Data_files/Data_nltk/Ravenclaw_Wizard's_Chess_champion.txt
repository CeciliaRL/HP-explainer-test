The Ravenclaw Wizard's Chess champion, as the title suggests, refers to the Wizard's Chess Captain for Ravenclaw House, at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.


== Biography ==
This girl came to be educated at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Ravenclaw House. Her Head of House was Professor Filius Flitwick, the Charms Master, and she shared a dormitory with four other girls in her year in the Ravenclaw Tower. She was skilled in Wizard's Chess and could be usually found playing in the Library.
