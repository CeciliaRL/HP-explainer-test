Mrs Flume was a British witch and the co-owner of Honeydukes alongside her husband Ambrosius Flume.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
Mrs Flume was born a witch and possibly attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry as her husband did. At some point in her life, Mrs Flume married Ambrosius Flume.


=== Honeydukes ===
By 1993, the couple lived in a flat above Honeydukes Sweetshop and ran together said shop. In 1993, during one of the weekend trips to Hogsmeade, Mrs Flume asked her husband to get some Jelly Slugs, as the shop was filled with Hogwarts students.


=== Battle of Hogwarts ===
It is possible that Mrs Flume participated in the Battle of Hogwarts alongside her husband and other residents of Hogsmeade village, although this is unconfirmed.
