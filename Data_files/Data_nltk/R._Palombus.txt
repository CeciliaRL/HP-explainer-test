R. Palombus was an American wizard or witch and reporter for The New York Ghost in the 1920s.


== History ==
Palombus wrote an article about the questioning of "suspicious wizards" by MACUSA that was published in the 6 December 1926 sunset issue of the newspaper. This was followed by a report by Palombus in the next day's sunset issue about how MACUSA was cooperating with the International Confederation of Wizards to crack down on "illegal wizards."
