This goblin worked at Gringotts Wizarding Bank during the wizarding management in 1997-1998 after the Ministry fell to the Death Eaters.


== Biography ==


=== 1998 ===
He was present at the bank when Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, Ron Weasley, and Griphook hosted a break-in in order to retrieve one of Lord Voldemort's Horcruxes, Helga Hufflepuff's cup from the Lestrange Vault.


=== Later life ===
It is unknown what happened to the goblin after the break-in but he may have been killed.


== Behind the scenes ==
The goblin was played by an unknown actor in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
