Guillory was a seemingly influential wizard during the 1920s. In 1927, Le Cri de la Gargouille reported that he had asked the French people not to join the army of Gellert Grindelwald.


== Behind the scenes ==
The name "Guillory" was inscribed onto a desk on Leta Lestrange's desk at Hogwarts. It is possible that this is the same Guillory or a relative.
