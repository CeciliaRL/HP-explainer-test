E. Lambini was an American wizard and sports reporter for The New York Ghost in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
They wrote an article about wizarding interest in the No-Maj game baseball that was published in the 6 December 1926 sunset issue of the newspaper. This was followed by an article by them in the next day's sunset edition examining whether soccer was the No-Maj equivalent of Quidditch.
