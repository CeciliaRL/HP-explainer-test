This girl was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was sorted into Hufflepuff in a unknown year. In 1995, she, along with Cho Chang and Fred and George Weasley, watched Argus Filch hammer notices into a wall, displaying Dolores Umbridge's Educational Decrees.
