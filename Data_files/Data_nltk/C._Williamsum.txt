C. Williamsum was an American wizard or witch and reporter for The New York Ghost in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
Williamsum wrote an article about a "sinister" aura puzzling the wizarding community that was published in the 6 December 1926 sunset issue of the paper. In the 7 December sunset edition, they co-wrote a report with M. Carneirus and R. Amorim about top Aurors being called to New York City Hall to investigate the death of Henry Shaw Junior, which was published on the front page.
