Limus Filhous was a wizard who worked as a reporter and columnist for the Daily Prophet. In 1927, Limus wrote an editorial entitled "Grindewald Escape: No conclusions yet pledge the I.C.W.".


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Eduardo Lima, a graphic artist who has worked on every Harry Potter film since Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
