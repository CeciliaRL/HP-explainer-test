Mrs Vane was the mother of Emma Vane. She signed the permission slip that was required for her daughter to visit Hogsmeade village on weekends in 1993.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is mentioned only on a prop permission slip that was featured in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
