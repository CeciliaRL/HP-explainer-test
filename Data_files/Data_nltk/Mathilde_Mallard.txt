Mathilde Mallard was a French witch famous for being a Chaser for the French National Quidditch team during the four-hundred and twenty-second Quidditch World Cup, in 1994.


== Etymology ==
Mathilde is an alternative name for Matilda.
Mallard is a common type of duck.
