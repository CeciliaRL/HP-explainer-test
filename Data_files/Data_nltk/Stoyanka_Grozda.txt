Stoyanka Grozda was a Bulgarian witch and a Chaser for the 2014 Bulgarian National Quidditch team.


== Etymology ==
Stoyanka is a name derived from Old Bulgarian стоити [stoiti], "to stand" or "to stay", equivalent and cognate to Constantine, given to a child as a blessing for a long life.
Grozda is a female given name derived from грозд [grozd], "grape cluster".


== Appearances ==
Pottermore (First mentioned)


== Behind the scenes ==
Grozda is a female given name and not a surname. The closest surname equivalent would be Grozdanova.
