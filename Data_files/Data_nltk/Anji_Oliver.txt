Anji Oliver was a Muggle from Ilford in Essex, who witnessed the Millennium Bridge disaster in 1996 and managed to film it on her mobile phone. Her footage showed blasts being fired onto the bridge, from multiple directions, with no identifiable source.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Anji Oliver, who worked as a producer coordinator on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
