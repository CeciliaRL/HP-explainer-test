Salomé Lestrange (née Volant) was a witch and a member of the pure-blood Lestrange family. She married Falco Lestrange and had two daughters with him, Eglantine and Leonie.


== Etymology ==
Salomé is an Aramaic name which was related to the Hebrew word שָׁלוֹם (shalom) meaning "peace".
