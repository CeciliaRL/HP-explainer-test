Ladislaw Zamojski (pl. Władysław Zamojski) was a Polish wizard who played as a Chaser for the Polish National Quidditch team. He was described as the best Chaser in Poland.
As of 2014, Zamojski did not play for the Polish National Quidditch team any more.


== Etymology ==
Ladislaw means "famous ruler" in Czech. Zamojski is a Polish surname meaning "de Zamość".
