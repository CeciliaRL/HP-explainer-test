Mrs Warren was a Muggle and the mother of Myrtle Elizabeth Warren, a Muggle-born witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where she was killed by a Basilisk on 13 June, 1943. She and her husband visited Hogwarts shortly after the tragic event occurred.
