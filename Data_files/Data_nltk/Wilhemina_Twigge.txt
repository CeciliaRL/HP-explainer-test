Wilhemina Twigge was a witch from Bohemia and the namesake of the Wilhemina Twigge Award.
A portrait of Wilhemina Twigge, painted in 1610, hung at Hogwarts Castle, in the Grand Staircase: she was depicted standing by a table with a crystal ball, in a yellow dress and wide pleated Medici collar, holding her wand.


== Behind the scenes ==
The painting depicting Wilhemina Twigge featured in the Harry Potter films was based on a c. 1610 painting of Princess Elizabeth, Queen of Bohemia and Electress Palatine by Robert Peake the Elder.
