Gwendoline Hedgeflower was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s and was Sorted into Hufflepuff house.
She participated and fought alongside most of her fellow Hogwarts students in the Battle of Hogwarts against Lord Voldemort and his army.


== Biography ==


=== Hogwarts years ===
 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			Gwendoline celebrating the Weasley twins' departure with fellow students 		 	 
During the 1995–1996 school year, she appeared in Harry's vision of Voldemort at the King's Cross station. Later that year, she celebrated in the Entrance Courtyard as the Weasley twins departed from Hogwarts.
During the 1997–1998 school year, Gwendoline continued to attend Hogwarts under Severus Snape's regime, implying that she was half-blood or pure-blood.

 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			Gwendoline during the Battle of Hogwarts 		 	 
On 2 May, 1998, she participated in the Battle of Hogwarts. After the Ousting of Severus Snape, Gwendoline was seen running up the Grand Staircase alongside Harry Potter. She stood next to Luna Lovegood and Draco Malfoy in the Entrance Courtyard during Voldemort's speech. It is unknown if she survived the battle.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Duelling: Gwendoline participated in the Battle of Hogwarts, fighting against Death Eaters, meaning she had a level of confidence and skill in duelling.


== Behind the scenes ==
Gwendoline Hedgeflower was portrayed by Rachel Lin in film adaptations Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, Half-Blood Prince, and Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
