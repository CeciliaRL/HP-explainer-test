Bagshot was either a Hogwarts student or an individual who visited Hogwarts Castle in the winter of 1993. His or her name appeared on the Marauder's Map.


== Behind the scenes ==
This person may have been the famous magical historian Bathilda Bagshot or a relative of hers.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film) (appears on the Marauder's Map)
