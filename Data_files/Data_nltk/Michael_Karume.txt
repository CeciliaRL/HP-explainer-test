Michael Karume was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
He told Harry Potter that he could find lost Gobstones in bushes during the 1996–1997 school year.


== Etymology ==
The name "Michael" comes from the Hebrew name מִיכָאֵל (Mikha'el) meaning "who is like God?". This is a rhetorical question, implying no person is like God.


== Behind the scenes ==
This particular student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
