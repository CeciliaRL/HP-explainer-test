This man was the manager of wizarding singer Celestina Warbeck. They married at some point and had one son together. However, after ten years of marriage, Celestina left him for Irving Warble.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is unknown who received custody of their child, or whether this man remained Celestina's manager after the divorce.
