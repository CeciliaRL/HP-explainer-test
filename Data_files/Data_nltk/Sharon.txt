Sharon was a witch who had a portrait of herself hung somewhere in Hogwarts Castle by the 1991-1992 school year.


== Behind the scenes ==
This portrait appears in the PC version of the video game adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone. In the game's files, it was called Sharon.bmp.
