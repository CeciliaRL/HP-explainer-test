This individual was a woman who lived in 20th century in Brazil. She had at least one granddaughter, Alanza Alves.
According to Alanza, her grandmother always said arriving without an invitation was impolite.


== Behind the scenes ==
She was possibly a witch, as Alanza said she came from a prominent wizarding family.
