Stuart Jones was a resident of number 17 Lanes End, Millburn, Dorset.


== Behind the scenes ==
The fictitious Stuart Jones is only mentioned on the F.A.Q. section of J. K. Rowling's official site, where a letter addressed to J. K. Rowling is stated to have been sent by him.
