Mr Limunus was a wizarding astrologer. He was writing the horoscope column for The Daily Prophet by 1997.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Eduardo Lima, a graphic artist who has worked on every Harry Potter film from Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban onward.


== Appearances ==
