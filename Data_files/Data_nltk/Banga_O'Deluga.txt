Banga O'Deluga was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.

== Etymology ==
The name "Banga" is a Shona name meaning "sword". Today, the name is most common in Zimbabwe, and other areas of Southern Africa.
