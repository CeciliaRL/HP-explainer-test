Magenta Comstock (1895–1991) was a witch and experimental artist, whose portraits' eyes not only followed the viewer around the room, but also followed them home.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
Magenta Comstock was born in 1895 to parents of unknown blood status.
She became an artist and began experimenting with her artwork. Her portraits were known for their staring eyes which would often follow viewers home.
She was on a Chocolate Frog Card by 1988.
At some time before or during the 1988–1989 school year, a book entitled Collected Artwork by Magenta Comstock was published.


=== Death ===
Comstock died in 1991 at the age of 96.


== Behind the scenes ==
Magenta Comstock was the Wizard of the Month on J. K. Rowling's official site in April 2007.
She died the year Harry Potter and his friends began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
