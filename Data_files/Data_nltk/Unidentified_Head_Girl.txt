This student was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the late 20th century. She was appointed the Head Girl.


== Biography ==
This witch began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1979. In her Seventh year, she was appointed the Head Girl. She dated the Head Boy, which might have been an unusual event.


== Possessions ==
Head Girl badge: She was awarded one of these special badges when she was made Head Girl at Hogwarts, for which she would have worn during her formal duties.
