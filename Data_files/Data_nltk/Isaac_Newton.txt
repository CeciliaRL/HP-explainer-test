== Behind the scenes ==
In real life, Isaac Newton spent a great deal of time studying alchemy, writing and transcribing around a million words on the subject over the course of his lifetime. J. K. Rowling briefly mentioned him on  Pottermore as one of the real historical figures who sought to make a Philosopher's Stone.
