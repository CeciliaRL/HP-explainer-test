This wizard was appointed Headmaster of Hogwarts at some unknown time. After his death, his portrait was hung in the Headmaster's office, in order to give advice to the current head. This wizard's hair is in the style of portraits painted during the reign of King Charles II of England (1630 - 1685). He was portrayed sleeping on an chair with a book in hand.
