Mrs Hesleden was the mother of Hogwarts student Susanna Hesleden.


== Behind the scenes ==
This particular individual was only featured in the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
If she attended Hogwarts, it is likely she was in Ravenclaw, as her daughter was in this house.
