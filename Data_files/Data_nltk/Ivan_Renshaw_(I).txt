Ivan Renshaw was a student who began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry between 1987 and 1989.


== Biography ==
By the 1993-1994 school year, he had been made a Prefect, and was tasked with patrolling areas of the school off limits to students.


== Etymology ==
Ivan is the new Slavic form of John. It means Yahweh (Herbrew God) is gracious.


== Appearances ==
