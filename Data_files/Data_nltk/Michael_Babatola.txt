Michael Babatola was a British Muggle nurse who worked at St David's Hospital.


== Biography ==
Babatola was an important eyewitness in an investigation led by Mathilda Grimblehawk and her partner, concerning the disappearance of Bilius Finbok, a Ministry of Magic employee.
