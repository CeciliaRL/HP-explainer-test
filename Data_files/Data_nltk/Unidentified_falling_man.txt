This man was a wizard who attended the 1994 Quidditch World Cup in August of 1994.


== Biography ==
Born sometime prior to 1994, he attended the 1994 Quidditch World Cup, supporting Bulgaria. He attempted to flee when the Death Eaters came and began levitating people and burning tents, but was pushed back, falling to the ground and screaming. Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger witnessed the man's falling as they emerged from their tent, but they did not try to help him. It is unknown what happened to him; he either managed to escape, was trampled by the crowd, or perhaps was killed by the Death Eaters.
