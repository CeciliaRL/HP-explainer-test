This headless ghost resided at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. In 1991, it was seen in the Great Hall, just standing. He visited the Grey Lady in the school's library, riding around on a horse. In 1993, he was seen chasing another ghost to get his head back.


== Behind the scenes ==
It's possible this ghost was Patrick Delaney-Podmore or another member of the Headless Hunt.
