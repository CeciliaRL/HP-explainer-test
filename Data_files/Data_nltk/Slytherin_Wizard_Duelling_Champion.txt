The Slytherin Wizard Duelling Champion as the title suggests, refers to the duelling champion for the Slytherin house. He is a selected student from the Slytherin house to represent the house in the Founder Duels.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
This boy attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s and was sorted into Slytherin House. His Head of House was Severus Snape, the Potions Master, and he shared a dormitory with four boys in his year in the Slytherin Dungeon, which is located below ground level.
