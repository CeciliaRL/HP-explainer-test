Martha Steward was a British Muggle. She was the mother of James Steward, who co-founded Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry with his wife, Isolt Sayre.


== Biography ==
Martha and her husband had at least one child, James Steward. They came from Britain. James left and went with the Plymouth settlement to the New World where he married a witch, Isolt Sayre and had two daughters, Martha Steward and Rionach Steward. Martha died sometime before 1632, since her granddaughter was said to have been named after her late grandmother.
