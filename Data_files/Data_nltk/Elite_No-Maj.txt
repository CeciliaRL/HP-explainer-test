This Elite No-Maj was an American No-Maj who was a New York socialite in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
This individual was one of the attendees of Henry Shaw Junior's Fundraising Dinner on the 6 December, 1926. During the dinner, he witnessed the sudden and horrific death of Henry Shaw Junior at the hands of Credence Barebone's Obscurial form, in which he and all of the No-Majs present witnessed magic with their own eyes.


== Behind the scenes ==
Elite No-Maj was portrayed by David Decio in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
