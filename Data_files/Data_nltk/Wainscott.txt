Nurse Wainscott was a witch who worked as a nurse at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry under Poppy Pomfrey during the 1990s. She attended to the fighters' injuries in the Great Hall during the Battle of Hogwarts.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
Little is known of Nurse Wainscott's early life; however, she was born presumably in Great Britain, sometime before 1981.


=== School and later career ===
She somehow learned magic, presumably at Hogwarts, though it is unknown exactly where or when. After having done so, she began her profession as a nurse at Hogwarts, working under Madam Poppy Pomfrey.


=== Battle of Hogwarts ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			Nurse Wainscott, her peers, and the Order of the Phoenix prepare for battle 		 	 
In 1998, Wainscott attended the Battle of Hogwarts, attending to the various injuries of the fighters; it is possible that she fought as well, though this is is unknown. It is also unknown if Wainscott survived the battle, but if she did participate in actual combat and simply cared for the wounded, it is likely she survived.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Healing magic: As a Nurse at Hogwarts School, Wainscott would have had to have been accomplished in the field of medical magic, such as with various magical health treatments, such as healing spells, potions and other magical and non-magical remedies and medications. She tended to the injured and wounded during the Battle of Hogwarts, showing she was a fully competent Healer.


== Behind the scenes ==
In Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2, Nurse Wainscott was portrayed by Pauline Stone.
