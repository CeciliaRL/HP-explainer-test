Gabriella Mather was a witch reported missing along with her relative, Emily, on the Potterwatch radio programme in 1997. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Etymology ==
The name "Gabriella" comes from the Hebrew name גַבְרִיאֵל (Gavri'el) meaning "strong man of God".


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of James Mather, who worked as a supervising sound editor on every Harry Potter film from Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix onward.
