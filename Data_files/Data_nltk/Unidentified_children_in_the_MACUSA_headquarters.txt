This group of wizarding children visited the Magical Congress of the United States of America headquarters in December 1926.


== History ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			These children exploring MACUSA 		 	 
In December 1926, these children visited the MACUSA headquarters and were shown around by a female guide, who gave them yellow pamphlets.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible these children were students from Ilvermorny, however they also could have been visiting from a different wizarding school.
