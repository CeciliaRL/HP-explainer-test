This individual was a wizard whose portrait hung at Hogwarts Castle in the 1990s, opposite the Fat Lady and next to a portrait of a group of journeying wizards. He was depicted wearing green robes, a ponted hat, a ruff around his neck, and a broomstick on his left hand.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Wizard was portrayed by Sean Francis George in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
