Eldred Worple (fl. 1970-1997) was a wizard and writer who was once a favourite student of Horace Slughorn. He studied and lived with vampires, even becoming friends with one named Sanguini, and wrote Blood Brothers: My Life Amongst the Vampires.


== Biography ==
Worple attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in his youth, where he became a favourite student of Professor Horace Slughorn, who made him a member of his elite Slug Club.
On 20 December 1996, Worple attended a Slug Club Christmas party at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and brought Sanguini as a guest. There, Professor Slughorn introduced them to Harry Potter. Worple tried to convince Harry to let him write his biography citing the money to be made, but Harry who was not fond of fame and already had money, declined, and quickly went to speak to his friend Hermione Granger. Worple also tried to distract Sanguini from some young women's necks by shoving a pasty in his hand.


== Physical appearance ==
Worple was described as "small, stout, and bespectacled".
