The unidentified spectators sitting amongst Hogwarts staff Filius Flitwick, Irma Pince, Severus Snape, and Pomona Sprout, along with Lucius Malfoy 		 	 
In November 1992, a number of unidentified spectators attended the Quidditch match between the Gryffindor and Slytherin Quidditch teams.


== Individuals ==


=== Spectator behind Filius Flitwick ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a wizard with grey hair and a beard. During the match, he sat behind Filius Flitwick and beside another wizard. He appluaded after the Slytherin Quidditch team scored, and was extremely shocked after Draco Malfoy was injured.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


=== Spectator behind Irma Pince ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a wizard with brown hair. During the match, he sat behind Irma Pince and another witch, and beside a white wizard and an Indian wizard.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


=== Spectator behind Lee Jordan ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a wizard with brownish hair. During the match, he was seated behind Lee Jordan, next to Albus Dumbledore and Gilderoy Lockhart. He applauded after the Slytherin Quidditch team scored, and was extremely shocked when Draco Malfoy was injured.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


=== Spectator behind Minerva McGonagall ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual (fl. 1992) was a wizard with grey hair. During the match, he sat behind Lee Jordan, the commentator, and Professor Minerva McGonagall and next to a brown-haired wizard.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


=== Spectator beside Pomona Sprout ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a witch with black hair, a blue hat and matching robes. During the match, she sat beside Pomona Sprout and a witch in black, and was extremely shocked after Draco Malfoy was injured.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


=== Spectator behind Pomona Sprout ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a wizard with black hair, a black and red hat and a black cloak. During the match, he sat behind Pomona Sprout and next to an Indian wizard.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


=== Spectator behind moustached wizard ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a wizard who wore black robes. During the match, he sat next to an old witch in blue, behind a moustached man and in front of a wizard who supported the Slytherin Quidditch team.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


=== Spectator beside Gilderoy Lockhart ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a witch with black hair and brown clothes. During the match, she sat next to Gilderoy Lockhart and Albus Dumbledore, and applauded politely after the Slytherin Quidditch team scored.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)

=== Slytherin-supporting spectator ===

=== Moustached spectator ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a wizard with black hair and a moustache. During the match, he sat next to an old witch in blue, behind a white-bearded wizard and in front of another man.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


=== Oriental spectator ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual (fl. 1992) was an Oriental witch with black hair, a green and gold hat and matching robes. During the match, she sat near Albus Dumbledore, next to an elderly wizard and in front of a moustached man.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film) (First appearance)


== Behind the scenes ==
Other unidentified individuals who also attended this match:
White-bearded Hogwarts employee: An elderly wizard with white hair and a beard who, during the match, sat by Lee Jordan and an Asian witch, and in front of an elderly witch in blue and a man with a moustache.
Elder female Duelling Club attendant: A witch in blue who, during the match, sat beside Albus Dumbledore and another wizard. She applauded politely when the Slytherin Quidditch team scored.
Black-haired female Hogwarts employee: A woman in brown clothes who, during the match, sat beside Albus Dumbledore and another wizard. She applauded politely when the Slytherin Quidditch team scored and spoke with a man who obviously supported Slytherin.
