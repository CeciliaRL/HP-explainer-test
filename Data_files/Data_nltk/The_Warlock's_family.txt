These individuals were family of The Warlock of the popular wizarding tale The Warlock's Hairy Heart.


== Biography ==

The elderly couple would ridicule their son for his aloof nature and inability to love, unaware that he used Dark Arts to remove his heart from his body. In due time, the couple died, to which their son was happy, as he inherited their castle and would transfer his heart to the depths of the dungeons.
