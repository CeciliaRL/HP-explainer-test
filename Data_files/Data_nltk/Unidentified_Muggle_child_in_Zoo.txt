This individual was a female Muggle child who visited the Zoo on 23 June, 1991. She was frightened by the boa constrictor that was accidentally set free by Harry Potter.


== Behind the scenes ==
The girl is played by Kieri Kennedy in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone.
