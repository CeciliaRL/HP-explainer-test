Ms Ollivander (d. 2011 or earlier) was the daughter of Garrick Ollivander and his wife. She had a brother, who survived her after she died in or before 2011.


== Behind the scenes ==
This individual was only mentioned on the original version of the "Mr Ollivander" article on the old Pottermore.
