Lara Sigg was a magical affairs correspondent for an unidentified German-language wizarding newspaper in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
She wrote an article about how a wizarding family was left stranded when a pretzel serving as a Portkey was accidentally eaten that was published in a 1926 issue of the newspaper.


== Appearances ==
Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film) (Mentioned in a newspaper)
