Tina Lundstrom was a witch who played as Seeker on the Nordic National Quidditch team. She was also the team's Captain.
During the 1996–1997 school year, Lundstrom's Chocolate Frog Card could be won by beating Craig Dunn at a Gobstones challenge.


== Etymology ==
Lundstrøm/Lundström is a Danish and Swedish surname, possibly indicating that Tina was either Danish or Swedish.
