These unnamed Narragansett children were two Native American witches who became two of the first students of Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 17th century. They were part of the Narragansett tribe. 


== Biography ==
Along with their mother and two children of the Wampanoag tribe, they were given wands made by Isolt Sayre and James Steward, and instructed in the use of them and in return they shared their own magical knowledge.
