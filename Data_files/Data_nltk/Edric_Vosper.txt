Edric Vosper was an English wizard who played as a Chaser on the English National Quidditch team during the four-hundred and twenty-second Quidditch World Cup, in 1994. He was immortalised on a Chocolate Frog Card by 1996.


== Etymology ==
The name "Edric" comes from Old English ead, meaning "rich", "blessed" and ric, "ruler". After the Norman conquest this Old English name was not commonly used. It has occasionally been revived in modern times.
