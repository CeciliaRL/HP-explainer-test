Laurence Wakefield  was a Muggle documentary filmmaker. He made several documentary television series, including The Australia Series, which was shown on British television in 1997.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Lauren Wakefield, who worked as graphic artist on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince,  Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.


== Appearances ==
