Luc Millefeuille was a French wizard and pâtissier who became famous for deliberately poisoning Muggles, as a form of Muggle-baiting. In his youth, he attended Beauxbatons Academy of Magic.


== Behind the scenes ==
A mille-feuille (more commonly called a vanilla slice, a cream slice, or a Napoleon, in the United Kingdom) is a pastry comprised of puff pastry, crème pâtissière, and glazed icing, in layers.
