Rutherford Poke was a Healer at and a spokesperson for St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries.


== Biography ==
In 2010, he lamented the incidence of injuries caused by people attempting to use homemade substitutes for Floo Powder, reminding people that the authentic article only cost two Sickles per scoop, and swearing he would refuse to treat the next wizard who came into St Mungo's with a burned bottom.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Healing magic: As a professional Healer, Rutherhood would have been highly adept and accomplished with medical magic, such as with healing spells, potions and other magical remedies.
