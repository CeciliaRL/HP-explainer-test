Britannia as depicted on the reverse of the fifty pence piece, sitting on a lion 		 	 
Britannia was the the female personification of Roman Britain, and is used in recent times to represent the modern Great Britain.
Britannia was depicted on the Dissected Map, as well as the reverse of the British fifty pence piece.
