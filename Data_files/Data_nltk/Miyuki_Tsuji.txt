Miyuki Tsuji was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Miyuki is a Japanese female given name, either meaning "beautiful snow", "deep snow" or "beautiful happiness". Tsuji is likewise Japanese and means "crossroad".


== Behind the scenes ==
Based on her name, she is most likely of Japanese descent.
