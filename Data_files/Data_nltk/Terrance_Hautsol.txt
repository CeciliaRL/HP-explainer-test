Terrance Hautsol was a wizard who owned of several Greenhouses across the globe in the 2010s-2020s.


== Biography ==
At some point during the Calamity, Terrance gives to Calamity Investigators some insight into the nature of plants and growth patterns worldwide, and they immediately turned this information into an increase in the ingredients they were able to produce when using their Greenhouse Charm.
He also showed them a method of casting Greenhouse Charm directly at the planting bed in his London Greenhouse - causing an extended window of effect for the spell.
