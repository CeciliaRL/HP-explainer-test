This Goblin worked at Gringotts Wizarding Bank during the wizard management in 1997-1998, after the Ministry of magic fell to the Death Eaters.


== Biography ==


=== 1998 ===
He was present at the bank when Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, Ronald Weasley, and Griphook hosted a break-in in order to retrieve one of Lord Voldemort's Horcruxes, Helga Hufflepuff's Cup from the Lestrange Vault.


=== Later life ===
It is unknown what happened to the goblin after the break-in, but he may have been killed.


== Behind the scenes ==
This goblin was portrayed by an unknown actor in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2. Despite not being credited, the character appears in a Behind the Scenes feature in disc 2.
