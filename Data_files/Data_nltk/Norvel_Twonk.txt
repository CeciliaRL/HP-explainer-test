Norvel Twonk, O.M. (First Class) (1888 - 1957) was a wizard, who died saving a Muggle child from a Manticore.


== Biography ==


=== Legacy ===
Twonk was posthumously awarded the Order of Merlin, First Class. There was a portrait of Twonk hung at Hogwarts Castle, guarding a secret shortcut between the fifth-floor landing of the Grand Staircase and the Entrance Hall side room. The password required to gain access to said shortcut was "Manticore".


== Behind the scenes ==
If Twonk attended Hogwarts in his youth, he might, depending on the exact date of his birth, have been in the same year as Theseus Scamander.
