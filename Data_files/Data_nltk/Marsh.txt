Madam Marsh was an elderly British witch who seemed to suffer from motion sickness. She frequently rode on the Knight Bus nevertheless, suggesting that she travelled often. 


== Biography ==


=== 1993 ===
In 1993, she travelled on the Knight Bus at the same time as Harry Potter. She got off the bus at Abergavenny looking queasy.


=== 1996 ===
In 1996, Madam Marsh was seen travelling again on the Knight Bus. This time she became sick and vomited. She was let off before Harry, Hermione, and the Weasleys.
