Maxwell Lazenby was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen wandering around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
