This boy was a wizard who went to Diagon Alley at the Weasley Wizard Wheezes in 1996 and bought some Puking Pastilles. Fred and George Weasley who were the shop owners, gave him a cauldron when he was about to vomit.


== Behind the scenes ==
This boy was portrayed by an unknown actor in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
