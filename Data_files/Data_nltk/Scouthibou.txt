Scouthibou was a brown Hogwarts school owl that inhabited the Owlery in the mid-1990s. 


== History ==
In early 1995, Harry Potter sent Sirius Black a letter on the odd events at Hogwarts surrounding Severus Snape and Alastor Moody via this owl.


== Behind the scenes ==
This owl is only named in the French translation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, by Jean-François Ménard. The owl remains unidentified in the original English version.
The suffix "hibou" is French for "owl".
