This individual was a witch and Merula Snyde's aunt. She became her legal guardian after Merula Snyde's mother and father were both sent to Azkaban for being Death Eaters after the First Wizarding War, although rather than living with her, she would let Merula live at the empty house of her parents, although she checked in regularly.


== Behind the scenes ==
She raised Merula to think that black cats are evil and bring bad luck.
