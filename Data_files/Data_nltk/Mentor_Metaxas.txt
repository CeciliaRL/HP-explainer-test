Mentor Metaxas was a Greek wizard and the chairman of the International Confederation of Wizards Quidditch Committee in 2014.


== Biography ==
He attended the final of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, on 11 July, 2014. Shortly before the match started, he was spotted by Daily Prophet reporter Rita Skeeter chatting to the President of the Argentinian Council of Magic, Valentina Vázquez.


== Behind the scenes ==
If this individual's name is indication of anything, he is probably Greek or of Greek descent — the name "Mentor" is an Ancient Greek name that features in Greek Mythology, and the surname Metaxas is also Greek: Greek archicted and marksman Anastasios Metaxas (1862-1937), Greek politician Andreas Metaxas (1790-1860), and Greek general and dictator Ioannis Metaxas (1871–1941) are some notable people who bore this surname.
