Fractcumulus was an employee of Gringotts Wizarding Bank. This individual was responsible for issuing visitor passes allowing people "limited, escorted" access to the bank. They also apparently handled transactions at the bank.


== Behind the scenes ==
"Fractcumulus" is presumably an altered spelling of "fractocumulus." This term is used to describe low, wispy, somewhat bulging clouds which often appear under nimbostratus clouds when it is raining.
The species of this individual is unknown but presumed to be a Goblin or human wizard or witch by some.
