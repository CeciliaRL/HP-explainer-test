George Eliot was the pen name of English Muggle novelist Mary Ann Evans (22 November, 1819 - 22 December, 1880). Bathilda Bagshot owned a copy of her novel The Mill on the Floss.


== Behind the scenes ==
J. K. Rowling has explained that Lily Potter's maiden name Evans was based on George Eliot's real name.
