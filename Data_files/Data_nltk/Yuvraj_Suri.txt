Yuvraj Suri was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.

== Etymology ==
Yuvraj is an Indian title for "Crown Prince." It is a popular name for Sikh and Hindu men in India. Suri is a name which in Persian means "red rose", although it is also used in India.


== Behind the scenes ==
This particular student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
