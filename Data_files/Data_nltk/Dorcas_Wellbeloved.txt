Dorcas Wellbeloved (1812 – 1904) was a witch famous for founding the Society for Distressed Witches. For her charity, she became immortalised on a Chocolate Frog Card.


== Etymology ==
The name "Dorcas" is derived from Greek δορκας (dorkas) meaning "gazelle".
