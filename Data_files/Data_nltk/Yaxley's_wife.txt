This witch was a British witch, who was married to Yaxley. She attended the British Ministry of Magic ball.


== History ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			This witch with Yaxley, Shafiq, and Theseus 		 	 
In 1927, she attended a ball and stood by her husband and Shafiq. She was at that point introduced to Theseus Scamander and shook hands with him.
