This individual was an African wizard who attended Uagadou. He was a skilled potioneer and represented his school at the Wizarding Schools Potions Championship.


== Biography ==


=== Uagadou ===
Like other students, the African wizard was most likely given a token by a Dream Messenger in his sleep for entrance into Uagadou.


=== Wizarding Schools Potions Championship ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			The wizard driven to madness by a Banshee 		 	 
The wizard became a skilled potioneer, and at age 17 or above participated in the Wizarding Schools Potions Championship, where he represented Uagadou, his school. In the championship, he came upon a Banshee, who screamed and drove him to madness. Later, the Hogwarts potions champion used a Laughing Potion on the Banshee to incapacitate it.


== Physical appearance ==
The wizard was dark-skinned with black hair and brown eyes, and wore a white shirt.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Potions: As a skilled potioneer, this potioneer was skilled at making potions.


== Behind the scenes ==
This is the first student from Uagadou introduced throughout the Harry Potter series.
