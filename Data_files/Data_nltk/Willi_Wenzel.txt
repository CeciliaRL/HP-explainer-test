Willi Wenzel was a wizard from Liechtenstein and a Chaser for the 2014 Liechtenstein National Quidditch team. During the fifth match of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, Liechtenstein versus Chad, Wenzel was the man of the match, scoring the third goal from sixty yards even after receiving two Bludgers to the head.
