Mrs Fudge was the wife of former Minister for Magic, Cornelius Fudge. Barty Crouch Snr once suffered an attack of delirium in which he mentioned that his wife and son were to attend a concert with the couple.


== Behind the scenes ==
As Barty Crouch Snr was delirious at the time he mentioned Mrs Fudge, it is possible that she does not exist any more than as a figment of Crouch's delusions.
