Edith was the name of a female individual of unknown origin and species. 


== Etymology ==
"Edith" is a female given name, from the Old English words, and it means 'riches or blessed'.


== Behind the scenes ==
Edith will be portrayed by German actress Ramona Kunze-Libnow in Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore.
