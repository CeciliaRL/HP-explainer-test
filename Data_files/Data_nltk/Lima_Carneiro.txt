Lima Carneiro was a Muggle perfumer who made the fragrance Divine Magic.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character's name first appears on a billboard in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
This character's name is tribute to graphic artist Eduardo Lima, who worked on every Harry Potter film from Prisoner of Azkaban onward, as well as Mauricio Carneiro, costume illustrator from Goblet of Fire onward.
