Eleanora Shanks, also known by the nickname Crazy Lamb, was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In or around 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Shanks stating that she had escaped from the New York Witches' Penitentiary, and was wanted for No-Maj murder. It offered a reward of 1500 Dragots for information leading to her capture.
When Shanks was booked and photographed, she was assigned the MACUSA Reg No 8153, but individuals were informed that she may appear much older as the picture was take some time ago.
