Jo King was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1950s. She was Captain of the Slytherin Quidditch team from 1952 to 1956.


== Behind the scenes ==
This individual's name appears to be a pun on the word "joking".
