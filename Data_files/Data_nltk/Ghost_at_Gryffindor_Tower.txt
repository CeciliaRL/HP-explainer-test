This individual was a wizard in his lifetime, having died of hanging. After his death he returned as a ghost and resided at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. In 1993, he was seen in the Gryffindor Tower, floating through the arriving students. When he walked through Dean Thomas, he reacted as if he had just walked through cobwebs.


== Behind the scenes ==
He may be Edgar Cloggs, Ambrose Swott or a recast of the Fat Friar.
