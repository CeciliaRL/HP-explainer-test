"Stubby" Lamont was the father of the Scottish Seeker Hector Lamont. After their defeat in the final of the 1990 Quidditch World Cup, in which his son missed the Snitch by millimetres, Hector famously blamed Stubby for not giving him longer fingers, in a post-match interview.
