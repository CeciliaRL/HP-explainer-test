Elodie Dembélé (b. 1995 or 1996) was an Ivorian witch who played as a Chaser for the 2014 Ivorian National Quidditch team. During the first match of the 2014 Quidditch World Cup against Norway, Dembélé scored seven of Ivory Coast's ten goals, despite her young age.
