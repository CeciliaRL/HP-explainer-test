This wizard attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1980s, and was a member of either Slytherin or Ravenclaw house. 


== Biography ==
During his time at Hogwarts, he befriended Erika Rath and another witch.


=== 1985–1986 school year ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			This boy during 1985–1986 school year 		 	 
At some point by the 1987–1988 school year, he became the Chaser of his house's Quidditch team.


=== 1987–1988 school year ===

He and other team members got into a confrontation with Jacob's sibling team, after Erika injured Orion Amari in a Quidditch friendly.
After the match with Jacob's sibling's team, which ended in a brawl between the teams, this boy, along with the rest of the players, served detention under the supervision of Rolanda Hooch. The teams managed to prove their dedication to sportsmanship and co-operation and were not disqualified from the competition for the Hogwarts Quidditch Cup.


== Behind the scenes ==
Which house he is in depends on that of Jacob's sibling (chosen by the player). If Jacob's sibling is in Gryffindor, Hufflepuff or Slytherin, this boy is in Ravenclaw. If Jacob's sibling is in Ravenclaw, he is in Slytherin.
