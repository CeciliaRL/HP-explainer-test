Dimitar Draganov was a Bulgarian wizard and a Beater on the 2014 Bulgarian National Quidditch team.


== Etymology ==
Dimitar is a Bulgarian name of Greek origin, stemming from the name of the Greek fertility goddess Demeter.
Draganov is the patronymic-turned-surname derivative of Dragan, a Slavic name originating from "драг" [drag], meaning "dear".
