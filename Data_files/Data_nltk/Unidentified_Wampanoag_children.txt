These unnamed Wampanoag children were two Native American wizards who became one of the first students of Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 17th century. They were part of the Wampanoag tribe.


== Biography ==
Along with a mother and her two daughters of the Narragansett tribe, they were given wands made by Isolt Sayre and James Steward, and instructed in the use of them and in return they shared their own magical knowledge.
