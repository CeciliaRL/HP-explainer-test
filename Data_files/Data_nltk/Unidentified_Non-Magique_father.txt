This unidentified Non-Magique father was a French Non-Magique resident of Paris in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
Together with his wife and child, he lived in an apartment block somewhere in Paris and was murdered by Gellert Grindelwald's acolytes under the orders of Grindelwald himself. They later used the family's home as a temporary headquarters, from where he conducted a search for Credence Barebone.


== Behind the scenes ==
The father was portrayed by an unidentified extra in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.
