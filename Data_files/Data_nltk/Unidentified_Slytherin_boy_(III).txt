This male Slytherin student was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Slytherin House.


== Biography ==
This boy started his magical education at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1991 and was Sorted into Slytherin House. He confronted Harry and Ron, and told them that Gryffindor was going to lose the match to Slytherin and that "Slytherin is Supreme". He managed to stun Ron, but he lost his duel to Harry.
