This witch was the grandmother of Ivy and Winifred Warrington.
When her granddaughters were growing up, Winifred accidentaly Vanished herself. In an effort to help Ivy forget what happened, she put a Memory Charm on her. Unfortunately, it caused Ivy to wholly forget about Winifred.  


== Personality ==
She was a caring, loving woman who couldn't bear to see those she loved in pain.  


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Charms: She cast the Memory Charm on her granddaughter to make her forget that  Winifred vanished to the point that she made her forget Winifred entirely.
