Glen was a wizard who had a portrait of himself hung somewhere in Hogwarts Castle by the 1991-1992 school year.


== Behind the scenes ==
This portrait appeared in the PC version of the video game adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone. In the game files, it was identified as Glen88.bmp.
