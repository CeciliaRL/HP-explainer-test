The Leaky Cauldron bartender was a British witch who worked at the Leaky Cauldron as a bartender.


== Biography ==
She helped during Mathilda Grimblehawk's investigation into an escaped magical beast. She lost a golden tooth in the fray and mistakenly hit a portrait of Ulick Gamp with a Confundus Charm. She later donated meat from the larder to help lure the beast into a trap.
