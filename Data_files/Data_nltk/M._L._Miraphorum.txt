M. L. Miraphorum was a reporter for No-Maj Bureau of The New York Ghost in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
They wrote an article out of New Jersey, published in the 6 December 1926 sunset issue of the newspaper, about a recent series of "mysterious" explosions causing widespread panic among No-Majs in New York. This was followed in the next day's sunset issue by another report by Miraphorum out of New Jersey about how MACUSA was working to protect New York's panicking No-Maj population.
