Katie Killick was a witch reported missing along with her relative, James, on the Potterwatch radio programme in 1997. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Rob Killick, who worked as a sound mix technician on Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, and as a dialogue editor on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
