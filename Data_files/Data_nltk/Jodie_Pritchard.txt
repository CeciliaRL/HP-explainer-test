Jodie Pritchard was a witch who was wrongly credited as having authored the book The Philosophy of the Mundane: Why the Muggles Prefer Not to Know, which was authored by Professor Mordicus Egg.
Alternatively, Jodie Pritchard might just have been the publisher of the 1992 version of the book.
