Simeon Woderuff was a wizard who worked at the Cauldron Workshop in Diagon Alley.


== Biography ==
He ended up disappearing when he was forced to train a Griffin, Tiffin, to guard Vault 619 belonging to Edwin Avarus at Gringotts Wizarding Bank. Due to Tiffin, the Beast Division investigated what happened to the wizard.
