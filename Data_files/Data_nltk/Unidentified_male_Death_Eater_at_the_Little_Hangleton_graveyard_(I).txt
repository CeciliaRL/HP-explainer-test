This wizard was a Death Eater who was present at the return of Lord Voldemort in the Little Hangleton graveyard in 1995, and witnessed the duel between Voldemort and Harry Potter.


== Behind the scenes ==
This Death Eater is portrayed by Barry Dowden in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.
