Ned was a Shrunken Head. He was a friend of one of the Shrunken heads in the Three Broomsticks.


== Behind the scenes ==
This Shrunken Head is mentioned only in the script of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban. The Shrunken Head's line about it was cut from the film.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film) (Script only, cut from final release)
