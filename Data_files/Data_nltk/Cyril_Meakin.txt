Cyril Meakin was a student who began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry between 1987 and 1989.


== Biography ==
By the 1993-1994 school year, he had been made a Prefect, and was tasked with patrolling areas of the school off limits to students.


== Etymology ==
Cyril means 'lord',which could relate to him being a Prefect.
Meakin is a surname,though it is feminine.
