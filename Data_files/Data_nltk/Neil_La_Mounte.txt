Neil La Mounte was a wizard whose name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of Professor Trelawney's Divination classroom.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named in honour of Neil Lamont, the supervising art director for the first six Harry Potter films, from Philosopher's Stone to Half-Blood Prince.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film) (Name seen on wood panel)
