This wizard was a Seeker on the Hufflepuff Quidditch team in 1991 and 1992. He was replaced by Cedric Diggory in 1993.


== Behind the scenes ==
The individual could be Cedric Diggory, as it is unknown whether he was already on the Hufflepuff Quidditch team in 1991.
