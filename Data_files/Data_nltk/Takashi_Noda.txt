Takashi Noda was a wizard and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Takashi is a Japanese masculine given name, which can have several meanings, depending on which Kanjis one uses to write the name with. Noda is a Japanese surname, meaning "rice paddy in the field":
