This secretary was an American No-Maj woman who worked for Gilbert Bingley at the central Steen National Bank branch in the early 20th century.
On 6 December 1926, when Jacob Kowalski came to the bank seeking a business loan, the secretary came to him to remind him that her boss was ready to see him.


== Behind the scenes ==
The secretary was portrayed by Lucie Pohl in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
This character's gender is not stated in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: The Original Screenplay, but instead shown in its film adaptation.
