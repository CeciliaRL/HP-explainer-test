This boy (fl. 1992) was a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry who was sorted into Gryffindor.


== Biography ==
In 1992, he fetched Harry Potter, who had just finished a Charms class with Filius Flitwick, and told him to go up to Gryffindor Tower, as Draco Malfoy had placed a Leg-Locker Curse on Neville Longbottom.
