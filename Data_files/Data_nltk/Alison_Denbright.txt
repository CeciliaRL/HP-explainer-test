Alison Denbright was a witch reported missing along with her relative, Jason, on the Potterwatch wireless programme in 1997. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Etymology ==
The name "Allison" is the Norman French diminutive of Aalis. It was common in England and France in the Middle Ages, and was later revived in the 20th century.
