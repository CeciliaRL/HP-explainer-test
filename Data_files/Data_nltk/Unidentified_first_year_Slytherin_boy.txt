This wizard was a student who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during Jacob's sibling's time at school.


== Biography ==

He was born somewhere in Great Britain or Ireland during the early 1970s. He started attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry on 1 September 1988 and was sorted into Slytherin house.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible that he is Lucian Bole or Peregrine Derrick.
