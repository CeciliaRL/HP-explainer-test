The Gryffindor Wizard's Chess champion, as the title suggests, refers to the Wizard's Chess Champion for Gryffindor House, at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.


== Biography ==
The boy came to be educated at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Gryffindor House. His Head of House was Professor Minerva McGonagall, the Transfiguration teacher, and he shared a dormitory with four other boys in his year in Gryffindor Tower. He was skilled in Wizard's Chess and could be usually found playing on a table in a niche in the common room.
