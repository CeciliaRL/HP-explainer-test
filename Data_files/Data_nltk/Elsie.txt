Elsie was a female creature kept by Newt Scamander in the menagerie beneath his apartment in 1927.


== Biography ==
In 1927 whilst talking to Bunty in the basement of his house, Newt asked about Elsie, to which Bunty replied, that her droppings were nearly normal again.
