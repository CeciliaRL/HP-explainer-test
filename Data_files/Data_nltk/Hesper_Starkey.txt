== History ==
In 1993-1994, a Portrait of her guarded a secret room at Hogwarts, to which the password (obtainable only from Fred and George Weasley) was Lunartickle.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Potions: Starkey was a skilled and renowned potioneer, who was highly proficient in the art of potion-making, as she discovered how the phases of the moon affected the fabrication of potions.
Astronomy: Starkey was also very knowledgeable in this branch of science, as she studied and discovered how the phases of the moon affected potion-brewing, a significant achievement in the field of Astronomy as well.


== Behind the scenes ==
Judging from her year of birth, Hesper may have been in the same year as Albus Dumbledore, if she attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
In some Harry Potter video game series, her surname is spelled Starky.  Pottermore, however, gives the name as Starkey.
