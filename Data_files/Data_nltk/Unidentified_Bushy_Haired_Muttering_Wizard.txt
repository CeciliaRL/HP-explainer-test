This individual was a wizard who worked at the British Ministry of Magic in the late 1990s. On September 2, 1997, he saw Harry Potter running in the Atrium causing him to call the security to arrest him.


== Physical appearance ==
The wizard had brown hair, wore a blue tie and brown jacket.


== Behind the scenes ==
The wizard was played by Daniel Hill in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1.
