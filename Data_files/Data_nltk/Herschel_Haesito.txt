Herschel Haesito was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s. His partner in crime was Raymund Lambus. The pair were collectively known as The Brothers.


== Biography ==
In 1926, the International Wizarding Police issued a double wanted poster for Haesito and Lambus, which stated they were wanted for "money laundering" and "No-Maj murder in Europe and Africa." It described them as "powerful wizards" who were "wanded and extremely dangerous." A reward of 5000 Dragots was offered for information leading to their capture.
