Lauren was a Muggle who wrote for My Teeth magazine. She contributed an article urging mothers to take better care of their gums to the 28th February-26th March 1997 issue. The ad for the article on the issue's front cover promised to reveal Lauren's "gum treat."


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Lauren Wakefield, who worked as a graphic artist on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
