== Etymology ==
'Manoj' means 'mental love' in hindi. 'Pandit' means 'wise man' in Hindi as well.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the NDS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)
Based on his name, he is most likely of Indian descent.
