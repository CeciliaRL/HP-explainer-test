Fergus (fl. 1979-1996) was an Irish wizard and Seamus Finnigan's cousin. He was known to annoy Seamus by apparating around him frequently.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Apparition: Fergus was capable of apparating at will, often doing it to simply annoy his cousin Seamus.
