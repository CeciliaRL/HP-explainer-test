Madame Miraforum, also known by the nickname Big Hands, was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In or around 1926, she escaped from the New York Witches' Penitentiary, where she was sent for "No-Maj smuggling and murder." The Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster, offering a reward of 3700 Dragots for information leading to her capture.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Miraphora Mina, a graphic artist who worked on Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them , as well as all eight of the Harry Potter films.
