Aidan Lynch was an Irish wizard who played as the Seeker for the Irish National Quidditch team during the 1994 Quidditch World Cup.


== Biography ==
Although his team won the World Cup with the most points, Seeker Viktor Krum caught the Golden Snitch. Lynch had a tendency to crash during dives, often resulting in him being dazed for a while afterwards. During the first dive, Lynch was just knocked out for a few minutes but after the second dive, he was left dazed for a long period of time.


== Behind the scenes ==
Aidan shares his last name with Irish-born actress Evanna Lynch, who plays Luna Lovegood in the films.
