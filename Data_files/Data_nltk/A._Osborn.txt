A. Osborn was a Muggle and the author of Shrubs & Trees for the Garden, a book published by Ward Lock & Co., about floriculture and gardening.


== Behind the scenes ==
In real life, Arthur Osborn (fl. 1933) was also head of the arboretum at the Royal Botanic Gardens at Kew.
