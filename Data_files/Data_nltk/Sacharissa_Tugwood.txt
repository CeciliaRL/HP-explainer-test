== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
Tugwood was born in 1874. She presumably attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry (c. 1885- 1893) where she would have excelled in Potions.


=== Career as Potioneer ===
In her adulthood, Tugwood would develop many cosmetic potions. She became famous for being the first witch to use such a potion. Sacharissa also discovered the pimple-curing properties of Bubotuber pus.


=== Death and legacy ===
Tugwood would eventually die in 1966, aged ninety-two. Her gravestone credited her to have made of the world a "more beautiful place". She was also featured on a Chocolate Frog Card.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Potions: Tugwood was a highly accomplished and talented professional Potioneer, having successfully invented numerous Beautification Potions, cosmetic concoctions which enhance the beauty of the drinker, an achievement she was immortalised on a Chocolate Frog Card for, showing her proficiency in this branch of both magic and science.
Herbology: Tugwood also discovered the pimple curing properties of Bubotuber pus, a natural substance extracted from the magical Bubotuber plant, an achievement in the field of Herbology, meaning she was knowledgeable of magical plants and their usages.


== Etymology ==
The name Sacharissa may be related to the verb saccharize which means to convert into sugar.
