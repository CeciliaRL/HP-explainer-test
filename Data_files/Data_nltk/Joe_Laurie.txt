Joe Laurie was a wizard who was reported missing on the Potterwatch wireless programme in 1997. His disappearance was most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Daniel Laurie, who worked as an ADR editor on Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, and Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, and as an ADR supervisor on Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
