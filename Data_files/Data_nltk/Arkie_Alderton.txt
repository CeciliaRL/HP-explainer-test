Arkie Alderton was a well-known broomstick designer and the owner of Arkie Alderton's Kwik-Repair Shop.
One of the Muggle-borns rounded up for the Muggle-Born Registration Commission in 1997 swore that he was a half-blood by claiming he was his son, but he was not believed.
