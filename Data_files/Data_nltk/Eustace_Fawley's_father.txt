Mr Fawley was a British wizard and a member of the Fawley family.


== Biography ==
He had at least one child, a son, Eustuce, to whom he gave his best set of gobstones to take to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Eustace lost the gobstones in a fire at the boathouse.
He was part of the Fawley family, who appeared in the Pure-Blood Directory as belonging to the Sacred Twenty-Eight.


== Etymology ==
Fawley is a name derived from the Old English word fugelere which means "hunter of wild birds, fowler".
