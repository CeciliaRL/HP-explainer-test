Caractacus was a wizard who, in his childhood, attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was singled out for membership in the Slug Club. Horace Slughorn kept a picture of him on his shelf.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible, as this individual was a former Slug Club member, that the person attended the Slug Club Christmas party in 1996.
