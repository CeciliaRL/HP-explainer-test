The grandfather of Porpentina and Queenie Goldstein was a breeder of owls. Queenie noted this fact to Jacob Kowalski after discovering via Legilimency that his own grandfather kept pigeons.


== Behind the scenes ==
As he was a breeder of owls, which have significance within the wizarding world, he was likely a wizard, although this has not been confirmed.
