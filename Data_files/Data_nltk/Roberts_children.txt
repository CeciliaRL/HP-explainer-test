These two individuals were the Muggle children of Mr Roberts and Mrs Roberts.


== Biography ==
The two children of Mr Roberts and Mrs Roberts were attacked by Death Eaters the day after the 1994 Quidditch World Cup. The youngest, a boy, was spun like a top sixty feet up in the air, while his mother, father, and older sibling floated nearby.
After the incident, their memories would have been altered to forget what had happened along with their parents.
