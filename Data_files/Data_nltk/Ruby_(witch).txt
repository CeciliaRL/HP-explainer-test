Ruby was an American witch who lived in the early 20th century.


== Biography ==
In 1926, Ruby was in a relationship with Sam, an Obliviator who worked for the Magical Congress of the United States of America. Unbeknownst to her, however, Sam was also romantically involved with his another woman Cecily. Queenie Goldstein, a Legilimens, uncovered Sam's infidelity by reading his mind, and convinced him not to wipe the memory of the No-Maj Jacob Kowalski by threatening to expose his secret to Ruby as she appeared smiling at him.


== Behind the scenes ==
Ruby was portrayed by Stephanie Aspin in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them, but was not listed in the film's end credits.
