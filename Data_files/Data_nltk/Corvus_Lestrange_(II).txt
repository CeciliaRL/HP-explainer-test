Corvus Lestrange was a French wizard and a member of the pure-blood Lestrange family. He was the son of Corvus Lestrange and Heloise Beaufort. He had a brother Falco. He married Minette Rosier and had three children with her: two daughters, Manon and Josette, and a son Corvus.
