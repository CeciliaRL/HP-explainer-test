Ron's frog in 1994 		 	 

Ronald Weasley had a frog which he kept in a fish tank on the windowsill in his bedroom at The Burrow. In the summer of 1992, the fish tank contained a large number of frog spawn, but by the summer of 1994, it contained just one extremely large frog.
