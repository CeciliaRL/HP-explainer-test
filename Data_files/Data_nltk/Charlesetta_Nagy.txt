Charlesetta Nagy, also known by the nickname Mother, was a wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Nagy, which stated that she was wanted for spell forging and elf trafficking. The poster warned that she was "unpredictable" and would "likely resist arrest." A reward of 4,500 Dragots was offered for help that led to her arrest.
