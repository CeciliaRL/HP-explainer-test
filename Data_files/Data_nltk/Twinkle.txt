Twinkle was a wizard or witch and the owner of Twinkle's Telescopes, a shop in Diagon Alley that sold Astronomical equipment such as telescopes, moon charts and star charts.


== Behind the scenes ==
Illustrator Jim Kay revealed that Twinkle's Telescopes was named after a real shop in Mansfield, "Sally Twinkles".
Twinkle as a surname is rather uncommon, with the highest concentration in Bangladesh, then India.
