Stephen Gould was a wizard and a former Captain for the Gryffindor Quidditch team.
By the early 1990s, there was a plaque on the mantlepiece at Gryffindor Tower listing him as a former Gryffindor Quidditch Captain.


== Behind the scenes ==
Stephen Gould is an artist with PlayStation Home and this naming is a tribute to him.
