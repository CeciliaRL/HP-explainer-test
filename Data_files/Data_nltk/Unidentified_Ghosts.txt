These female ghosts often haunted the corridors of Hogwarts during the 1991-1992 and 1992-1993 school years. They could be found haunting various places of the castle at night, including classrooms and the Hogwarts Library.


== Behind the scenes ==
They never appear in any of the books or films. They appear to be simply generic representations of ghosts to haunt the castle provided in the first two video games.
