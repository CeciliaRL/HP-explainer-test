This individual was a wizard who stood at Platform 9 3/4 in 2017, saying goodbye to his children at the Hogwarts Express on their travel to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.


== Behind the scenes ==
He is portrayed by Michael Aston in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
