Tony Silva was a Brazilian wizard and the Seeker for the 2014 Brazilian National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
On the first match of the quarter-finals, Brazil versus Wales, on 4 June, 2014, Silva performed a spectacular dive in the 131st minute and caught the Snitch from under rival Seeker Eurig Cadwallader's nose.
