This wizard was a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Hufflepuff in the 1990s. He fought during the Battle of Hogwarts in 1998 and carried the injured Hogwarts-protectors. It is unknown if he survived.


== Behind the scenes ==
This boy is played by Chris Chan in Part 2 of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows.
