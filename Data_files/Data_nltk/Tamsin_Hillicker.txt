Tamsin Hillicker was a witch who was reported missing along with her relative, Iola, on the Potterwatch wireless programme in 1997. Her disappearance is most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Tamsin Hilliker, the niece of Stuart Hilliker, a sound mixer who worked on the final three Harry Potter films.
