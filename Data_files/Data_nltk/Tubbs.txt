Mrs Tubbs was a witch who owned the Tuck Shop. This store sold wizarding snacks like Cauldron Cakes and Pumpkin Pasties.


== Behind the scenes ==
A tuck shop is a term used to describe a store that is very near to, or on the grounds of, a school. In the UK they are usually associated with selling sweets or other snacks and may also sell school supplies. This indicates that the store may be on Hogwarts grounds.
