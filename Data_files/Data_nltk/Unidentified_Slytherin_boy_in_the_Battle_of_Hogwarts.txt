This individual was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Slytherin House. He was locked up in the dungeons with the rest of his house and fought in the Battle of Hogwarts after when the cell had exploded.


== Behind the scenes ==
He was played by George Christodoulou in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
