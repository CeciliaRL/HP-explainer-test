Lennox Campbell was a British wizard and the Seeker for the Montrose Magpies.He once failed to catch the Golden Snitch when it was kicked at him by a Chaser on his team. The Chaser who did this was presumably Alasdair Maddock, who had an obsession with Muggle sports, and was known to have tried football techniques during at least two matches.


== Etymology ==
"Lennox" is from a Scottish surname which was derived from the name of a district in Scotland, the Levenach.
