Raul Almeida was a Brazilian wizard and the Keeper for the 2014 Brazilian National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
On the first match of the quarter-finals, Brazil versus Wales, on 4 June, 2014, Almeida was struck to the head by a vicious Bludger by Welsh Beater Iefan Rice, while the Quaffle was at the other end of the pitch. This earned Brazil a penalty.
