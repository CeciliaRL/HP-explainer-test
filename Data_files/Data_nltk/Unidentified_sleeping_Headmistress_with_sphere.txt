This witch was appointed Headmistress of Hogwarts before Professor Dippet. After her death, her portrait was hung in the Headmaster's office, in order to give advice to the current head. Her portrait is hung over the entryway to the Headmaster's Office and depicts her sleeping on a chair holding a transparent sphere.
