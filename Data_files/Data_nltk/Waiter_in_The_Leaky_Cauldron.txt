This wizard was a waiter at the Leaky Cauldron pub in London, assisting barman Tom in his duties by wandlessly and non-verbally collecting plates to be washed.


== Physical appearance ==
The waiter had brown hair and white skin, and wore a dark blue uniform.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Nonverbal and Wandless magic: This wizard could magically carry a large stack of plates without saying a single incantation and without the use of a wand, which is highly advanced magic.
