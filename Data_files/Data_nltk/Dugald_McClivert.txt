Dugald McClivert was the head of the McClivert clan. According to legend, he was killed by the hand of the MacBoon clan, Quintius MacBoon in a drunken duel. His death led to the McClivert clan transfiguring the MacBoons into dangerous creatures known as Quintapeds.
