This wizard attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1980s.


== Biography ==
During his time at Hogwarts, he was Sorted into an unknown house. At some point during his time at school, he became a reserve Chaser of an unknown Quidditch team.


=== 1987–1988 school year ===

In the 1987–1988 school year, after Erika Rath injured Orion Amari in a Quidditch friendly, he became a regular team's member.  As a result, he played on the team during the first Quidditch match.


== Behind the scenes ==
His house is the same as that of Jacob's sibling, which is chosen by the player.
This Chaser's appearance was initially used for another Chaser on the same Quidditch team.
