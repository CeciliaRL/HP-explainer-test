Martha was an American No-Maj woman who lived in the early 20th century.


== Biography ==
While at the scene of a mysterious attack in New York in 1926, a newspaper reporter asked his photographer colleague if he felt like getting a drink, to which the photographer replied that he had promised Martha (presumably his wife or girlfriend) that he would abstain from consuming alcohol.
