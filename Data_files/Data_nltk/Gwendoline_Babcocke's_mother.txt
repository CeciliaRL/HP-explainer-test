Gwendoline Babcocke's mother was a Scottish witch who lived in Hogsmeade. Her daughter worked at The Magic Neep. Gwendoline decided that she would never leave Hogsmeade to pursue her dream of working with dragons because it would break her mum's heart to see her go.
