Marilias Kistmanus was an American wizard and reporter for The New York Ghost in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
He wrote an article about a tenement burglary that was foiled when the culprit was ensnared by a Venomous Tentacula that was published in the paper's 6 December 1926 morning edition.
