This unidentified fifth year asked Harry Potter to go with her to the Yule Ball with her in 1994. Harry, however, turned her down.


== Physical appearance ==
She was described as "quite good looking" by Ron Weasley. She was a foot taller than Harry Potter, which Harry thought would look awkward if he tried to dance with her at the ball. Harry also described her as looking as though she might knock him out if he refused her invitation.
