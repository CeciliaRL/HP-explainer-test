Anna Chiwawa was a Muggle singer in the early 20th century. 
In 1926, posters were put up around New York's harbour promoting a series of twelve performances staged by G. Forbes Productions, which were slated to begin on Saturday 11 December. This included an appearance by Anna Chiwawa, who was billed as a "lovely singing star."
