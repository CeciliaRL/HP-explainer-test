This man was an American No-Maj Police Officer working for the New York Police Department.


== Biography ==
This officer participated in shooting at Credence Barebone in his Obscurial form in December 1926, when he attacked New York City.


== Behind the scenes ==
This Policeman was portrayed by Lee Asquith-Coe in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them, but was uncredited.
