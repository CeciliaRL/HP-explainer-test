This individual was an American No-Maj who worked as an employee at the Steen National Bank in the 20th century.


== Biography ==
On 6 December, 1926, this individual was working at Steen National Bank when Newton Scamander entered the bank to find his escaped niffler. The employee, suspicious of Newt due his furtive glances around the bank, asked him if he needed any help to which Newt replied that he was only waiting.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Employee was portrayed by Cory Peterson in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
