Jimmy Kiddell was a British wizard and wandmaker who owned the shop Jimmy Kiddell's Wonderful Wands in Diagon Alley. He is presumably an inferior wandmaker to Garrick Ollivander as most wizard children get their wands at Ollivanders.


== Behind the scenes ==
Jimmy Kiddell might be a reference to Jimmy Kimmel, an American television host and comedian, currently hosting Jimmy Kimmel Live!, a late-night talk show that airs on ABC.
