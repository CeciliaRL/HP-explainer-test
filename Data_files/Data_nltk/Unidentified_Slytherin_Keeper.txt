This individual was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He played as the Keeper for the Slytherin Quidditch team in 1996.


== Biography ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			The Keeper trying to catch Ginny Weasley's Quaffle 		 	 
In the Slytherin versus Gryffindor Hogwarts Quidditch Cup match in 1996, he collided with another Slytherin Quidditch member and hit the hoop, when Ginny Weasley threw the Quaffle.
