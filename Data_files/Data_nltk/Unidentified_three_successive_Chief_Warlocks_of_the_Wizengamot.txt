These three individuals were wizards who headed the Wizengamot in successive terms in office. Prior to their education in the magical arts, they bought their wands (presumably at Ollivanders Wand Shop), and the three of them were chosen by wands of chestnut and unicorn hair, the combination, as Garrick Ollivander noted, showing interest in matters of justice and law.
