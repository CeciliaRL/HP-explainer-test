Karl Wang was a Norwegian wizard and the Keeper for the 2014 Norwegian National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
After their defeat on the second quarter-final match of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, against Bulgaria, Norwegian Seeker Sigrid Kristoffersen flew directly to the ground and banged her head on it until she was dragged to her feet by Karl Wang.
