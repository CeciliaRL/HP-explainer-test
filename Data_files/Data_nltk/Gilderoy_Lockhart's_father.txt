Mr Lockhart was the Muggle father of Gilderoy Lockhart and two older Squib daughters.
The Healer who looked after Gilderoy at St Mungo's, Miriam Strout, stated he never got visitors in December 1995. This meant that either his father was deceased by then, or that he did not wish to visit his son.
