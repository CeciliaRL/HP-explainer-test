Robert Wood was a resident of number 32a, North Street, Crowford, Brookshire. He may be a relative of Mr, and Oliver Wood.


== Behind the scenes ==
The fictitious Robert Wood is only mentioned on the F.A.Q. section of J. K. Rowling's official site, where a letter adressed to J. K. Rowling is stated to have been sent by him.
