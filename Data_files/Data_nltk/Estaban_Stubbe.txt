Estaban Stubbe was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In or around 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Stubbe, stating that he was wanted for No-Maj smuggling & murder and "bearing wand, most menacing and extremely dangerous" with a reward of 4850 Dragots.
