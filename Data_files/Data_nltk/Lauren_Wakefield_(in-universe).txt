Lauren Wakefield was a Muggle circus performer. She was one of the performers whose talents were featured in Le Cirque du Vaga Lume.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Lauren Wakefield, a graphic artist who worked on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, and both parts of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows.
