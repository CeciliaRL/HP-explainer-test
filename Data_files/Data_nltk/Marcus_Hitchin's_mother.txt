Mrs Hitchin was the mother of Marcus Hitchin, a Ravenclaw at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Her son wanted a new broomstick for Christmas.


== Behind the scenes ==
This particular individual was only featured in the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
