Red was a goblin bellboy who worked for the Magical Congress of the United States of America during the 1920s. He helped operate the lifts which transported passengers to any of the hundreds of floors within the MACUSA headquarters.


== Behind the scenes ==
Red was portrayed by Dan Hedaya in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
