Eduardo Lima was a British wizard who the Ministry of Magic kept tabs on while it was under Death Eater control in 1997. His file was signed by Chief Registration Authority F. L. Wakefield.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is tribute to Eduardo Lima, a graphic artist who worked on every film from Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban onward, and whose photo is featured in the prop file.
