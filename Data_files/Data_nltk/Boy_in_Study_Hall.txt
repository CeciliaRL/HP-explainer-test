This boy was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s, who was a member of Hufflepuff house. In 1992, he was studying in the Study Hall in 1992 along with Hannah Abbott, Ernie Macmillan and another Hufflepuff boy.


== Behind the scenes ==
He was portrayed by British actor Robert Ayres in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets.
