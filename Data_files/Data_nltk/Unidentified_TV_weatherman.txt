This TV weatherman was giving the weather forecast the day two Dementors attacked Harry Potter and Dudley Dursley in Little Whinging. Petunia Dursley was watching him on television when the two got to 4 Privet Drive.


== Behind the scenes ==
The weatherman was portrayed by Miles Jupp in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix.
