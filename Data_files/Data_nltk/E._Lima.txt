E. Lima was a wizard or witch who competed in duels. The Daily Prophet published a special report in 1996 about a duel between E. Lima and M. Mina that was the final match of a duelling competition.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Eduardo Lima, a graphic artist who worked on every Harry Potter film from Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban onward.
