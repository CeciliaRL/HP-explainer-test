Dougal was a male Demiguise that accompanied Newt Scamander as he researched Magizoology and visited New York in 1926.


== Biography ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			Dougals container in Newt's suitcase 		 	 
He was one of the creatures that escaped Newt Scamander's suitcase and had to be recaptured after causing a disturbance in a local department store.


== Personality and traits ==
Dougal seemed to be a rather kind-hearted and benevolent creature, as he kindly took care of a young Occamy by giving it food and tending to its needs.
