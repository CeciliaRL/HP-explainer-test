This ghost resided in Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. In 1992 during the Hallowe'en feast the ghosts had a reenactment of Sir Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington's death in 1492, and this particular ghost had the honour of playing the executioner of Sir Nicholas.
