This individual was a French witch who worked as a receptionist in the foyer of the French Ministry of Magic.


== Biography ==
In September 1927, she informed Queenie Goldstein that her sister Tina had not been registered at the Ministry.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Receptionist was portrayed by Olivia Popica in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.
