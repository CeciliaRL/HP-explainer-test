These individuals were friends of Oliver Wood, once Captain for the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry Gryffindor Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
They joined Oliver in the Battle of Hogwarts on the 2 May 1998, defending Hogwarts School by fighting against Lord Voldemort's army of Death Eaters and dark creatures.
