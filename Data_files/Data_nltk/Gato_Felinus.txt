Gato Felinus (also known as Mittens - The Cat) was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the United States of America in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Felinus, which stated that he was wanted for No-Maj and elf murder and offered a reward of 2500 dragots for his arrest.
