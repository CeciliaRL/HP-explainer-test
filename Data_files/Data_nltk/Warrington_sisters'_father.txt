An unknown man was a Muggle and the father of two daughters, Ivy and Winifred Warrington. Both of his daughters were witches.


== Biography ==
During her childhood,  Winifred accidentally  vanished herself, and their grandmother put a Memory Charm on Ivy so she would forget what happened, but it caused her to completely forget Winifred. Ivy later started attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
