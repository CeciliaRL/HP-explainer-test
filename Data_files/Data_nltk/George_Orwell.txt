George Orwell (25 June, 1903 – 21 January 1950) was a Muggle author who wrote Animal Farm.


== Etymology ==
The name "George" comes from the Greek name Γεωργιος (Georgios) which was derived from the Greek word γεωργος (georgos) meaning "farmer, earthworker", itself derived from the elements γη (ge) "earth" and εργον (ergon) "work".
