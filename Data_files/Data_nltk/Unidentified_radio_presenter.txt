This witch or wizard (fl. 1992) was heard from an old radio at the Weasley family's home (The Burrow) announcing that "Witching Hour, with the popular singing sorceress, Celestina Warbeck" was coming up.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is likely that this individual was Glenda Chittock, although it may have been a substitute for her.
