This King (fl. Medieval times) was the husband of Malodora Grymm. A Hag, Grymm used a Beautification Potion to transform herself into a beautiful woman, and married him.


== Behind the scenes ==
Malodora Grymm's husband is based on the King from the tale of Snow White.
