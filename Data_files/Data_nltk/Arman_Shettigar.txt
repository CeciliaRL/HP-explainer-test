Arman Shettigar was a wizard and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
He competed in the school's Gobstones tournament during the 1996-1997 school year.


== Etymology ==
The name "Arman" means "dream" in Kazakh, of Persian origin.
