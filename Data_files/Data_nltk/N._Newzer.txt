N. Newzer was a reporter for The New York Ghost in 1926.


== Biography ==
They co-wrote a report with K. Machadus and D. Figueiredus out of Portland, Oregon about the sourcing of Stinksap extract in a popular brand of toothpaste, which was published in the 1 December 1926 issue of the newspaper.
