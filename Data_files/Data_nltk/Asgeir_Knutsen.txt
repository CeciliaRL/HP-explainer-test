Asgeir Knutsen was a witch who played as a Beater on the Nordic National Quidditch team.
During the 1996–1997 school year, Knutsens Chocolate Frog Card could be won by beating Alison Denshaw at an Exploding Snap challenge.


== Etymology ==
The name "Asgeir" is from the Old Norse name Ásgeirr (or possibly the modern Icelandic name Ásgeir), derived from the elements áss meaning "god" and geirr meaning "spear". It is, however, a masculine name.
