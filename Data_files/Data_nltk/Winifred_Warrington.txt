Winifred Warrington was a witch,  the daughter of a Muggle father. Her grandmother and her sister, Ivy Warrington were witches.
During her childhood, Winifred accidentally caused herself to vanish, and their grandmother put a Memory Charm on her so she would forget what happened to her, but it caused her to completely forget Winifred. 


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible that she was related to Cassius Warrington.
