This newspaper seller was selling Muggle newspapers on Shaftesbury Avenue on 1 August, 1997.


== Behind the scenes ==
In the video game adaptation of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, after Disapparating from Bill and Fleur's wedding, Ron wanted Harry, while under the Invisibility Cloak, to check if this individual was a Muggle, or in fact a Death Eater, for which he was thankfully the former.
