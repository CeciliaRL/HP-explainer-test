Emma Vane was a Muggle fashion designer. Clothing designed by her was featured in the Costume Materialization collection opening that was held on 28 September, 1997, along with the work of fellow fashion designers Julia Dehoff and Mauricio Carneiro.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Emma Vane, who worked as a draughtsman on several Harry Potter films.
