This hobo was an American No-Maj man living on the streets of New York in 1926. 


== History ==
He was one of the witnesses when some of Newt Scamander's creatures escaped from his briefcase that year.
When a policeman asked questions about the situation, the drunken hobo was about to tell about what he really saw while another witness thought it was gas explosion, his memories were obliviated by Newt leaving him and the crowd to agree that it was actually gas.


== Behind the scenes ==
The hobo was portrayed by Paul Birchard in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
