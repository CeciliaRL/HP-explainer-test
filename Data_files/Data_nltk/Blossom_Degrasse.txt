Blossom Degrasse was a witch, who was a famous herbologist, author and potion master.


== Biography ==
In late 2010s, she released the book "A Humble Guide for Potion Brewing You Cannot Live Without".
At some point during Calamity, she met a member of the Statute of Secrecy Task Force and lauded their role in fighting the Calamity. She also sent a request for dragon-hide gloves to the SOS Task Force.
