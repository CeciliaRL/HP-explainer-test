This Dark Wizard was a Death Eater who fought at the Battle of Hogwarts.


== Biography ==
On 2 May 1998, this Death Eater joined his comrades in the assault of Hogwarts Castle, firstly by attempting to break down the protective enchantments around the castle with spells. He later fought near the Marble Staircase. Later, during the Battle of Hogwarts, he was cornered while fighting in a first-floor corridor while being hit with the Incarcerous Spell cast by Harry Potter at the Marble Staircase.
He was later seems laying down next to the staircase, implying he had died from his injuries, or was simply unconscious.


== Behind the scenes ==
This Death Eater was played by James Williamson in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
