Kaori Shimizu (清水 香織 Shimizu Kaori) was a witch and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s who was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
The name Kaori means "fragrance, fragrant" (香) (ka) and "weave, weaving" (織) (ori).
Kaori's surname Shimizu means "clear, pure, clean" (清) (shi) and "water" (水) (mizu).


== Behind the scenes ==
Based on her name, she is most likely of Japanese descent.
