Deliverance Dane (died 15 June 1735) was an American woman accused of witchcraft during the Salem Witch Trials of 1692 and 1693.


== History ==
In 1926, Tina Goldstein used her name as an exclamation of surprise, asking Newt Scamander "Why in the name of Deliverance Dane did you let that thing loose?"
