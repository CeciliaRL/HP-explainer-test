Lisa Smith was a Muggle who was resident of Number 6, Wisteria Walk, Little Whinging.


== Behind the scenes ==
The fictitious Lisa Smith is only mentioned on the F.A.Q. section of J. K. Rowling's official site, where a letter addressed to J. K. Rowling is stated to have been sent by her.
Lisa was aged 12 at the time that she sent her letter.
