Mr Warbeck was a Welsh wizard who worked as a minor functionary in the Muggle Liaison Office in the early 20th century.


== Biography ==
He met his wife, a Muggle actress, when she was attacked by a Lethifold which had disguised itself as a stage curtain. The couple had at least one child, Celestina, who was born 18 August 1917.
Celestina proved to have an exceptional voice at a young age. She grew up to become an internationally famous wizarding singer.
