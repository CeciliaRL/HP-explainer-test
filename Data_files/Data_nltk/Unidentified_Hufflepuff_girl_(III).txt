This unidentified Hufflepuff girl attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry from 1992 to 1999, and was Sorted into Hufflepuff.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
This girl was born in approximately 1981, to unknown parents. Whether she was born a Muggle-born, half-blood, or pure-blood is unknown.


=== Hogwarts years ===


==== Early years ====
This unidentified girl started Hogwarts in 1992. She was sorted into Hufflepuff, in the same year as Ginny Weasley and Luna Lovegood.


==== Third year ====
In this girl's third year, the Triwizard Tournament was held, as well as, the Yule Ball. In December, she asked Harry Potter to the ball. Taken aback, Harry said no so abruptly that he hurt her feelings. He then had to endure the taunts of Ron Weasley, Seamus Finnigan, and Dean Thomas.
