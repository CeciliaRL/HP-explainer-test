Nicholas Saunders was a Muggle circus performer. He was one of the performers whose talents were featured in Le Cirque du Vaga Lume.
Saunders also adapted the William Shakespeare play Twelfth Night for the Shakespeare in Shaftsbury event that was held as part of the Soho Play Summer Art Festival in London in 1997.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Nicholas Saunders, a graphic artist who worked on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, as well as Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1.
