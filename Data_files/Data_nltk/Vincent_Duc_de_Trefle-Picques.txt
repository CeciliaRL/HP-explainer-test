Vincent Duc de Trefle-Picques (fl. 1794) was a French wizard and nobleman, active around the time of the French Revolution. In his youth, he attended Beauxbatons Academy of Magic. He managed to escape the Reign of Terror of 1793-1794 by casting a Concealment Charm on his neck and pretending that his head had already been cut off.
