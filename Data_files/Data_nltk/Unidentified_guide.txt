This individual was an American witch who worked as a tour guide for visitors around the Magical Congress of the United States of America.


== Biography ==
In 1927, the guide was giving a visitor a tour around the Magical Congress of the United States of America building when Porpentina Goldstein was taking Newton Scamander in for exposing Magic in the Steen National Bank.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Guide was portrayed by Christy Meyer in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
