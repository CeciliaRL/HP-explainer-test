Nicolas Grimmett was a student who began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry between 1987 and 1989.


== Biography ==
By the 1993-1994 school year, he had been made a Prefect, and was tasked with patrolling areas of the school off limits to students.


== Etymology ==
Nicolas is the French form of the Greek name Nikolaos, which means "victory of the people."
Grimmet is an English surname derived from the Germanic personal name Grimhild, meaning "fierce in war."
