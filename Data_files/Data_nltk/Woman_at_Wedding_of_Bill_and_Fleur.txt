This individual was a witch who attended Bill Weasley and Fleur Delacour's wedding in 1997 at The Burrow. She also helped fight the Death Eaters that attacked after the fall of the Ministry of Magic was announced by Kingsley Shacklebolt's Patronus.


== Behind the scenes ==
In Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 she is played by Annette Elstob.
