This family of goblins lived somewhere near Nottingham.


== Biography ==
During the First Wizarding War, they were murdered by Voldemort for an unknown reason. Arthur Weasley recalled this event during a discussion about the loyalty of the goblin community with his son Bill and Remus Lupin, citing this as the reason why the goblins might be against the current Voldemort regime.
