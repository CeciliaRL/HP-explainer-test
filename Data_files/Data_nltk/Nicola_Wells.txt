Nicola Wells (b. 1980s or 1990s) was a resident of number twenty-five, Wildbrook Crescent, Lowercroft, Scotland.


== Behind the scenes ==
The fictitious Nicola Wells is only mentioned on the F.A.Q. section of J. K. Rowling's official site, where a letter addressed to J. K. Rowling is stated to have been sent by her.
