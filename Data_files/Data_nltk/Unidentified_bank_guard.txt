This individual was an American No-Maj who worked as a guard at the central Steen National Bank in the early 20th century.


== History ==
On 6 December, 1926, this guard was on duty when Newton Scamander and Jacob Kowalski broke into the bank to find the escaped niffler. The guard attempted to arrest them before they apparated.


== Behind the scenes ==
The guard was portrayed by Jake Samuels in the Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
