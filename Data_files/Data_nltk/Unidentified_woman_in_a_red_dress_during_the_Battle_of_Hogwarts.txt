This individual was a witch who fought during the Battle of Hogwarts with a group of students on the First-floor corridor. She survived the battle.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character may be Septima Vector, Irma Pince, Bathsheda Babbling or another as yet unidentified professor. Alternatively, she may be the teacher of Advanced Arithmancy Studies, Ancient Studies, Music, Muggle Music, Art, Muggle Art or Ghoul Studies. Similarly she could be one of the members of the Order of the Phoenix.
