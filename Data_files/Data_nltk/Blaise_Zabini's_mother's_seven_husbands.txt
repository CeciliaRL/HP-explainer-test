These seven individuals (d. before 1996) were all married to Blaise Zabini's mother at some point, later having died mysteriously and leaving the widow with "mounds of gold". Which, if any of them, was Blaise's biological father is unknown.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible that they were murdered by their wife, given how they all died under mysterious circumstances and how their widow inherited significant fortune from them.
