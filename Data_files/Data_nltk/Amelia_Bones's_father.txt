Mr Bones was the father of Amelia and Edgar Bones. He and his wife were killed by Voldemort during the First Wizarding War.


== Behind the scenes ==
Mr Bones must have had a third child, as his granddaughter, Susan Bones, was niece to both his daughter Amelia and his son Edgar.
