The Hufflepuff Wizard Duelling Champion as the title suggests, refers to the duelling champion for the Hufflepuff house. He is a selected student from the Hufflepuff house to represent the house in the Founder Duels.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
This boy attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s and was Sorted into Hufflepuff House. His Head of House was Pomona Sprout, the Herbology Professor, and he shared a dormitory with four boys in his year in the Hufflepuff Basement, which is located below ground level and near the Hogwarts kitchens.
