This unidentified magical creature was owned by Rubeus Hagrid and lived in his hut. It was a plant-like creature with an appearance similar to the Mandrake plant.


== History ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			Hagrid covering up the creature 		 	 
It was implied to be an illegal creature, when Hagrid hid it under a tea towel when the Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures, including Albus Dumbledore, Cornelius Fudge and Walden Macnair the Executioner, arrived for the disposal of Buckbeak the Hippogriff some days after Hagrid's hearing with the Hogwarts Board of Governors, in 1994.


== Behind the scenes ==
The creature has a somewhat similar resemblance to the Sarlaac in Return of the Jedi.
