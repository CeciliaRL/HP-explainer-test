This man was a British wizard and the father of Jacob and Jacob's sibling. He did not believe that the fabled Cursed Vaults really existed at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
He was implied to be dead or estranged from the rest of his family long before Jacob's sibling began attending Hogwarts.
