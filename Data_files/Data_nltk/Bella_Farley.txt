Bella Farley was a witch reported missing along with her relatives Jake, Charlie, and Madge on the Potterwatch radio programme in 1997. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Etymology ==
"Bella" is the short form of Isabella and other names ending in bella. It is also associated with the Italian word meaning "beautiful".
