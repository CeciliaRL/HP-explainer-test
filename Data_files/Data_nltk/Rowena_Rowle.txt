Rowena Rowle was a British witch who worked at the British Ministry of Magic in the 2010s.


== Biography ==
At some point in the Calamity, she lets a Calamity Investigator into the Department of Mysteries to share theories about Grim Fawley. As she monologues, the Investigator stares into the Tank of Brains - accidentally inspiring one to leap onto them. Rowena saves the Investigator.


== Behind the scenes ==
Unless they are related, it is possible that the name "Rowena Rowle" is a continuity error and is supposed to be Regina.
