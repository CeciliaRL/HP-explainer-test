Rahul Tandel was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Rahul is an Indian name, meaning "capable". Rahul was the name of the son of Buddha. Tandel is a surname of Indian origin, widely found in the Gujarat state.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.
