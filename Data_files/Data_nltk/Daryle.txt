Daryle was a witch who had a portrait of herself hung somewhere in Hogwarts Castle by the 1991-1992 school year.


== Behind the scenes ==
This portrait was meant to appear in the PC version of the video game adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone. Though the portrait cannot be found in the game, its texture, identified as daryle128.bmp, can be found in the game's files.
