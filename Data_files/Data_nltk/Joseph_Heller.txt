Joseph Heller (1 May, 1923 – 12 December, 1999) was a Muggle author who wrote Catch 22.


== Etymology ==
The name "Joseph" comes from Ioseph, the Latin form of Greek Ιωσηφ (Ioseph), which was from the Hebrew name יוֹסֵף (Yosef) meaning "he will add".
