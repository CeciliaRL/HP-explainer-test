This individual was a witch who fought during the Battle of Hogwarts. She led some students to the First-floor corridor. It is unknown if she survived.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character may be Septima Vector, Irma Pince, Bathsheda Babbling, Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank or another as yet unidentified professor. Alternatively, she may be the teacher of Advanced Arithmancy Studies, Ancient Studies, Music, Muggle Music, Art, Muggle Art, Ghoul Studies or Alchemy. Similarly she could have been one of the members of the Order of the Phoenix.
