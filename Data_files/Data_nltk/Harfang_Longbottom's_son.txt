The only son of Harfang Longbottom and Callidora Black was a pure-blood wizard. He had a sister. He was a first cousin of Arthur Weasley and possibly Bartemius Crouch Snr through his mother. He was also possibly related to Frank Longbottom and his son through his father.


== Behind the scenes ==
This individual was not featured on the Longbottom family tree shown on Pottermore.
