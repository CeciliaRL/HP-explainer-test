Grugwyn Rufford (fl. 1998) was a Welsh wizard who was a member of the National Welsh Gobstones Team. 


== Biography ==
In 1998, Rufford complained to the Daily Prophet because they did not cover the Welsh victory over Hungary. On 31 July that year, the editor replied to him via an editorial saying that people found that Gobstones were "deeply boring".


== Etymology ==
It is possible that this name 'Grugwyn' comes from Welsh grug gwyn "white heather".
