Franciscus Fieldwake was an American wizarding author and reporter for the The New York Ghost.


== Biography ==
He wrote the book Protection Charm Your Mind: A Practical Guide to Counter Legilimensy.
The New York Ghost published a report by him about a wizard or witch from Idaho caught drunk broom-riding after winning a foxtrot competition in its 6 December 1926 morning issue.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Lauren Wakefield, a graphic artist who worked on the last three Harry Potter films, and on Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
