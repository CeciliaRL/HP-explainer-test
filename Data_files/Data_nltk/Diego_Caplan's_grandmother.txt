This individual was the grandmother of Diego Caplan.


== Biography ==
In the 1990–1991 school year, as her grandson was writing a letter to her in the Hogwarts Library — with the attachment of a recipe she had asked for — he spilled ink on the piece of parchment.
It is possible she was married to Diego's duellist grandfather.
