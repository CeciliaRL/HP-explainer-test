Dominique Maestro was a wizard or witch whose name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of Professor Trelawney's Divination Classroom.


== Behind the scenes ==
The name of this character is a tribute to Dominic Masters, who worked as an assistant art director on the first three Harry Potter films.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film) (First mentioned) (Name seen on wood panel)
