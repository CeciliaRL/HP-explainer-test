This individual was a Japanese wizard who attended Mahoutokoro. He was a skilled potioneer and represented his school at the Wizarding Schools Potions Championship.


== Biography ==


=== Mahoutokoro ===
The Japanese wizard attended Mahoutokoro for his education.


=== Wizarding Schools Potions Championship ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			The wizard running past the River Troll underneath the bridge 		 	 
The wizard became a skilled potioneer and at age seventeen or above competed in the Wizarding Schools Potions Championship for the golden cauldron, where he represented his school, Mahoutokoro. At the start of the championship in the enchanted garden, he headed towards the bridge, hoping to get an early lead. However, while crossing the bridge, he awakened a River Troll. Fortunately, the Troll was incapacitated by the Hogwarts potions champion, who used a Shrinking Solution on it.


== Physical appearance ==
The wizard wore his school uniform and had black hair.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Potions: This individual was highly proficient in the magical art and science of potion-making, being talented enough that he represented his school in the Wizarding Schools Potions Championship.


== Behind the scenes ==
This is the first student of Mahoutokoro introduced throughout the Harry Potter series.
