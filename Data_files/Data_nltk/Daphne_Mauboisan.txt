Daphne Mauboisan was a witch who lived nearby Armando Dippet in the early 1990s. On 1 September, 1992, when Harry Potter and Ronald Weasley flew a Ford Anglia to Hogwarts Castle (a breach of the International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy), Mauboisan, along with Leonora Gore, blamed elderly Armando Dippet, who was known for his erratic flying.


== Behind the scenes ==
Given that the newspaper in Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets is non canon, this woman may or may not exist.
