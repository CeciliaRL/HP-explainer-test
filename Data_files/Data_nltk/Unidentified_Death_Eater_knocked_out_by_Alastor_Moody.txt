This individual was a Dark Wizard and Death Eater who fought during the Battle of the Department of Mysteries in the Second Wizarding War. He was about to cast a spell when Alastor Moody used his staff to magically knock him out, possibly with the Knockback Jinx or a similar spell.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible that this Death Eater is either Avery or Mulciber.
