This individual was a wizard or witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the late 1800s.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===
This individual received a late acceptance to the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where their unusual ability to perceive and master Ancient Magic was discovered.


== Behind the scenes ==
This individual is the protagonist of Hogwarts Legacy. Their name, gender, house and physical appearance are chosen by the player.
This individual was possibly a Late-bloomer, since they were said to have received their acceptance letter late.
