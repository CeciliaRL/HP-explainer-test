MacDuff was a wizard who lived during the early 20th century. He was a member of Gellert Grindelwald's Alliance, and was among the dark wizard's foremost acolytes.


== Biography ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			MacDuff in Paris 		 	 
In 1927, he accompanied Gellert Grindelwald to Paris to assist with his plans. He wore a chain decorated with a number of items, including a rabbit's foot and a collection of human teeth, during his time assisting Grindelwald.


== Behind the scenes ==
MacDuff was portrayed by Andrew Turner in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.
