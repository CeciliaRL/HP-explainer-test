== 1991 Start-of-Term Feast attendants and attendees ==


=== Attendant with a moustache and goatee ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This unidentified individual was employed by Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry from at least 1991 to 1992.

 	 	 	 		 		 		 			This wizard with Septima Vector during Christmastime 		 	 
In 1991, this individual attended the Welcoming Feast and watched the first years being Sorted, sat by Severus Snape. He attended the Gryffindor vs. Slytherin Quidditch match that year, and watched as Harry Potter nearly swallowed the Golden Snitch. === Hogwarts wizard in the Middle Courtyard ===

In the 1991–1992 school year, this wizard attended the Start-of-Term Feast and watched the First years being Sorted. == The crowd around Albus Dumbledore's dead body ==


=== Wizard with white hair and beard ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This male individual is among a large group of Hogwarts students and staff gathered around the body of Dumbledore, which had fallen from the Astronomy Tower. He arrived at the place with the other staff and whilst the crowd looked on at Harry Potter, who had crouched down beside Dumbledore. When the time came, he followed Professor McGonagall's sign of respect and lit his wand, and raised his arm in the air with the crowd of students to show their respects to the late headmaster; in doing so the combined faculty and students of Hogwarts managed to rid the sky of the overshadowing Dark Mark.
