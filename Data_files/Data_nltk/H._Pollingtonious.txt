H. Pollingtonious was a Healer who compiled The Healer's Helpmate. This book featured home remedies to common wizarding ailments.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is presumably a tribute to Heather Pollington, a graphic artist who worked on Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, as well as Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
