This girl was a witch and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s, who was a member of Hufflepuff house. She participated in the Battle of Hogwarts and survived.


== Behind the scenes ==
She was portrayed by Jade Stanger in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
