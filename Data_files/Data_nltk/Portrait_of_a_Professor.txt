This portrait depicted a Professor sat in a desk with a crystal ball on top and a terrestrial globe nearby. On the background, one could see bookshelves and a high window. It was hung in Gryffindor Tower by the 1990s.


== Behind the scenes ==
In The Wizarding World of Harry Potter, this portrait, along with the portrait of a female etiquette teacher and the portrait of a Quidditch player, explains in magical terms how the ride portion of Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey will work.
