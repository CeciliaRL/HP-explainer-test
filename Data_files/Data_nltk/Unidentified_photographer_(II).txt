This individual was an American No-Maj who worked as photographer in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
He interviewed a witness who saw a building get destroyed by an Obscurus in New York in 1926. After interviewing, he was asked by a reporter colleague if he felt like getting a drink, to which the photographer replied that he had promised Martha (presumably his wife or girlfriend) that he would abstain from consuming alcohol.


== Behind the scenes ==
The photographer was portrayed by Tom Clarke Hill in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
