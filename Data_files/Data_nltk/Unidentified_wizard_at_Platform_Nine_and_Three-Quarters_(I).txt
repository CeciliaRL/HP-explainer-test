This individual was a wizard who stood at Platform 9 3/4 helping his daughter carry her owl, who was aboard the Hogwarts Express on their way to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.


== Behind the scenes ==
He is portrayed by Sean Francis George in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
