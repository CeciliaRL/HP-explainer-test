Franklin Greenwood was a No-Maj author in the early 20th century.
He conceived a story idea in collaboration with Mattie Lovett. This served as the basis for a book by author Maxwell Lima. Lima's book was in turn adapted into the musical The New York Affair by playwright Maurice Willard.
