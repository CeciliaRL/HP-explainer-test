Olivia Gleaves was a witch who was reported missing along with her relative, Toby, on the Potterwatch wireless programme in 1997. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Peter Gleaves, an ADR mixer who worked on every Harry Potter film, excepting Philosopher's Stone and Goblet of Fire.
