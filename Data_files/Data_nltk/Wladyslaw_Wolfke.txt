Wladyslaw Wolfke (pl. Władysław Wroński) was a Polish wizard who played as the Seeker for the 2014 Polish National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
He played in the seventh round match of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, against Japan. Wolfke was described as "a daring and graceful flier" by Ginny Potter. Wolfke was unlucky enough to miss the Snitch early in the game, and was only narrowly beaten to it by Japanese Seeker Noriko Sato in the 59th minute.
