Ernestina Abutor (also known as Red Panther or Tina Estora) was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the United States of America in the 1920s.
In 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Abutor, which stated that she was a No-Maj kidnapper and murderer and offered a reward of 4850 dragots.


== Etymology ==
Abutor is a Latin verb meaning "to consume," "to waste," or "to abuse."
