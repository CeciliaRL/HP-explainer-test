This witch (born 1979 or 1980) starting attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1991.


== Biography ==


=== Education at Hogwarts ===
She was sorted into Slytherin House at the Start-of-Term Feast for the 1991-1992 school year, and sat by Draco Malfoy at the Slytherin table after being sorted.


== Physical description ==
She had short dark hair and wore glasses.
