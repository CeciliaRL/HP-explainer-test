Gulliver Pokeby (1750 - 1839) was a wizard, Magizoologist, expert on magical birds and the author of Why I Didn't Die When the Augurey Cried. He was the first to identify that the Augurey's cries meant rain was coming, rather than impending death. He was immortalised on a Chocolate Frog Card, which featured him wearing his golden pocket watch.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Magizoology: Pokeby was an expert of magical ornithology, the study of magical birds, having famously discovered the true explanation of the Augurey's cries meaning impending rain rather than death, a very important scientific discovery in this field. He wrote the book on the subject Why I Didn't Die When the Augurey Cried, showing his experience and knowledge of his research.


== Etymology ==
Gulliver is French for glutton.
