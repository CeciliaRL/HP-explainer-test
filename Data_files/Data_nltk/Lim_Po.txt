Lim Po was a wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In or around 1926, the International Wizarding Police issued a wanted poster for Po, which stated that he was wanted for "No-Maj and elf murder[s]" committed in Asia and the United States, as well as for "counterfeiting wands." He was described as "powerful" and "extremely dangerous." A reward of 5000 dragots was offered for information leading to his capture.
