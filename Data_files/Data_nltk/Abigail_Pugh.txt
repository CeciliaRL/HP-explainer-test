Abigail Pugh was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993–1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
The name "Abigail" comes from the Hebrew name אֲבִיגָיִל ('Avigayil) meaning "my father is joy".
