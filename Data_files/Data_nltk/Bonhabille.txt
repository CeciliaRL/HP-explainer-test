Madame Bonhabille (fl. 1876) was an established French witch and hat-maker, based in Paris in the late 19th century.


== Biography ==
In 1876, Professor Eupraxia Mole, Headmistress of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, commissioned Madame Bonhabille to make a new custom-made hat for Peeves, the resident Poltergeist. This was in an attempt to calm Peeves down after then-caretaker Rancorous Carpe devised an "elaborate trap" to banish him from the school premises.


== Etymology ==
In French, her name translates to well-dressed.
