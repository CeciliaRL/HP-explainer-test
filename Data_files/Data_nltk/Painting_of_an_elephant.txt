This painting of an elephant hung at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1993-1994 school year, on the seventh-floor level of the Grand Staircase, among paintings of other animals such as a caged monkey, a tiger and a hippopotamus. When the Fat Lady was attacked by Sirius Black this elephant was one of them that was shocked.
