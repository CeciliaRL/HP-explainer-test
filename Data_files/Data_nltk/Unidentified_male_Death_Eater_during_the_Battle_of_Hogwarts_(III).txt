This individual was a Dark Wizard and a Death Eater who served Lord Voldemort.


== Biography ==
In 1997, he was present during the Meeting at Malfoy Manor. In 1998, he participated in the Battle of Hogwarts, and was present in the Forbidden Forest when Voldemort killed Harry Potter. He was later seen when the Death Eaters were fleeing from the battle. After Voldemort's defeat he was possibly killed or imprisoned in Azkaban afterwards.


== Physical Appearance ==
This Death Eater seemed to have fairly large ears, fairly short brown hair, and sunken eyes. He also had a large "Adam's Apple" around his neck area.


== Behind the scenes ==
This Death Eater was portrayed by Tony Kirwood in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
