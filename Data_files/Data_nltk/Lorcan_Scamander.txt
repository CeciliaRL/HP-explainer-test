Lorcan Scamander was one of the twins born to Luna Lovegood and Rolf Scamander. He has a twin brother named Lysander.


== Biography ==
When his mother travelled to the Patagonian Desert, in Argentina, to attend the final of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, in July 2014, Lorcan was left at home with his grandfather, along with Lysander.


== Etymology ==
Lorcan means "little fierce one" in Gaelic.
