Edwardus Lima was a wizard and the author of the magizoology tome The Monster Book of Monsters.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Eduardo Lima, a graphic artist who has worked on every Harry Potter film since Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, on concept art of The Monster Book of Monsters by his colleague, Miraphora Mina.
