This individual was a wizard who fought during the Battle of Hogwarts at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1998. 


== Biography ==
On 2 May 1998, he was one of the many wizards and witches who fought bravely in the Battle of Hogwarts against Lord Voldemort and the Death Eaters. During the final stage of the battle, he was seen fighting a bald Death Eater in the Great Hall, who he managed to kill while duelling ferociously against the Death Eaters. It is unknown if he survived the battle.
