Georgia Clark-Day was a witch who was reported missing on the Potterwatch wireless programme in 1997. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Behind this scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Georgia Clarke-Day, the daughter of Mark Day, an editor who worked on the final four Harry Potter films, including Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1. Clarke-Day is an actress and played a Hogwarts student in the film adaptations of Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix and Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
