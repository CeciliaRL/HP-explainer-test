Corvus Lestrange was a French wizard and a member of the pure-blood Lestrange family. He was the son of Corvus Lestrange and Minette Lestrange. He had two sisters, Manon and Josette. He married his cousin Eglantine Lestrange and had a son with her, Corvus.
