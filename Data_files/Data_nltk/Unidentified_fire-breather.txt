This individual was a fire-breather in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			The Fire-breather with the Circus 		 	 
He was part of the Circus Arcanus, a travelling freak show.
During the 2020s, he appeared as a Foundable during the Calamity. Volunteer members of the Statute of Secrecy Task Force were able to free him, allowing he to return to his original time.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is unknown if fire-breathing was an ability he was naturally born with, or something he archived through magical means, e.g. through use of the Fire-Breathing Potion.
