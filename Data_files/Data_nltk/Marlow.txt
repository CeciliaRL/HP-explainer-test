Marlow was one of the six Bowtruckles kept by Newt Scamander, along with Pickett, Titus, Finn, Poppy, and Tom.


== Behind the scenes ==
Eddie Redmayne named this Bowtruckle after his goddaughter Marlowe Sturridge (child of his friends Sienna Miller and Tom Sturridge).
