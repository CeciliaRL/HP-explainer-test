This individual was the brother of Constance Pickering.


== Biography ==
Before the start of Constance first year at Hogwarts, their mother wouldn't buy her a new cauldron. She gave Constance her brother's old, used one and bought him a new one. According to Constance, he was absolutely rubbish at potions.
