Sloan Travers was a witch or wizard who knew Constance Pickering. When they heard she was trying to be an Animagus, they told her about a friend of a friend who had botched the spell and became a half wizard, half naked mole-rat. Constance then decided not to continue and did not drink the potion needed to complete the process.
