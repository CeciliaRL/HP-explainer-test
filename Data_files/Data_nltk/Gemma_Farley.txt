Gemma Farley was a witch who began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry between 1985 and 1987.


== Biography ==
Sorted into Slytherin, she was made a prefect by 1991, and welcomed first years when they initially arrived in her House. Among them would have been boys Draco Malfoy, Gregory Goyle, Vincent Crabbe, Blaise Zabini, and Theodore Nott and girls Pansy Parkinson, Millicent Bulstrode, Daphne Greengrass and Tracey Davis.
