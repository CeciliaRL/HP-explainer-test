This unnamed Death Eater and Walden Macnair were sent by Lord Voldemort to convince the Giants to join him in 1995. They arrived and tried to convince the new Gurg Golgomath to join them. He eventually agreed and then told this Death Eater and Macnair how Rubeus Hagrid and Olympe Maxime had tried to convince the old Gurg Karkus to join the Order of the Phoenix led by Albus Dumbledore. Then this Death Eater and Macnair started searching the caves but soon after Hagrid and Maxime left.
