Eduaphora Mergus was a witch who wrote an article titled "My Week Without Runes!" that was published in an issue of The Quibbler in 1996.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character's unique given name was presumably created as a tribute to the Harry Potter film graphic artists Eduardo Lima and Miraphora Mina.
