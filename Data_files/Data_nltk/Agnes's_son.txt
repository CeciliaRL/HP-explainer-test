An unknown individual was the son of Agnes, a patient in the long-term Janus Thickey Ward at St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries. He intended to visit her over Christmas in 1995. When the Healer Miriam Strout told Agnes of the owl he had sent informing them that he would be coming, her only reply was to give several loud barks.
