The daughters of Caspar Crouch and Charis Black were pure-blood witches.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible that their brother was Bartemius Crouch Senior, although this is unconfirmed.
It is also possible that Araminta Meliflua is one of them as she is Walburga's first or second cousin.
