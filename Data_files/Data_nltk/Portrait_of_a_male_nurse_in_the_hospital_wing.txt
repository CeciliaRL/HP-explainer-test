This was a portrait in the Hospital Wing at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry of a man looking after a patient lying in bed.


== Behind the scenes ==
This portrait is a copy of the 1891 portrait "The Doctor", by British painter and illustrator Sir Samuel Luke Fildes (1843-1927).
