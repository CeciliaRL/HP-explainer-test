This individual was a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. During the Triwizard Tournament in 1994, she was asked to the Yule Ball by a Durmstrang student.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is possible that this student is Marietta Edgecombe, who was described as strawberry blonde in the books.
