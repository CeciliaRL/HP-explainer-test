Dr. D. Shaman was a wizard doctor and advice columnist.
He wrote the "Ask D. Shaman" advice column for the Daily Prophet starting from the November 29 1926 issue onward.
He also wrote an article about Wrackspurts, entitled "Wrackspurts: Unfuss the Mystery", for a 1996 issue of The Quibbler.
