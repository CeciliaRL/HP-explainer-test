This centaur was a member of the Forbidden Forest centaur colony.


== History ==
In 1996 during the Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest, he suggested to the Forbidden Forest centaur colony that, after they attacked Dolores Umbridge, they do the same to Harry Potter and Hermione Granger. Fortunately, he and the other centaurs were chased away by Grawp.
