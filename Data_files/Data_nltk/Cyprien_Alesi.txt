Cyprien Alesi was a French witch and a Quidditch referee.


== Etymology ==
"Cyprien" or 'Cyprian' is from the Roman family name Cyprianus which meant "from Cyprus" in Latin. Saint Cyprian was a 3rd-century bishop of Carthage and a martyr under the emperor Valerian.
