Saint Brutus was a saint whom St Brutus's Secure Centre for Incurably Criminal Boys was named after.


== Etymology ==
Brutus was an Ancient Roman name, meaning "heavy", "slow", "foolish".


== Behind the scenes ==
As there is no actual Saint Brutus, it is possible Vernon Dursley made up the school as a cover school for Harry.
