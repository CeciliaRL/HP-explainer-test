This wizard was appointed Headmaster of Hogwarts at some unknown time. After his death, his portrait was hung in the Headmaster's office, in order to give advice to the current head. He was depicted sleeping in an chair while studying a book with a celestial globe on the table.


== Behind the scenes ==
This portrait was painted by artist Steve Mitchell. Mitchell created several portraits for the Harry Potter films.
The portrait that hangs in the Headmaster's office set at The Making of Harry Potter contains text in the upper right corner that was not present on the original portrait by Steve Mitchell.
