This Dark witch was a Death Eater, who was summoned to the graveyard in Little Hangleton on the night of 24 June, 1995, after the newly resurrected Lord Voldemort summoned his followers to his side.


== Biography ==


=== First Wizarding War ===
In order to have answered Voldemort's call on the night of his return, this witch must have been a Death Eater during the First Wizarding War, and had to have somehow avoided imprisonment.


=== Voldemort's return ===
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			Lucius Malfoy standing with her whilst watching Voldemort torture Harry Potter 		 	 
This witch arrived at the graveyard in Little Hangleton after Lord Voldemort summoned his Death Eaters there on the night of 24 June, 1995. She Apparated into the area, wearing a Death Eater mask and hood.
She witnessed Harry Potter being put under the Cruciatus Curse by Voldemort. When Harry made to escape, she stood by, as Voldemort had ordered the Death Eaters not to do anything.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Dark Arts: Being a Death Eater, this witch would have been accomplished with dark magic, most likely including the Unforgivable Curses and other dark charms.
Apparition: This witch was capable of apparition, as she was capable of apparating to her master's side in the Little Hangleton graveyard.


== Behind the scenes ==
This woman was portrayed by Olivia Higginbottom in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.
This woman may be Alecto Carrow, a known female Death Eater.
