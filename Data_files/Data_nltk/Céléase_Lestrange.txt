Céléase Lestrange (née Volant) was a French witch born in 1611 who married into the pure-blood Lestrange family. She died in 1635 and was laid to rest in the Lestrange family Mausoleum in Cimetière du Père-Lachaise, Paris, France.


== Behind the scenes ==
Céléase may be related to Salomé Volant and Jodelle Volant, both of whom also married into the Lestrange family.
