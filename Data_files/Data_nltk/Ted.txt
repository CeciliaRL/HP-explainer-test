Ted was a Muggle television newsreader in 1981, working alongside Jim McGuffin. Following the downfall of Lord Voldemort, he reported on the unusual amount of owl activity during the day as messages were passed throughout the Wizarding world, while being unaware that the activity was a result of the celebrations of Lord Voldemort's apparent demise.
