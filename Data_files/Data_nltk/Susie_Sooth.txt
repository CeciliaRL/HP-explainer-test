Susie Sooth was a British witch and Seer who did readings for a living. She was certified by the Divinus Haruspex Association of Clairvoyants. An advertisement promoting her services appeared inside the Knight Bus in 1993.


== Behind the scenes ==
The name of this character was presumably intended as a combination of the word soothsayer, meaning "one who predicts the future," and the name of English musician Siouxsie Sioux (pronounced "SOO-zee soo"), who is best known as the lead singer of the band Siouxsie and the Banshees.
