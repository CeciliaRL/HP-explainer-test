These Hogwarts students started at Hogwarts presumably in 1992, who were members of Ravenclaw house.


== Biography ==
In 1995, they appeared on the first day of classes at Hogwarts and appeared to be frightened by Harry Potter. Depending on their gender, some of them may have shared a dormitory with Luna Lovegood.
