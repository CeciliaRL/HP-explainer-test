Lukas Karuzos was the author of A New Theory of Numerology, and thus presumably an accomplished Numerologist, as Arithmancy student Hermione Granger anticipated receiving a copy of his book.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is presumably named after Luca Caruso, the son of Miraphora Mina, a graphic artist who worked on all eight Harry Potter films.
