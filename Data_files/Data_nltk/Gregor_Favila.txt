Gregor Favila was an American  wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In or around 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Favila, which stated he was wanted for "No-Maj murder." It described him as a "vicious wizard" and a "malicious killer," warning that he was "bearing [a] wand, most menacing and extremely dangerous." A reward of 4,500 Dragots was offered for information leading to his capture.
