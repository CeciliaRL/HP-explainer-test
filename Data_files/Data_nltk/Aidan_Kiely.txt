Aidan Kiely was an Irish wizard who played as the Seeker for the Kenmare Kestrels Quidditch team during the 1990s.


== Biography ==
In a game against the Montrose Magpies, Aidan managed to catch the Snitch against opposing seeker Alasdair Maddock when the former attempted to use Muggle Football techniques during the game.


== Behind the scenes ==
Aidan Kiely was named for Aidan Kiely, the author's close friend's brother who died as a child.
