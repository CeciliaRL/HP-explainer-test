William I (c. 1028 - 1087), also known as William the Conqueror, was the first Norman King of England.


== Biography ==
He led the Norman conquest of England in 1066. The wizard Armand Malfoy was among his invading army. Sometime after being crowned king, William granted Malfoy a piece of land in Wiltshire, in return for questionable services which Armand had rendered him.
