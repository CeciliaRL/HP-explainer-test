Gladys Gudgeon was a witch and fan of Gilderoy Lockhart. She wrote fan letters to him every week, and continued to do so when Lockhart was admitted to St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries, even though Lockhart, having lost his memory, had no idea why she was writing to him. 


== Etymology ==
The name "Gladys" is from the old Welsh name Gwladus, possibly derived from gwlad "country".
