Abel Treetops (fl. 1832) was an American wizard from Cincinnati who claimed to have patented a method of taming Wampus cats for use as guards over wizarding houses. Treetops was exposed as a fraud when MACUSA officials raided his home and found him putting Engorgement Charms on Kneazles.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Charms: Treetops appeared to be skilled with the casting of charms, as he successfully used the Engorgement Charm to enlarge the size of several Kneazles to make them appear as Wampus cats.
