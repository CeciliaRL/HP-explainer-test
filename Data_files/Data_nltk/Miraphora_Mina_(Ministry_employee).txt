Miraphora Mina (b. 21st January to 18th February) was a witch who worked in the Runes & Symbols department of the Ministry of Magic.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Miraphora Mina, a graphic artist who worked on every film in the Harry Potter film series and Fantastic Beasts film series.
