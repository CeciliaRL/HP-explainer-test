Josefina Calderon was a 17th-century Mexican witch and Healer who became the wife of Chadwick Boot, one of the founders of Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
The couple established the Calderon-Boot family, which remains one of America's most prominent wizarding families today.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Healing magic: As a professional Healer, Calderon would have been very accomplished with medical magic, such as with healing spells, potions and other magical remedies.
