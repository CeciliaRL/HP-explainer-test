Frazier was a wizard who had a portrait of himself hung somewhere in Hogwarts Castle by the 1992-1993 school year.


== Behind the scenes ==
This portrait appears in the PC version of the video game adaptation of Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets. In the game's files, it was called Frazier.bmp.
