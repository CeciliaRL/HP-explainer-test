Anjali Kapoor was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Anjali is an Indian name of Sanskrit origin, it means "homage" or "tribute". Kapoor is a North Indian surname, widely found in the state of Punjab.


== Behind the scenes ==
Based on her name, she is most likely of Indian descent.
