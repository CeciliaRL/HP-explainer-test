Two young German-speaking children and their mother were murdered with the Killing Curse by Lord Voldemort on 1 September, 1997.


== Biography ==
Lord Voldemort had come to the family's home looking for the wandmaker Gregorovitch. It is unknown if they were related to Gregorovitch or simply moved into the house after he left. The children and their mother were murdered by Voldemort with the Killing Curse, and Harry Potter was forced to witness the deaths of the children and their mother due to his connection with Voldemort.
