Napoléon, born Napoleone di Buonaparte, was a French military and political leader who led successful campaigns in the French Revolution. He was the de facto leader of the French Republic, then Emperor of the French from 1804 until 1814 and again in 1815.
In 1927, Le Cri de la Gargouille reported that a book proposing Napoléon to be a wizard was newly released.
