The son of Celestina Warbeck was the product of her second marriage to a wizard who worked as her manager. His parents split after ten years of marriage when Celestina took up with Irving Warble.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is unknown whether both parents shared custody of their son, whether Celestina gained custody, or whether this child lived with his father.
