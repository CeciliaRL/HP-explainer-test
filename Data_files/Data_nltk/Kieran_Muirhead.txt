Kieran Muirhead was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during 1993-1994.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2, Xbox and GameCube versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
