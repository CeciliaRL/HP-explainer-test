Mrs Aldermaston was the mother of Hogwarts student Dennis Aldermaston. In 1996, her son hoped to receive a new broomstick from her for Christmas.


== Behind the scenes ==
This particular individual is only mentioned in the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
