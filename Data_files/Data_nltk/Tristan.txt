Tristan was an ancient Celtic hero who tried to kill the giant Morholt. He was wounded with a poisoned sword by the giant in the process.


== Behind the scenes ==
Tristan was one of the main characters of the Celtic legend Tristan and Iseult. He was a Knight of the Round Table and nephew of King Mark of Cornwall.
