Greg "Crash" Ruczinski was an American wizard who played as a Beater on the American National Quidditch team. He wore a helmet with a brown pumpkin design on it. By 1996, he was featured on a Chocolate Frog Card.

Harry Potter: Quidditch World Cup (First appearance)
