This was a Hippogriff with a chestnut brown coat and a member of the Hogwarts Hippogriff herd.
In 1993, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley approached it during a Care of Magical Creatures lesson, watched by Harry Potter.


== Behind the scenes ==
He/she may be Stormswift, Fleetwing or Hothoof.
This may have been the the Hippogriff newly acquired by Professor Silvanus Kettleburn in the "Magical Creatures Everywhere" quest in Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery.
