Anna Hill-Hall (fl. 1996) was a Muggle who worked as the data retrieval officer for the Metropolitan Police of London. Her name appeared in a July 1996 cover story in the The Guardian, having been quoted as saying that "nothing of value" had been found in all of the retrieved CCTV footage of the collapse of the Millennium Bridge.
