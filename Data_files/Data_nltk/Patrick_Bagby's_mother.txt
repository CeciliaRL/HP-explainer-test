Mrs Bagby was the mother of the Hufflepuff student Patrick Bagby. She loved Quidditch and would have loved for her son to have been Seeker.


== Behind the scenes ==
This particular individual was only mentioned in the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.


== Appearances ==
