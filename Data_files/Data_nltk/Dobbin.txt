Dobbin was a Hogwarts student in the early 1990s. They played as one of the two Beaters for one of the School's Quidditch teams.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game) (First mentioned)


== Behind the scenes ==
In Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, Sybill Trelawney refers to the Centaur Firenze using this unflattering nickname. "Dobbin" is a rural slang term for a common horse.
