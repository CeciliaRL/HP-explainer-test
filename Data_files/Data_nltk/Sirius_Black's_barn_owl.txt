This barn owl was owned by Sirius Black. He used it to deliver a letter to his godson Harry Potter.
Harry found the owl in his dormitory after being informed by his best friend Ron (whom both had temporarily fallen out at the time) that it was there.


== Behind the scenes ==
In the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, there is a different owl which delivered Sirius' letter to Harry, which also had a tendency to bite people. The owl in the book did not bite Harry.
