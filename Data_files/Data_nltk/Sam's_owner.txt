This individual was a Muggle Londoner who was present during the collapse of the Millennium Bridge in 1996.


== Biography ==
This individual was crossing the bridge with his pet puggle Sam when the collapse took place. When both were thrown down into River Thames, this individual was left unconscious. Sam, then dragged him as he swam half a mile down the river, to safety. The Guardian later went on to report that Sam was to become the first dog ever to be honoured with a medal for gallantry.
