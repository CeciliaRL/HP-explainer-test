This cook was a British wizard or witch who worked at the Leaky Cauldron in 1993. For unknown reasons, Ron Weasley showed this cook the Daily Prophet article about his family's trip to Egypt.


== Behind the scenes ==
This cook seems to be a skilled and perhaps somewhat morbid witch or wizard, as the pea soup at the Leaky Cauldron, presumably made by him or her, is noted to be able to eat the person who orders it.
