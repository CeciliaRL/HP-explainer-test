This individual was a witch and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1910s who was sorted in Gryffindor house.


== Biography ==
Sometime in the 1910s, whilst attending Hogwarts, she and her friend spitefully gossiped about Leta Lestrange about being an unwanted child and for the death of her half-brother, before her friend got hit with the dark charm Oscausi by Leta out of retaliation, sealing her mouth shut.


== Behind the scenes ==
This girl was portrayed by Jemima Woolnough in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.
