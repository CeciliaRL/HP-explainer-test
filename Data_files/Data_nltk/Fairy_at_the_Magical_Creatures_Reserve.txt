This female fairy lived in the Magical Creatures Reserve in the 1980s.


== Biography ==
In the 1980s, Jacob's sibling would often bond with this fairy. They once went to the Potions Classroom together to distract Severus Snape so that they could rescue another fairy, whose wings were intended to be used as a potion ingredient.
This fairy and her friends also helped create fairy light at the Valentine's Day Ball.
