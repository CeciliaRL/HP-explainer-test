This individual was a Ministry of Magic official after the Fall of the Ministry of Magic.


== Biography ==
On 2 September, 1997, he calmed the Ministry pamphlet makers down after Harry Potter released a bunch of Decoy Detonators in order to distract them and gain entrance to Dolores Umbridge's office.


== Behind the scenes ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 

This individual was portrayed by George Potts in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, credited as "Bald Wizard".
