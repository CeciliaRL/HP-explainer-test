This Policeman was an American No-Maj who worked for the New York Police Department in 1926.


== Biography ==
He and fellow officer caught Newton Scamander and Jacob Kowalski believing that they were robbing a jewellery store before they saw a lion that escaped from the Central Park Zoo, leaving the two suspects to escape.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Policeman was portrayed by Joseph Macnab in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
