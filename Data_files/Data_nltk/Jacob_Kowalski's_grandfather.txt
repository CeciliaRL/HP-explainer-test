The grandfather of Jacob Kowalski lived in the early 20th century.
During his lifetime, he enjoyed keeping pigeons, a hobby that he apparently passed on to his grandson. Queenie Goldstein learned about his pigeon-keeping while reading Jacob's thoughts in 1926 on a rooftop.
