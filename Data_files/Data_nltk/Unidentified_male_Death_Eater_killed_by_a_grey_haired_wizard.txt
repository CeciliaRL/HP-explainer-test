This individual was a Dark Wizard and Death Eater who served Lord Voldemort during the Second Wizarding War.


== Biography ==
He became a member of the Death Eater sometime prior to or during the Second Wizarding War, presumably participating in numerous battles with his fellow Death Eaters, such as the Battle of the Department of Mysteries, Battle of the Astronomy Tower and Battle of the Seven Potters. On 2 May, 1998, he fought at the Battle of Hogwarts as a member of Lord Voldemort's army of Death Eaters, Snatchers and Dark creatures. During the final stage of the battle, he was killed during the Skirmish at the Great Hall by a grey haired wizard.
