The following is a list of Durmstrang Institute students whose exact identity is not known.


== Unidentified Durmstrang boy (I) ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 
This individual was a wizard who attended Durmstrang Institute, and came to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for the Triwizard Tournament in 1994. While he and his classmates were coming in down the Great Hall, he blew out a fiery bird-symbol that represented the school. He put his name into the Goblet of Fire, but Viktor Krum was chosen as his school's Triwizard Champion. He appeared, watching Krum, in every task, and danced at the Yule Ball. At the end of the year after Cedric Diggory had been murdered, the student attended the Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory.

Behind the scenes
This student is portrayed by Ponciano Almeida in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.
Appearances
Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film) (First appearance)


== Unidentified Durmstrang boy (II) ==
This individual was a wizard who attended Durmstrang Institute in the 1990s. He was one of the students who travelled to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for the Triwizard Tournament and put his name in the Goblet of Fire, however Viktor Krum was chosen as Champion. He also attended the memorial feast of Cedric Diggory following his murder.

Behind the scenes
This student is played by Oliver Miceli in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.
Appearances
Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film) (First appearance)


== Unidentified Durmstrang boy (III) ==

This individual was a student at Durmstrang Institute. During the Triwizard Tournament at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, he asked a Ravenclaw student to go to the Yule Ball with him, which she agreed to.

Appearances
Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film) (First appearance)
