Edmund Bollifant was a publisher based in London in the late 16th century. His imprint published A Perambulation of Kent in 1596.


== Behind the scenes ==
This publisher was mentioned on a prop book spine label featured in the Hogwarts library scenes throughout the Harry Potter films. This label was based on the title page of an actual 1596 edition of A Perambulation of Kent by William Lambarde.
