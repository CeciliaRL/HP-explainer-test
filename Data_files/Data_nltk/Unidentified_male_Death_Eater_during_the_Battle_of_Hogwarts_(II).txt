The Death Eater in the crowd 		 	 
This Dark Wizard was a Death Eater and one of Lord Voldemort's servants during the Second Wizarding War. He fought during the Battle of Hogwarts in 1998, although it is unknown if he was killed during the battle, abandoned the battle or was defeated, arrested and sentenced to imprisonment in Azkaban for his crimes as as a Death Eater.
