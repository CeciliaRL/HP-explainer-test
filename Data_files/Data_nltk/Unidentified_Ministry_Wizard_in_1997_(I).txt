This wizard worked at the Ministry of Magic in 1997 during Lord Voldemort's reign after the death of Rufus Scrimgeour. He was at the Ministry when the trio went into the Atrium when they infiltrated the Ministry.


== Behind the scenes ==
He is portrayed by Pete Perry in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1.
