M. Carneiro was a Muggle stage director and production designer. Carneiro directed and designed the adapted production of Romeo and Juliet that was staged at the Regency Playhouse in the summer of 1997.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Mauricio Carneiro, who worked as a costume illustrator for every Harry Potter film from Goblet of Fire onward, and as a costumer for Order of the Phoenix and Half-Blood Prince.
