This goblin was a spokesgoblin employed by Gringotts Wizarding Bank in the early 1990s. After the break-in at the bank on 31 July, 1991, this spokesgoblin gave an interview to the Daily Prophet, advising wizards to keep their noses out of Gringotts business.
