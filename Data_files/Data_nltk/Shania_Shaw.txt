Shania Shaw was an American witch, feminist and member of the Statute of Secrecy Task Force.


== Biography ==
In her adult life, she became a women's activist and joined the Salem Witches' Institute. At some point during 2020, she and a cadre of its members joined the Statute of Secrecy Task Force, bolstering the efforts to contain the Calamity.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Charms: She was an expert on the usage of the Hour-Reversal Charm. Her knowledge of the time manipulating spell allowed her to illuminate her colleagues, and to be seen as a "master" in its use.
