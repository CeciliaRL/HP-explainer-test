This individual was a wizard, who was a student that attended the wizarding school of Castelobruxo in Brazil.


== Biography ==
At some point, he became pen-friends with Bill Weasley, and their two families were invited to participate in a student exchange programme. Unfortunately, due to the Weasley family's poor financial situation, Bill was unable to go. His pen-friend was extremely offended and sent Bill a cursed hat, which made his ears shrivel up.


== Behind the scenes ==
In the German and Brazilian translations, this individual is a girl.
