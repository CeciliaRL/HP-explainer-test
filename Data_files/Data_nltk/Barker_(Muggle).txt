Barker was an American No-Maj man who worked as an assistant to Henry Shaw Senior at the Shaw Tower in New York in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
When Langdon Shaw arrived with the Barebone family on 6 December 1926 hoping to see his father, Barker nervously told him that Shaw Snr was in a meeting with his brother, but Langdon pushed past him. Barker then profusely apologised to his boss for Langdon's interruption.


== Behind the scenes ==
Barker was portrayed by Tom Hodgkins in the film Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
Christopher Wilson was Barker's stand-in for the film.
