This wizard was a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Gryffindor.


== Biography ==


=== Early years ===
He was born during the 1980s and attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he was sorted into Gryffindor.


=== Fifth year ===
In 1995, he and his friend were walking in the corridors when Dolores Umbridge came by. She used a spell to tidy their ties and pull their shirts into their trousers.


=== Later life ===
It is unknown what became of him next during the Second Wizarding War or during the Battle of Hogwarts.
