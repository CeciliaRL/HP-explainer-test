This witch was responsible for helping visitors and telling patients which floor to go to at St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries. 


== Biography ==
When Harry Potter visited Arthur Weasley with the Weasleys at Christmas 1995, the Welcome Witch appeared to be a typical secretary type; somewhat rude, brief, and monotonous, which was likely used to be humorous when Harry Potter read a sign, reading "Our Welcome Witch will be happy to assist you".


== Physical description ==
She was a squat little woman with short, blonde hair and blue eyes.
