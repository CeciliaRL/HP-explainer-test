Lana Tuttle was the older sister of Liz Tuttle. The two were very close, with Liz saying she would do anything for her sister.
Lana owned a crup that had cruppies in the early 1980s; she wrote to her sister at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry to let her know.
