Glynnis Griffiths was a British witch who was the Seeker for the Holyhead Harpies. She caught the Snitch after a seven-day match against the Heidelberg Harriers in 1953.


== Behind the scenes ==
Griffiths has the same surname as the late British actor Richard Griffiths, who portrayed Vernon Dursley in the Harry Potter film series.
