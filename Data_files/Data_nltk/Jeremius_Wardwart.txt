Jeremius Wardwart was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the United States of America in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Wardwart, which stated that he was wanted for No-Maj kidnap and murder and "bearing wand, most menacing & extremely dangerous." A reward of 4500 Dragots was offered for assistance in his capture.
