Babayaga was a medieval Russian hag who regularly ate human children for breakfast, and presumably lunch and tea as well. She lived in a hut that stood on giant chicken legs. She appeared on a hag Chocolate Frog Card.


== Behind the scenes ==
The name Babayaga is based on the Russian folkloric legend of the Baba Yaga.
In the Russian myth of Baba Yaga, she lures children in to her house - which stands on giant chicken legs - and feeds on them. When they least expect it, she then throws them in the oven to cook and eat them.
