The niece of Great Britain's Muggle Prime Minister was delighted when he gave her a pet gerbil as a gift. What her uncle hadn't told her, however, was that the gerbil had originally been a teacup, which then-Minister for Magic Cornelius Fudge had transformed to prove that he was a wizard during their first meeting.
