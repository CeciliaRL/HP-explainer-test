Jan Kowalski was a Polish nobleman who lived during an unknown period. He may have been a relative of the American factory worker Jacob Kowalski.


== Behind the scenes ==
Jan Kowalski is played by Kamil Lemieszewski in the film inspired by Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
