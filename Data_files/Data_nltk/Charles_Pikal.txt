Charles Pikal (1935-1972) was a Muggle who lived in Little Hangleton and died of unknown reasons at age 37. He was buried in the Little Hangleton graveyard.


== Behind the scenes ==
In the video game, Charles Pikal's gravestone is repeated and, as such, the game makes it seem that he had multiple tombstones.
