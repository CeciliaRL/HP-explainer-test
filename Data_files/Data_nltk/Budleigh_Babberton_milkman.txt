This individual was a Muggle served Budleigh Babberton in the capacity of milkman during the 1990s.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character does not appear during the main game, however, his milk float can be seen and interacted with. He is one of the game's unlockable characters, and appears nearly identical to another milkman in the first LEGO game.
