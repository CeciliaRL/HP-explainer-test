Thomas Paley was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was usually seen spending time by the Viaduct with Terrence Fogarty during the 1996–1997 school year. He confessed to Harry Potter that he would like a new broom and admitted it was a disaster in Potions.


== Etymology ==
The name "Thomas" is the Greek form of the Aramaic name תָּאוֹמָא (Ta'oma') which meant "twin".


== Behind the scenes ==
This particular student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.
