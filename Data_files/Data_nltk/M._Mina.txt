M. Mina was a wizard or witch who competed in duels. The Daily Prophet published a special report in 1996 about a duel between M. Mina and E. Lima that was the final match of a duelling competition.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Miraphora Mina, a graphic artist who worked on every Harry Potter film.
