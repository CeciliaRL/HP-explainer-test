This individual was a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was Sorted into either Ravenclaw or Slytherin, and was very keen on the subject of earth magic which he expected to study in the 1991–1992 school year.


== Biography ==


=== Early life ===

This individual was born no later than 1980.


=== Hogwarts years ===

He started at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in or before 1991 and was to be Sorted into either Ravenclaw or Slytherin. He was not very fond of Draco Malfoy, congratulating Harry Potter after the latter beat him in a duel.
He was expecting to join the earth magic classes in the start of the 1991–1992 school year. While en route to Hogwarts on 1 September, 1991, he sat on a compartment while picking his nose and eating his bogeys.
