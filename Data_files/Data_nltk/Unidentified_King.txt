This King was the monarch of a land in the Middle Ages. Leticia Somnolens, the local Hag, was jealous of the King's daughter and sent her into a deep sleep by means of the Draught of Living Death. His story appears to be the source of inspiration for the Muggle fairy tale, Sleeping Beauty.
