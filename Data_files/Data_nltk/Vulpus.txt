Professor Vulpus was a wizard. It is unknown if he was a Muggle-born, half-blood or pure blood.


== Biography ==


=== Lifetime ===
Professor Vulpus was appointed as Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry sometime before Armando Dippet.


=== Post-mortem ===
After his death, his portrait was hung in the Headmaster's office in order to give advice to the current Head.


== Etymology ==
The name Vulpus may come from Vulpes, the Latin word for "fox".
