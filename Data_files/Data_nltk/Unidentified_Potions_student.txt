This student was a wizard or witch and a student of Horace Slughorn's during his first tenure as Potions Master at Hogwarts (between c. 1920 and 1981). They were the first and only student before Harry Potter to brew a Draught of Living Death with sufficient quality to earn a small vial of Felix Felicis.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student may have been a member of the Slug Club, as Professor Slughorn accepts talented students.
It is possible that this student may have been Tom Riddle, but is more likely it may have been Severus Snape or Lily Evans who were both known to have had an aptitude for Potions.
