Luxo Karuzos (fl. 15th century) was wizarding artist. He provided the original illustrations featured in the book The Tales of Beedle the Bard.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Luca Caruso, the son of Miraphora Mina, a graphic artist who worked on all eight Harry Potter films.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (First mentioned) (Name seen on book cover)
