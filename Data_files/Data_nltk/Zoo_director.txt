The zoo director was a British Muggle who was in charge of the Zoo in the summer of 1991. 


== Biography ==
On the 23 June, 1991, when the boa constrictor escaped from the reptile house after the glass in front of its cage mysteriously vanished, the zoo director made Petunia Dursley a cup of strong, sweet tea while he apologised over and over again for the incident.
