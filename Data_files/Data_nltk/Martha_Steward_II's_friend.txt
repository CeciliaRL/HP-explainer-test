This individual was a member of the Pocomtuc people during the 17th century. They were magical, but had a brother who was not, although it unknown whether the siblings were, respectively, a No-Maj-born wizard/witch and a No-Maj, or a wizard and Squib born into a wizarding family. This person was friends with Martha Steward, the Squib daughter of Isolt Sayre and James Steward, and their brother eventually married her.
