Sigrid Kristoffersen was a Norwegian witch who played as the Seeker for the 2014 Norwegian National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
During the first match of the 2014 Quidditch World Cup against the Ivory Coast, Kristoffersen beat her opposite Sylvian Boigny to the Golden Snitch after 128 minutes of play, securing a win for Norway.
