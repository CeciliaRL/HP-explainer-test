This individual was an ugly wizard who was a member of the Wizarding Examinations Authority.


== History ==
In 1996, he administered Ronald Weasley's practical Divination O.W.L.. Ron inadvertently insulted him when he seeing "an ugly man with a wart on his nose" in the crystal ball, only to realise he was describing the examiner's reflection.
