Narinder Singh was a Fijian witch who played as Beater for the 2014 Fijian National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
During the second match of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, against Nigeria, Singh and her fellow Beater Quintia Qarase "lacked the ferocity" of the Nigerian Beaters, Aliko Okoye and Mercy Ojukwu. This resulted in the Bludgers doing much harm to the Fijian Chasers, which contributed to the team's bruising defeat.


== Behind the scenes ==
Based on her name, it can be presumed that she was a Fijian of Indian descent.
