Merwyn Finwick was a British wizard and the Keeper for the Tutshill Tornados. Brevis Birch, the team's captain, blamed their unexpected loss in a match against the Ballycastle Bats on a bout of narcolepsy suffered by Merwyn. The Tornados had been at the top of the league before this defeat.
