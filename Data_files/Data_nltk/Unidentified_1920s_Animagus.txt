This individual was a wizard and an Animagus who lived in the early 20th century. In 1927, Le Cri de la Gargouille reported that he was somehow implicated in the "affaire du canard".


== Behind the scenes ==
The newspaper title that mentions this individual is probably a pun: "canard", which means "duck" in French, can also refer to an untrustworthy newspaper, or a false or misleading report.
