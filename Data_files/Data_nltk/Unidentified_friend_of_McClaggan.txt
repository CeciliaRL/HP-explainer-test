This individual was a British Wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in Gryffindor house in the 1920s. At that time, he became friends with another student named McClaggan.


== Behind the scenes ==
McClaggan's friend was portrayed by Isaac Cortinovis Johnson in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.
