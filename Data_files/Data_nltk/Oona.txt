Oona (fl. 12th century) was an English witch lived in the same village as Goodwin Kneen, and worked in the local inn as a Barmaid. After the Kwidditch (Quidditch) team won a match against a team from Ilkley, she gave the team free mead.


== Etymology ==
The name "Oona" is possibly derived from Irish uan meaning "lamb".
