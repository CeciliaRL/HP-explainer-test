This Housewife was an American No-Maj who lived in New York in 1926.


== History ==
She was one of the witnesses when some of Newt Scamander's creatures escaped from his briefcase that year.
When a policeman asked questions about the situation, the Housewife told him that she thought she heard a gas explosion, as she often had problems with them during her life as a housewife, telling the officer she would not take the children back in their apartment until the problem was fixed.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Housewife was portrayed by Anne Wittman in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
