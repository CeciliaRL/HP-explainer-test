Maxine O'Flaherty was a witch that attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
She was sorted sometime before 1994 into Hufflepuff. She was one of the Beaters for the Hufflepuff Quidditch team at the time that it was captained by Cedric Diggory.
