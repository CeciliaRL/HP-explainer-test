Tut was a wizarding greengrocer who owned the Tut's Nuts produce shop in Diagon Alley.


== Etymology ==
"Tut" is the nickname for the Egyptian pharaoh Tutankhamun.


== Behind the scenes ==
Illustrator Jim Kay used to work at the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew; at the time, they had a collection of seeds from Tutankhamun's tomb, which were known as "Tut's Nuts", which was how Kay came up with the idea for the shop.
