This wizard was appointed Headmaster of Hogwarts at some unknown time. After his death, his portrait was hung in the Headmaster's office, in order to give advice to the current head. His portrait is often found down and left from the portrait of the Pink-faced Hogwarts Headmaster immediately above the cabinet containing the Pensieve.
