These nuns were witches who, after their deaths, returned as ghosts.


== Biography ==
On 31 October, 1992, they attended Sir Nicholas de Mimsy Porpington's five-hundredth Deathday party, where they stood around the dance floor.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is unknown if they resided at Hogwarts Castle or if they only came there to celebrate Sir Nicholas's Deathday.
