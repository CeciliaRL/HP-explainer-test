This individual was a wizard or witch attended a Quidditch match in Norfolk in 1357, most likely as a spectator. For reasons unknown, they killed the match's referee, Cyprian Youdle, with a curse and fled, thus becoming the only known person in history to kill a Quidditch referee during the course of a match. The individual was never caught for their crime or identified.
