This individual was an American wizard who worked as an Executioner for Magical Congress in 1926.


== Biography ==
Along with Bernadette and another witch, he assisted in the execution of Newton Scamander and Porpentina Goldstein after being sentenced to Death by Gellert Grindelwald (disguised as Percival Graves) for violating the International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy. The execution was stopped when Newt and Tina were rescued by a Swooping Evil.
During the fight, Newt knocked him out by whacking him in the face with his (Newt's) handcuffs.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Executioner was portrayed by Cristian Solimeno in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
He was the only of the three Executioners not to be mentioned in the original screenplay.
