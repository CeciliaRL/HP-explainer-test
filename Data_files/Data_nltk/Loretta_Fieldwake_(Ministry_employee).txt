Loretta Fieldwake was a Ministry of Magic employee, who worked as Special Advisor for Elf Legislation for the Minister for Magic's Support Staff.


== Etymology ==
Loretta Fieldwake is named after Lauren Wakefield, one of the graphic artists that produced many of the paper props seen in the Harry Potter films.
