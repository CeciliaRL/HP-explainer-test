Mr Whiskers was the name given to a male Manticore somehow raised from infancy by Theresa Stadt, a British witch.
When the Ministry of Magic was notified of this Manticore, they sent a Magizoologist member of the Statute of Secrecy Task Force to retrieve it, which proved a "remarkably ghastly affair".
