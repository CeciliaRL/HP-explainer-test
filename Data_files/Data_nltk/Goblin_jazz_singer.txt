This singer was an American female goblin who sung jazz at the wizarding speakeasy The Blind Pig during the 1920s. She would use her wandless magic to create smoky images to accompany her singing.


== Biography ==
She started working at the The Blind Pig, sometime before 1926. She was performing with a band of goblin musicians on 6 December 1926 when Newton Scamander and his companions came to obtain information from Gnarlak regarding Newt's escaped beasts.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Wandless magic: Being a Goblin, she was shown to be proficient in wandless magic, being able to magically create smoky images to visually assist her musical performances, to a very precise and beautiful effect.


== Behind the scenes ==
The goblin was portrayed by Aretha Ayeh through motion reference and her singing voice was performed by Emmi in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
She is the first female goblin featured in any Harry Potter media.
