This girl was a small, dark-haired witch who began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1995, and was Sorted into Gryffindor.


== Biography ==
In September 1995, Fred and George Weasley had her test out their Fainting Fancies, along with several other Gryffindor first years. Hermione Granger, unable to stand around and watch Fred and George test their products on innocent first years, intervened. When the girl came to after passing out from the sweet she was given, George asked her if she felt okay, to which she replied, "I think so."
