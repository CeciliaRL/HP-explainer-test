This individual (fl. 1998) was a Dark Wizard and Death Eater who fought during the Second Wizarding War.


== Biography ==
At some point in his life, he learned about the Dark Arts and joined the Death Eaters, as a loyal follower and servant of Lord Voldemort. On the 2 May 1998, he was called upon by his master to fight in Battle of Hogwarts, which he did alongside his fellow Death Eaters. He fought in the onslaught at the Viaduct against the suits of armour. He later witnessed the believed death of Harry Potter in the Forbidden Forest at the hands of Lord Voldemort. Eventually, after Harry Potter revealed he was alive, he would have either abandoned the battle and his master, or continued to fight. He was either killed in battle, defeated, arrested and sent to Azkaban for his crimes as a Death Eater, or became an outlaw.
