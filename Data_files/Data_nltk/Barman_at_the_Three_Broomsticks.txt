This barman was employed at the Three Broomsticks Inn by Madam Rosmerta during the 1990s. In 1996, he gave three glasses of Butterbeer to Harry Potter, Hermione Granger and Ronald Weasley.


== Behind the scenes ==
He, along with the Waiter at the Three Broomsticks, appears instead of Madam Rosmerta in Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film).
