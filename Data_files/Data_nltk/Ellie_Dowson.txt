Ellie Dowson was a witch reported missing along with her relative, Rob, on the Potterwatch radio programme in 1997. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a Death Eater attack.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Mike Dowson, a sound mixer who worked on the final three Harry Potter films.
