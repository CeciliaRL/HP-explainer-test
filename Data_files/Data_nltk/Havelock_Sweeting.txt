== Magical abilities and skills ==
Care of Magical Creatures: Sweeting was highly talented with communicating with and understanding Unicorn, powerful magical beasts for which only certain individuals can tame, establishing numerous reservations for these creatures throughout Great Britain. His gift with Unicorns was also very impressive and distinctive for a wizard, as Unicorns usually prefer the touch of a witch.
