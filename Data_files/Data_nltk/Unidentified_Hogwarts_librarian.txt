This librarian was a wizard or witch who worked at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the alternate reality created by Albus Potter and Scorpius Malfoy on their journey to save Cedric Diggory in 2020. They may or may not have still worked in the Hogwarts Library in the primary reality.
