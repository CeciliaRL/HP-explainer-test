This individual was an American No-Maj who worked as an announcer for the election of Henry Shaw Junior in 1926.


== Biography ==
This individual was the announcer for the election of Henry Shaw Junior at his Fundraising Dinner in December 1926. Not long after having announced Shaw during his speech, Shaw was attacked and murdered by the rogue Obscurial form of Credence Barebone, which he would have witnessed, much to the horror and shock of everyone present.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Announcer was portrayed by Todd Boyce in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
