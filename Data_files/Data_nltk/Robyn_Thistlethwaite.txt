Robyn Thistlethwaite was a witch who started attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 2008 and was sorted into Gryffindor.


== Behind the scenes ==
Robyn was a Hufflepuff in the first beta release of Harry Potter: Magic Awakened, but has been changed to Gryffindor in subsequent releases.
