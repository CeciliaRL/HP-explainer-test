Eduardus Limus, also known by the nickname Pretty Face, was a wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In 1926, the International Wizarding Police issued a wanted poster for Limus, stating that he was wanted for "counterfeiting wands" and for committing "No-Maj murder" in Asia and Europe. It described him as a "powerful wizard" who was "wanded and dangerous." A reward of 5000 Dragots was offered for information leading to his capture.
