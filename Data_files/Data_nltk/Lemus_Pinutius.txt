Lemus Pinutius, also known by the nickname The Vampire, was a wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In or around 1926, the International Wizarding Police issued a wanted poster for Pinutius, which stated that he was wanted for grand larceny and No-Maj smuggling in Europe, Africa, and Asia. It described him as a "powerful wizard" who was "wanded and extremely dangerous." It offered a reward of 5000 Dragots for information leading to his capture.
