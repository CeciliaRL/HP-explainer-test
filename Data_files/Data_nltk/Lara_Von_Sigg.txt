Lara Von Sigg was an American witch and reporter for the The Wizard's Voice in 1926. Reporting from Houston, she wrote an article revealing the secret wand core used by New Orleans wandmaker Violetta Beauvais to be rougarou hair, which was published in the 6 December 1926 issue of the newspaper.
