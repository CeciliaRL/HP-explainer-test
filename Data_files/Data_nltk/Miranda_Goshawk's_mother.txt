Mrs Goshawk (fl. 1915-1921) was the wife of Mr Goshawk, and the mother of the nine Goshawk sisters, including Diadema, Romilda, Tangwystl and Miranda Goshawk.
This individual had several siblings-in-law and, through their marriages, many nieces and nephews.
