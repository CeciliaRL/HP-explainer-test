This individual was a witch who worked at Eeylops Owl Emporium during the early 1990s. She could be found on the shop's ground floor, helping customers.


== Behind the scenes ==
It is unknown if this individual owned the shop or simply worked there. If the former is correct, her name may be Eeylops.
