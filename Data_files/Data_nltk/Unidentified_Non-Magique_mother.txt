This woman was a French Non-Magique resident of Paris in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
Together with her husband and their child, she lived with them in a apartment on Boulevard Haussmann but was murdered by Carrow under the orders of Grindelwald himself. 
They later used the family's home as a temporary headquarters, from where he conducted a search for Credence Barebone.


== Behind the scenes ==
The mother was portrayed by Kayt Webster-Brown in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.
