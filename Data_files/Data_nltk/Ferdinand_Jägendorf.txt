Ferdinand Jägendorf was a wizard from Liechtenstein, who, in the 2010s, worked as coach and manager of the Liechtenstein National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
During the celebrations following the quarter-final match of the 2014 Quidditch World Cup, in which the USA defeated Liechtenstein on 8 June, 2014, Hans was reported to have been kidnapped by celebrating American fans. Jägendorf issued the following statement: "Das finden wir nicht lustig" ("we don't find that funny").
