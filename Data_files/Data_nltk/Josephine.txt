Josephine was a baby Zouwu in the care of the London Zoo's Magizoology wing.


== Biography ==
In 2021, Josephine escaped the Magizoology wing and ran outside the Zoo into the Marylebone district of London, where she was fully visible to the Muggle population. After a chase, and one of the largest mass Memory Charms in history, she was safely returned to the zoo.
