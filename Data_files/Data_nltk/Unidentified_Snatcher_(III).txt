This individual was a Snatcher, in 1998. He was among the group of Snatchers, led by Fenrir Greyback, who captured Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger and brought them to Malfoy Manor. He later participated in the Battle of Hogwarts on 2 May 1998.
