Catriona McCormack was a British witch who was a Chaser for the Pride of Portree in the 1960s.


== Biography ==
She captained the team to two League Cups. She played for the Scottish National Quidditch team thirty-six times.
Catriona had two children, Meaghan McCormack (current Keeper for the Pride of Portree), and Kirley Duke (lead guitarist of the The Weird Sisters).
Catriona McCormack was on the cover of Quidditch Times on 29 February 1964, the magazine's 3000th issue, having been interviewed about her League wins and on "how to wear purple with flair".
