Matildus Heizenbert, also known by the nickname Billie, was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Heizenbert, stating that she had escaped from the New York Witches' Penitentiary and was wanted for "atrocities against the wizarding community." It described her as a "vicious witch" who was "bearing [a] wand  and extremely dangerous." A reward of 4850 Dragots was offered for information leading to her capture.
