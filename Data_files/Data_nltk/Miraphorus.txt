Miraphorus was a witch after whom the Miraphorus Magic Set, a Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes product, was named. Every set included a replica pair of the distinctive red gloves she wore.


== Behind the scenes ==
The name of this character is presumably a tribute to Miraphora Mina, a graphic artist who has worked on every Harry Potter film, and who helped to design all the Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes props along with Eduardo Lima, Nicholas Saunders, and Lauren Wakefield.
