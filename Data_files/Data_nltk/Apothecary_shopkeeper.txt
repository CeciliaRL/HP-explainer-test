This individual (fl. 1991) was a wizard who ran the Apothecary in Diagon Alley, in the early 1990s.


== Biography ==
On 31 July, 1991, Rubeus Hagrid took Harry Potter to Diagon Alley for the first time to purchase school supplies for his first year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Harry looked around the Apothecary while Hagrid dealt with the man behind the counter.
