This "magic goblin" worked at Gringotts Wizarding Bank during the wizard management in 1997-1998, after the Ministry of Magic fell to the Death Eaters.
He was present at the bank when Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, Ronald Weasley and Griphook hosted a break-in in order to retrieve one of Lord Voldemort's Horcruxes, Helga Hufflepuff's Cup, from the Lestrange Vault.


== Behind the scenes ==
This goblin was played by Harrison Davis in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2. Despite not being credited, the character is identified as "Magic Goblin" in a Behind the Scenes feature. in DVD2.
It's unknown why this character is called Magic goblin, as all goblins have magical abilities. Presumably, he was particularly proficient at magic compared to the average Gringotts goblin, and his job in Gringotts may have revolved around this skill.
