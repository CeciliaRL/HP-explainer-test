This crow sat on the top of a wooden marker, holding Buckbeak the Hippogriff. The crow appeared to be a kind of guard, biting Harry Potter when he tried to free Buckbeak. The crow then flew away when the Executioner used his axe to cut a pumpkin from Rubeus Hagrid's patch in two.


== Behind the scenes ==
This crow was portrayed by Cheyenne.
