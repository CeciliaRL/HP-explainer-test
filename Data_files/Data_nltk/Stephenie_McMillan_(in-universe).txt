Stephenie McMillan was a witch who was an expert in interior design. She wrote the interior design guide A Companion to Wizardly Decor.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is a tribute to Stephenie McMillan, the set decorator for all eight Harry Potter films, who passed away in 2013. The portrait featured on the cover of the prop book is based on her likeness.
