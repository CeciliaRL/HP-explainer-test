Maud Ashborn was a witch and a world-renowned Healer.


== Biography ==
Maud Ashborn was murdered in 2002. Her killer was never caught.
In 2021, during the Calamity, Harry Potter saw a recurring nightmare memory, in which an unidentified wizard pleaded Maud to cure his daughter of her blood curse. Maud, however, said that the magic simply did not exist.
