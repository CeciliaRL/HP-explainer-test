Ngapo Ponika (born c. 1993) was a wizard from New Zealand who was the Seeker for the 2014 New Zealand National Quidditch team. During the first round match of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, against Bulgaria, Ponika was beaten to the Snitch by Viktor Krum.
