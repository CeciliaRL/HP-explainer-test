This boy was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted in Ravenclaw House. During the 1991 Christmas holidays at Hogwarts, he appeared to have left the school in order to go home for the holiday.


== Behind the scenes ==
He is played by Derek Hough in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, but is uncredited.
