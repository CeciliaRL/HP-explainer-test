This individual was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and a substitute Seeker on their Quidditch team in 1991 to 1992. He was filling in when the actual Seeker was unable to play.


== Behind the scenes ==
In the PC versions this Seeker substitute doesn't appear, having instead been replaced by another student.
