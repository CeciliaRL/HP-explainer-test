Abigail Nicola was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. She once signed up for Quidditch training at 7pm on Thursday on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the bulletin board in the Gryffindor Common Room.


== Etymology ==
The name "Abigail" comes from the Hebrew name אֲבִיגָיִל ('Avigayil) meaning "my father is joy".
