This goblin worked at Gringotts Wizarding Bank during the wizard management in 1997-1998, after the Ministry fell to the Death Eaters.


== Biography ==


=== 1998 ===
He was present at the bank when Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, Ron Weasley, and Griphook hosted a break-in in order to retrieve one of Lord Voldemort's Horcruxes, Helga Hufflepuff's Cup, from the Lestrange Vault.


=== Death ===
When the Gringotts dragon started to escape, the goblin became shocked and most likely got killed by it when he appeared not to be running from his seat, but his fate is unknown.
