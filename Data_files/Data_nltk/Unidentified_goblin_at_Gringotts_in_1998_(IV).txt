This goblin worked at Gringotts Wizarding Bank from at least 1991-1998.


== Biography ==


=== 1991 ===
He was present at the bank when Harry Potter went to withdraw his money in 1991.


=== 1998 ===
In 1998, he worked at the bank when Harry, Hermione Granger, Ron Weasley, and Griphook hosted a break-in in order to retrieve one of Lord Voldemort's Horcruxes, Helga Hufflepuff's cup, from the Lestrange Vault.


=== Later life ===
It is unknown what happened to him after the break-in but he may have been killed.


== Physical description ==
He had brown bunches of hair and wore a brown suit; he also carried a book with him.


== Behind the scenes ==
This goblin was portrayed by an unknown actor in the film adaptations of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
