This individual was a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry who was a member of Ravenclaw house.


== Biography ==
He was sorted into Ravenclaw, possibly in 1991 or 1992. In 1998, he became a member of Dumbledore's Army and fought during the Battle of Hogwarts on the 2 May, against Lord Voldemort's Death Eaters. It is unknown if he survived the battle or not.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (First appearance)
LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7 (Appears as a generic Ravenclaw student)
