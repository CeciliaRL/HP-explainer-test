Ethel was a British witch who worked as the secretary to Minister for Magic Hermione Granger.


== Biography ==
In August 2020, Hermione remarked to Harry Potter, Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement, that her husband, Ron, said she saw more of her secretary at the Ministry than him. In October of the same year, Hermione told Ethel to cancel her appointment with Gringotts Wizarding Bank goblin representatives upon learning of Albus Potter and Scorpius Malfoy's disappearance.
