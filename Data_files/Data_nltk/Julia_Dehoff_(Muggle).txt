Julia Dehoff was a Muggle fashion designer. Some of her designs were featured in the Costume Materialization collection opening that was held on 28 September, 1997, alongside the work of fellow fashion designers Emma Vane and Mauricio Carneiro.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Julia Dehoff-Bourne, who worked as a draughtsman on several Harry Potter films.
