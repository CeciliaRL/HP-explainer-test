Jack Jodieman was a wizard whose name was carved into a wood panel on a wall of Professor Trelawney's Divination classroom.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Jody Jackman, an art department coordinator on every Harry Potter film, excepting Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone.


== Appearances ==
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film) (Name seen on a wood panel)
