This male Slytherin student was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Slytherin House.


== Biography ==
In early 1997, he stole a Noisy Hitter from a Gryffindor girl. When Harry went to go get it back from him, he taunted him and told him that Professor Severus Snape would give him detention for assaulting a Slytherin. The two duelled, and he lost, with Harry managing to get the Noisy Hitter back from him.
