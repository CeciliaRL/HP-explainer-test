Blythe Parkin was an English witch who played as Seeker on the English National Quidditch team in 1994. Whilst playing Quidditch, she flew on a Silver White Nimbus 2000 broomstick. She may be a relative to Walter Parkin and his children.
By 1996, she was included on a Chocolate Frog Card for her achievements in the sport.


== Etymology ==
The name "Blythe" comes from a surname which meant "cheerful" in Old English.
