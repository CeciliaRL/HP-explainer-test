Nitin Divekar was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Nitin is a male given name of Indian origin. It means "master of the right path". Divekar is an Indian surname, most often found in the state of Maharashtra.


== Behind the scenes ==
Based on his name, he is most likely of Indian descent.
