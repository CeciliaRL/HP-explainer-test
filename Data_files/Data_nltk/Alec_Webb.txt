Alec Webb was a British Muggle concert director. He directed a performance of Mozart's Sinfonia Concertanti that was held at St. Mary's Church Hall at 7:30 in the evening on Sunday 7 May in a year prior to 1997, which featured Emanuel Hurwitz on violin, Kay Hurwitz on viola, and the Dollis Chamber Ensemble. Hermione Granger had a poster for this concert stuck up on the bulletin board in her bedroom in the Granger family's house.
