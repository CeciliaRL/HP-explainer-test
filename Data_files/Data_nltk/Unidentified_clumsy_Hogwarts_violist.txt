This unidentified individual was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was also a member of the Hogwarts orchestra as a violist but was said to be clumsy. In 1994, he was part of the orchestra during the Yule Ball.


== Behind the scenes ==
This student was played by Jimmy Song in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.
