This wizard was one of the habitual clients of the Leaky Cauldron. In 1991 he commented with Dedalus Diggle on the return of Harry Potter as he was accompanied by Hagrid to access Diagon Alley.


== Relationships ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			The wizard talking to Dedalus at the Leaky Cauldron in 1991 		 	 
He appeared to have a friendly relationship with Dedalus Diggle and Rubeus Hagrid, and was likely also on cordial terms with Tom, the Leaky Cauldron barman.
