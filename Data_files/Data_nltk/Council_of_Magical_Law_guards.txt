These individuals were guards in the Ministry of Magic Council of Magical Law.


== Biography ==
In 1981, they brought in Igor Karkaroff for a hearing about names. Later, they also brought in Bartemius Crouch Jnr and sent him to Azkaban.


== Behind the scenes ==
While not confirmed, given their similar duties, these individuals might be Watchwizards.
These wizards do not appear in Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.
