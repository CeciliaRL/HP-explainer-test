Professor Brian Gagwilde was a wizard and a Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Professor Gagwilde also wrote Jiggery Pokery and Hocus Pocus.
His portrait, in which he was depicted as playing a game of chess with Walter Aragon, was placed in the Headmaster's office, to give advice to the current Head.
