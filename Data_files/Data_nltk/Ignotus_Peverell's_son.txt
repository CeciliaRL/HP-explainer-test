Mr Peverell (fl. 1291) was a pure-blood wizard who lived during the 13th century, the son of Ignotus Peverell. He had a daughter, Iolanthe Peverell, who inherited the Cloak of Invisibility, one of the three Deathly Hallows, and married Hardwin Potter. He was also an ancestor of the members of the Potter family.


== Behind the scenes ==
In LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7, he appears as a generic Muggle orphan.
