Mrs Bones was the mother of Amelia Bones and Edgar Bones. She was killed, along with her husband, by Voldemort during the First Wizarding War.


== Behind the scenes ==
Mrs Bones presumably had a third child, as her granddaughter, Susan Bones, was the niece of both her daughter Amelia and her son Edgar.
