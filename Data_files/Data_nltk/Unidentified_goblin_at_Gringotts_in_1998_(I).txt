This goblin worked at Gringotts Wizarding Bank in 1998 after the Ministry of Magic's fall to the Death Eaters.


== Biography ==


=== 1998 ===
He is present on 1 May, 1998 when Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, Ron Weasley and Griphook planned a Break-in in order to get one of Lord Voldemort's Horcruxes, Helga Hufflepuff's Cup, from the Lestrange Vault.


=== Later life ===
It is unknown what happened to the goblin after the break-in but he may have been killed.
