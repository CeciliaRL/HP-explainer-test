Vogel was an individual of unknown species and origin. 


== Etymology ==
"Vogel" is a surname in German and Dutch-speaking countries, and it also means "bird".


== Behind the scenes ==
There are rumours that Oliver Masucci's role in the Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore is Vogel. Masucci is German and Vogel is a German name. There is also the fact that Vogel has a bodyguard listed as an extra played by Paul Parker. It would also be reasonable to assume that such a high ranking politician as the Head of the International Confederation of Wizards would have a bodyguard.
