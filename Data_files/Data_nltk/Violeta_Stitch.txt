Violeta Stitch was a witch and the author of Extreme Incantations, a Charms spellbook.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is mentioned only in the script of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban. The cover of Extreme Incantations — and thus its author's name — is not shown in the film.
