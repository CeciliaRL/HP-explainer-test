This individual was a wizard and an associate of Mundungus Fletcher. He was seen dealing with Mundungus in Diagon Alley in 1997.


== Behind the scenes ==
Mundungus's associate was portrayed by Martin Ballantyne in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1.
