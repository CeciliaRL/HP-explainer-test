Sara Farnham was an English Muggle and neighbour of Bilius Finbok, a wizard and employee of the Department of Intoxicating Substances at the British Ministry of Magic.


== Biography ==
During an investigation into Finbok's disappearance, Farnham aided investigator Mathilda Grimblehawk and her partner, providing an eyewitness account that she found Finbok injured and unconscious in his home. She likely aided in transporting Finbok to St David's, a Muggle hospital.


== Behind the scenes ==
Before she introduces herself to the player, Sara Farnham's lines are identified as those of "Muggle Neighbour".
