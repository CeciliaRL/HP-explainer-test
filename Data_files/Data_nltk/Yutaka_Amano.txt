Yutaka Amano was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.


== Etymology ==
Yutaka is a common male Japanese name. The most common way of writing the name with kanji characters is è±Š, in that case, Yutaka means "rich". Amano is a Japanese name, which means "heavenly plain".


== Behind the scenes ==
Given his name, it is likely that Amano is of Japanese descent.
