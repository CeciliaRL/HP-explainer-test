Jackie Jernigan was a Welsh witch and a Chaser for the 2014 Welsh National Quidditch team. On the first match of the quarter-finals of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, Brazil versus Wales, on 4 June, 2014, Jernigan scored one of the tournament's most stunning goals from a distance of fifty yards from the goalposts.
