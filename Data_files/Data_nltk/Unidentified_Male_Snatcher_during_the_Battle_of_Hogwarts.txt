This wizard was a Snatcher who fought during the Battle of Hogwarts. He took part in the skirmish at the Wooden Bridge, chasing after Neville Longbottom. He died when the bridge collapsed.


== Behind the scenes ==
He is portrayed by Mike Gentry in the second part of the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows.
