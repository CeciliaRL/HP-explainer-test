This male Red Cap lived in his hole in the Forbidden Forest.


== Biography ==


=== 1987–1988 school year ===
During the 1987–1988 school year, this Red Cap encountered Charles Weasley, Jacob's sibling, and another student in his hole using Sickleworth, a Niffler, to try and search for the jewelled head of Torvus' arrow.

 	 	 	 		 		 		 			The Red Cap after having been doused with Beautification Potion 		 	 
He drove them out of his hole, but they came back later with some Beautification Potion and used it on him. He was alarmed by his slightly more handsome look and climbed away, allowing Sickleworth to find the arrowhead.
At the end of the school year, due to an Acromantula in his hole, the Red Cap was afraid of going back in there. He was seen batting fairies in the Forest Grove. Jacob's sibling and Barnaby Lee distracted him using a piece of jewellery.


=== 1988–1989 school year ===
In the next school year, this Red Cap met Jacob's sibling and Ben Copper who came back to find Sickleworth. Ben struck it down with the Knockback Jinx.


=== 1989–1990 school year ===
During the 1989–1990 school year, this Red Cap found Jacob's sibling, Fred and George Weasley, and another student from the Weasley family in his hole. The Weasleys threw a Dungbomb at him.
