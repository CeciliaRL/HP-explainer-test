Minister Venusia Crickerly was Minister for Magic from 1903 to 1912, being elected at least twice.


== Biography ==
A former Auror, Minister Crickerly was considered both competent and likeable. She died in office, however, following a Mandrake-related freak gardening accident.


== Etymology ==
Venusia might be a reference to Venus, who was the Roman goddess of love, beauty, desire, fertility and prosperity.
