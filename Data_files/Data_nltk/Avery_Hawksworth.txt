Avery Hawksworth was an English wizard who played as a Chaser on the English National Quidditch team during the four-hundred and twenty-second Quidditch World Cup, in 1994. He was also the team's Captain.
By 1996, he was depicted on a Chocolate Frog Card for his achievements in Quidditch.
