Nelson Selgado was a No-Maj composer and lyricist in the early 20th century. 
Selgado composed the music and lyrics for Two to Tango by playwright Cecil Edwards. This musical was staged at The Kingly Court in New York in 1926.
He also orchestrated a production of the musical It's Just Heavenly that was staged at the Pegasi Theatre in New York in 1926.
