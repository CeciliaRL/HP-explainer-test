Herb Salient was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
In or around 1926, the Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for Salient, which stated he was wanted for "arson and No-Maj murder," and that they were offering a reward of 3,500 dragots for information leading to his capture.
