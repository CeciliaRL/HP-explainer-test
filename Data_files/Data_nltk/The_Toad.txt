The Toad was an animal ghost at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. It repeatedly left ectoplasm all over the school classrooms.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Toad was one of the original ghosts created by J. K. Rowling but did not survive final edits.
