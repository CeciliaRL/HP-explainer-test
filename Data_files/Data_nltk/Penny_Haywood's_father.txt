Mr Haywood was a Muggle man from the United Kingdom. He married a muggle-born witch and had two half-blood witch daughters, Penny and Beatrice.
His wife took him flying on a broomstick once, which scared him too much to try again.
He started playing Christmas music in October, which drove his wife mad.
