This unnamed phoenix was cared for by Credence Barebone in 1927.


== Biography ==
 	 	 	 		 		 		 			Credence nurturing the baby chick 		 	 
In September 1927, Credence and Nagini met at the bird market in Paris, and stole some birdseed. Later that day in the afternoon, while on top of a derelict rooftop, Credence fed the birdseed to the phoenix chick.

 	 	 	 		 		 		 			This phoenix in Nurmengard 		 	 
Sometime in late 1927, while Credence and Gellert Grindelwald were at Nurmengard Castle, discussing Credence's heritage, the phoenix chick stepped onto Gellert's palm, who eagerly threw it into air, erupting it into a burst of flame, birthing the phoenix.
