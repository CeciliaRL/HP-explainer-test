This individual was a wizard who worked for Bartemius Crouch Snr until Crouch's death in 1995.


== Biography ==
In 1981 after Lord Voldemort's downfall he attended the trial of Igor Karkaroff in which Karkaroff named Death Eaters.
In 1994 he attended the Quidditch World Cup between Ireland and Bulgaria. When the Dark Mark appeared in the sky he helped Bartemius Crouch Snr and some other wizards and witches to find the "man" in the ruins of the campsite owned by Roberts.


== Behind the scenes ==
He is portrayed by Campbell Graham in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.
