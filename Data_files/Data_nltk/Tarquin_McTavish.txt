Tarquin McTavish (b. 1955) was a wizard who was imprisoned in Azkaban for trapping his Muggle neighbour in a teakettle.


== Etymology ==
The name "Tarquin" is from the Roman name Tarquinius, which is of unknown meaning, possibly Etruscan in origin. This was the name of two early kings of Rome.


== Behind the scenes ==
If he attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, he may have been in the same year as Narcissa Malfoy, and five years above the Marauders.
