This wizard was a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1980s and was Sorted into Ravenclaw.


== Biography ==
He lived somewhere in Great Britain or Ireland and started attending Hogwarts on 1 September 1984.
He had Transfiguration class with Jacob's sibling and didn't like the other students talking during lectures.
By his fifth year, this student attended Professor McGonagall's lectures on transforming ferrets to feather dusters and switching ferrets with feather dusters.
