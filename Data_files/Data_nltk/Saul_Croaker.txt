Professor Saul Croaker (fl. 1994) was a British wizard and Unspeakable for the Department of Mysteries, having spent his entire career studying time-magic.

== Etymology ==
A "Croaker" is someone who habitually predicts evil; it has that sense because croaking is a noise made by crows and ravens, who are considered death omens, as they are carrion eaters.
