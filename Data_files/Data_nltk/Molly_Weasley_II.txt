Molly Weasley (b. 2000s) was the elder daughter of Percy and Audrey Weasley. She had a younger sister, Lucy. She was named after her paternal grandmother.

== Etymology ==
Molly is an English name of Irish origin, meaning "sea of bitterness," and is a diminutive form of Mary, with the Latin meaning "star of the sea".
