Elsie Valentine Schroeder was a witch who was reported missing on the Potterwatch wireless programme in 1997. Her disappearance was most likely linked to some sort of Death Eater attack.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Bjorn Ole Schroeder. Schroeder worked as a sound effects editor on Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, and as a dialogue editor on the following five Harry Potter films.
