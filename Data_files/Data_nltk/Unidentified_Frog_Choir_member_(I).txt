This individual was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Hufflepuff House. During his school years, he performed in the Frog Choir in the 1990s.


== Biography ==
In 1993, after the sorting, he appeared with other members singing Something Wicked This Way Comes before Dumbledore spoke.
In 1997, he was part of the group who sang In Noctem before Headmaster Albus Dumbledore was killed by Professor Severus Snape.
It is unknown if he survived the Second Wizarding War.
