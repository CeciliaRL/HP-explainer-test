Tao was an individual listed in Nicolas Flamel's phoenix-embossed book, although Tao was not present when Nicolas needed help.


== Behind the scenes ==
Based on their name, it is possible that they were Chinese or of Chinese descent, as Tao is both a first name and last name in China.
Tao will be a important character in Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore, as this individual was close friends with Nicolas Flamel and has their own supporter.
