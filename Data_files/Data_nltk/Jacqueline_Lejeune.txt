Jacqueline Lejeune was a French witch and Quidditch Commentator during the Quidditch World Cup matches.


== Physical appearance ==
Jacqueline had fair, smooth-looking skin and wide brown eyes. She wore a brown lace hat atop her brown hair, and had very white teeth.


== Etymology ==
"Lejeune" is most likely derived from French "le", meaning "the" and "jeune", meaning "young" - essentially, her name is "Jacqueline the Young".
