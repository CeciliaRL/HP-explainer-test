Imogen Stretton was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.


== Biography ==
She attended during the 1993–1994 school year and blocked access to an area of the school grounds along with Holden Ledbury in the 1996–1997 school year.


== Etymology ==
The name "Imogen" was the name of a princess in the play Cymbeline (1609) by Shakespeare. He based her on a legendary character named Innogen, but the name was printed incorrectly and never corrected. The name Innogen is probably derived from Gaelic inghean meaning "maiden".
