Elicia was a popular Britain Muggle television actress during the 1990s. She gave an interview in an issue of the British periodical Radio Times that was published sometime during the summer of 1997. The interview explored how she achieved such great success in her career.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was presumably named after Elicia Scales, who worked as an archivist on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
