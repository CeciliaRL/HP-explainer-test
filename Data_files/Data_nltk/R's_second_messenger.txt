This individual was a Dark Wizard or Witch who attacked Jacob's sibling and Ben Copper at the start of the 1988–1989 school year of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.


== Biography ==
At the start of the 1988–1989 school year, when Jacob's sibling and Ben Copper were talking in the Courtyard, they subdued both students in the Hogwarts courtyard with a Full Body-Bind Curse before telling Jacob's sibling that they warned them that "death" was coming to Hogwarts, and that they needed them alive, but that before the school term ended, one of their friends would have to die.


== Physical appearance ==
This individual's physical features were completely unidentifiable, due to being hidden by a red cloak.


== Magical abilities and skills ==
Dark Arts: This individual appeared to be an accomplished Dark Wizard, as they were capable of performing the Full Body-Bind Curse proficiently.


== Behind the scenes ==
Unlike with the first messenger, the identity of his individual is not revealed in the game. It is unknown if they will be indentified in future game information.
