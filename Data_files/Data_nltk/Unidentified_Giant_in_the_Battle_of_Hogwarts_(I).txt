This Giant was a member of the Giant colony who fought in the Battle of Hogwarts fighting for Lord Voldemort and his Death Eaters.


== Biography ==
Along with the rest of the Giant colony, he fought in the Battle of Hogwarts on 2 May, 1998. During the bloody and violent battle, he destroyed the suits of armour with one of the Quidditch goal hoops. His fate after the battle is unknown, although it is possible that he was killed during the battle. 


== Behind the scenes ==
This giant is played by Philip Wright in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows.
In LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7 he also appears with a Quidditch goal hoop, but instead he stands in front of an entrance and blocks it. The trio uses a phonogragh to make him dance then kill him with a Portable Swamp in a Weasley box.
