Madame Miraforum was a Ministry of Magic employee, who worked at Quill Control & Rune Translation for the Minister for Magic's Support Staff.


== Behind the scenes ==
Madame Miraforum is named after Miraphora Mina, one of the graphic artists that produced many of the paper props seen in the Harry Potter films.
