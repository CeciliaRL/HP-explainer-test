Quintia Qarase was a Fijian witch who played as a Beater for the 2014 Fijian National Quidditch team.


== Biography ==
During the second match of the 427th Quidditch World Cup, against Nigeria, Qarase and her fellow Beater Narinder Singh "lacked the ferocity" of the Nigerian Beaters, Aliko Okoye and Mercy Ojukwu. This resulted in the Bludgers doing much harm to the Fijian Chasers, which contributed to the team's bruising defeat.
