Julia Dehoff was a current affairs correspondent for an unidentified German-language wizarding newspaper in the 1920s.


== Biography ==
She wrote an article about the German Minister for Magic calling for a crisis meeting (presumably to address the threat of Gellert Grindelwald) that was published in a 1926 issue of the newspaper.


== Behind the scenes ==
This character was named after Julia Dehoff-Bourne, who worked as a draughtsman on several Harry Potter films.


== Appearances ==
Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film) (Mentioned in a newspaper)
