This unidentified man tried to pay Muggle Mr Roberts with Galleons, or as Roberts described it big gold coins, at the 1994 Quidditch World Cup. 


== Biography ==
This man tried to pay with "big gold coins" to get into the Quidditch World Cup Campsite, but was unsuccessful as Roberts was a Muggle and so did not recognise the currency.
