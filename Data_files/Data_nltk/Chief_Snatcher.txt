The Chief Snatcher was the second in command of the Snatchers during the Second Wizarding War. He was second to Fenrir Greyback who was the leader of the group.


== Biography ==
During the Battle of Hogwarts, the Chief Snatcher participated the Skirmish at the Covered Bridge along with Scabior and many others and possibly fell to his death when the bridge collapsed.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Chief Snatcher was portrayed by David Decio in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
Matthew Bancroft was a photo-double for the Chief Snatcher in the film adaptations of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows.
