The voice of the lifts at the Ministry of Magic was described as sweet. Its job is to inform the person about which level and location he or she are in and/or going to.


== Behind the scenes ==
The voice was provided by Daisy Haggard in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1.
