Smirnoff was an American No-Maj who worked as a banker in the early 20th century. He encountered Newt Scamander during his time in New York, and asking him if he needed assistance, to which Newt replied no, he was just "looking for something".


== Behind the scenes ==
Smirnoff is portrayed by Denis Khoroshko in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
