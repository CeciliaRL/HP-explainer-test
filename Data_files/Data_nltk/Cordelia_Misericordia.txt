Cordelia Misericordia (1298-1401) was a British hag who lived in the Middle Ages. She represented the hags at a fourteenth century summit of the Wizards' Council.


== Etymology ==
Misericordia is a Latin word meaning "mercy", "pity", "compassion" or "sympathy".
