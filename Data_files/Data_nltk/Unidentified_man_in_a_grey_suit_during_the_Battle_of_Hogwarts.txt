This individual was a wizard who fought at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1998 during the Battle of Hogwarts, and was present during Lord Voldemort's "victory" speech. He survived the battle.


== Physical appearance ==
This individual was a male human with light skin and a bald head. During the Battle of Hogwarts, he wore a grey suit.
