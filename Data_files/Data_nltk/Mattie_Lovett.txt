Mattie Lovett was a No-Maj author in the early 20th century.
She conceived a story idea in collaboration with Franklin Greenwood. Maxwell Lima wrote a book based on Lovett and Greenwood's idea. Lima's book was in turn adapted into the musical The New York Affair by playwright Maurice Willard.
