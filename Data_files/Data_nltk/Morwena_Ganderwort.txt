Morwena Ganderwort, also known as "The Snake", was an American wizarding criminal who operated in the 1920s.
The Magical Congress of the United States of America issued a wanted poster for her in or around 1926 for the crimes of exploitation of minor Elves and No-Maj murder and offered as reward of 3659 dragots.
