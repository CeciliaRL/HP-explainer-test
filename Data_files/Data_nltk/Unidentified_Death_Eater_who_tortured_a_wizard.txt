This unidentified Death Eater was a Dark Wizard, Death Eater and servant of Lord Voldemort, who fought for his cause in the Second Wizarding War.


== Biography ==
He was one of the Death Eaters who participated and fought in the Battle of Hogwarts with his fellow Death Eaters and Voldemort's army, on the 2 May 1998. During the battle of Hogwarts, he was seen torturing an unknown wizard, before Voldemort called of the battle and commanded all his Death Eaters to return to him in the Forbidden Forest, which he did accordingly.
