G. F. was a married witch from Bulwark, South Wales. Her testimonial was included in an advertisement for the TerrorTours travel agency that was printed in the 1 June 1999 issue of the Daily Prophet. In the testimonial, she claimed her grandfather "enjoyed himself right up until the last moment," suggesting he died during a TerrorTours holiday.
