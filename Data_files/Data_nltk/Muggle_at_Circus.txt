The Muggle at (the) Circus was an unknown muggle in 1927 that was close by Circus Arcanus.


== Behind the scenes ==
The Muggle at the Circus was portrayed by Irish actor Dave Simon in the upcoming sequel to Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
