Mervyn Fenwick was the Keeper for the Tutshill Tornados in the 1990s.
He fell ill with a bout of sleeping sickness right before a match against the Ballycastle Bats in 1999. This may have been caused by him receiving a blow from a Bludger directed at him by Finbar Quigley.
