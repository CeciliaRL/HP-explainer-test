Mrs Skower (born likely after 1492) was the witch who gave her name to Mrs Skower's All-Purpose Magical Mess Remover. It is unknown whether she was the product's inventor or simply its manufacturer.


== Etymology ==
"Skower" is an obsolete form of the verb scour, meaning "to clean, polish, or wash something by scrubbing it vigorously."


== Behind the scenes ==
The name could possibly be made up akin to "Mr Clean" in the Muggle world.
