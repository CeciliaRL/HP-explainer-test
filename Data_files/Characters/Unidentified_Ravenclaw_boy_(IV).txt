This '''boy''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was sorted in [[Ravenclaw|Ravenclaw House]]. During the [[1991]] [[Christmas holidays]] at Hogwarts, he appeared to have left the school in order to go home for the holiday.<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}} - Chapter 21 (''Christmas Gift'')</ref>
