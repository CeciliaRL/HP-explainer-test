'''Avery Hawksworth''' was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as a [[Chaser]] on the [[English National Quidditch team]] during the four-hundred and twenty-second [[Quidditch World Cup]], in [[1994]]. He was also the team's Captain.<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

By [[1996]], he was depicted on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] for his achievements in [[Quidditch]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>
