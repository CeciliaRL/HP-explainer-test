Ralphous''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] and reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="businessinsider">"[http://www.businessinsider.com/ginger-witch-harry-potter-fantastic-beasts-2016-11 Harry Potter designers have been hiding a character in the movies' fake newspapers for years]" from Business Insider UK {{See image|File:NYGhostGingerWitch.png}}</ref>
