}}'''Hemani Roshan''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
She gave [[Harry Potter]] some powdered [[moonstone]] in exchange for two [[Wizard's Chess|chess pieces]]. After then she frequently said, ''"Thanks for the chess pieces, Harry."'' She could be found hanging around on the [[Wooden Bridge]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Hermani is an Indian name meaning "made of gold", and is a 
name for Parvati. Roshan means "Light" in various sanskirt languages.

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console versions]] of {{POA}}.
*Based on her name, it can be presumed that she is of Indian descent.
