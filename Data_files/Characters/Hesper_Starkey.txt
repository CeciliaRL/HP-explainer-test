'''Hesper Starkey''' ([[1881]] – [[1973]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who studied how the [[moon]]'s phases affected [[potion]] fabrication.<ref>{{PS|G}}</ref><ref>{{COS|G}}</ref>

==History==
In [[1993–1994 school year|1993-1994]], a [[Portrait]] of her guarded a secret room at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]], to which the [[password]] (obtainable only from [[Fred Weasley|Fred]] and [[George Weasley]]) was ''Lunartickle''.<ref>{{POA|G}} - PC version</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Starkey was a skilled and renowned [[potioneer]], who was highly proficient in the art of potion-making, as she discovered how the phases of the [[moon]] affected the fabrication of potions.
*'''[[Astronomy (class)|Astronomy]]''': Starkey was also very knowledgeable in this branch of [[science]], as she studied and discovered how the phases of the [[moon]] affected potion-brewing, a significant achievement in the field of Astronomy as well.

==Behind the scenes==
*Judging from her year of birth, Hesper may have been in the same year as [[Albus Dumbledore]], if she attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].
*In some [[Harry Potter (video game series)|''Harry Potter'' video game series]], her surname is spelled '''Starky'''. {{PM}}, however, gives the name as Starkey.
