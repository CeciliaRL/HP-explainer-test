'''Yatin Bhagat''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Yatin is an Indian male given name, meaning "ascetic, devotee". Bhagat is a Punjabi and Gujarati surname, meaning "devotee". In Sikhism and Hinduism, a Bhagat is a holy person who leads humanity towards God.

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}}.
*Based on his name, he is most likely of Indian descent, probably originating from the north-west of [[India]].
