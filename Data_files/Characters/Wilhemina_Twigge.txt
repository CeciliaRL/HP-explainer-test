'''Wilhemina Twigge''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] from [[Bohemia]]<ref name="ThomasinaSmith">[http://www.thomasinasmithart.com/projects/film-and-media/painting-harry-potter-2001-2010 Tomasina Smith's official website: "Film and Media: "Painting Harry Potter 2001-2010"]</ref> and the namesake of the [[Wilhemina Twigge Award]].<ref name="OOTPG">{{OOTP|G}}</ref><ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

A portrait of Wilhemina Twigge, painted in [[1610]], hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]], in the [[Grand Staircase]]: she was depicted standing by a table with a [[crystal ball]], in a yellow dress and wide pleated Medici collar, holding her [[wand]].<ref name="ThomasinaSmith"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*The painting depicting Wilhemina Twigge featured in the [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]] was based on a [https://www.npg.org.uk/collections/search/portrait/mw08140/Princess-Elizabeth-Queen-of-Bohemia-and-Electress-Palatine c. 1610 painting of Princess Elizabeth, Queen of Bohemia and Electress Palatine] by [[Wikipedia:Robert Peake the Elder|Robert Peake the Elder]].
