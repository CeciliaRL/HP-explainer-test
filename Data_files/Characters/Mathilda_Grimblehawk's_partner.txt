[[Mathilda Grimblehawk]]'s '''partner''' was an Investigator of the [[Ministry of Magic]] in the [[Beast Division]] in the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]].

==Biography==
Sometime after attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]<ref>{{FBC|7}}</ref>, they joined the Ministry as a new recruit. On their first day, they helped investigate the attack on [[Gordon Horton]], [[Quidditch]] [[keeper]] for the [[Chudley Cannons]].<ref>{{FBC|1|1}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Magizoology]]''': This individual was knowledgeable of [[magic]]al [[creature]]s and worked as an employee in the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]] under the [[Beast Division]], and frequently dealt with various different [[beast]]s during their career fieldwork.
*'''[[Charm]]s''': They were proficient with charm-work, being able to successfully cast many useful charms including the [[Scouring Charm]], [[Mending Charm]], [[Unlocking Charm]], [[Memory Charm]], [[Revelio Charm]] and ''[[Tergeo]]''.
*'''[[Study of Ancient Runes]]''': They were able to decipher the runes in [[Winifred Whittle]]'s journal, showing they were skilled and knowledgeable in the field of ancient [[rune]]s.
*'''[[Healing magic]]''': They had considerable talent in the field of healing magic, given how they were able to successfully heal a [[Grindylow]].

==Possessions==
*'''[[Mathilda Grimblehawk's partner's wand|Wand]]''': It is presumed they purchased this [[wand]] at age eleven before attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. Their wand seemed to have a brass inlay and a very polished handle.
*'''[[Magnifying glass]]''': They seemed to own a magnifying glass, which they presumably used during their investigations.

==Behind the scenes==
