'''Emily Mather'''<ref>Closed captions on the DVD/Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] reported missing along with her relative, [[Gabriella Mather|Gabriella]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' radio programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of [[James Mather]], who worked as a supervising sound editor on every ''Harry Potter'' film from ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'' onward.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0558450/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cr274/ James Mather's profile on IMDb]</ref>
*She may be related to New England Mather family, of whom, Increase, who, along with his son was responsible for several writings and involvement in the Salem Witch Trials.<ref>https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Increase_Mather</ref> Increase's father, Richard, was born in Lancashire, England.<ref>https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Mather</ref>
