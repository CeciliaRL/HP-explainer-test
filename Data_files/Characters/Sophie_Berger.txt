'''Sophie Berger''' was the sports reporter for an unidentified [[French]]-language [[Unidentified French wizarding newspaper|wizarding newspaper]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="fb-paper">"[http://mashable.com/2016/12/02/fantastic-beasts-opening-harry-potter-easter-eggs All the 'Harry Potter' Easter eggs you missed in the 'Fantastic Beasts' opening]" from Mashable {{see image|File:FrenchNewspaper.jpg}}</ref>

She wrote an article about [[Beauxbatons Academy of Magic]] being at the "head" of the [[European Quidditch League]] that was published in a [[1926]] issue of the newspaper.<ref name="fb-paper" />
