'''Aki Sugiyama''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Hufflepuff]] student who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
She was seen wandering around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Aki is a [[Japanese]] female given name which can mean "autumn," "bright," or "sparkle" depending on the character used to write it.<ref>"[http://www.behindthename.com/php/search.php?nmd=n&terms=Aki Aki]" on Behind the Name</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console version]] of {{POA}}.
