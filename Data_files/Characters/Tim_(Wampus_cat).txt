'''Tim'''<ref name="HM710"/> was a male [[Wampus cat]] that terrorised [[Hogsmeade]] in the [[1990–1991 school year]].<ref name="HM79"/><ref name="HM710"/>

==Biography==
During the [[1990–1991 school year]], he attacked [[Dolores Umbridge]] and [[Ismelda Murk]]. He hypnotised them both afterwards to make them forget the attack, making them both only remember seeing his yellow eyes. This led Umbridge to suspect the [[beast]] that attacked her was in fact a [[werewolf]].<ref name="HM79">{{HM|7|9}}</ref>

[[File:Jacob's sibling clashing with Tim.jpg|250px|thumb|right|[[Jacob's sibling]] clashing with Tim]]
Later, at [[Hogsmeade station]], he was lured out by a [[niffler]] and had a [[Attack at Hogsmeade station|fight]] with [[Jacob's sibling]]. He knocked [[Jacob's sibling's second wand|their wand]] out of their hand, but they [[Stunning Spell|Stunned]] him [[Wandless magic|wandlessly]]. Afterwards, [[Liz Tuttle]] named the [[beast]] Tim. They discovered that Tim had been transported from [[North America]] to [[Great Britain]] using a [[Portkey]].<ref name="HM710">{{HM|7|10}}</ref> They later delivered Tim to [[Rubeus Hagrid]] and [[Silvanus Kettleburn|Professor Kettleburn]] to let them care for him.<ref name="HM711">{{HM|7|11}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Hypnosis]]''': Being a Wampus cat, Tim was capable of performing [[magic]]al hypnosis. He hypnotised both [[Dolores Umbridge]] and [[Ismelda Murk]] after attacking them so they would not remember the event, with them both only remembering his yellow eyes.<ref name="HM79"/>
*'''[[Legilimency]]''': Being a Wampus cat, Tim was naturally capable of performing Legilimency. He could use this to [[Mind-reading|read the minds]] of [[human]]s so he could hypnotise them.<ref name="HM79"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*In the German translation of {{HM}}, Tim is female and his name is changed to Tila.
