==Biography==
In [[1997]], he and some [[Unidentified Snatchers in Diagon Alley|fellow Snatchers]] patrolled [[Diagon Alley]] in their search for [[Muggle-born]]s. He and his fellow Snatchers died when the [[Wooden Bridge|Covered Bridge]] were blown up by [[Neville Longbottom]], [[Seamus Finnigan]] and other students.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref><ref name="LEGOY2">{{LEGO|Y2}}</ref>
