These unnamed '''[[Narragansett]] children''' were two [[Native Americans|Native American]] [[Wizardkind|witches]] who became two of the first students of [[Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[17th century]]. They were part of the Narragansett tribe.<ref name="Pottermore" /> 

==Biography==
Along with their [[Unidentified Narragansett mother|mother]] and two [[Unidentified Wampanoag children|children]] of the [[Wampanoag]] tribe, they were given [[wand]]s made by [[Isolt Sayre]] and [[James Steward]], and instructed in the use of them and in return they shared their own [[magic]]al knowledge.<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|ilvermorny}}</ref>
