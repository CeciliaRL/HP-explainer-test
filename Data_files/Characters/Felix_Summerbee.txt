'''Felix Summerbee''' ([[1440s|1447]]-[[1500s|1508]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who invented the [[Cheering Charm]].<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Spell creation]]''': Summerbee mastered the highly complicated and advanced [[magic]]al art of inventing new [[spell]]s, having successfully created the [[Cheering Charm]] in his lifetime, a highly impressive magical achievement which gained him recognition on a silver [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref name="COSG"/>
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Summerbee was highly talented with charm-work, given how he successfully invented the [[Cheering Charm]], a charm which makes the target feel elated and cheerful, hence the name.<ref name="COSG"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Felix Summerbee was the first [[Wizardkind|wizard]] featured on [[J.K.Rowling Official Site|J. K. Rowling's official website]]'s [[Wizard of the Month]] feature in May, 2004. He was featured again in May, 2005, and was the only character to have been featured twice.

==Etymology==
The name "Felix" comes from a Roman cognomen, meaning "lucky", "successful" in Latin. It was acquired as an agnomen, or nickname, by the 1st-century BC Roman general Sulla.
