}}This '''Housewife''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Muggle|No-Maj]] who lived in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==History==
She was one of the witnesses when some of Newt Scamander's creatures escaped from his briefcase that year.<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

When a [[NYPD Police Officer No. 743|policeman]] asked questions about the situation, the Housewife told him that she thought she heard a gas explosion, as she often had problems with them during her life as a housewife, telling the officer she would not take the children back in their apartment until the problem was fixed.<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
The Housewife was portrayed by [[Anne Wittman]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
