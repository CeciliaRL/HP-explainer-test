'''Iefan Rice''' was a [[Wales|Welsh]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a [[Beater]] for the [[Welsh National Quidditch team]] in the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]]<ref name="PM">[[2014 Quidditch World Cup]]</ref>. He shot such a ferocious [[Bludger]] at [[Brazilian National Quidditch team|Brazilian]] [[Keeper]] [[Raul Almeida]] that Almeida very nearly lost his head<ref name="PM"/>. Despite this, Rice was permitted to stay on and play, and had to drag manager [[Gwenog Jones]] off the pitch after she attacked Brazilian manager [[José Barboza]]<ref name="PM"/>.
