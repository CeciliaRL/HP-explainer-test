'''Bronwyn Trigg''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] reported missing along with her relative, [[Harry Trigg]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' radio programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Bronwyn is an alternate spelling of Bronwen, a Welsh name derived from the elements ''bron'', "breast", and ''gwen'', "white, fair, blessed".<ref>"[http://www.behindthename.com/name/bronwen Bronwen]" on Behind the Name</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Derek Trigg, a foley editor who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]], with the exception of ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire]]''.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0872740 Derek Trigg's profile on IMDb]</ref>
