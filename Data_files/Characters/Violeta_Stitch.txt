'''Violeta Stitch''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and the author of ''[[Extreme Incantations]]'', a [[Charms (class)|Charms]] spellbook.<ref>[http://www.imsdb.com/scripts/Harry-Potter-and-the-Prisoner-of-Azkaban.html ''Prisoner of Azkaban'' script] on IMSDb (archived [http://web.archive.org/web/20090216093016/http://imsdb.com/scripts/Harry-Potter-and-the-Prisoner-of-Azkaban.html here] via the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine)</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is mentioned only in the script of ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]''. The cover of ''Extreme Incantations'' — and thus its author's name — is not shown in the film.
