'''Nelson Selgado''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] composer and lyricist in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:Two to Tango.png}}</ref><ref name="neonsign">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:OrpheumNewYork.jpg}}</ref> 

Selgado composed the music and lyrics for ''[[Two to Tango]]'' by playwright [[Cecil Edwards]].<ref name="poster" /> This musical was staged at [[The Kingly Court]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="poster" /><ref name="neonsign"/>

He also orchestrated a production of the musical ''[[It's Just Heavenly]]'' that was staged at the [[Pegasi Theatre]] in New York in 1926.<ref name="poster2">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:It's Just Heavenly.png}}</ref>
