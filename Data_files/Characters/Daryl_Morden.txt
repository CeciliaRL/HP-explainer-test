'''Daryl Morden''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. 
He played informal [[Quidditch]] matches with other students during the [[1996-1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
The name "Daryl" is from an English surname which was derived from Norman French d'Airelle, originally denoting one who came from Airelle in France.
