'''Hector Lamont''' was a [[Scotland|Scottish]] [[wizardkind|wizard]], and the [[Seeker]] for the [[Scottish National Quidditch team|Scottish International Quidditch team]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[Quidditch World Cup 1990 - 2014]] (Archived)</ref>

He played in the final of the [[1990 Quidditch World Cup]] where he missed the Snitch by millimetres, leading to his team's defeat. After the game, Lamont famously lambasted his father ([[Lamont|"Stubby" Lamont]]) for not giving him longer fingers.<ref name="PM"/>
