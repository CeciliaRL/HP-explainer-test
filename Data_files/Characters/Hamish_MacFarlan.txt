'''Hamish MacFarlan''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was a [[Quidditch]] player for the [[Montrose Magpies]], [[Quidditch Captain|captain]]ing the side from [[1957]] to [[1968]]. After retiring from the game, MacFarlan became the head of the [[Department of Magical Games and Sports]].<ref name="QTAR">{{QTA|R}}</ref>
