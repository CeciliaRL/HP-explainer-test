'''Gwendoline Hedgeflower'''<ref name="age">[http://web.archive.org/web/20190307200721/https://www.starnow.co.uk/rachellinactor Rachel Lin's StarNow profile] {{c|Archived}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Hufflepuff]] house.

She participated and fought alongside most of her fellow Hogwarts students in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] against [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] and his army.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Hogwarts years===
[[File:Students celebrating OOTPF.jpg|thumb|left|Gwendoline celebrating the Weasley twins' departure with fellow students]]
During the [[1995–1996 school year]], she appeared in Harry's vision of [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]] at the [[King's Cross station]]. Later that year, she celebrated in the [[Viaduct Courtyard|Entrance Courtyard]] as the [[Weasley family|Weasley]] [[Fred and George Weasley|twins]] [[Fred and George Weasley's departure from Hogwarts|departed]] from [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]].<ref name="OOTPF">{{OOTP|F}}</ref>

During the [[1997–1998 school year]], Gwendoline continued to attend Hogwarts under [[Severus Snape]]'s regime,<ref name="DHF2"/> implying that she was [[half-blood]] or [[pure-blood]].<ref name="Blood"/>

[[File:Luna Lovegood, Draco Malfoy and Gwnedoline Hedgeflower DHF2.jpg|thumb|left|Gwendoline during the Battle of Hogwarts]]
On [[2 May]], [[1998]], she participated in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]]. After the [[Ousting of Severus Snape]], Gwendoline was seen running up the [[Grand Staircase]] alongside [[Harry Potter]]. She stood next to [[Luna Lovegood]] and [[Draco Malfoy]] in the Entrance Courtyard during Voldemort's speech.<ref name="DHF2"/> It is unknown if she survived the battle.

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Duelling]]''': Gwendoline participated in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]], fighting against [[Death Eaters]], meaning she had a level of confidence and skill in duelling.<ref name="DHF2"/>
