}}This '''individual''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Muggle|No-Maj]] woman who lived in [[New York]] in the [[1920s]].

==History==
She visited [[Kowalski Quality Baked Goods]] in [[1926]]. She became so impressed with the designs of the bakery's [[bread]] that she asked the owner [[Jacob Kowalski]] where he came up with his creativity, to which he replied that they just came to him.<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
The Female Customer was portrayed by [[Jane Perry]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
