This '''goblin''' worked at the British [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] in the [[1990s]] after [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]] fell in [[1981]] and later rose to power and took over the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] in [[1998]].

==Biography==
On [[31 July]], [[1991]], this [[goblin]] was present at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] when [[Harry Potter]] came to pick up his money in [[Vault 687]].<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}}</ref>

The goblin continued working in [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank|Gringotts]] when [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] took over the [[Wizarding world]] and was present when [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] and [[Hermione Granger]] went to Gringotts to [[Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)|steal]] [[Helga Hufflepuff's Cup]] in the [[Lestrange Vault]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The goblin was played by [[Mike Edmonds]] in the film adaptation of {{PS}} and [[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2|Part 2]] of the film adaptation of {{DH}}.
