'''E. Limus''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch or wizard]] who worked as a reporter and columnist for the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''. In [[1993]], Limus wrote an article on how [[Sirius Black]], then believed to be a fugitive mass-murderer, had been sighted near [[Dufftown]].<ref name="PA/f 11"/> By [[1995]], Limus had a column on page five of the paper, presumably dedicated to advice or opinion.<ref name="OOTPF20">{{OOTP|F}} - Chapter 20 (see [[:File:E. Limus.JPG|this image]])</ref> In mid-[[1997]], his column moved to page six.<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>

Limus also wrote the book ''[[Olde and Forgotten Bewitchments and Charmes]]''.<ref name="book">[[:File:Olde & Forgotten Bewitchments and Charmes.jpg|Book cover]] shown in ''Harry Potter Film Wizardry''</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Eduardo Lima]], a graphic artist who has worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] since ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]''.
