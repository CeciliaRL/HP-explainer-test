'''Miss Boyle''' was a [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] woman who was part of the upper echelons of [[New York]] society in the [[1920s]].<ref name="NewYorkStar">{{CB}} {{see image|File:The New York Star - Tuesday December 1926.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
She was one of the attendees of the [[Fundraising Dinner for the Re-election of Senator Henry Shaw|fundraising dinner]] for the re-election of [[Henry Shaw Junior|Senator Henry Shaw]] in December 1926. When the function was interrupted by [[Credence Barebone]]'s [[Obscurus]]'s attack on the Senator, Miss Boyle was at a loss as to what to do as chaos ensued. She later was quoted in a report on the incident published in ''[[The New York Star]]''.<ref name="NewYorkStar"/>
