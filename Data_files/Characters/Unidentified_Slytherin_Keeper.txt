This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He played as the [[Keeper]] for the [[Slytherin Quidditch team]] in [[1996]].<ref name="HBPF"/>

==Biography==
[[File:Quidditch gameplay.png|thumb|left|The Keeper trying to catch [[Ginevra Weasley|Ginny Weasley]]'s [[Quaffle]]]]
In the [[Slytherin]] versus [[Gryffindor Quidditch team|Gryffindor]] Hogwarts [[Inter-House Quidditch Cup|Quidditch Cup]] match in [[1996]], he collided with another Slytherin Quidditch member and hit the hoop, when [[Ginevra Weasley|Ginny Weasley]] threw the [[Quaffle]].<ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}}</ref>
