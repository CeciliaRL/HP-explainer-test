'''Stephenie McMillan''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] who was an expert in interior design. She wrote the interior design guide ''[[A Companion to Wizardly Decor]]''.<ref name="wwohp-jp">{{WWHP}} (Universal Studios Japan) {{see image|File:ACompanionToWizardlyDecor.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is a tribute to [[Stephenie McMillan (real-world)|Stephenie McMillan]], the set decorator for all eight [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]], who passed away in [[2013]]. The portrait featured on the cover of the prop book is based on her likeness.
