'''Angelina Appleby''' (b. [[1680s]]) was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1690s]] and [[1700s]]. She was once [[Quidditch Captain|Captain]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]] from [[1696]] to [[1700]].<ref>{{HPE}} {{See image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Plaque.jpg}}</ref>
