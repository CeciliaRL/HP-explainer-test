'''Harold Grace''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] composer and lyricist in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:TheNewYorkAffair.jpg}}</ref>

Grace scored the musical ''[[The New York Affair]]'' in collaboration with [[Lawrence Feldman]].<ref name="poster" /> He wrote the lyrics to the play's songs on his own.<ref name="poster" />
