'''Mrs Goldstein''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] who lived during the early [[20th century]].<ref name="fb44" /> She was the mother of [[Porpentina Goldstein|Porpentina]] and [[Queenie Goldstein]].<ref name="fb44" />

==Biography==
{{Quote|Come on Pumpkin.. it's time to go to bed.|[[Goldstein sisters' mother|Mrs Goldstein]] telling a young Porpentina to get ready for bed|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: The Original Screenplay}}
[[File:Goldstein family dancing.gif|250px|left|thumb|Mrs Goldstein, dancing with her daughter and husband]]
Sometime in her daughters' childhood, the [[Goldstein family]] danced together while dressed up.<ref name="FBS69">{{FB|S|69}}</ref><ref name="FBF">{{FB|F}}</ref>

She and [[Goldstein sisters' father|her husband]] died from [[Dragon Pox]] when their daughters were young.<ref name="fb44" /> This left the girls to "raise each other," which lead them to forge a life-long bond.<ref name="fb44" /><ref name="collider">"[http://collider.com/fantastic-beasts-and-where-to-find-them-things-to-know/?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=collidersocial ‘Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them’: 43 Things to Know about the New Wizarding World Story]" from Collider</ref><ref name="pottermorevideo">[https://twitter.com/pottermore/status/693594721097101312 Behind-the-scenes video] from the official {{PM}} account on Twitter</ref>

After being sentenced to [[death]] in [[1926]], Tina Goldstein had her happy [[memory|memories]] extracted from her head by [[Bernadette]], who then cast them into the [[Death potion|execution potion]] in the [[Death Cell]].<ref name="fb67">{{FB|S|67}}</ref> To keep Tina sedated, the potion replayed the memories for her, including one of her mother calling her "pumpkin" and asking if she was ready for bed.<ref name="fb67" />

==Behind the scenes==
*Mrs Goldstein was played by [[Fanny Carbonnel]] in flashback scenes in the film ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.<ref name="Carbonnel">[http://www.fantasticbeastsmovies.com/index.php/2015/12/08/fanny-carbonnel-cast-as-mrs-goldstein/ "Fanny Carbonnel Cast As Mrs Goldstein"] at [http://www.fantasticbeastsmovies.com/ FantasticBeastsMovies.com]</ref> At 27-years-old when the film was made, she was actually younger than actresses [[Katherine Waterston]] and [[Alison Sudol]], who played her daughters as adults.<ref name="Carbonnel"/>
