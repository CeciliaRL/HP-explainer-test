This '''girl''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Gryffindor|Gryffindor House]]. She and other students were watching the first game of [[Gryffindor]]'s [[Quidditch]] Team with [[Rubeus Hagrid]] in [[1991]] and celebrated with [[Dean Thomas|Dean]] and [[Ronald Weasley|Ron]] after the match was over.<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}}</ref>

==Biography==
This girl started her [[magic]]al education at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1991]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Gryffindor|Gryffindor House]]. This girl, like the other Gryffindor students, was very happy to go see the first game, and followed [[Rubeus Hagrid]] to the [[Quidditch]] field.<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}}</ref>
[[File:Girl -7 with Rony and Dean.jpg|250x250px|thumb|left|The girl with [[Ronald Weasley|Ron]] and [[Dean Thomas|Dean]] going to the [[Quidditch]] field]]

Also in [[1991]], she celebrated when the Gryffindor team was victorious in the first game. She and other [[Gryffindor]] students greeted [[Harry Potter|Harry]] in the middle of the [[Quidditch]] field.<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}}</ref>
