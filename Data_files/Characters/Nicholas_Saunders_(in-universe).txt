'''Nicholas Saunders''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] circus performer. He was one of the performers whose talents were featured in [[Le Cirque du Vaga Lume]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:LeCirqueduVagaLumePoster.jpg}}</ref>

Saunders also adapted the [[William Shakespeare]] play ''[[Twelfth Night]]'' for the [[Shakespeare in Shaftsbury]] event that was held as part of the [[Soho Play Summer Art Festival]] in [[London]] in [[1997]].<ref>''Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 - Behind the Magic'' {{see image|File:ShakespearePoster.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Nicholas Saunders]], a graphic artist who worked on ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince]]'', as well as {{DH|F1}}.
