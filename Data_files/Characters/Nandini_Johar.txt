She also admitted to being a "disaster" in [[Potions (class)|Potions]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} - NDS version</ref>

==Etymology==
Nandini is an Indian surname, and can either mean "daughter", "one who brings joy", "Ganga" or "Goddess Durga". Johar is a surnme of Indian origin.
