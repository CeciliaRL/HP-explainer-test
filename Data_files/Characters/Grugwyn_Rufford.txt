'''Grugwyn Rufford''' (fl. [[1998]]) was a [[Wales|Welsh]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was a member of the [[Welsh National Gobstones Team|National Welsh Gobstones Team]].<ref name="DP">{{DP}}: [[31 July]], [[1998]], Page 3</ref> 

==Biography==
In [[1998]], Rufford complained to the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' because they did not cover the [[Wales|Welsh]] victory over [[Hungarian National Gobstones Team|Hungary]]. On [[31 July]] that year, the editor replied to him via an editorial saying that people found that [[Gobstones]] were "''deeply boring''".<ref name="DP" />

==Etymology==
It is possible that this name 'Grugwyn' comes from Welsh ''grug gwyn'' "white heather".
