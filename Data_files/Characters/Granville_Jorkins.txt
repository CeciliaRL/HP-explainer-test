'''Granville Jorkins''' was the son of [[Albert Jorkins|Albert]] and [[Primrose Jorkins]]<ref name=":0">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[8 February]], [[1999]], Page 2</ref>. He had a brother, [[Grimwold Jorkins|Grimwold]], and a sister, [[Griselda Jorkins|Griselda]].

==Etymology==
The name "Granville" comes from an English surname which was derived from a Norman place name meaning "large town" in Old French.
