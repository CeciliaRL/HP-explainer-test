'''Lemus Pinutius''', also known by the nickname '''The Vampire''', was a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].

==Biography==
In or around [[1926]], the [[International Wizarding Police]] issued a wanted poster for Pinutius, which stated that he was wanted for grand larceny and [[Muggle|No-Maj]] smuggling in [[Europe]], [[Africa]], and [[Asia]]. It described him as a "powerful wizard" who was "[[wand]]ed and extremely dangerous." It offered a reward of 5000 [[Dragot]]s for information leading to his capture.<ref>''[[Inside the Magic: The Making of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]'' {{see image|File:Lemus Pinutius - wanted poster.png}}</ref>
