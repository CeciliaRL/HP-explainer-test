'''Minette Lestrange''' (née '''Rosier''') was a [[France|French]] [[wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She married [[Corvus Lestrange (II)|Corvus Lestrange]] and had three children with him: two daughters [[Manon Perrot|Manon]] and [[Josette Gage|Josette]], and son [[Corvus Lestrange (III)|Corvus]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:LestrangeFamilyTree HighRes.png}}</ref>
