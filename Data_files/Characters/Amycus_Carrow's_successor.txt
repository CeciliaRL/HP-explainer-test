This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] who taught [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] sometime after the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] and the removal of the [[Jinx on the post of Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher|jinx]] that was placed on the post by [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]]. As such, Hogwarts was able to keep this good teacher in the post going forward. From time to time, the professor allowed [[Harry Potter]] to give lectures on the subject, after Potter became the head of the [[Auror Office|Auror Department]].<ref name="AQ">[http://www.accio-quote.org/articles/2007/0726-today-vieira1.html www.accio-quote.org/articles/207/0726-today-vieira1.html]</ref>
