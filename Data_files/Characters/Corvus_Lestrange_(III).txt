'''Corvus Lestrange''' was a [[France|French]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. He was the son of [[Corvus Lestrange (II)|Corvus Lestrange]] and [[Minette Lestrange]]. He had two sisters, [[Manon Perrot|Manon]] and [[Josette Gage|Josette]]. He married his cousin [[Eglantine Lestrange]] and had a son with her, [[Corvus Lestrange (IV)|Corvus]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:LestrangeFamilyTree HighRes.png}}</ref>
