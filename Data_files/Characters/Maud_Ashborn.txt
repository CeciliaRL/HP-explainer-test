'''Maud Ashborn''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a world-renowned [[Healer]].<ref name="WUPPandAUI">{{WU}}, Adversaries Event: [[Peter Pettigrew]] and Ancient [[Ukrainian Ironbelly]]</ref>

==Biography==
Maud Ashborn was murdered in [[2002]]. Her killer was never caught.<ref name="WUPPandAUI"/>

In [[2021]], during the [[Calamity]], [[Harry Potter]] saw a recurring nightmare [[memory]], in which an unidentified wizard pleaded Maud to cure his daughter of her [[Blood malediction|blood curse]]. Maud, however, said that the [[magic]] simply did not exist.<ref name="WU Narcissa Malfoy">{{WU}}, Adversaries Event: [[Narcissa Malfoy]]</ref>
