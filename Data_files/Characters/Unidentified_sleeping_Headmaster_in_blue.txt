This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was appointed [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] before [[Armando Dippet|Professor Dippet]]. After his death, his [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head. His portrait is commonly located below that of Armando Dippet and next to the [[Sword of Gryffindor]].<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}}</ref><ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref><ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}}</ref><ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>
