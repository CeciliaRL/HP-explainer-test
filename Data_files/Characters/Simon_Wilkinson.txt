'''Simon Wilkinson''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He used a purple-and-red marbled notebook in his [[third year]].<ref>See [[:File:SimonWilkinsonNotebook.jpg|this image]].</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is a tribute to [[Simon Wilkinson (real-world)|Simon Wilkinson]], who worked as a propman on all eight [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0929484/ Simon Wilkinson]" on IMDb</ref>
