'''Mauricio Carneiro''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] fashion designer. Some of his creations were were featured in the [[Costume Materialization]] collection opening that was held on [[28 September]], [[1997]], along with other designs from fellow fashion designers [[Emma Vane (Muggle)|Emma Vane]] and [[Julia Dehoff]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:CostumeMaterialization.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after Mauricio Carneiro, who worked as a costume illustrator for every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]'' onward, and as a costumer for ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Order of the Phoenix]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Half-Blood Prince]]''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1769188/ Mauricio Carneiro]" on IMDb</ref>
