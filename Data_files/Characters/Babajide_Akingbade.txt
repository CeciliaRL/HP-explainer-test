'''Babajide Akingbade''' was an [[Africa]]n [[wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Uagadou]] graduate who succeeded [[Albus Dumbledore]] as the [[Supreme Mugwump]] of the [[International Confederation of Wizards]]<ref name="PM">{{WW|uagadou}}</ref> in [[1995]], when the latter was [[Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter|voted out]] by [[British Ministry of Magic|British Ministry]] wizards after he made a speech announcing [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s [[Return of Lord Voldemort|return]].<ref name="OOTP5">{{OOTP|B|5}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Babajide came from a Yoruba words "baba" meaning "father" and "dziade" meaning "raise from the dead". Babajide means "father has come to life again" or "father has returned".

{{W|Akingbade}} is a Yoruba surname meaning "strong/brave one who wears the crown".
