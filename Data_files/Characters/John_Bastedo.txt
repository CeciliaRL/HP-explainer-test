'''John Bastedo''' (also known as '''Wolf''') was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].<ref name="vogue">"[http://www.vogue.com/slideshow/eddie-redmayne-fantastic-beasts-where-to-find-them-annie-leibovitz#5 ''Fantastic Beasts'' Star Eddie Redmayne Goes in Search of the Seasonâ€™s Most Magical Fashions]" from ''Vogue'' {{see image|File:John Bastedo.png}}</ref>

In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Bastedo, stating that he was wanted for "[[house-elf|elf]] trafficking" and "[[Muggle|No-Maj]] murder in [[Europe]]."<ref name="vogue" /> A reward of 5,000 [[Dragot]]s was offered for information leading to his capture.<ref name="vogue" /> The wanted poster was hanging in [[The Blind Pig]] when [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] visited it in [[December]] 1926.<ref name="vogue" />
