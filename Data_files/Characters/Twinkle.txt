'''Twinkle''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] and the owner of [[Twinkle's Telescopes]], a shop in [[Diagon Alley]] that sold [[Astronomy (class)|Astronomical]] equipment such as [[telescope]]s, [[moon chart]]s and [[star chart]]s.<ref>{{PS|IE}} {{see image|File:Twinkle's Telescopes.png}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Illustrator [[Jim Kay]] revealed that Twinkle's Telescopes was named after a real shop in {{w|Mansfield}}, "Sally Twinkles".<ref name="JimKayComment">[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PDPcGdt40RU Jim Kay Illustrator - Create Expo 2016] {{c|from 26:05 to 26:12}}</ref>
*Twinkle as a surname is rather uncommon, with the highest concentration in [[Bangladesh]], then [[India]].<ref>[https://forebears.io/surnames/twinkle Twinkle Surname Origin, Meaning & Last Name History] at Forebears: Names & Genealogy Resources</ref>
