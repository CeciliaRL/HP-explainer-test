These '''nineteen individuals''' (fl. [[1997]]) were [[Wizardkind|wizards]] who worked at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] under its [[Fall of the Ministry of Magic|Death Eater rule]].

==Biography==
They adulated [[Minister for Magic]] [[Pius Thicknesse]] excessively. On the morning of [[2 September]], [[1997]], these individuals followed Minister Thicknesse around Level One of the Ministry.<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}} - Chapter 12 (''Secret Files'')</ref>
