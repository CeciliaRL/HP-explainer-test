'''Mrs Ali''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] woman who worked as an engineer. She disliked the fact that Muggle technology did not work at her [[wizardkind|witch]] daughter [[Badeea Ali|Badeea's]] [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|school]].<ref name="HM51d">{{HM|5|1|d}}</ref> She took her daughter bowling, but her daughter always ended up colouring.<ref name="HM643t">{{HM|6|43|t}}</ref> Sometime before [[1 September]] [[1989]], [[Badeea Ali|her daughter]] cast the [[Star Shower Spell]] on top of their house, which made her angry.{{fact}} She was also known for being able to cook a delicious [[fish stew]] that reminded Badeea of home.<ref name="TGHC">{{HM|5|THE GREAT HOGWARTS COOK-OFF|1}}</ref>
