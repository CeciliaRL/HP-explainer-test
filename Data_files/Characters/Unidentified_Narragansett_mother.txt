This '''individual''' was a [[Native Americans|Native American]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who became one of the first students of [[Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[17th century]]. She was part of the [[Narragansett]] tribe.<ref name="Pottermore" />

==Biography==
Along with her two daughters and two other [[Native Americans|Native American]] [[Unidentified Wampanoag children|boys]] of the [[Wampanoag]] tribe, they were given [[wand]]s made by [[Isolt Sayre]] and [[James Steward]] and instructed in the use of them and in return they shared their own magical knowledge.<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|ilvermorny}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*She is the first known adult student of a wizarding school, though she may only have learned how to use a wand and was already able to use magic before. It's unknown if she was Sorted into one of the Houses and stayed at Ilvermorny as long as younger students.
