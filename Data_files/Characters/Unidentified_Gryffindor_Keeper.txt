This '''individual''' was a witch or wizard who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the late [[1970s]] or early-mid-[[1980s]] and was Sorted into [[Gryffindor]]. By the [[1985–1986 school year]], he or she had been made [[Keeper]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]], and was known for misplacing the [[Broom Wax]].<ref>{{HM|2|Team Gryffindor}} {{c|Gryffindor players only}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This information would only be available if the player of {{HM}} made [[Jacob's sibling]] a [[Gryffindor]].
*It is possible that this individual is the [[unidentified Keeper]] of the player's Quidditch team seen in Quidditch quests, as Quidditch Season 1 is unlocked in Year 2 and this individual is only mentioned if Jacob's sibling is in Gryffindor.
