This '''individual''' was a [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] [[Customs]] officer who checked [[Newton Scamander]]'s [[Newton Scamander's suitcase|suitcase]] when he arrived in [[New York]] in [[1926]]. Seeing one of the latches was flicked open, Newt ensured the case was set to its 'Muggle-worthy' setting, which displayed his clothes and goods to the officer rather than the beasts he cared for, so as to keep him from discovering the [[wizarding world]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
