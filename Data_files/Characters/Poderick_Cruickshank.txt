'''Poderick Cruickshank''' (born c. [[1492]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] whose [[portrait]] hung at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="MHP" />

His portrait was inscribed "''Anno MDXXVII''", indicating that it was painted in [[1527]], and "''Ætatis Suæ XXXV''", indicating he was 35 at the time the portrait was made, as ''ættatis suæ'' is [[Latin]] for "''of his age''"''.''<ref name="MHP" /><ref>"[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Latin_phrases:_A List of Latin Phrases: A]" on Wikipedia</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Poderick Cruickshanks's portrait seems to have been based on a [https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:King_Henry_VII.jpg 1505 portrait] of [[Henry VII]] of England by an unknown artist.
