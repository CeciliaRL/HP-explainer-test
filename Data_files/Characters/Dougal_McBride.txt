'''Dougal McBride''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the [[Seeker]] for the [[Pride of Portree]].<ref name="wizardsm">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/m.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: M]" on The Harry Potter Lexicon</ref>

==Biography==
During a match with the [[Appleby Arrows]], he crashed into the opposing team's Seeker, [[Gregory Cotton]], while both were racing to catch the [[Golden Snitch]].<ref name="wizardsm" /><ref name="wizardsc">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/c.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: C]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon</ref><ref name="dp">"[http://www.hplex.info/about/sources/source_dp.html Daily Prophet newsletters]" on The Harry Potter Lexicon</ref> Cotton came out holding the Snitch, but an altercation with McBride ensued, during which [[jinx]]es were illegally exchanged, and McBride [[transfiguration|transfigured]] Cotton's head into a cabbage.<ref name="wizardsc" /><ref name="dp" /> McBride defended his actions to the referee, [[Josiah Plunkett]], by claiming that Cotton had hit him with a [[Jelly-Fingers Curse]] during the race for the Snitch, and thus deserved to be "cabbaged."<ref name="wizardsm" /><ref name="wizardsp">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/p.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: P]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Dougal" is the anglicised form of the Gaelic name Dubhghall, which meant "dark stranger" from dubh "dark" and gall "stranger".
