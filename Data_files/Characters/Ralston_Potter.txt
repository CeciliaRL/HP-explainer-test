'''Ralston Potter''' was a member of the [[Wizengamot]] from [[1612]] to [[1652]]. He was a staunch advocate of the [[International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy]], in contrast to his more militant peers, who wished to declare war against [[Non-magic people|Muggle]]s.<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|the-potter-family}}</ref>
