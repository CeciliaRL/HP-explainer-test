'''Sara Farnham''' was an [[England|English]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] and neighbour of [[Bilius Finbok]], a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and employee of the [[Department of Intoxicating Substances]] at the [[British Ministry of Magic]].<ref name=":0">{{FBC|5|1}}</ref>

==Biography==

During an investigation into Finbok's disappearance, Farnham aided investigator [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]] and her [[Mathilda Grimblehawk's partner|partner]], providing an eyewitness account that she found Finbok injured and unconscious in his home<ref name=":0" />. She likely aided in transporting Finbok to [[St David's Hospital|St David's]], a Muggle hospital.

==Behind the scenes==
*Before she introduces herself to the player, Sara Farnham's lines are identified as those of "Muggle Neighbour".
