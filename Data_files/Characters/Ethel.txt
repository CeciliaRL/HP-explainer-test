'''Ethel''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|witch]] who worked as the [[secretary]] to [[Minister for Magic]] [[Hermione Granger]].<ref name="Act1Scene5"/>

==Biography==
In [[August]] [[2020]], [[Hermione Granger|Hermione]] remarked to [[Harry Potter]], Head of the [[Department of Magical Law Enforcement]], that her husband, [[Ronald Weasley|Ron]], said she saw more of her secretary at the Ministry than him.<ref name="Act1Scene5">{{CC|B|1|5}}</ref> In [[October]] of the same year, Hermione told Ethel to cancel her appointment with [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] [[goblin]] representatives upon learning of [[Albus Potter]] and [[Scorpius Malfoy]]'s disappearance.<ref name="Act3Scene17">{{CC|B|3|17}}</ref>
