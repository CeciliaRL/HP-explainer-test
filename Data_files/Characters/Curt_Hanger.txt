'''Curt Hanger''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

Curt added his name to a sign-up sheet for the [[Hogwarts Gobstone Club]] that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in [[Gryffindor Tower]].<ref>{{See image|File:GobstonesClubSignUpSheet.jpg}} - a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref> He also registered for a trip to [[Hogsmeade]] on another sign-up sheet posted on the Gryffindor bulletin board, which was scheduled for Saturday the 15th in an unknown month.<ref name="hogsmeade">{{See image|File:HogsmeadeTripSignUpSheet.jpg}} - a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character's name is a play on the word "coathanger."
