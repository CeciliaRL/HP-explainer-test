===Hogwarts wizard in the Middle Courtyard===
[[File:Unidentified professor PS 1.jpg|thumb]]
This '''individual''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] working at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] from, at least, [[1991]] to [[1993]].

In the [[1991–1992 school year]], this wizard attended the [[Start-of-Term Feast]] and watched the [[First year]]s being [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]]. He also helped some students in the [[Middle Courtyard]] with their homework some days later, one of them being [[Hermione Granger]]. He attended the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team|Gryffindor]] vs. [[Slytherin Quidditch team|Slytherin]] [[Quidditch]] match that year, and watched as [[Harry Potter]] nearly swallowed the [[Golden Snitch]].

In the [[1992–1993 school year]], the wizard attended the [[Gryffindor]] vs. [[Slytherin]] [[Quidditch]] match, as well as the [[End-of-Term Feast]].

===Wizard with white hair and beard===
[[File:Male-employee-XXIII-HBP.jpg|thumb]]
