This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1980s]].

==Biography==
During her time at Hogwarts, she was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into an unknown [[Hogwarts Houses|house]]. At some point by the [[1985–1986 school year]], she became a [[Beater]] of an unknown Quidditch team.<ref name="q1">{{HM|2|Q1|1}}</ref> She used to do ballet.<ref>{{HM|3|Q2|5}}</ref>

She stood on her head every night before a match as a way to inspire good fortune. At some point before the final match in the 1985–1986 school year, she also received a rock with a hole in it from her mother for good luck.<ref name="qc">{{HM|2|The Quidditch Cup}}</ref>

She continued playing on the team the following year. As her fellow Beater, [[Bean]], was suspended from the team, she played in the position alongside [[Jacob's sibling]], who had been a [[Chaser]] in the previous year.<ref>{{HM|3|Q2|1}}</ref>

At some point in the [[1989–1990 school year]], she talked to [[Andre Egwu]] on the [[Training Grounds]].<ref>{{HM|6|36}}</ref>

==Possessions==
*'''Adder stone''': Her mother gave her a stone with a hole in it for good luck.<ref name="qc">{{HM|2|The Quidditch Cup}}</ref>
