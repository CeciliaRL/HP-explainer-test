'''Cyrille Lestrange''' was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. Cyrille had a brother [[Corvus Lestrange (I)|Corvus]]. He married [[Darenne Lestrange|Darenne Moreau]], and had two sons with her, [[Cyrille Lestrange (II)|Cyrille]] and [[Fulcran Lestrange (I)|Fulcran]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
