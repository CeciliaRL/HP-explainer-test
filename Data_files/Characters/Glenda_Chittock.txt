'''Glenda Chittock''' (b. [[1964]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who was host of the [[Wizarding Wireless Network]] programme, ''[[Witching Hour]]''. Being host of this enormously famous radio programme, she is featured on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*If Glenda attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]], her [[first year]] would have been the [[Marauders]]' [[fifth year]].

==Etymology==
"Glenda" is a name created in the 20th century from the Welsh elements ''glân'' "pure, clean" and ''da'' "good".
