This '''barn owl''' was owned by [[Sirius Black]]. He used it to deliver a [[Sirius Black's letter to Harry Potter (1994) IV|letter]] to his godson [[Harry Potter]].<ref name="GOF18"/>

Harry found the owl in his dormitory after being informed by his best friend [[Ronald Weasley|Ron]] (whom both had temporarily fallen out at the time) that it was there.<ref name="GOF18">{{GOF|B|18}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
In {{GOF|FA}}, there is a [[Sirius Black's owl|different owl]] which delivered Sirius' letter to Harry, which also had a tendency to bite people. The owl in the book did not bite Harry.
