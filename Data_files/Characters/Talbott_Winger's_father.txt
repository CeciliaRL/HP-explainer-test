'''Talbott Winger's father''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who opposed [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] during the [[First Wizarding War]], though neither he nor [[Talbott Winger's mother|Talbott's mother]] joined any defence organisation.

==Biography==
He wrote for the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'', and had at one point, snuck in his son [[Talbott Winger]]'s poems to be published within.<ref name="HM3B">{{HM|3|Birds Of A Feather|3}} - ([https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iLVeSra3TJo&t=57m24s see this video])</ref>

He and Talbott's mother were killed by [[Death Eaters]], though their son managed to escape by transforming into his [[animagus]] eagle form.<ref name="HM3A">{{HM|3|Become an Animagus|3}}</ref>

In the [[1986–1987 school year]], [[Barnaby Lee]] made up the claim that Talbott's father was a [[vampire]].<ref name="HM3A1">{{HM|3|Become an Animagus|1}}</ref>
