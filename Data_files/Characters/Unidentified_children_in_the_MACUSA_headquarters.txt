==History==
[[File:Ilvermorny students in MACUSA.gif|220px|left|thumb|These children exploring MACUSA]]
In [[December]] [[1926]], these children visited the MACUSA headquarters and were shown around by a [[Unidentified guide|female guide]], who gave them yellow pamphlets.<ref name="FBF">{{FB|F}}</ref>
