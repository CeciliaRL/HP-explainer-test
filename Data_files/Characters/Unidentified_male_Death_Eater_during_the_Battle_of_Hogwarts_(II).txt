This '''[[Dark Wizard]]''' was a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] and one of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s servants during the [[Second Wizarding War]]. He fought during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] in [[1998]], although it is unknown if he was killed during the battle, abandoned the battle or was defeated, arrested and sentenced to imprisonment in [[Azkaban]] for his crimes as as a Death Eater.<ref>{{DH|F2}}</ref>
