This '''witch with a hairy chin''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]], who was the individual who banished the [[Bandon Banshee]].<ref name="COS16"/>

==Biography==
At some point before [[1992]], she banished the [[Bandon Banshee]]. However, shortly after her deed, [[Gilderoy Lockhart]] tracked her down, and tricked her into revealing in detail of how she defeated the [[banshee]]. After Lockhart got all the information he needed, he cast a [[Memory Charm]] on the witch to erase her [[memory]] of the meeting and of her actions, and published the book ''[[Break with a Banshee]]'' with all of the information fraudulently under his own credentials.<ref name="COS16">{{COS|B|16}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Defence Against the Dark Arts]]''': This witch was skilled with defensive [[magic]] in what she was able to defeat the [[Bandon Banshee]], a dangerous [[Dark Arts|dark]] [[creature]].<ref name="COS16"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*In the original edition of the text, she was referred to as having a "harelip," but this was deemed insensitive and changed in future editions.<ref name="lexicon">[http://www.hp-lexicon.org/about/books/cs/changes_cs.html Harry Potter Lexicon - ''Chamber of Secrets'' - Changes to the text]</ref>
