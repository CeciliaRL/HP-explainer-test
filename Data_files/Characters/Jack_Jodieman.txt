'''Jack Jodieman''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] whose name was carved into a wood panel on a wall of [[Sybill Trelawney|Professor Trelawney's]] [[Divination (class)|Divination]] [[Divination Classroom|classroom]].<ref>{{POA|F}} - Chapter 21 (''Professor Trelawney's Prediction'') {{see image|File:JackJodieman.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after Jody Jackman, an art department coordinator on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]], excepting ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone]]''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1227854/ Jody Jackman]" on IMDb</ref>
