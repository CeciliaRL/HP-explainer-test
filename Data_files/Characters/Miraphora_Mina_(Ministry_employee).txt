'''Miraphora Mina''' (b. 21st January to 18th February) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who worked in the [[Runes & Symbols]] department of the [[Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="HPLE" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Miraphora Mina]], a graphic artist who worked on every film in the [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' film series]] and [[Fantastic Beasts (film series)|''Fantastic Beasts'' film series]].
