This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] whose portrait hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] in the [[1990s]], near the first-floor landing of the [[Grand Staircase]], next to a [[Girl with Flowers|portrait of a girl with flowers]] and a portrait of [[Hengist Rawkes]]. He was depicted wearing red robes and a pointed hat, reading a book through a magnifying glass. He was polite, greeting the first year newcomers to the [[Hogwarts Castle|Castle]] in [[1991]].<ref name="PSf13">{{PS|F}} - Chapter 13 (''Nick and Other Residents'')</ref>
