'''B. C.''' was a [[wizardkind|witch or wizard]] who apparently worked as a mountain climber.

==Biography==
In their childhood, he or she attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], and was singled out for membership in the [[Slug Club]]. [[Horace Slughorn]] kept a picture of them on a snowy windy mountain top on his shelf.<ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}} - Chapter 2 (''Horace Slughorn'')</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*B. C. is someone's initials. It is currently unknown what this individual's actual name is, although a number of characters in canon have B.C. as their initials: [[Bartemius Crouch Senior|Bartemius Crouch Snr]], [[Bartemius Crouch Junior|Bartemius Crouch Jnr]] and [[Buckley Cooper]].
*It is possible, as this individual was a former [[Slug Club]] member, that the person attended the [[Slug Club Christmas party|Christmas party]] in [[1996]].
