'''K. Machadus''' was a reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in [[1926]].<ref name="FB-dec1">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:NewYorkGhost-1December1927.jpg}}</ref>

==Biography==
They co-wrote a report with [[N. Newzer]] and [[D. Figueiredus]] out of [[Portland]], [[Oregon]] about the sourcing of [[Stinksap]] extract in a popular brand of toothpaste, which was published in the [[1 December]] 1926 issue of the newspaper.<ref name="FB-dec1" />
