'''Duncan Ashe''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the second half of the 20th century and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Slytherin]].

==Biography==
He was friends with [[Jacob (Hogwarts student)|Jacob]] from his first day at school until his dying day. The two were noted to be mischief makers by others, and frequently visited [[Zonko's Joke Shop]]. At some point, he and Jacob also broke their [[Broomstick|brooms]].<ref name="HM57">{{HM|5|7}}</ref> He was also friends with the school [[poltergeist]], [[Peeves]].<ref name="HM522">{{HM|5|22}}</ref> Sometime during the [[1970s]] or [[1980s]], he died when compelled by [[R]] to concoct an [[Erumpent Potion]], which exploded while he was preparing it. After his death, Duncan returned as a [[ghost]] and stayed at Hogwarts, though he rarely showed himself to the other residents of the Castle.<ref name="HM56">{{HM|5|6}}</ref>

He helped Jacob's sibling appease Peeves by unleashing various forms of chaos upon the school in order to persuade Peeves to hand over the [[Vault portrait]].<ref name="HM522"/>

==Personality and traits==
When he was alive, Duncan loved making mischief and was noted to be a mischief maker by others along with his friend, [[Jacob (Hogwarts student)|Jacob]]. After he died, he isolated himself and rarely showed himself to the other residents of the Castle. He was angry and resentful at Jacob for taking the blame for [[R]] and joining them.

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Duncan was adept in the art of making potions, as he made a powerful [[Erumpent Potion]].<ref name="HM56"/>
*'''[[Flight|Flying]]''': [[Rolanda Hooch|Madam Hooch]] described Duncan Ashe as an "excellent flier", meaning he was highly skilled with flying on a [[broomstick]].<ref name="HM57"/>
