'''Dr Medusa''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch or wizard]]. Page twenty of the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' was named after the doctor, so they presumably wrote an advice column for the periodical.<ref>{{OOTP|F}} - Chapter 30</ref>

==Etymology==
In [[Greece|Greek]] mythology, Medusa is the name of a [[Gorgon]] who turns people into stone.
