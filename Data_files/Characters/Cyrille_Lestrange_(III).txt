'''Cyrille Lestrange''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. He was the son of [[Cyrille Lestrange (II)|Cyrille Lestrange]] and [[Balbine Lestrange (I)|Balbine Leroy]]. He married [[Luminosa Lestrange|Luminosa Trouche]] and had a two sons with her, [[Cyrille Lestrange (IV)|Cyrille]] and [[Floxel Lestrange|Floxel]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
