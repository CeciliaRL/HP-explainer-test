F. claimed that he "enjoyed himself right up until the last moment," suggesting he died during the trip.<ref name="dp3">''[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]'', Issue 3 (see [https://www.hp-lexicon.org/character/mrs-g-f/ this entry] on the Harry Potter Lexicon)</ref>
