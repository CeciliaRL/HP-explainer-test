The following is a list of '''[[Durmstrang Institute]] students''' whose exact identity is not known.

==Unidentified Durmstrang boy (II)==
This '''individual''' was a wizard who attended Durmstrang Institute in the 1990s. He was one of the students who travelled to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for the Triwizard Tournament and put his name in the Goblet of Fire, however Viktor Krum was chosen as Champion. He also attended the memorial feast of Cedric Diggory following his murder.<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref>

;Behind the scenes
*This student is played by [[Oliver Miceli]] in {{GOF|FA}}.
