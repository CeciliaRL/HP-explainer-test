'''Nozéa Lestrange''' (née '''Trouche''') was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] born in [[1754]] who married into the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She died in [[1799]] and was laid to rest in the [[Lestrange Mausoleum|Lestrange family Mausoleum]] in [[Cimetière du Père-Lachaise]], [[Paris]], France.<ref name="Archive" />
