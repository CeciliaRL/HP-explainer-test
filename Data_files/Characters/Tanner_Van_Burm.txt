'''Tanner Van Burm''' was a [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. He once signed up for [[Quidditch]] training at 7pm on a Tuesday on the sign-up sheet that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in the [[Gryffindor Common Room]].<ref>{{See image|File:QuidditchTrainingSignupSheet.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Tanner" comes from an English surname meaning "one who tans hides".
