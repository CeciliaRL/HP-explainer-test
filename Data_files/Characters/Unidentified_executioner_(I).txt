==Biography==
Along with [[Bernadette]] and [[Unidentified executioner (II)|another wizard]] assisted in the execution of [[Newton Scamander]] and [[Porpentina Goldstein]] after being sentenced to [[Death]] by [[Gellert Grindelwald]] disguised as [[Percival Graves]] for violating the [[International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy]]. The execution was stopped when Newt and Tina were rescued by a [[Swooping Evil]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>
