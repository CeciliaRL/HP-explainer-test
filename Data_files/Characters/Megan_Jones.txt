'''Megan Jones''' was a [[half-blood]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1991]]. She was in the same year as [[Harry Potter]] and was Sorted into [[Hufflepuff]] house.<ref name="PM"/>

==Biography==
===Early life===
Megan was born in either 1979 or 1980. She may be related to [[Hestia Jones|Hestia]], [[Peter Jones|Peter]] and [[Gwenog Jones]].

Megan began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in September 1991.

She would have boarded the Hogwarts Express and arrived at [[Hogsmeade station|Hogsmeade Station]] to the school. Along with the rest of the first years, she would have boarded an enchanted boat and be welcomed by [[Minerva McGonagall]] in the [[Chamber of Reception|Entrance Hall Antechamber]].

They proceeded to the [[Great Hall]] where the [[Sorting ceremony]] was held. The [[Sorting Hat]] placed Megan in [[Hufflepuff|Hufflepuff House]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}}</ref> She shared a dormitory in the [[Hufflepuff Basement]] with [[Hannah Abbott]] and [[Susan Bones]].
