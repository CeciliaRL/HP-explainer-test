'''Dan''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who had a [[portrait]] of himself hung somewhere in [[Hogwarts Castle]] by the [[1991-1992 school year]]. Dan attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in his youth, and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Gryffindor]] [[Hogwarts Houses|House]], and wore the Gryffindor [[Lion]] emblem on his long brown cloak which we wore as an adult, as shown in his portrait.<ref>{{PS|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This portrait appeared in the PC version of the [[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game)|video game adaptation]] of {{PS}}. In the game files, it was identified as dan104.bmp.
