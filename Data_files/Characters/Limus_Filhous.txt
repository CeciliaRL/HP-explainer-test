'''Limus Filhous''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked as a reporter and columnist for the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''. In [[1927]], Limus wrote an editorial entitled "''Grindewald Escape: No conclusions yet pledge the [[International Confederation of Wizards|I.C.W.]]''".<ref>{{COG}} (see [[:File:The Daily Prophet 1927.png|this image]]).</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Eduardo Lima]], a graphic artist who has worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] since ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]''.
