This '''individual''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] who worked as an announcer for the election of [[Henry Shaw Junior]] in [[1926]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Biography==
This individual was the announcer for the election of [[Henry Shaw Junior]] at his [[Fundraising Dinner for the Re-election of Senator Henry Shaw|Fundraising Dinner]] in [[December]] [[1926]]. Not long after having announced Shaw during his speech, Shaw was attacked and murdered by the rogue [[Obscurial]] form of [[Credence Barebone]], which he would have witnessed, much to the horror and shock of everyone present.<ref name="FBS"/>

==Behind the scenes==
The Announcer was portrayed by [[Todd Boyce]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
