'''Dougal''' was a male [[Demiguise]] that accompanied [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] as he researched [[Magizoology]] and visited [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="FBS"/>

==Biography==
[[File:Dougal's container.png|250px|thumb|right|Dougals container in Newt's suitcase]]
He was one of the creatures that escaped [[Newton Scamander's suitcase|Newt Scamander's suitcase]] and had to be recaptured after causing a disturbance in a local department store.<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Personality and traits==
Dougal seemed to be a rather kind-hearted and benevolent [[creature]], as he kindly took care of a young [[Occamy]] by giving it food and tending to its needs.<ref name="FBS"/>
