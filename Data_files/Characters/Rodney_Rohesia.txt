'''Rodney Rohesia''' was an individual who in [[1927]] lived at "door number twenty one, second lane on the right, past the fourth hedge on the left". In that year, a letter addressed to Rohesia was written by the [[British Ministry of Magic]]'s [[Wizengamot Administration Services|Wizengamot Administrative Department]].<ref name="Archive">{{AM}}</ref>
