This '''individual''' (fl. [[1981]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] who held a clerical post at the [[Council of Magical Law]] in the [[1980s]].

==Biography==
This wizard was present at [[Igor Karkaroff]]'s hearing in order to provide [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] names, and provided [[Bartemius Crouch Senior|Bartemius Crouch Snr]], who presided over the Council, with records of the [[Death Eaters]] already caught by the Ministry.<ref name="GOFF21">{{GOF|F}} - Chapter 21 (''The Pensieve'')</ref>
