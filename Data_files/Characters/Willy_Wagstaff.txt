'''"Honest" Willy Wagstaff''' was a street vendor who worked in [[Diagon Alley]]. The [[Department of Magical Equipment Control]] issued a warning about him in the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'', alleging that he sold defective merchandise, including loose-bottomed [[cauldron]]s and [[wand]]s that had to be recalled after several people were burned by them.<ref name="DP">{{DP}}: [[31 July]], [[1998]], Page 1</ref>
