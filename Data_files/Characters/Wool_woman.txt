This '''woman''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]], who in her elderly years became a resident at [[St Oswald's Home for Old Witches and Wizards]]. She was present in St Oswalds on [[1 September]], [[2020]], when [[Albus Potter]] and [[Scorpius Malfoy]] abandoned the [[Hogwarts Express]] to come looking for [[Amos Diggory]]. When the boys asked her where Amos could be found, she in return asked them why they would wish to see such a "miserable old sod".<ref name="CC113">{{CC|B|1|13}}</ref>
