'''Simeon Woderuff''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked at the [[Cauldron Workshop]] in [[Diagon Alley]].

==Biography==
He ended up disappearing when he was forced to train a [[Griffin]], [[Tiffin]], to guard [[Vault 619]] belonging to [[Edwin Avarus]] at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]]. Due to Tiffin, the [[Beast Division]] investigated what happened to the wizard.<ref name="FBC14">{{FBC|14}}</ref>
