'''Merula Snyde's father''' was a [[Dark Wizard|Dark]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] of [[Great Britain|British]] origin. He and [[Merula Snyde's mother|his wife]] were imprisoned in [[Azkaban]] for their allegiance to [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]].<ref>{{HM|3|8}}</ref> He had one daughter,<ref>{{HM|5|28}}</ref> [[Merula Snyde|Merula]], born circa [[1973]].

==Biography==
In his youth, Merula's father attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin]].<ref>After loosing a duel with [[Jacob's sibling]], Merula says that she is a fourth generation [[Slytherin]].</ref> During his Hogwarts years, he played [[Quidditch]] for the [[Slytherin Quidditch team]] in an unknown position.<ref>{{HM}}, During a practise match with Jacob's sibling, Merula said that both her parents played for Slytherin's Quidditch team.</ref> Later is his life, he married his [[Merula Snyde's mother|wife]], and they had one daughter together, [[Merula Snyde|Merula]].

===First Wizarding War===
He and his wife both became [[Death Eaters]], loyal and devoted servants of the [[Dark Wizard|Dark Lord]] [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]]. Both he and his wife participated in the [[First Wizarding War]], they likely fought against many [[Auror]]s and members of the [[Order of the Phoenix]].

===After the war===
On [[Merula Snyde's tenth birthday|Merula's tenth birthday]], he and his wife gave Merula a broom as a present. They took turns taking Merula on the broom. Less than a year later, he and his wife were incarcerated in [[Azkaban]] for their crimes committed as Death Eaters.<ref>{{HM|4|Cast Your Patronus}}, information revealed if the player chooses to use [[Legilimency]]</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Dark Arts]]''': Being a Death Eater, Merula's father would have been accomplished in dark magic, including the casting of [[Dark charm]]s and most likely the [[Unforgivable Curses]].
*'''[[Duelling]]''': Considering how Death Eaters are proficient in [[martial magic]], Merula's father would have been a competent duellist who fought against [[Auror]]s and members of the [[Order of the Phoenix]] during the [[First Wizarding War]].
*'''[[Flight|Flying]]''': Whilst attending Hogwarts in his youth, Merula's father played [[Quidditch]] for the [[Slytherin Quidditch team]], showing he was talented with [[broomstick]] flying.
