'''Heinrich Eberstadt''' was a [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was a delegate to the [[International Confederation of Wizards]].<ref name="FB60"/>

==Biography==
He participated in an emergency meeting alongside fellow members of the Confederation in [[New York]] in [[1926]] to discuss the potential exposure of the wizarding world.<ref name="FB60"/>

[[President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America]], [[Seraphina Picquery]], rebuked his criticisms, noting that she would not be lectured by the man who let [[Gellert Grindelwald]] slip through his fingers.<ref name="FB60">{{FB|S|60}}</ref>
