'''Gabriella Mather'''<ref>Closed captions on the DVD/Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] reported missing along with her relative, [[Emily Mather|Emily]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' radio programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.

==Etymology==
The name "Gabriella" comes from the Hebrew name גַבְרִיאֵל (Gavri'el) meaning "strong man of God".

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of [[James Mather]], who worked as a supervising sound editor on every ''Harry Potter'' film from ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'' onward.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0558450/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cr274/ James Mather's profile on IMDb]</ref>
