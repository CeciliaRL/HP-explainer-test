'''Mattie Lovett''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] author in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:TheNewYorkAffair.jpg}}</ref>

She conceived a story idea in collaboration with [[Franklin Greenwood]].<ref name="poster" /> [[Maxwell Lima]] wrote a book based on Lovett and Greenwood's idea.<ref name="poster" /> Lima's book was in turn adapted into the musical ''[[The New York Affair]]'' by playwright [[Maurice Willard]].<ref name="poster" />
