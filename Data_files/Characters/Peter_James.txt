'''Peter James''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. During his [[second year]], he lost his copy of ''[[Simple Spells for Year 2]]'', and posted a notice on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in the [[Gryffindor Tower|Gryffindor common room]] asking for help finding it.<ref name="HPE">See [[:File:PeterJamesLostBook.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>
