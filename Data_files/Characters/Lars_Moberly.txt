'''Lars Moberly''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].<ref name="moberly">{{CB|119}} {{see image|File:LarsMoberlyWanted.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Moberly, which stated he was wanted for [[Muggle|No-Maj]] murder.<ref name="moberly" /> It described him as a "malicious killer" and "bearing [[wand]], most menacing and extremely dangerous."<ref name="moberly" /> A reward of 3000 [[dragot]]s was offered for information leading to his capture.<ref name="moberly" /> The poster was hanging in [[The Blind Pig]] when [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] paid it a visit in December 1926.<ref name="moberly" />
