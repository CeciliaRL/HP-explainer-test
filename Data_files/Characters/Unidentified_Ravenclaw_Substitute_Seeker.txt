This '''individual''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and a substitute [[Seeker]] on their [[Quidditch]] [[Ravenclaw Quidditch team|team]] in [[1991]] to [[1992]]. He was filling in when [[Unidentified Ravenclaw Seeker|the actual Seeker]] was unable to play.<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*In the PC versions this Seeker substitute doesn't appear, having instead been replaced by [[Unidentified Ravenclaw Seeker|another student]].
