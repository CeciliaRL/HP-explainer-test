This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was appointed [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] at some unknown time. After his death, his [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head.<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}}</ref> His portrait is often found down and left from the portrait of the [[Pink-faced Hogwarts Headmaster]] immediately above the cabinet containing the [[Pensieve]].<ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}}</ref><ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>
