'''Kousuke Yoshioka''' was a [[Hufflepuff]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Kousuke is a Japanese name meaning "inlet" and "clear". Yoshioka is a Japanese surname, which means "lucky (or good) hill".

==Behind the scenes==
*Based on his name, he is most likely of [[Japan]]ese descent.
