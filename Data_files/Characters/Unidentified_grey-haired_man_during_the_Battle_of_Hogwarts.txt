This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who fought during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1998]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref> 

==Biography==
On [[2 May]] [[1998]], he was one of the many [[Wizardkind|wizards and witches]] who fought bravely in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] against [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] and the [[Death Eaters]]. During the final stage of the battle, he was seen fighting a bald Death Eater in the [[Great Hall]], who he managed to kill while [[duelling]] ferociously against the Death Eaters. It is unknown if he survived the battle.<ref name="DHF2"/>
