Revd. Dr '''Stephen Swain''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] and the vicar of the [[St Jerome's Church]], in [[Godric's Hollow]] during the [[1990s]].<ref name="DH1VG">{{DH|G1}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named in honour of [http://www.film-architecture.com/blog/?page_id=44 Stephen Swain], who worked in the Art Department in all the [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]], first as an assistant, then as a draughtsman, and finally as an assistant art director.
