This '''individual''' was the secretary of [[Vernon Dursley]], the executive of [[Grunnings]], a [[drill]]-making company.<ref name="PS1"/>

==Biography==
On [[1 November]] [[1981]], she was told not to disturb Vernon while he decided to make a [[telephone]] call to his wife, [[Petunia Dursley|Petunia]]. Vernon decided against making this call, however.<ref name="PS1">{{PS|B|1}}</ref>
