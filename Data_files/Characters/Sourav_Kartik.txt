'''Sourav Kartik''' was a [[Slytherin]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Sourav is a common male Indian name, meaning "fragrance", generally that of flowers. Kartik, a male given name, is commonly used in the southern part of India. The name stands for the Hindu god "Karthikeya", a son of Shiva.

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}}.
*Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of [[India]]n descent.
