'''Jatin Agarkar''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
"Jatin" is a name for Shiva, and means "He who has matted locks". Agarkar is an [[India]]n surname most commonly found in the  Maharashtra state.

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}}.
*Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.
