'''Madhumati Parmar''' was a [[Hufflepuff]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Madhumati is a sanskrit name meaning 'rich in honey'. Parmar is an indian name meaning 'one that strikes the enemy', from Sanskrit ''para'' 'alien, enemy' + ''mara'' 'strike, kill'.

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}}.
*Based on her name, it can be presumed that she is of [[India]]n descent.
