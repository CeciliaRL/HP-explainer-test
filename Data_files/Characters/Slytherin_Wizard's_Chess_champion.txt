The '''Slytherin Wizard's Chess champion''', as the title suggests, refers to the [[Wizard's Chess]] Champion for [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]], at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="OOTPG">{{OOTP|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
The boy came to be educated at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]]. His [[Head of House]] was [[Professor]] [[Severus Snape]], the [[Potions (class)|Potions]] [[Potions Master|Master]], and he shared a dormitory with four other boys in his year in the [[Slytherin Dungeon]]. He was skilled in [[Wizard's Chess]] and could be usually found playing at the [[Great Hall]].<ref name="OOTPG"/>
