'''Momolu Wotorson''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] who was a delegate to the [[International Confederation of Wizards]].<ref name="FB60"/>

==Biography==
{{Quote|Theseus Scamander? The war hero?|Momolu mistaking Newt Scamander for his brother Theseus|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: The Original Screenplay}}
On [[6 December]] [[1926]], he attended an emergency meeting at the [[Pentagram Office]] to discuss the [[magic]]al disturbances in [[New York]], inquiring if [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] was his brother [[Theseus Scamander|Theseus]] when [[Hector Podmore]] addressed him by his surname.<ref name="FB60">{{FB|S|60}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Momolu Wotorson was portrayed by [[Christian Dixon]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.<ref>{{IMDb|nm3956045|Christian Dixon}}</ref>
*His attire is notably similar to that of [[Auror]] [[Kingsley Shacklebolt]], suggesting a cultural similarity between the two.
*Given his name, he was most likely an [[Africa]]n wizard.
