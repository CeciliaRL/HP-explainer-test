'''Eduaphora Mergus''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who wrote an article titled "My Week Without [[Rune]]s!" that was published in an issue of ''[[The Quibbler]]'' in [[1996]].<ref>See [https://harrypotter.wikia.com/wiki/File:Quibbler-Spectrospecs.jpg this image].</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character's unique given name was presumably created as a tribute to the [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] graphic artists [[Eduardo Lima]] and [[Miraphora Mina]].
