'''Minaka Hirakata''' was a [[Japan]]ese [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as a [[Beater]] for the [[Japanese National Quidditch team]]. She was notable for having electric blue coloured hair.<ref>{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Hirakata's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Nerissa Brody]] at an [[Exploding Snap]] challenge.<ref>{{HBP|G}} {{c|NDS edition}}</ref>
