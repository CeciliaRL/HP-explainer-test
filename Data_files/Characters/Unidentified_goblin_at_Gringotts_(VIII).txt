This '''goblin''' worked at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] during the wizarding management in [[1997]]-[[1998]] when the Ministry fell to the [[Death Eaters]].<ref name="DHF2"/>

==Biography==
===1998===
He was present at the bank when [[Harry Potter]], [[Hermione Granger]], [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]], and [[Griphook]] hosted a break-in in order to retrieve one of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s [[Horcrux]]es, [[Helga Hufflepuff's cup]] from the [[Lestrange Vault]]. He appeared to be one of the first goblins who discovered they were impostors as he appeared to have heard Griphook's voice speak.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

===Later life===
It is unknown what happened to the goblin after the break-in but he may have been killed.
