'''Oddvar Spillum''' was a [[Norway|Norwegian]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] who, in [[2014]], coached the [[Norwegian National Quidditch team]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[BULGARIA VERSUS NORWAY]] (Archived)</ref>

==Biography==
When his team, one of the joint favourites of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]], suffered a heavy defeat against [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team|Bulgaria]] (170 to 20), Oddvar Spillum had no post-match comment for reporters, apart from broken sobs.<ref name="PM"/>
