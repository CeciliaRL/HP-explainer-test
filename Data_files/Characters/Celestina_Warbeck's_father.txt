'''Mr Warbeck''' was a [[Wales|Welsh]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] who worked as a minor functionary in the [[Muggle Liaison Office]] in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="Pottermore" />

==Biography==
He met his [[Celestina Warbeck's mother|wife]], a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] actress, when she was attacked by a [[Lethifold]] which had disguised itself as a stage curtain.<ref name="Pottermore" /> The couple had at least one child, [[Celestina Warbeck|Celestina]], who was born [[18 August]]<ref name="Pottermore" /> [[1917]].<ref name="chocolatefrog">[[Chocolate Frog Card|Famous Wizard Card]] ([[Celestina Warbeck]])</ref>

Celestina proved to have an exceptional voice at a young age. She grew up to become an internationally famous wizarding singer.<ref name="Pottermore" />
