'''Tiffin''' was a [[Griffin]] who guarded [[Vault 619]] belonging to [[Edwin Avarus]] at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]]. [[Simeon Woderuff]] was forced to train Tiffin. Later, the [[Beast Division]] investigated Tiffin when Woderuff went missing, to find out what happened.<ref name="FBC14">{{FBC|14}}</ref>
