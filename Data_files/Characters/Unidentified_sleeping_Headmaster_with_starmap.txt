This '''wizard''' was appointed [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] at some unknown time.<ref name="mohp">{{MHP}} {{See image|File:MoHP-Headmaster_with_starmaps-text.png}}</ref> After his death,<ref name="DOD" /> his [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head.<ref name="mohp" /> He was depicted sleeping in an chair while studying a book with a celestial globe on the table.<ref name="mohp" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This portrait was painted by artist [[Steve Mitchell]].<ref>[http://www.stevemitchellscenicartist.co.uk/photo_11369132.html Portrait page] on Steve Mitchell's official site</ref> Mitchell created several portraits for the [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref>[http://www.stevemitchellscenicartist.co.uk/section694708_572232.html Portrait portfolio] on Steve Mitchell's official site</ref>
*The portrait that hangs in the Headmaster's office set at {{MHP}} contains text in the upper right corner that was not present on the original portrait by Steve Mitchell.
