'''Elizabeth Mkapa''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==

Elizabeth is derived from the Hebrew 'Elisheva', which means "my God is an oath". The surname Mkapa is Tanzanian, borne by Benjamin Mkapa, the third president of Tanzania, and probably indicates an African ancestry.
