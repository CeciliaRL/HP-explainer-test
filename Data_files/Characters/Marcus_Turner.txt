'''Marcus Turner''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="CCP">[http://www.castingcallpro.com/uk/actor/profile/sam-morris-1 Sam Morris's profile on Casting Call Pro]</ref><ref name="SN">[http://www.starnow.ca/samadammorris Sam Morris's profile on StarNow]</ref> He was made a [[Prefect]] for his house by the [[1993–1994 school year]].<ref name="SN" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is featured only in the films ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire]]''. Portrayed by [[Sam Morris]], his name was not revealed in either film, but was listed on Morris's casting agency profile page.<ref name="CCP" />
