}}This '''[[Giant]]''' was a member of the [[Giant colony]] who fought in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] fighting for [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] and his [[Death Eaters]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

==Biography==
Along with the rest of the [[Giant colony]], he fought in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] on [[2 May]], [[1998]]. During the bloody and violent battle, he destroyed the [[Suit of armour|suits of armour]] with one of the [[Quidditch]] goal hoops.<ref name="DHF2>{{DH|F2}}</ref> His fate after the battle is unknown, although it is possible that he was killed during the battle. 

==Behind the scenes==
*This giant is played by [[Philip Wright]] in the [[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2|film adaptation]] of {{DH}}.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0942712/ Philip Wright at IMDB]</ref>
*In {{LEGO|Y2}} he also appears with a [[Quidditch]] goal hoop, but instead he stands in front of an entrance and blocks it. The trio uses a phonogragh to make him dance then kill him with a [[Portable Swamp]] in a [[Weasley box]].
