'''Darren Floyd''' was a [[Wales|Welsh]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a [[Beater]] for the [[Welsh National Quidditch team]] in the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]]<ref name="PM"/>. During their match against [[Brazil]], he single-handedly prevented at least seventeen Brazilian goals. 

After Brazil's victory, he had to help fellow Beater [[Iefan Rice]] drag their manager [[Gwenog Jones]] off the pitch after she attacked Brazilian manager [[José Barboza]] in retaliation for calling the Welsh Chasers "talentless hags".<ref name="PM"/>
