'''Gato Felinus''' (also known as '''Mittens - The Cat''') was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[United States of America]] in the [[1920s]].

==Biography==
In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Felinus, which stated that he was wanted for [[Muggle|No-Maj]] and [[House-elf|elf]] murder and offered a reward of 2500 [[dragot]]s for his arrest.<ref>{{FB|F}} {{see image|File:Gato Felinus - wanted poster.png}}</ref>
