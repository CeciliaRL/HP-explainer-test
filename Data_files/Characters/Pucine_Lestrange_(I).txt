'''Pucine Lestrange''' (née '''Leroy''') was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She married [[Fulcran Lestrange (I)|Fulcran Lestrange]], and had a son with him, [[Fulcran Lestrange (II)|Fulcran]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref> She might have been the sister of [[Balbine Lestrange (I)|Balbine Lestrange]].
