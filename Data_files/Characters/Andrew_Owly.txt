'''Andrew Owly''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] and a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="list">See [[:File:LibraryBooksToBeReturned.png|this image]] and [[:File:LibraryBooksToBeReturned2.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

His name appeared on a list of students with overdue [[Hogwarts Library|library]] books that was posted on the noticeboard in the [[Gryffindor Tower|Gryffindor common room]] by [[Irma Pince|Madam Pince]] sometime in the 1990s.<ref name="list"/>
