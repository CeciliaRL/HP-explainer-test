'''Elsie Valentine Schroeder''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who was reported missing on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' wireless programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to some sort of [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Bjorn Ole Schroeder. Schroeder worked as a sound effects editor on ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]'', and as a dialogue editor on the following five [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0775452/ Bjorn Ole Schroeder's profile on IMDb]</ref>
