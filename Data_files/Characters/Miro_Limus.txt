'''Miro Limus''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]]<ref name="gender" /> who wrote the revised edition of ''[[Quidditch Teams of Britain and Ireland|Quidditch Teams of England and Ireland]]''.<ref name="MHP">{{MHP}} {{see image|File:QuidditchTeamsofBritainandIreland.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*This character was named after [[Miraphora Mina]] and [[Eduardo Lima]], the graphic artists who produced many of the paper props seen in the [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]].
