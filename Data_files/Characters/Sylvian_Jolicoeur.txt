'''Sylvian Jolicoeur''' was a [[Haiti]]an [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as the [[Seeker]] for the [[2014]] [[Haitian National Quidditch team]].

==Biography==
During the third match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], Haiti versus [[Brazilian National Quidditch team|Brazil]], Jolicoeur came within inches of capturing the Snitch and securing the win, but fellow [[Beater]] [[Jean-Baptiste Bloncourt]]'s devastating mis-hit of the [[Bludger]] knocked him out cold (the second such incident of the match, the first one left [[Keeper]] [[Lenelle Paraison]] with a broken nose).<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[BRAZIL VERSUS HAITI]] (Archived)</ref>

The [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]] proceeded to get caught up in [[Jean-Baptiste Bloncourt]]'s sleeve, who incurred in [[Snitchnip]] when he wrestled it out of his undergarments and grabbed it. The team was immediately disqualified.<ref name="PM"/> Following the match, Jolicoeur was reported to have been making a good recovery.<ref name="PM"/>
