'''Professor Limebert''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] sometime before [[Armando Dippet]].<ref name="HeadPortrait">See [[:File:Headmaster Limebert.jpg|this image]]</ref>

==Biography==
===Lifetime===
[[File:Headmaster Limebert.jpg|thumb|left|Limebert in his portrait in [[1992]]]]
At some point in his life, Limebert became the [[Headmaster]] of Hogwarts.<ref name="HeadPortrait"/>

===Post-mortem===
Professor Limebert died sometime in or before [[1992]].<ref name="DOD" /> A portrait of him was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in order to give advice to the current Head.<ref name="HeadPortrait"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Limebert's portrait seems to have been based on an [https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:1851._BrullovPort_Lanchi.jpg 1851 portrait of archaeologist Michelangelo Lanci] by [[Wikipedia:Karl Bryullov|Karl Bryullov]].
