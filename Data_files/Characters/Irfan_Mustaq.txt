'''Irfan Mustaq''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
He was a member of the [[Duelling Club]] run by [[Karl Limpley]] in the [[Clock Tower Courtyard]] during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
The name "Irfan" means "knowledge, learning" in Arabic.
