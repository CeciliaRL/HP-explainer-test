'''Stebbins''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] and student in the same year as the [[Marauders]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="OOTP28">{{OOTP|B|28}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[June]] [[1976]], during the [[Ordinary Wizarding Level|O.W.L.]] exam period, Stebbins refused to stop writing with their [[quill]] even after an examination was finished, and was admonished for it by [[Filius Flitwick|Professor Flitwick]].<ref name="OOTP28"/>
