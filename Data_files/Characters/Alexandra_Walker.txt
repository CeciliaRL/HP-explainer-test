'''Alexandra Walker''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]]. She was charged with attempted robbery of [[Ollivanders]] in the early summer of [[1996]]; this was recorded in the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''.<ref>{{OOTP|F}} - Chapter 30 (''The Lost Prophecy'')</ref>

==Behind the scenes==

*This character was presumably named after Alexandra Walker, an art director on ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'', as well as the two [[Harry Potter (film series)|films]] preceding it.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1023180/ Alexandra Walker]" on IMDb</ref>
