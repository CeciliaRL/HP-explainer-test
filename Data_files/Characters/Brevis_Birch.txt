[[Brevis Birch]] was the [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Tutshill Tornados]].<ref name="wizardsb">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/b.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: B]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon</ref> He blamed the team's unexpected loss in a match against the [[Ballycastle Bats]] in early [[1999]] on a bout of [[narcolepsy]] suffered by their [[Keeper]] [[Merwyn Finwick]].<ref name="wizardsb" /><ref>"[http://www.hplex.info/quidditch/q_teams_britain.html#Tutshill Teams of the World: Great Britain and Ireland]" on The Harry Potter Lexicon</ref> The Tornados had been at the top of the [[British and Irish Quidditch League|league]] before this defeat.<ref>"[http://www.hplex.info/about/sources/source_dp.html Daily Prophet newsletters]" on The Harry Potter Lexicon</ref>
