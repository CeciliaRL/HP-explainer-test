{{Real world subject}}
'''Isaiah''' was a [[Judaism|Jewish]] prophet who lived in the [[B.C. era|8th century B.C.]].<ref name="wikipedia">"[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isaiah Isaiah]" on Wikipedia</ref><ref name="oxford">"[http://www.oxfordreference.com/view/10.1093/oi/authority.20110803100011986 Isaiah]" from OxfordReference.com</ref> He gave his name to one of the books of the [[Bible]], which was believed to contain his writings, as one well those of at least one later prophet.<ref name="wikipedia" /><ref name="oxford" />

A quote from the Book of Isaiah ("the eyes of the blind shall be opened") was included on the cover of a [[Witches Live Among Us!|pamphlet]] published by the anti-[[magic|witchcraft]] group the [[New Salem Philanthropic Society]] in [[1926]].<ref>[https://twitter.com/FBAWTFTmovie/status/738056877318213632/photo/1 Tweet] by Fantastic Beasts Fan (@FBAWTFTmovie) on Twitter {{see image|File:Fighting Modern Evils - FB.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Specifically, the quote on ''Fighting Modern Evils'' is from [https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Bible_(King_James)/Isaiah#Chapter_35 Isaiah 35:5] in the {{wplink|King James Version}} of the Bible, and reads in full "Then the eyes of the blind shall be opened, and the ears of the deaf shall be unstopped".
