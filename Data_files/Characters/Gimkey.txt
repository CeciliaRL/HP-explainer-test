'''Gimkey''' was a [[house-elf]] that worked at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1980s]]. He worked in the [[Hogwarts kitchens|kitchens]] along with the other [[Hogwarts house-elves]].<ref name="HM"/>

==Physical appearance==
The elf had long, pig-like ears and a pointy, beakish nose. He wore what appeared to be a potato sack emblazoned with a small Hogwarts crest.<ref name="HM" />
