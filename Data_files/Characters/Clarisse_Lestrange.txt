'''Clarisse Lestrange''' (née '''Tremblay''') was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She was the daughter of [[Bernard Tremblay]] and [[Leonie Tremblay|Leonie Lestrange]], and the sister of [[Falco Tremblay|Falco]]. She married [[Corvus Lestrange (IV)|Corvus Lestrange]] and had a son with him, [[Corvus Lestrange (V)|Corvus]].<ref name="FB88">{{FB|S|88}}</ref><ref name="FB96">{{FB|S|96}}</ref><ref name="COGteaser">{{COG}} {{see image|File:LestrangeFamilyTree HighRes.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
Clarisse Tremblay was the second wife of [[Corvus Lestrange (IV)|Corvus Lestrange]], her first cousin.<ref name="COGteaser" /> She married him after his first wife, Laurena, had died in childbirth. In [[1901]], at the age of at least twenty one, she gave birth to a son, named Corvus after his father.<ref name="COGteaser" /> At some point thereafter, her son was separated from his family.<ref name="FB88" /><ref name="FB96" /> Her later fate is unknown.

==Etymology==
*The name Clarisse stems from the Latin word "clarus", meaning "bright or clear."
*Tremblay is a topographical name that stems from the Old French word "tremblaie", meaning "aspen" or "a place planted with aspens."
