'''Alexandra Rokonski''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] whose name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of [[Sybill Trelawney|Professor Trelawney]]'s [[Divination]] [[Divination Classroom|classroom]]. The last name suggests that she was Slavic, most probably Polish or at least had Slavic (Polish) ancestors.<ref>{{POA|F}} - Chapter 21 (''Professor Trelawney's Prediction'')</ref><ref>[http://www.the-leaky-cauldron.org/2004/5/15/los-angeles-motion-picture-arts-sciences-contemporary-set-exhibit-report ''Prisoner of Azkaban'' set report] from the Leaky Cauldron</ref>
