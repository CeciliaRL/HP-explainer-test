'''Lysander Scamander'''<ref>{{WU}}</ref> was one of the twins born to [[Luna Lovegood]] and [[Rolf Scamander]]. He has a twin brother, [[Lorcan Scamander|Lorcan]].<ref name="Family">[[J.K. Rowling: A Year in the Life]] {{see image|File:Weasley Family Tree.jpg}}</ref>

==Biography==
When his mother travelled to the [[Patagonian Desert]], in [[Argentina]], to attend the final of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], in [[July]] [[2014]], Lysander was left at home with his grandfather, along with [[Lorcan Scamander|Lorcan]].<ref name="dumbledores-army-reunites" />

==Etymology==
*Lysander means "liberator of mankind" or simply "liberator" in Greek.
*Lysander is the name of a principal character in [[Wikipedia:William Shakespeare|Shakespeare's]] ''[[Wikipedia:A Midsummer Night's Dream|A Midsummer Night's Dream]]''. The play takes place largely at night, ''"lit by moonlight"'' as Oberon states, which hearkens back to Lysander's mother, Luna.

==Behind the scenes==
*He may have identified as [[Judaism|Jewish]], as his great-grandmother was [[Porpentina Goldstein]].
