'''Jonny Anomaly''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] magician in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster-lego2" /><ref name="poster-lego">{{LEGO|D}} {{See image|File:G. Forbes Productions.png}}</ref>

In [[1926]], posters were put up around [[New York]]'s harbour promoting a series of twelve performances staged by [[G. Forbes Productions]], which were slated to begin on Saturday [[11 December]].<ref name="poster-lego" /> This included a show featuring the "amazing skills" of Jonny Anomaly.<ref name="poster-lego" /> Another poster was put up advertising a performance by the "magnificent" Anomaly at the [[Packer Theatre]] on the same day.<ref name="poster-lego2" />
