'''Albert''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], who was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Hufflepuff]] in the [[1990s]]. He said that [[Argus Filch]] talked to his [[Mrs Norris|cat]] suggesting that he was a character in the [[Plot to open the Chamber of Secrets|plot]] to open the [[Chamber of Secrets]], and that Filch punished him for being out of bed at night time. He said that he wished there would be an Heir of Hufflepuff and asked [[Harry Potter]] if he could say Albert in [[Parseltongue]]. He once got a bag of beetle pincers by [[owl]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}} {{C|PC version}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*He may be related to [[Marilyn]] and [[Arthur (Hufflepuff student)|Arthur]] by the fact that he is a [[Hufflepuff]], shared the same love for beetle pincers and the out-of-universe fact that he only appeared in the PC version of the game.
