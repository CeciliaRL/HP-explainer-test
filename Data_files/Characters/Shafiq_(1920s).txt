'''Shafiq''' was a British [[wizardkind|wizard]] born into the prominent [[Shafiq family]], and a [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] official.

==Biography==
[[File:Theseus shaking hands with Shafiq and Yaxley.png|250px|left|thumb|Shafiq with Theseus Scamander, Yaxley and his wife]]
In [[1927]], Shafiq attended a wizarding ball and stood socialising with [[Yaxley (1920s)|Yaxley]] and [[Yaxley's wife|his wife]] when the group was greeted by [[Theseus Scamander]]. Shafiq enthusiastically embraced Scamander, who congratulated him on "moving upstairs", possibly referring to a promotion at the [[British Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="COG">{{COG}}</ref>
