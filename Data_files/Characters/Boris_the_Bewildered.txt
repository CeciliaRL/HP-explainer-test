'''Boris the Bewildered'''<ref name="GOF25"/><ref name="OOTPG"/> was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] from an unknown time period. 

==History==
A statue of him stands on the fifth floor of [[Hogwarts Castle]], near the [[Prefects' bathroom]]. His statue featured a lost-looking wizard with his gloves on the wrong hands.<ref name="GOF25">{{GOF|B|25}}</ref> A [[portrait]] of Boris also hung on [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts']] walls, guarding a secret passageway between the second floor corridor and the [[Third-floor corridor|third floor corridor]]. The password was ''Forget-Me-Never''.<ref name="OOTPG">{{OOTP|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Boris died sometime prior to [[1995]], as portraits hung at [[Hogwarts Castle|Hogwarts]] depict deceased individuals.<ref name="DOD"/>

==Etymology==
"Boris" is from the Turkic name ''Bogoris'', perhaps meaning "short" or "wolf".
