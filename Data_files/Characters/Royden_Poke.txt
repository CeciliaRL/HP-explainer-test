'''Royden Poke''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] employed by the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]]<ref name=":0">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 June]], [[1999]], Page 1</ref>. Representing the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]], he was part of the Ministry delegation that met with the [[Brotherhood of Goblins]] in [[Chipping Clodbury]] to discuss a [[Bill of Goblin Rights|Goblin Bill of Rights]]<ref name=":0" /> (meeting that erupted into a [[Chipping Clodbury riot|riot]]).
