'''Liza Cherks''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="list">See [[:File:LibraryBooksToBeReturned.png|this image]] and [[:File:LibraryBooksToBeReturned2.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

Sometime in the [[1990s]], the [[Hogwarts Library|library]] books she took out were overdue, and thus her name was included on a list of students with overdue books placed on the noticeboard in [[Gryffindor Tower]] by [[Irma Pince|Madam Pince]].<ref name="list"/>
