'''Magenta Comstock''' ([[1895]]–[[1991]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and experimental artist, whose [[portrait]]s' eyes not only followed the viewer around the room, but also followed them home.<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
Magenta Comstock was born in [[1895]] to parents of unknown [[blood status]].<ref name="JKRS"/>

She became an artist and began experimenting with her artwork. Her portraits were known for their staring eyes which would often follow viewers home.<ref name="JKRS"/>

She was on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] by [[1988]].<ref>{{HM}} - One of the questions in [[Badeea Ali]]'s art quiz is about who is on a Chocolate Frog Card and Magenta Comstock is one of the correct answers. Badeea Ali is unlocked as friend in {{HM|5|4}}, taking place in the [[1988–1989 school year]]. Later, in {{HM|5|9}}, [[Rubeus Hagrid]] states that his birthday is soon. As Hagrid was born on [[6 December]], this means all chapters in Year 5 up to Chapter 9 must take place at some point between [[1 September]] [[1988]] and 6 December in the same year.</ref>

At some time before or during the [[1988–1989 school year]], a book entitled ''[[Collected Artwork by Magenta Comstock]]'' was published.<ref name="TGHC">{{HM|5|THE GREAT HOGWARTS COOK-OFF}}</ref>

===Death===
Comstock died in [[1991]] at the age of 96.<ref name="JKRS"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Magenta Comstock was the [[Wizard of the Month]] on {{JKRS}} in April [[2007]].
*She died the year [[Harry Potter]] and his friends began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].
