'''Anna Chiwawa''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] singer in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster-lego">{{LEGO|D}} {{See image|File:G. Forbes Productions.png}}</ref> 

In [[1926]], posters were put up around [[New York]]'s harbour promoting a series of twelve performances staged by [[G. Forbes Productions]], which were slated to begin on Saturday [[11 December]].<ref name="poster-lego" /> This included an appearance by Anna Chiwawa, who was billed as a "lovely singing star."<ref name="poster-lego" />
