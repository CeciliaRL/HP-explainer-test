'''Mrs Westenberg'''<ref name="name">Closed captions for the deleted scene "The Burrows Shed" on the Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}.</ref> (d. [[1997]]) was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who lived in the north of [[England]].<ref name="DHF1"/> 

==Biography==
She and [[Westenberg|her husband]] were supporters of the [[Order of the Phoenix]] and had, on occasion, provided shelter for its members. The couple was found murdered in their cellar in the summer of [[1997]].<ref name="DHF1">[http://www.oclumencia.com.br/2011/01/divulgado-roteiro-completo-de-reliquias-da-morte-parte-1/ "Divulgado roteiro COMPLETO de ''Relíquias da Morte: Parte 1'' - FULL ''Deathly Hallows: Part 1'' script released″] on [http://www.oclumencia.com.br/ Oclumencia.com.br.] (translation [http://babelfish.yahoo.com/translate_url?doit=done&tt=url&intl=1&fr=bf-home&trurl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.oclumencia.com.br%2F2011%2F01%2Fdivulgado-roteiro-completo-de-reliquias-da-morte-parte-1%2F&lp=pt_en&btnTrUrl=Translate here])</ref>
