'''M. L. Miraphorum''' was a reporter for [[No-Maj Bureau]] of ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="nyghost-dec6">{{WWMM|1}} {{see image|File:NewYorkGhost-6December1926.jpg}}</ref><ref name="nyghost-dec7">{{CB}} {{see image|File:The New York Ghost - 7 Dec 1926.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
They wrote an article out of [[New Jersey]], published in the [[6 December]] [[1926]] sunset issue of the newspaper, about a recent series of "mysterious" explosions causing widespread panic among [[Non-magic people|No-Majs]] in [[New York]].<ref name="nyghost-dec6" /> This was followed in [[7 December|the next day's]] sunset issue by another report by Miraphorum out of New Jersey about how [[Magical Congress of the United States of America|MACUSA]] was working to protect New York's panicking No-Maj population.<ref name="nyghost-dec7" />
