This '''waitress''' was a female [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] employee at [[Treats|Treats Café]],<ref name="script">The café is named on the [http://warnerbros2009.warnerbros.com/assets/screenplays/hpathbp_screenplay.pdf ''Half-Blood Prince'' script] released on the [http://warnerbros2009.warnerbros.com/#/movies/harrypotter/foryourconsideration/ Warner Bros. Awards website]</ref> a café in [[Surbiton Station]] in [[1996]]. She flirted with [[Harry Potter]] during the summer of [[1996]] and came close to discovering the [[wizarding world]].<ref name="HBPF"/>

==Biography==
[[File:Harry_café_HBP_2.jpg|200px|thumb|left|Harry Potter reads the Daily Prophet in the café]]
She flirted with [[Harry Potter]] in [[Treats|Treats Café]], at [[Surbiton Station]], at some point in the summer of [[1996]]. Harry was a regular at the café in which she worked as he found taking trains kept his mind off things and would often spend nights there. She almost became aware of the existence of the [[Wizarding world]] when Harry allowed her to see a copy of the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' with moving images, but she brushed off her suspicions due to working late.<ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}} - Chapter 1 (''Who's Harry Potter?'')</ref>
