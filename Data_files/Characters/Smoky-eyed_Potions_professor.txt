This '''individual''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] who started teaching [[Potions (class)|Potions]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[2021]]. According to [[Scorpius Malfoy]], she was old and "smoky-eyed".<ref name="Act4Scene14">{{CC|B|4|14}}</ref>

==Biography==
In the end of the [[2020–2021 school year]], [[Scorpius Malfoy]] joked that [[Albus Potter]] was probably romantically attracted to the new [[Potions (class)|Potions]] [[professor]], in reference to his "[[Delphini|thing about older women]]".<ref name="Act4Scene14"/>
