'''Gwendolyn Morgan''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] who played [[Quidditch]] for the [[Holyhead Harpies]], and was the team Captain in [[1953]]. Following a seven day match against the [[Germany|German]] team [[Heidelberg Harriers]] in 1953, the Harriers Captain, [[Rudolf Brand]], proposed marriage to her. She promptly concussed him with her [[Cleansweep Five]].<ref name="QTA7">{{QTA|R|7}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Gwendolyn" means "white ring", derived from the Welsh elements gwen "white, fair, blessed" and dolen "ring". This was the name of a mythical queen of the Britons who defeated her husband in battle, as told by Geoffrey of Monmouth.
