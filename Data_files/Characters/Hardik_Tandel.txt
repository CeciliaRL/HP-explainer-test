'''Hardik Tandel''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around during the [[1993-1994 school year]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Hardik is an [[India]]n male given name, meaning "heartfelt". Tandel is a surname of Indian origin, widely found in the Gujarat state.

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console version]] of {{POA}}.
*Given his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.
