This '''individual''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] taxi driver who worked in [[London]] during the early [[1990s]].<ref name="COSF"/>

==Biography==
In [[1992]], he witnessed [[Harry Potter]] and [[Ronald Weasley]] leave [[King's Cross Station]] in the flying [[Flying Ford Anglia|Ford Anglia]] on [[1 September]], [[1992]]. He was sitting in his cab, and was stunned by what he saw, leaning out his window to get a better look.<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}}</ref>

The [[Evening Prophet]] later reported on the [[FLYING FORD ANGLIA MYSTIFIES MUGGLES|incident]], stating that the flying car had been seen by ''"no less than seven Muggles."''<ref name="COSF"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*This individual, along with [[Angus Fleet]], was presumably one of the [[Non-magic people|Muggles]] that [[Molly Weasley]] mentioned to her son [[Ronald Weasley|Ron]], saying that he caused [[Arthur Weasley]] to face an inquiry at [[British Ministry of Magic|work]].<ref name="CS6">{{COS|B|6}}</ref>
