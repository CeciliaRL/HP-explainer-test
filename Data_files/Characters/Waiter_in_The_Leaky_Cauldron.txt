This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was a waiter at the [[Leaky Cauldron]] pub in [[London]], assisting barman [[Tom]] in his duties by wandlessly and non-verbally collecting plates to be washed.<ref name="POAF">{{POA|F}}</ref>

==Physical appearance==
The waiter had brown hair and white skin, and wore a dark blue uniform.<ref name="POAF"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Nonverbal spell|Nonverbal]] and [[Wandless magic]]''': This wizard could magically carry a large stack of plates without saying a single [[incantation]] and without the use of a [[wand]], which is highly advanced [[magic]].<ref name="POAF"/>
