'''Cecilia Gallerani''' ([[1473]]–[[1536]]) was an [[Italy|Italian]] [[Wikipedia:Renaissance|Renaissance]] [[wizardkind|witch]], and the favourite and most celebrated of the many mistresses of [[Wikipedia:Ludovico Sforza|Ludovico Sforza, Duke of Milan]]. 

==Biography==
She is best known as the subject of one of [[Leonardo Da Vinci]]'s portraits. She used to arrange meetings in which Milanese intellectuals discussed philosophy and other subjects, and Cecilia herself presided over these discussions.

By the [[1990s]], there was a [[portrait]] of Gallerani hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]], on the stairway leading to the [[Dungeons]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}} - PS1 version</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*[[Wikipedia:Cecilia Gallerani|Cecilia Gallerani]]'s portrait can be seen during the end credits of the [[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)|PS1 video game adaptation]] of {{COS}}, representing one of the designers of the game Anna Larke.
