This '''wizard''' was appointed [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] at some unknown time. After his death, his [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head. This wizard's hair is in the style of portraits painted during the reign of King Charles II of England ([[1630]] - [[1685]]).<ref>[[The Paintings of Hogwarts: Masterpieces from the School of Witchcraft and Wizardry Sets]]</ref> He was portrayed sleeping on an chair with a book in hand.
