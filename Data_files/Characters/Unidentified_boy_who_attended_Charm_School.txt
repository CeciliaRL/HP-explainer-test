This '''individual''' (born c. [[1980]]) attended [[Charm School]] in the [[1980s]] but later attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. During a [[Charms (class)|Charms lesson]], he compared Hogwarts to Charm School, saying that ''it was nothing like this''.<ref name="PSgbc">{{PS|G}} - GBC version</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
{{Quote|I hope I get into Hufflepuff. That was my dad's house.|The boy|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game)}}
This individual was born somewhere in the British Isles in around [[1979]] or [[1980]], to a [[Unidentified boy who attended Charm School's father|former Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts]]. He was apparently born into a [[half-blood]] family, as he appeared to have some contact with the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] society.<ref name="bloodstatus"/> Sometime in his childhood, he attended [[Charm School]].<ref name="PSgbc"/>

===Hogwarts years===
{{Quote|Are they going to show a film?|The boy, while [[Hogwarts Express|en route]] to [[Hogwarts Castle]], on [[1 September]], [[1991]]|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game)}}
He started at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1991]], and hoped to be [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into his [[Hufflepuff|father's old house]], but was Sorted in [[Slytherin]] instead. In his first week of classes, he commented that his old [[Charm School]] was nothing like [[Filius Flitwick|Professor Flitwick]]'s [[Charms (class)|Charms]] classes.<ref name="PSgbc"/>
