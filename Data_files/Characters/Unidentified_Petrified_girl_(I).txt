This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] during the [[1980s]].

==Biography==
This individual began attending Hogwarts between [[1 September]] [[1983]] and 1 September [[1989]].<ref name="age">She was attending Hogwarts during the [[1989–1990 school year]], and she could have been in any year.</ref>  

In the [[1989–1990 school year]] she was found [[Petrification|Petrified]]. She was later brought to the [[Hospital Wing]] along with other Petrified students.<ref>{{HM|6|24}}</ref> 

Eventually, she was cured with the other victims.<ref name="HM643">{{HM|6|43}}</ref>
