'''Jenna Jovius''' was a individual who lived at 34 Lippincot road, in the borough of Chelsea, [[London]] in [[1927]]. In that year the [[British Ministry of Magic]] wrote a letter addressed to Jovius which was validated by ministry official [[D. Dussal]].<ref name="Archive">{{AM}}</ref>
