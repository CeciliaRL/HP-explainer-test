'''Matey''' was a pet [[goat]] owned by [[Aberforth Dumbledore]].<ref name="OOTPF"/>

==History==
On [[5 October]], [[1995]], when [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]], and [[Hermione Granger]] walked through the front door of Aberforth's pub in [[Hogsmeade]], the [[Hog's Head]], where they had scheduled the first meeting of what was to become [[Dumbledore's Army]], Matey darted out from behind the bar counter suddenly. Aberforth followed Matey into another room, calling out, "''Matey, come back here.''".<ref name="OOTPF">{{OOTP|F}}</ref>
