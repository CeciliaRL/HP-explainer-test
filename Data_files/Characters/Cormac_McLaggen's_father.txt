'''Mr McLaggen''' was the father of [[Cormac McLaggen]]. On [[1 September]], [[1997]], when [[Death Eaters]] [[Raid on the Hogwarts Express|stopped]] the [[Hogwarts Express]] en route to [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and boarded it to search for [[Harry Potter]], Cormac threatened them that his father would hear about their actions. Death Eaters, however, completely ignored Cormac and continued their search.<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}} </ref>
