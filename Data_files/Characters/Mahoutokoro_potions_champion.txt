This '''individual''' was a [[Japan]]ese [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Mahoutokoro]]. He was a skilled [[potioneer]] and represented his school at the [[Wizarding Schools Potions Championship]].<ref name="WBP"/>

==Biography==
===Mahoutokoro===
The Japanese wizard attended [[Mahoutokoro]] for his education.<ref name="WBP"/>

===Wizarding Schools Potions Championship===
[[File:River troll bridge.png|thumb|The wizard running past the [[River Troll]] underneath the bridge]]
The wizard became a skilled [[potioneer]] and at age seventeen or above competed in the [[Wizarding Schools Potions Championship]] for the golden cauldron, where he represented his school, Mahoutokoro. At the start of the championship in the [[Enchanted garden (Book of Potions)|enchanted garden]], he headed towards the bridge, hoping to get an early lead. However, while crossing the bridge, he awakened a [[River Troll]]. Fortunately, the [[Troll]] was incapacitated by the [[Hogwarts potions champion]], who used a [[Shrinking Solution]] on it.<ref name="WBP"/>

==Physical appearance==
The wizard wore his [[Mahoutokoro uniform|school uniform]] and had black hair.<ref name="WBP"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': This individual was highly proficient in the [[magic]]al art and [[science]] of potion-making, being talented enough that he represented his school in the [[Wizarding Schools Potions Championship]].<ref name="WBP">{{WBP}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This is the first student of Mahoutokoro introduced throughout the ''Harry Potter'' series.
