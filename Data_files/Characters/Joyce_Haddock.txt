'''Joyce Haddock''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] woman. A swimming examiner at [[Smeltings Academy]], she presented [[Distance Award|an award]] to [[Dudley Dursley]] for swimming five metres, or one-quarter the full length of the pool in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HPPS 1">{{HPPS}}, p. 402: "There's one from 'Smeltings Academy' with a leaping salmon for swimming a quarter of the pool," McMillan laughs, "awarded by Mrs Joyce Haddock."</ref><ref name="HPPS 2">{{HPPS}}, p. 403 {{see image|File:DudleyAward4.jpg}}</ref>
