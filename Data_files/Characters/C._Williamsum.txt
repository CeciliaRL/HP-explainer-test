'''C. Williamsum''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] and reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="nyghost-dec6">{{WWMM|1}} {{see image|File:NewYorkGhost-6December1926.jpg}}</ref><ref name="nyghost-dec7">{{CB}} {{see image|File:The New York Ghost - 7 Dec 1926.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
Williamsum wrote an article about a "sinister" [[aura]] puzzling the [[wizardkind|wizarding]] community that was published in the [[6 December]] [[1926]] sunset issue of the paper.<ref name="nyghost-dec6" /> In the [[7 December]] sunset edition, they co-wrote a report with [[M. Carneirus (American)|M. Carneirus]] and [[R. Amorim (American)|R. Amorim]] about top [[Auror]]s being called to [[New York City Hall]] to investigate the death of [[Henry Shaw Junior]], which was published on the front page.<ref name="nyghost-dec7" />
