'''Y. Blishen''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizard or witch]] who worked for the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="cob">{{CB}} {{see image|File:Memorandum Rodentium.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
On [[7 December]] [[1926]], Blishen sent a [[Memorandum Rodentium|rat memo]] to "all 2nd Floor Sorcery Staff," including [[J. Ward]], [[L. O'Sullivan]], and [[F. Wakefield]]. The memo notified recipients that, following a vote approving the [[Calufrax Edict]], wand permit requests from aliens (non-U.S. citizens) would have to be filed with a variety of new codes.<ref name="cob" />
