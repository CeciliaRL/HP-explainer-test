'''Glinda Crook''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who worked for the [[Campaign for Greater Freedom for Wizards]]. She spoke on behalf of the organisation in the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'', stating its opposition to new [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] regulations, which were intended to limit [[magic]]al activity on [[Hallowe'en]].<ref name="DP">{{DP}}, [[1999]], Page 1</ref>
