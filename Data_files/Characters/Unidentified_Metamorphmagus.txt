This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]]''' was a [[Metamorphmagus]] and [[Infiltration of the British Ministry of Magic (1990)|infiltrated]] the [[British Ministry of Magic Headquarters]] during the [[1990–1991 school year]], disguising as several individuals including [[Reginald Cattermole]], [[Amos Diggory]] and [[Nymphadora Tonks]].<ref name="HM77"/>

After Jacob's sibling updated [[Corey Hayden]] with the events, Corey suspected the [[Dark Witch from Knockturn Alley]] to be this Metamorphmagus due to the known transfiguration of her in the [[1989–1990 school year|previous school year]].<ref name="HM78">{{HM|7|8}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
<!--- Is there a image which definitely depicts this individual in disguise?--->
*'''[[Metamorphmagus]]''': This individual possessed the natural born ability to [[magic]]ally change all features of their physical appearance at will. They used this ability to impersonate various individuals so they could infiltrate the [[British Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="HM77"/>
