This '''[[Slytherin]] girl''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who started attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1991]], where she was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin]].<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}} - Chapter 7 (''Mad Eye Moody'')</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*She was portrayed by [[Charlotte Ritchie]] in {{GOF|FA}}.
*In {{GOF|FA}}, this student is shown to be present in the same [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] class with the [[trio]], regarding the [[Unforgivable Curses]].<ref name="GOFF"/> However, her appearance in this lesson cannot be considered canon, as the book clearly states that this specific lesson was only for [[Gryffindor]] students.<ref name="GOF14">{{GOF|B|14}}</ref>
*She may possibly be [[Daphne Greengrass]], [[Tracey Davis]], [[Millicent Bulstrode]], [[Emma Vane (witch)|Emma Vane,]] [[Sophie Roper]], [[Lily Moon]], or [[Sally-Anne Perks]].
