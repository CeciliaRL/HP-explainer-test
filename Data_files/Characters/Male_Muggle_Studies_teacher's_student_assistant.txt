This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Hufflepuff]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts School]] during the [[1990s]].<ref name="CS GBC" />

He loved [[Muggle Studies]] class and worked in the capacity of an assistant to [[Unidentified male Muggle Studies teacher|the Muggle Studies Professor]].<ref name="CS GBC">{{COS|G}} - GBC version</ref>

==Biography==
===1992===
When [[Harry Potter]] gained possession of a torch during his second year, he thought he should give it to [[Unidentified male Muggle Studies teacher|the Muggle Studies teacher]]. Finding out he was absent from his [[Muggle Studies Classroom|classroom]], he gave it to his assistant. In exchange for the torch, the assistant gave Harry a harmonica.<ref name="CS GBC" />
