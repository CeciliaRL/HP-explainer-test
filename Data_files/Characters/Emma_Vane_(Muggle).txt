'''Emma Vane''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] fashion designer. Clothing designed by her was featured in the [[Costume Materialization]] collection opening that was held on [[28 September]], [[1997]], along with the work of fellow fashion designers [[Julia Dehoff]] and [[Mauricio Carneiro]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:CostumeMaterialization.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Emma Vane (real-world)|Emma Vane]], who worked as a draughtsman on several [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref>{{IMDb|nm0888985|Emma Vane}}</ref>
