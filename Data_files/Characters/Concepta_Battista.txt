'''Concepta Battista''' (b. [[1680s]]/[[1690s|90s]]) was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1700s]]. She was once [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]] from [[1704]] to [[1708]].<ref name="HPE">{{HPE}} {{See image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Plaque.jpg}}</ref>
