'''Gringotts Guards'''<ref>{{DH|G2}}</ref> are [[Wizardkind|wizards]] who are assigned to guard the entrance of [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank|Gringotts]], the bank of the [[wizarding world]].

==History==
During the [[Death Eaters]]' reign over the wizarding world, security was tightened, and guards used [[Probity Probe]] to search anyone attempting to enter the bank.

On [[1 May]], [[1998]] [[Marius]] and [[Unidentified Gringotts guard (I)|another guard]] were [[Confundus Charm|confunded]] by [[Harry Potter]], who [[Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)|broke into Gringotts]] with [[Hermione Granger]] (disguised as [[Bellatrix Lestrange]]) and [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] (disguised as Dragomir Despard), and the former-employee [[Griphook]], in order to steal one of [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]]'s [[Horcrux]]es from the [[Lestrange Vault]].
