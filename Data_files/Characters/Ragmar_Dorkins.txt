'''Ragmar Dorkins''' was the manager of the [[Chudley Cannons]] [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|team]]. 

==Biography==
In [[June]] [[1998]], Ragmar gave a statement to the [[Daily Prophet]] pleading patience from Chudley Cannon fans after his team lost 350-0 in a game against the [[Appleby Arrows]]<ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[31 July]], [[1998]], Page 2</ref>. 

Later, in February 1999, the Daily Prophet took note of Ragmar's delight when the Chudley Cannons drew a match against the [[Caerphilly Catapults]], breaking their 16 game losing streak<ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[8 February]], [[1999]], Page 3 </ref>. 

In June 1999, the Chudley Cannons losing streak was broken when they won a game against the [[Wigtown Wanderers]], and Ragmar told the Daily Prophet that it was "the happiest day of my life"<ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 June]], [[1999]], Page 4</ref>.

In October 1999, the Daily Prophet noted Ragmar "collapsed in shock" after the Chudley Cannons won their second game in a row<ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 October]], [[1999]], Page 2</ref>, against the [[Falmouth Falcons]].
