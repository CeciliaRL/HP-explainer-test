'''Naoki Tsuchiya''' was a [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Naoki is Japanese, and means ''直 ''(nao) "honest, straight" and ''樹 ''(ki) "tree". Tsuchiya is a Japanese surname, meaning "earthen house".

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}}.
*Based on his name, he is most likely of Japanese descent.
