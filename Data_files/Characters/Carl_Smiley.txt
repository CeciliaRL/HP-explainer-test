'''Carl Smiley''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] theatre lighting designer in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:It's Just Heavenly.png}}</ref>

He served as the lighting designer on a production of the musical ''[[It's Just Heavenly]]'' that was staged at the [[Pegasi Theatre]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="poster" />
