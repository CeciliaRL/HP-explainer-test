'''Jody Jacknife''' was a [[Slytherin]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1960s]]. She was [[Quidditch Captain|Captain]] of the [[Slytherin]] [[Quidditch]] [[Slytherin Quidditch team|team]] from [[1960]] to [[1964]].<ref>See [[:File:Slytherin House Captain's Plaque.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref><ref>[http://ab.fanrus.com/SMI/ViP/september/51/213/ ''Harry Potter: The Exhibition'' report] {{Comment|in Russian}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after Jody Jackman, an art department coordinator on every [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' film]], excepting ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone]]''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1227854/ Jody Jackman]" on IMDb</ref>
