'''Abel Treetops''' (fl. [[1832]]) was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] from [[Cincinnati]] who claimed to have patented a method of taming [[Wampus cat]]s for use as guards over wizarding houses. Treetops was exposed as a fraud when [[Magical Congress of the United States of America|MACUSA]] officials raided his home and found him putting [[Engorgement Charm]]s on [[Kneazle]]s.<ref name="FB2017">{{FB|2017}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Treetops appeared to be skilled with the casting of charms, as he successfully used the [[Engorgement Charm]] to enlarge the size of several [[Kneazle]]s to make them appear as [[Wampus cat]]s.<ref name="FB2017"/>
