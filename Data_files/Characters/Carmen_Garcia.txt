'''Carmen García''' was a [[Spain|Spanish]] [[wizardkind|witch]] who played as a [[Chaser]] on the [[Spanish National Quidditch team]] in [[1994]].<ref>{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Garcia's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Toby Lennox (Ravenclaw)|Toby Lennox]] at a [[Quidditch]] challenge.<ref>{{HBP|G}} {{C|NDS edition}}</ref>
