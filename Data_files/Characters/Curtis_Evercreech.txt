'''Curtis Evercreech''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HBPG"/>

==Biography==
He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993–1994 school year]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

Once during the [[1996–1997 school year]], he ate too many [[Cauldron Cake]]s, stating he felt so sick that he couldn't even look at those he had left. However, [[Harry Potter]] offered to take the Cauldron Cakes away for him, though he would not part with them unless he was given five [[Fanged Frisbee]]s.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
"Curtis" is a personal name from an English surname which originally meant "courteous" in Old French.

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console versions]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
