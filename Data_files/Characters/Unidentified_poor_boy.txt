}}This '''poor boy''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] who lived in [[New York]] in the early [[20th century]].

==Biography==
In [[1926]], the boy went to the [[Second Salem Church]], where poor children were fed in exchange for distributing leaflets. While he was waiting in line for soup, the boy asked [[New Salem Philanthropic Society]] leader [[Mary Lou Barebone]] if the birthmark on [[Unidentified boy with birthmark|another boy]]'s face was a "[[Wizardkind|witch]]'s mark," to which she replied that it was not.<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
