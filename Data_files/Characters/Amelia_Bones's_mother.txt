'''Mrs Bones''' was the mother of [[Amelia Bones]] and [[Edgar Bones]]. She was killed, along with her [[Amelia Bones' father|husband]], by [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]] during the [[First Wizarding War]].<ref>[http://www.accio-quote.org/articles/2000/1000-livechat-barnesnoble.html 20 October, 2000 chat with J.K. Rowling] at [http://www.accio-quote.org Accio Quote!]</ref>
==Behind the scenes==
*Mrs Bones presumably had a third child, as her granddaughter, [[Susan Bones]], was the niece of both her daughter Amelia and her son Edgar.
