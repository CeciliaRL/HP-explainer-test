This '''individual''' was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who worked as a receptionist in the foyer of the [[French Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="COGS"/>

==Biography==
In [[September]] [[1927]], she informed [[Queenie Goldstein]] that her sister [[Porpentina Goldstein|Tina]] had not been registered at the Ministry.<ref name="COGS">{{COG|S|51}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
The Receptionist was portrayed by [[Olivia Popica]] in {{COG}}.
