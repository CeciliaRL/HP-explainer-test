'''Wandkeepers''' were [[Wizardkind|wizards]] employed by [[Garrick Ollivander]]<ref>[http://www.houmatoday.com/article/20100621/ENTERTAINMENT/100629957?p=3&tc=pg "Theme park may turn people on to the books" (page 3 of 5)] at [http://www.houmatoday.com/ HoumaToday.com]</ref> to run the various branches of [[Ollivanders]] located throughout [[Wizarding world|wizarding]] [[Great Britain|Britain]], especially [[Hogsmeade]], and to a further extent, the main store in [[Diagon Alley]], whilst the [[wandmaker]] was away.<ref name="WWHP">{{WWHP}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
In order to be eligible for the job title of Wandkeeper, the person in question must have had an expertise in the following:
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Must have been able to cast many charms proficiently, such as the [[Mending Charm]],<ref name="cody">[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_vyeuak78RM&feature=related "Ollivanders Wand Shop June 2nd"] on [http://www.youtube.com/ Youtube].</ref> [[Meteolojinx Recanto|weather-related charms]],<ref name="cody" /> [[Freezing Charm]]<ref name="immobulus">[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LGI7eQ96w8U&feature=related "Ollivanders Wand Shop in The Wizarding World of Harry Potter"] on [http://www.youtube.com/ Youtube].</ref> [[Herbivicus Charm]],<ref name="hayley">[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZx85592Za8&feature=related "Wand Fitting at Ollivander's"] on [http://www.youtube.com/ Youtube].</ref> [[Levitation Charm]],<ref name="cody" /> [[Water-Making Spell]],<ref name="hayley" /> [[Wand-Lighting Charm]],<ref name="cody" /> ''[[Descendo]]''<ref name="hayley" /> and the [[Summoning Charm]].<ref name="Katie">[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QeKepV6rXUM&feature=related "Choosing a Wand at Ollivander's"] on [http://www.youtube.com/ Youtube].</ref>
*'''[[Divination]]''': Must have been able to foresee costumers arriving in the shop.<ref name="cody" />
*'''[[Nonverbal spell|Nonverbal magic]]''': Must have been able to cast nonverbal [[spell]]s smoothly, such as the [[Herbivicus Charm]]<ref name="hayley" /> and [[Freezing Charm]].<ref name="Katie" />
*'''[[Wandlore]]''': Must have been knowledgeable in the complex science and [[magic]] of wandlore.<ref name="wandlore">[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6RPDBmZwzN4&feature=related "Ollivanders wand shop *third day*:: Universal Studios Harry Potter"] on [http://www.youtube.com/ Youtube].</ref>
