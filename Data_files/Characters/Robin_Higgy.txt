'''Robin Higgy''' (b. [[1710s]]/[[1720s]]) was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1730s]]. This individual was [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Slytherin]] [[Quidditch]] [[Slytherin Quidditch team|team]] from [[1732]] to [[1736]].<ref>{{HPPS}}, p. 349 {{see image|File:Slytherin Captains Plaque 2.jpg}}</ref>
