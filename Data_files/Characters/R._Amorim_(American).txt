'''R. Amorim''' was a reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="nyghost-dec7">{{CB}} {{see image|File:The New York Ghost - 7 Dec 1926.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
In the [[7 December]] sunset edition, they co-wrote a report with [[M. Carneirus (American)|M. Carneirus]] and [[C. Williamsum]] about top [[Auror]]s being called to [[New York City Hall]] to investigate the death of [[Henry Shaw Junior]], which was published on the front page.<ref name="nyghost-dec7" />
