'''Luciano Limus''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="dec6sunrise">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:The New York Ghost - 6 Dec 1926 - Sunrise Early Edition.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
He wrote an article about the conviction of a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] entertainer for performing a [[Magic|magical]] juggling act in public that was published in the paper's [[6 December]] [[1926]] morning edition.<ref name="dec6sunrise" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Eduardo Lima]], a graphic artist who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' onward, as well as the [[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film series)|''Fantastic Beasts'' films]].
