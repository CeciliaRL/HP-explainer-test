'''Archibald Bienbon''' was a [[France|French]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and a prominent member of the [[Académie française des sorciers]]. 

Bienbon, along with [[Boniface Toubeau]], was in charge of a committee of experts in charge of the translation of the originally British [[Quidditch]] terms into French.<ref name="QA">''[[Quidditch Through the Ages (real)|Le Quidditch à travers les âges]]''</ref>
