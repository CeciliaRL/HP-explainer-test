'''Arsenia Gonzales''' was an [[United States of America|America]]n [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a [[Chaser]] for the [[American National Quidditch team]], and competed in the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]].

==Biography==
During the play-offs for third place against [[Japan]] on [[9 July]], her and her fellow American Chasers, [[Mercy Wardwell]] and [[Quentin Kowalski]], scored twelve goals. The Japanese Seeker, [[Noriko Sato]], went into a spectacular dive and grabbed the [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]] from under Wardwell's heel, allowing Japan to win with a score of 330-120.
