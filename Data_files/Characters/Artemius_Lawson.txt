'''Artemius Lawson''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was an outspoken advocate for the restraint of [[troll]]s. A quote from the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''<ref name="DP">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]], [[8 February]], [[1999]], Page 1</ref> revealed he thought that they should not be allowed to roam free, stating that they were creatures who weighed a ton, but had brains the size of a bogey.<ref name="wizardsl">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/l.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: L]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon</ref>
