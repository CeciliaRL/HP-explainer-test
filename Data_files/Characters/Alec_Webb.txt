}}'''Alec Webb''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] concert director. He directed a performance of [[Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart|Mozart]]'s ''[[Sinfonia Concertanti]]'' that was held at [[St. Mary's Church Hall]] at 7:30 in the evening on Sunday [[7 May]] in a year prior to [[1997]], which featured [[Emanuel Hurwitz]] on [[violin]], [[Kay Hurwitz]] on [[viola]], and the [[Dollis Chamber Ensemble]].<ref name="hple">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:Emanuel&KayHurwitzConcertPoster.jpg}}</ref> [[Hermione Granger]] had a poster for this concert stuck up on the bulletin board in [[Hermione Granger's bedroom|her bedroom]] in the [[Granger family]]'s [[Grangers' house|house]].<ref name="hple" /><ref name="dhp1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>
