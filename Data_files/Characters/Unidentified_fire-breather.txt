This '''individual''' was a fire-breather in the [[1920s]].

==Biography==
[[File:Circus Arcanus Fire-breather.gif|275px|thumb|left|The Fire-breather with the Circus]]
He was part of the [[Circus Arcanus]], a travelling freak show.<ref name="WU"> {{WU}} "Brilliant Event: Circus Calamitous"</ref>

During the [[2020s]], he appeared as a [[Foundable]] during the [[Calamity]]. Volunteer members of the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]] were able to free him, allowing he to return to his original time.<ref name="WU"> {{WU}} "Brilliant Event: Circus Calamitous"</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*It is unknown if fire-breathing was an ability he was naturally born with, or something he archived through magical means, e.g. through use of the [[Fire-Breathing Potion]].
