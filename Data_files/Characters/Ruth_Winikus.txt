'''Ruth Winikus''' was a [[Gryffindor]] student who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="gobstones">See [[:File:GobstonesClubSignUpSheet.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

==Biography==
Ruth signed up to join the [[Hogwarts Gobstone Club]] on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in [[Gryffindor Tower]].<ref name="gobstones" /> She also registered for a trip to [[Hogsmeade]] on another sign-up sheet posted on the same bulletin board, which was scheduled for Saturday the 15th in an unknown month.<ref name="hogsmeade">See [[:File:HogsmeadeTripSignUpSheet.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is a tribute to [[Ruth Winick]], who worked as a graphic artist on ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Philosopher's Stone]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)|Chamber of Secrets]]'', and as a draughtsman on ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0935101/ Ruth Winick]" on IMDb</ref>
