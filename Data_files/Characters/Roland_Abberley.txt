'''Roland Abberley''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HBPG"/>

==Biography==
He owned a pair of [[dragon-hide gloves]], which were stolen by [[Vincent Crabbe]] and [[Gregory Goyle]] shortly before the [[Christmas holidays]] in [[1996]]. [[Harry Potter]] recovered these gloves by [[duelling]] with Crabbe and Goyle, and, as a reward, Roland gave them to Harry. Harry went on to give the gloves to [[Fred and George Weasley]] as a [[Christmas]] present.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} - NDS version</ref>

==Etymology==
*"Roland" was a French hero celebrated in medieval chansons de geste as the nephew of Charlemagne and defender of Christianity who was killed in battle against the Saracens at Roncesvalles in A.D. 778.{{Fact}}
*"Abberley" is an English surname.<ref name="name">[http://www.searchforancestors.com/surnames/origin/a/abberley.php Abberley Surname Origin & Family History - Ancestor Search]</ref>
