This '''[[centaur]]''' lived in the [[Forbidden Forest]] in the [[2000s]].<ref name="MA19"/>

==Biography==
On [[2 September]], [[2008]], he was attacked by two [[Wizardkind|wizards or witches]] when they were found by [[Ivy Warrington]] and [[Unidentified 2000s Hogwarts student|another student]], who helped him get rid of the attackers. Later, he returned the favours by aiding a [[Attack in the Forbidden Forest|battle]] between them and a [[Norwegian Ridgeback]]. After they won, the centaur warned the students of the perils in the [[Forbidden Forest]] and advised them to be brave and honest.<ref name="MA19"/>
