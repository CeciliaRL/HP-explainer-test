'''Lancaster Pyke''', also known as '''Magic Joe'''<ref name="poster" />, was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[United States of America]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="CinemaBlend">"[http://www.cinemablend.com/news/1554929/10-fantastic-things-we-learned-on-the-fantastic-beasts-set?story_page=4 10 Fantastic Things We Learned On The Fantastic Beasts Set]" from CinemaBlend</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Pyke offering 4850 [[dragot]]s for is arrest, which described him as "older" and stated that he was wanted for "embezzlement of [[spell]]s and [[Muggle|No-Maj]] murder".<ref name="poster" />
