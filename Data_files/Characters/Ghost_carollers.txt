The '''ghost carollers''' were a choir of [[ghost]]s at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] that sang Christmas carols at winter. Among their members was [[Lord Draben]]. They sang ''[[Christmas at Hogwarts]]'' in the [[Entrance Hall]] in [[December]], [[1991]].<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}}, Chapter 21</ref>
