'''Tristan''' was an ancient [[Celts|Celtic]] hero who tried to kill the [[giant]] [[Morholt]]. He was wounded with a [[poison]]ed sword by the giant in the process.<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Tristan was one of the main characters of the Celtic legend ''Tristan and Iseult''. He was a [[Knights of the Round Table|Knight of the Round Table]] and nephew of King Mark of Cornwall.<ref>[[Wikipedia:Tristan|Tristan]] at [[Wikipedia:Main Page|Wikipedia]]</ref>
