[[Merwyn Finwick]] was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the [[Keeper]] for the [[Tutshill Tornados]].<ref>"[http://www.hplex.info/quidditch/q_teams_britain.html#Tutshill Teams of the World: Great Britain and Ireland]" on The Harry Potter Lexicon</ref> [[Brevis Birch]], the team's [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|captain]], blamed their unexpected loss in a match against the [[Ballycastle Bats]] on a bout of [[narcolepsy]] suffered by Merwyn.<ref name="wizardsb">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/b.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: B]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon</ref> The Tornados had been at the top of the [[British and Irish Quidditch League|league]] before this defeat.<ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[8 February]], [[1999]], Page 3</ref>
