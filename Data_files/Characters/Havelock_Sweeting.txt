'''Havelock Sweeting''' ([[1634]]–[[1710]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Magizoologist]] well-known for being an expert on [[Unicorn]]s. He started many [[Unicorn reservation]]s in [[Great Britain]]. For his dedication to Unicorns, he was immortalised on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Care of Magical Creatures]]''': Sweeting was highly talented with communicating with and understanding [[Unicorn]], powerful [[magic]]al [[beast]]s for which only certain individuals can tame, establishing numerous [[Unicorn reservation|reservations]] for these creatures throughout [[Great Britain]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref> His gift with Unicorns was also very impressive and distinctive for a wizard, as Unicorns usually prefer the touch of a witch.<ref name="FBR">{{FB|R}}</ref>
