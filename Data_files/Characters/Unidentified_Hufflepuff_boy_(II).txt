This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was a [[Hufflepuff]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="GOFF"/>

==Biography==
{{Quote|He's a cheat|The boy blames [[Harry Potter]]|Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)}}He was a great supporter of [[Cedric Diggory]] and yelled his surname when Cedric was chosen as champion in the [[Triwizard Tournament]] in [[1994]]. Along with [[Hogwarts student that blames Harry Potter|another student]] he blamed [[Harry Potter]] for being chosen as the fourth champion and for being a cheat.<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref>
