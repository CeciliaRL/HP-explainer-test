'''M. Carneirus''' was a business and finance reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="dec1">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:NewYorkGhost-1December1927.jpg}}</ref><ref name="nyghost-dec6">{{WWMM|1}} {{see image|File:NewYorkGhost-6December1926.jpg}}</ref><ref name="dec6sunrise">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:The New York Ghost - 6 Dec 1926 - Sunrise Early Edition.png}}</ref><ref name="nyghost-dec7">{{CB}} {{see image|File:The New York Ghost - 7 Dec 1926.png}}</ref> They also sometimes contributed to general news stories.<ref name="nyghost-dec7" />

==History==
===Business reporting===
Carneirus wrote an article about the "medical modes" of famous, wealthy people, which was published in the [[1 December]] [[1926]] issue.<ref name="dec1" /> Five days later, in the [[6 December]] sunrise edition, they wrote an article about whether record sales of non-[[magic]] [[broomstick]]s over [[Hallowe'en]] represented an investment opportunity.<ref name="dec6sunrise" /> An article by them about how low [[potion]]s imports were impacting the value of the [[Dragot]] was featured in the 6 December sunset edition.<ref name="nyghost-dec6" /> This was followed by a report by them in the [[7 December]] sunset edition about how fear generated by the recent magical attacks in [[New York]] was negatively impacting [[WizTour]].<ref name="nyghost-dec7" />

===General reporting===
Carneirus co-wrote a story with [[C. Williamsum]] and [[R. Amorim (American)|R. Amorim]] about top [[Auror]]s being called to [[New York City Hall]] to investigate the death of [[Henry Shaw Junior]] that was featured on the front page of the 7 December sunset edition of the ''Ghost''.<ref name="nyghost-dec7" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after [[Mauricio Carneiro (real-world)|Mauricio Carneiro]], who worked as a costume illustrator for every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]'' onward, and as a costumer for ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Order of the Phoenix]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Half-Blood Prince]]''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1769188/ Mauricio Carneiro]" on IMDb</ref>
