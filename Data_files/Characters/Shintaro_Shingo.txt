'''Shintaro Shingo'''<ref name="LB">{{PM}} - [[PLACE YOUR BETS WITH LUDO BAGMAN]] (Archived)</ref> was a Japanese wizard who played as a [[Beater]] for the [[2014]] [[Japanese National Quidditch team]]. During the seventh round match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], against [[Polish National Quidditch team|Poland]], Shingo, along with fellow Beater [[Masaki Hongo|Hongo]], was largely responsible for Japan's decisive victory by preventing Poland's [[Seeker]], Wladyslaw Wolfke from catching the Snitch.<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[JAPAN VERSUS POLAND]] (Archived)</ref>
