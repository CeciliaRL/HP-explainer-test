This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' was a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] at least during the [[1997-1998 school year]]. She participated in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] and helped a [[Unidentified Hogwarts employees#Black Hogwarts witch at the Viaduct Courtyard before the Battle of Hogwarts|teacher]] to go to the [[Viaduct Courtyard]]. It is unknown if she survived.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>
