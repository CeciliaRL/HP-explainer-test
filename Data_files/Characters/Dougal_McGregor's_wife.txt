'''Mrs McGregor''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] woman who resided in [[Caithness]], [[Scotland]]. She was the daughter of a local farmer, and married the son of [[Dougal McGregor's father|another farmer]], [[Dougal McGregor]].<ref name="pottermore">{{PM}} biography of [[Minerva McGonagall]]  (transcription available [http://forums.darklordpotter.net/showpost.php?s=4a2e751bddc00f9618a0b1bb6d9ce6fc&p=481284&postcount=443 here])</ref>

[[Dougal McGregor]] and his wife were murdered in a random anti-[[Non-magic people|Muggle]] attack by the Death Eaters during the height of the [[First Wizarding War]] in the [[1970s]], along with their [[Dougal McGregor's children|children]].<ref name="shortstories">[[Pottermore Presents]] - ''[[Short Stories from Hogwarts of Heroism, Hardship and Dangerous Hobbies]]'', Chapter 1 (''Minerva McGonagall'')</ref>
