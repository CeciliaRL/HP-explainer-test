This '''individual''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[London]]er who was present during the collapse of the [[Millennium Bridge]] in [[1996]].<ref name="HPLE"/>

==Biography==
This individual was crossing the bridge with his pet puggle [[Sam (dog)|Sam]] when the collapse took place. When both were thrown down into [[River Thames]], this individual was left unconscious. Sam, then dragged him as he swam half a mile down the river, to safety. ''[[The Guardian]]'' later went on to report that Sam was to become the first dog ever to be honoured with a medal for gallantry.<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:TheGuardian.png|this image]])</ref>
