'''Lucy Hurst''' was a [[Gryffindor]]<ref name="house" /> [[wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
During the [[1996–1997 school year]], she competed in the school [[Exploding Snap]] tournament, and also played informal [[Quidditch]] matches with other students.

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
