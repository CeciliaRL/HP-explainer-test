This '''individual''' (fl. [[1997]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was visiting the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] during the [[Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic]], on [[2 September]], [[1997]]. He wore a black cloak and hat, and had a big white curled moustache. He was rudely pushed aside by [[Corban Yaxley|Yaxley]] who was preventing [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley]] and [[Hermione Granger]] to leave the Ministry.<ref>{{DH|F1}} - Chapter 13 (''Lifting the Locket'')</ref>
