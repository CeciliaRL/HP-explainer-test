'''Dr Filibuster''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] that created the [[Dr Filibuster's Fabulous Wet-Start, No-Heat Fireworks|Fabulous Wet-Start, No-Heat Fireworks]], which the [[Fred and George Weasley|Weasley twins]] were known to use, or at least purchase, from time-to-time.<ref>{{COS|B|4}}</ref> [[Harry Potter]] once predicted that the Weasley twins' [[Explosive Enterprises|line of fireworks]] would put Filibuster out of business, though whether this actually happened or not is unknown.<ref name="OOTP28">{{OOTP|B|28}}</ref>
