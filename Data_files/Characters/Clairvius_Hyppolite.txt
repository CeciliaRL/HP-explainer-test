'''Clairvius Hyppolite''' was a [[Haiti]]an [[wizardkind|wizard]] who played as a [[Chaser]] for the [[2014]] [[Haitian National Quidditch team]].

==Biography==
During the third match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], Haiti versus [[Brazilian National Quidditch team|Brazil]], Hyppolite scored eight of the total nine of his team's goals. Hyppolite's performance, however, did not stop the team from being disqualified after [[Beater]] [[Jean-Baptiste Bloncourt]] [[Snitchnip|illegally captured]] the [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[BRAZIL VERSUS HAITI]] (Archived)</ref>
