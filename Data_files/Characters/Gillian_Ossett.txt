'''Gillian Ossett''' was a [[Hufflepuff]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
As a lover of [[Wizard's Chess]], she collected chess pieces, and traded her extra ones with other students for various objects. She could usually be found in the [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] [[Classroom 3C|classroom]] during the [[1996-1997 school year]].
