This female '''[[Chimaera]]''' was owned by [[Professor]] [[Silvanus Kettleburn]] during the [[1980s]].<ref name="HM41"/>

==Biography==
[[Professor]] [[Silvanus Kettleburn]] brought this Chimaera from [[Greece]] to the [[Hogwarts Castle|Hogwarts grounds]] in the summer of [[1987]].<ref name="ITHLD">{{HM}}, Year ?, Side Quest "Into the Hybrid Lion's Den"</ref>

During the [[1987–1988 school year]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], Professor Silvanus Kettleburn repeatedly said that his Chimaera had escaped and was roaming the castle unleashed.<ref name="HM41"/> The Chimaera was still loose at the beginning of the [[1988–1989 school year|following school year]].<ref name="Prefect">{{HM|5|Yer a Prefect!}}</ref> [[Jacob's sibling]] found her in the [[Forbidden Forest]], but was unable to subdue her with magic. [[Liz Tuttle]] managed to calm her down.<ref name="HM58">{{HM|5|8}}</ref>

Later during the same school year, Jacob's sibling battled this Chimaera again to impress [[Peeves]]. Kettleburn arrived with Liz and [[Barnaby Lee|Barnaby]] before Jacob's sibling lost control.<ref name="HM522">{{HM|5|22}}</ref>
