'''Lukas Karuzos''' was the author of ''[[New Theory of Numerology|A New Theory of Numerology]]'',<ref name="HPLEG">{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:ANewTheoryOfNumerology.png|this image]])</ref> and thus presumably an accomplished [[Numerology|Numerologist]], as [[Arithmancy (class)|Arithmancy]] student [[Hermione Granger]] anticipated receiving a copy of his book.

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably named after Luca Caruso, the son of [[Miraphora Mina]], a graphic artist who worked on all eight [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref name="talkswith">Rick Gerardy, ''Talks with Emanuel Hurwitz: 82 Years with the Violi'', Page 109</ref>
