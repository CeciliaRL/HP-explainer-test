'''Ignis Nagy''' was a [[Hungary|Hungarian]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who had dedicated her life to [[Dragonologist|the study of dragons]], having authored at least [[From Egg to Inferno: a Dragon-Keeper's Guide|one book]] on the subject.<ref name="WUDragon">{{WU}} (see [https://wizardsunitehub.info/profession-skill/Dragon-Lady/ this])</ref> A controversial figure, as a scholar, she was noted for her keen interest in the elemental force of [[fire]], often studying it and its magical qualities up close.<ref name="WUFire">{{WU}} (see [https://wizardsunitehub.info/profession-skill/into-the-fire/ this])</ref>

==Biography==
In the [[2010s]], Ignis Nagy encountered and spoke with members of the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]], who were pursuing the careers of [[Auror]] and [[Magizoologist]] respectively. With the former, she helped the individual in question to embrace the study of flames, and with the latter, she had a brief chat on the subject that inspired said person to re-read her work, and both of these meetings ended up being useful in their field work.<ref name="WUDragon"/><ref name="WUFire"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Magizoology]]''': As a professional Dragonologist, she was highly knowledgeable about [[dragon]]s. She was known to have authored [[From Egg to Inferno: a Dragon-Keeper's Guide|one book]] on the subject, showing her expertise in her field.<ref name="WUDragon"/>
*'''Knowledge of Elemental [[Magic]]''': Nagy was an expert on the magical properties of [[fire]], with her excellent knowledge of this field allowing her to teach [[Auror]] of the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]] how to withstand flames, and increase their stamina.<ref name="WUFire"/>
