This '''individual''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who ran the [[Trading Card Shop]] in [[Diagon Alley]], in the early [[1990s]].

In the summer of [[1992]], he and [[Derek (Wizard Card Collectors' Club)|Derek]] had a brief conversation with [[Harry Potter]] regarding the new card combinations that had been put into circulation that summer.<ref>{{COS|G}} - GBC version</ref>
