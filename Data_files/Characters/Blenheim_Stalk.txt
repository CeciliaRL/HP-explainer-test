'''Blenheim Stalk''' (b. [[1920]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and author who was a well-known expert on [[Non-magic people|Muggles]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
Blenheim was born in [[1920]].<ref name="COSG"/> He became an expert on [[Non-magic people|Muggles]] and authored many books on the subject of them, including ''[[Muggles Who Notice]]'', a book published in [[1972]].<ref name="PM"/> The book references the [[Ilfracombe Incident]] and the story of [[Dirk|"Dodgy" Dirk]].<ref name="FBR">{{FB|R}}</ref>

By the 1990s, he was featured on a famous Wizard Card and was featured wearing a [[Blenheim Stalk's pocket watch|golden pocket watch]].<ref name="COSG"/>

He was awarded recognition for his noteworthy and valuable research into [[Muggle Studies]] in the [[Famous Witches and Wizards Cards]] set, on a silver [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref name="COSG"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Muggle Studies]]''': Stalk was an expert on [[Non-magic people|Muggles]] and their ways of living.<ref name="COSG"/> He authored many books on the subject of Muggles, including the book [[Muggles Who Notice]], which discusses the rare cases of Muggles who witness [[magic]] and the [[wizarding world]], showing he was very knowledgeable about his subject.<ref name="PM"/>
