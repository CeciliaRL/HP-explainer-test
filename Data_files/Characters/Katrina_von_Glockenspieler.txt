'''Katrina von Glockenspieler''' was a [[Germany|German]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as a [[Beater]] on the [[German National Quidditch team]] during the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup|422nd Quidditch World Cup]], in [[1994]]. She was also the team's [[Captain]].<ref>{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], von Glockenspieler's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Dean Thomas]] at a [[duelling]] challenge.<ref>{{HBP|G}} {{C|NDS edition}}</ref>
