'''Joe Laurie'''<ref>Closed captions on the DVD/Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was reported missing on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' wireless programme in [[1997]]. His disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Daniel Laurie, who worked as an ADR editor on ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire]]'', ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'', and ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince]]'', and as an ADR supervisor on {{DH|F1}} and {{DH|F2}}.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0491392/ Daniel Laurie's profile on IMDb]</ref>
