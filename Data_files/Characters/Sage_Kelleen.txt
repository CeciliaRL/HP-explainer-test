'''Sage Kelleen''' was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. Sage once signed up for [[Quidditch]] training at 7pm on Thursday on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in the [[Gryffindor Common Room]].<ref>{{See image|File:QuidditchTrainingSignupSheet.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Sage" comes from the English word sage, which denotes either a type of spice or else a wise person.
