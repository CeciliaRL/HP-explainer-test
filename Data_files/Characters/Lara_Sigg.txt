'''Lara Sigg''' was a [[magic]]al affairs correspondent for an unidentified [[German]]-language [[Unidentified German wizarding newspaper|wizarding newspaper]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="fb-paper">"[http://mashable.com/2016/12/02/fantastic-beasts-opening-harry-potter-easter-eggs All the 'Harry Potter' Easter eggs you missed in the 'Fantastic Beasts' opening]" from Mashable {{see image|File:GermanWizardingNewspaper.jpg}}</ref>

==Biography==
She wrote an article about how a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] family was left stranded when a pretzel serving as a [[Portkey]] was accidentally eaten that was published in a [[1926]] issue of the newspaper.<ref name="fb-paper" />
