'''Thomas Fillman''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] author in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:WhenBroadwaySleeps.jpg}}</ref>

One of his books was adapted into the comedy play ''[[When Broadway Sleeps]]''.<ref name="poster" /> This play was staged at the [[Glovers Comedy Theatre]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="poster" />
