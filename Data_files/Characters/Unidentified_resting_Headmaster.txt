This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was appointed [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] before [[Armando Dippet|Professor Dippet]].

==Biography==
This wizard became the [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] at an unknown point in history, although it is known his tenure was before that of [[Armando Dippet]]. After his [[death]], his [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head.<ref>{{DH|F2}} (''The Prince's Tale'')</ref> At the top of his golden frame is printed ''"Seraphini Cherubini Throni"'' which translates roughly as ''"angelic cherub throne"''.<ref>{{MHP}} - {{See image|File:Unidentified_Resting_Headmaster_with_frame.png}}</ref>

A portrait also hung in the [[Lower Chambers Portrait Corridor]] next to a [[Portrait of a bearded man holding a book]] in [[Hogwarts Castle]] during the [[1993–1994 school year]]. He was portrayed to have been resting in an armchair.<ref name="POAF">{{POA|F}}</ref>
