This '''individual''' was a [[Dark Wizard|Dark]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and, in the [[Second Wizarding War]], one among the [[Death Eaters]] faithful to [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]].

==Biography==
This Death Eater, along with [[Unidentified Male Death Eater (VIII)|another Death Eater]], [[Raid on the Hogwarts Express|raided]] the [[Hogwarts Express]] on [[1 September]], [[1997]], to search the train for [[Harry Potter]]. They were both disappointed to find he was absent, with [[Neville Longbottom]] insulting them as "losers".<ref>{{DH|F1}}</ref>

This Death Eater was at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] as [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley]], [[Hermione Granger]] and [[Griphook]] staged a [[Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)|break-in]] to retrieve [[Helga Hufflepuff's Cup]] from the [[Lestrange Vault]].

Later that day, he was [[Killing Curse|killed]] by [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] in a fit of rage at [[Malfoy Manor]], after the [[Dark Wizard|Dark Lord]] was informed by [[Goblin]]s working at Gringotts that [[Harry Potter]] had managed to steal [[Helga Hufflepuff's Cup]] from him.<ref>{{DH|F2}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] was portrayed by [[Simon Grover]] in {{DH|F1}} and ''[[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2|Part 2]]''.
