'''Ngapo Ponika''' (born c. [[1993]]) was a wizard from New Zealand who was the [[Seeker]] for the [[2014]] [[New Zealand National Quidditch team]]. During the first round match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], against [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team|Bulgaria]], Ponika was beaten to the [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]] by [[Viktor Krum]].<ref name="PM">[[BULGARIA VERSUS NEW ZEALAND]] (Archived from {{PM}})</ref>
