This '''merman''' was an individual [[Merpeople|Merperson]] who was active during the [[Triwizard Tournament]] in [[1994]].<ref name="GOF26"/>

==Biography==
During the Second Task, [[Harry Potter]] tried to borrow his spear to cut the ropes binding the hostages, but the merman refused, laughing as he yanked the spear back from Harry.<ref name="GOF26">{{GOF|B|26}}</ref> It is unknown if he was present at [[Funeral of Albus Dumbledore|Dumbledore's funeral]] in [[1997]], along with the rest of his colony.<ref name="HBP30">{{HBP|B|30}}</ref>

==Physical appearance==
{{Quote|...he swam swiftly toward a seven-foot tall merman with a long green beard and a choker of shark fangs...|[[Harry Potter]]'s description on the merman|Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire}}
The merman was large, rising to almost seven feet tall. He bore a choker of [[shark fang]]s around his neck and had a long green beard.<ref name="GOF26"/>

==Personality and traits==
Despite he was unhelpful to [[Harry Potter]] when he tried to have access to his spear, he was loyal, obeying the orders to not aid any of the champions during the task.<ref name="GOF26"/>
