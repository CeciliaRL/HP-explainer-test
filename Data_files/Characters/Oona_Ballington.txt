'''Oona Ballington''' (b. [[1680s]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1700s]]. She was once [[Quidditch Captain|Captain]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]] from [[1700]] to [[1704]].<ref>{{HPE}} {{See image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Plaque.jpg}} and {{see image|File:Gryffindor Captains Plaque.jpg}}</ref>
