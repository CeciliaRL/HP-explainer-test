This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who fought during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]]. She led some students to the [[First-floor corridor]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref> It is unknown if she survived.

==Behind the scenes==
*This character may be [[Septima Vector]], [[Irma Pince]], [[Bathsheda Babbling]], [[Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank]] or another as yet unidentified professor. Alternatively, she may be the teacher of [[Advanced Arithmancy Studies]], [[Ancient Studies]], [[Music]], [[Muggle Music]], [[Art]], [[Muggle Art]], [[Ghoul Studies]] or [[Alchemy (class)|Alchemy]]. Similarly she could have been one of the members of the [[Order of the Phoenix]].
