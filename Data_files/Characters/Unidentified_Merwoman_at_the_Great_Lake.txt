This '''merwoman''' was an individual [[Merpeople|Merperson]] that lived in the [[Great Lake Merpeople colony]].<ref name="HM637"/>

==Biography==
The merwoman [[Skirmish in the Great Lake|fought]] [[Jacob's sibling]] when they entered the [[Great Lake|Black Lake]] for the first time.<ref name="HM637"/> When the [[Weird Sisters]] were holding a [[Weird Sisters concert at the Great Lake|concert]] near the Lake, she surfaced and listened in joy. When Jacob's sibling entered the Lake for a second time, she led them into the [[Merpeople village]] and introduced them to the [[Merqueen]].<ref name="HM638">{{HM|6|38}}</ref>
