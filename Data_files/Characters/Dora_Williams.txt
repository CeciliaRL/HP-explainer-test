'''Dora Williams''' was a [[Hufflepuff]]<ref name="house" /> student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. 

==Biography==
She competed in the school [[Exploding Snap]] tournament during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
Dora is a short form of Dorothy, Isidora, Theodora or other names containing "dora". "Williams" is a Welsh surname, which means "son of William".
