'''Blossom Degrasse''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]], who was a famous [[herbologist]], author and [[Potioneer|potion master]].<ref name="WU">{{WU}}</ref>

==Biography==
In late [[2010s]], she released the book "''[[A Humble Guide for Potion Brewing You Cannot Live Without]]''".<ref name="WU"/>

At some point during [[Calamity]], she met a member of the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]] and lauded their role in fighting the Calamity. She also sent a request for [[dragon-hide gloves]] to the SOS Task Force.<ref name="WU"/>
