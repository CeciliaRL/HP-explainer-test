'''Daphne Mauboisan''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who lived nearby [[Armando Dippet]] in the early [[1990s]]. On [[1 September]], [[1992]], when [[Harry Potter]] and [[Ronald Weasley]] flew a [[Flying Ford Anglia|Ford Anglia]] to [[Hogwarts Castle]] (a breach of the [[International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy]]), Mauboisan, along with [[Leonora Gore]], blamed elderly [[Armando Dippet]], who was known for his erratic flying.<ref name="COSF">See [[:File:Flying Ford Anglia Mystifies Muggles.png|this image]].</ref>
