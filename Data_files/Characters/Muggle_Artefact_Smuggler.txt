This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' smuggled [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] artefacts, [[Charm|enchanted]] them and sold them back to Muggles during the [[1990–1991 school year]].<ref name="HM712A1"/>

==Biography== 
During the [[1990–1991 school year]], this wizard sold a [[cursed kettle]] to an oblivious [[Unidentified Muggle witness|Muggle]]. The wizard was seen by a [[Unidentified witness (II)|witness]] on the [[Charing Cross Road]] and also outside the [[Leaky Cauldron]].<ref name="HM712A1"/>

[[Jacob's sibling]] helped investigate and track down the wizard as a part of the [[Ministry of Magic student programme]], and at the end of the assignment, [[Alastor Moody]] reported that he would soon be apprehended.<ref name="HM712A1"/>
