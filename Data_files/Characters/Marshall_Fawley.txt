'''Marshall Fawley''' (died c. [[1997]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|wizard]], the husband of [[Lucretia Fawley]], and the father of [[Grim Fawley]].

Mr Fawley and his wife were both killed during the height of the [[Second Wizarding War]], while their son Grim was a student at Hogwarts.<ref name="WU">{{WU}} (see [https://wizardsunite.gamepress.gg/guide/mysteries-chapter-2-grim-fawley-background this])</ref>
