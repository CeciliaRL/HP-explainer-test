'''Edwardus Lima''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the author of the [[magizoology]] tome ''[[The Monster Book of Monsters]]''.<ref name="hppts">{{HPPS}}, p. 505 {{see image|File:MonsterBookConceptArt1.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Eduardo Lima]], a graphic artist who has worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] since ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]'', on concept art of ''[[The Monster Book of Monsters]]'' by his colleague, [[Miraphora Mina]].<ref name="hppts" />
