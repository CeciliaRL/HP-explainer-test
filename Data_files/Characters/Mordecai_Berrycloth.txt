'''Mordecai Berrycloth''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] employed by the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] in [[London]] in the capacity of [[Auror]],<ref name="WU">{{WU}} ([https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hCIfSWgMX-c see video])</ref> under head of [[Auror Office|department]], [[Harry Potter]].

==Biography==
In the [[2010s]], Mordecai Berrycloth visited the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]], where he held a course in [[Martial magic|combative magic]] for their field members; [[Magizoologist]] members of the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]] were also in attendance.<ref name="WU" />
