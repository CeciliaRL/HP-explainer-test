'''Lina Tande''' (born c. [[1982]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Hufflepuff]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She allowed [[Harry Potter]] to borrow her [[Cleansweep Broom Company|Cleansweep]] in exchange for two [[Wizard's Chess|chess]] pieces in [[1996]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Lina" means either "palm tree" or "tender" in Arabic, and "absorbed, united" in Sanskrit. Tande nickname for someone with prominent or otherwise remarkable teeth, from tand 'tooth'.

==Behind the scenes==
*Based on her name, it can be presumed that she is of Indian descent.
