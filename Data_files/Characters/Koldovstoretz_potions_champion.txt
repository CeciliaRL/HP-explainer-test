This '''individual''' was a [[Russia]]n [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Koldovstoretz]], in [[Russia]].<ref name="WBP"/>

A skilled potioneer, the individual was elected to represent the Koldovstoretz at the [[Wizarding Schools Potions Championship]], in which he competed against students from [[Mahoutokoro School of Magic]], [[Uagadou School of Magic]] and [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="WBP"/>

==Biography==
===Koldovstoretz===
The Russian wizard attended [[Koldovstoretz]] for his magical education.<ref name="WBP"/>

===Wizarding Schools Potions Championship===
[[File:Redcapbattlefield.png|thumb|300px|The wizard enters the battlefield]]The wizard became a skilled potioneer, and at age 17 or above competed in the [[Wizarding Schools Potions Championship]] for the golden cauldron, where he represented Koldovstoretz, his school. In the championship, the wizard arrived at the battlefield first, and attempted to cross. However, he disturbed the [[Red Cap]]s, who knocked him out unconscious. Afterwards, the [[Hogwarts potions champion]] used a [[Beautification Potion]] to scare the Red Caps away.<ref name="WBP"/>

==Physical appearance==
The student had light skin, with dark hair and chiselled features. He was thin, and wore purple robes with white fur lining and a white fur hat.<ref name="WBP"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': As a skilled [[potioneer]], he was highly talented at making potions.<ref name="WBP">{{WBP}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This is the first and, thus far, only student of Koldovstoretz introduced throughout the ''Harry Potter'' series.
