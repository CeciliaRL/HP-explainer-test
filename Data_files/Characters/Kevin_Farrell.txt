'''Kevin Farrell''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who started attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[2008]] and was sorted into [[Gryffindor]].<ref name="MA">{{MA}} ([https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=mHjRnuLXayg see video])</ref>{{MATimeRef}}

==Biography==
Kevin began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] at the age of eleven. He spoke to [[Ivy Warrington]] in a [[First year|first-year]] boat on the way up to the school, and thought she was nice. He told [[Minerva McGonagall|Professor McGonagall]] at the [[Sorting ceremony]] how he saw and spoke to her when they found out she had vanished. Kevin was sorted into [[Gryffindor]].<ref name="MA"/>

Kevin later helped a student and fellow Gryffindor, [[Robyn Thistlethwaite]] search for Ivy. He thought it was very easy to get lost in the school, and they had to try not to get lost too. He asked a [[portrait]] if he had seen Ivy pass through the [[Grand Staircase]], but he hadn't and warned Kevin he would tell [[Argus Filch|Mr Filch]] he saw the group.<ref name="MA"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Dark Arts]]''': Kevin once swiftly used the [[Jelly-Legs Curse]] to tackle an aggressive [[Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington's doorman|security troll]], showing he had a level of skill with [[dark charm]]s.<ref name="MA"/>
