'''Johan Hoffman''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and professional [[Seer]], who made a [[prophecy]] in [[January]] of [[1665]]. His prophecy was stored in the [[Hall of Prophecy]] in the [[Department of Mysteries]] of the [[British Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*He may have been the [[Unidentified male Seer]] whose prophecy was knocked over by [[Bellatrix Lestrange]] and [[Lucius Malfoy]] in the Hall of Prophecy.
