This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Hufflepuff]] in the [[1990s]]. He fought during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] in [[1998]] and carried the injured Hogwarts-protectors. It is unknown if he survived.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>
