'''Kelly Whakkaarangapawarau''' was an [[Australia]]n [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as a [[Beater]] on the [[Australian National Quidditch team]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996â€“1997 school year]], Whakkaarangapawarau's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[James Tuckett (II)|James Tuckett]] at a [[Quidditch]] challenge.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} {{C|NDS edition}}</ref>

==Etymology==
''Kelly'' is a given name from the [[Ireland|Irish]] surname meaning "war".<ref>[http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/kelly Dictionary.com]</ref>

''Wakaaranga'' is a Maori word literally meaning "the resting place of a canoe."<ref>[http://www.newzealandeducated.com/int/en/institution/604 New Zealand Educated]</ref>

''Pawarau'' as a term is most closely related to the Pawar clan in India.<ref>[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pawar Wikipedia]</ref>
