This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1910s]] who was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] in [[Gryffindor]] house.<ref name="COGS">{{COG|S}}</ref>

==Biography==

Sometime in the [[1910s]], whilst attending Hogwarts, she and her [[Unidentified Gryffindor girl 2 (1910's)|friend]] spitefully gossiped about [[Leta Lestrange]] about being an unwanted child and for the [[death]] of her [[Corvus Lestrange (V)|half-brother]], before her friend got hit with the [[dark charm]] ''[[Oscausi]]'' by Leta out of retaliation, sealing her mouth shut.<ref name="COGS"/>
