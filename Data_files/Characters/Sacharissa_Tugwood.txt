'''Sacharissa Tugwood''' ([[1874]] â€“ [[1966]]) was a famous [[Potioneer]], and the first [[Wizardkind|witch]] to use [[Beautification Potion|beauty]] [[potion]]s, having developed quite a few of them herself.<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref><ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
Tugwood was born in [[1874]]. She presumably attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] (c. 1885- 1893) where she would have excelled in [[Potions (class)|Potions]].

===Career as Potioneer===
In her adulthood, Tugwood would develop many [[Beautification Potion|cosmetic potions]]. She became famous for being the first [[Wizardkind|witch]] to use such a potion. Sacharissa also discovered the pimple-curing properties of [[Bubotuber pus]].<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref><ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

===Death and legacy===
Tugwood would eventually die in [[1966]], aged ninety-two. Her gravestone credited her to have made of the world a "''more beautiful place''". She was also featured on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref><ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Tugwood was a highly accomplished and talented professional [[Potioneer]], having successfully invented numerous [[Beautification Potion]]s, cosmetic concoctions which enhance the beauty of the drinker, an achievement she was immortalised on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] for, showing her proficiency in this branch of both [[magic]] and [[science]].
*'''[[Herbology]]''': Tugwood also discovered the pimple curing properties of [[Bubotuber pus]], a natural substance extracted from the magical [[Bubotuber]] plant, an achievement in the field of Herbology, meaning she was knowledgeable of magical plants and their usages.

==Etymology==
The name Sacharissa may be related to the verb [http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/saccharise saccharize] which means to convert into sugar.
