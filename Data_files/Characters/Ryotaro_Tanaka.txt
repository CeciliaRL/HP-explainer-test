'''Ryotaro Tanaka''' was a Japanese [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was the [[Japanese National Quidditch team]]'s captain during the four-hundred and twenty-second [[Quidditch World Cup]], in [[1994]]. He played as a [[Chaser]] and was the team's Captain.

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Tanaka's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Scott Ancrum]] at an [[Exploding Snap]] challenge.<ref>{{HBP|G}} {{C|NDS edition}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Flight|Flying]]''': As a former professional [[Chaser]] of the [[Japanese National Quidditch team]], he is a proficient flyer.
