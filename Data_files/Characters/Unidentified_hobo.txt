}}This '''hobo''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Muggle|No-Maj]] man living on the streets of [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref> 

==History==
He was one of the witnesses when some of [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]]'s creatures escaped from his [[Newton Scamander's suitcase|briefcase]] that year.<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

When a [[NYPD Police Officer No. 743|policeman]] asked questions about the situation, the drunken hobo was about to tell about what he really saw while [[Unidentified housewife|another witness]] thought it was gas explosion, his memories were [[Memory Charm|obliviated]] by Newt leaving him and the crowd to agree that it was actually gas.<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>
