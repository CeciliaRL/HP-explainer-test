'''Shizuka Watanabe''' was a [[Japan]]ese [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as [[Seeker]] on the [[Japanese National Quidditch team]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Watanabe's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Dora Williams]] at an [[Exploding Snap]] challenge.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} {{c|NDS edition}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Shizuka" is from Japanese 静 (shizu) "quiet" combined with 夏 (ka) "summer" or 香 (ka) "smell, perfume". However, it can also simply mean, "quiet", from the adjective 静か(Shizuka).
