'''Jason Charmer''' (b. [[1700s]]) was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1720s]]. He was once [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]] from [[1720]] to [[1724]].<ref>{{HPE}} {{See image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Plaque.jpg}}</ref>
