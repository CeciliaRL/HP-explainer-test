This '''individual''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] who worked as a guard at the central [[Steen National Bank]] in the early [[20th century]].

==History==
On [[6 December]], [[1926]], this guard was on duty when [[Newton Scamander]] and [[Jacob Kowalski]] broke into the bank to find the escaped [[Newton Scamander's niffler|niffler]]. The guard attempted to arrest them before they [[Apparition|apparated]].<ref>{{FB|S}}</ref>
