This '''individual''' was an employee at the [[Floo Regulation Panel]] at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] in the [[1990s]]. At [[Arthur Weasley]]'s request, this individual was responsible for the afternoon-long connection of the fireplace at [[4 Privet Drive]] to the [[Floo Network]] on [[24 August]], [[1994]].<ref name="GF4">{{GOF|B|4}}</ref>
