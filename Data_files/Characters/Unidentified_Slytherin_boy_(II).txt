This '''male Slytherin student''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]].<ref name="HBPG"/>

==Biography==
In early [[1997]], he stole a [[Noisy Hitter]] from a [[Gryffindor]] girl. When Harry went to go get it back from him, he taunted him and told him that [[Professor]] [[Severus Snape]] would give him [[Discipline at Hogwarts|detention]] for assaulting a Slytherin. The two [[Duelling|duelled]], and he lost, with Harry managing to get the Noisy Hitter back from him.<ref name="HBPG"/>
