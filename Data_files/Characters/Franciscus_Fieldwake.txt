'''Franciscus Fieldwake''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] author and reporter for the [[The New York Ghost]].<ref name="book">{{FB|F}} {{see image|File:ProtectionCharmYourMind.jpg}}</ref><ref name="dec6sunrise">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:The New York Ghost - 6 Dec 1926 - Sunrise Early Edition.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
He wrote the book ''[[Protection Charm Your Mind: A Practical Guide to Counter Legilimensy]]''.<ref name="book" />

''[[The New York Ghost]]'' published a report by him about a [[wizardkind|wizard or witch]] from [[Idaho]] caught drunk [[broomstick|broom]]-riding after winning a [[foxtrot]] competition in its [[6 December]] [[1926]] morning issue.<ref name="dec6sunrise" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Lauren Wakefield]], a graphic artist who worked on the last three [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]], and on ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
