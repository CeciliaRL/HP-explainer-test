'''Dugald McClivert''' was the head of the [[McClivert clan]]. According to legend, he was killed by the hand of the [[MacBoon clan]], [[Quintius MacBoon]] in a drunken duel. His death led to the McClivert clan [[Transfiguration|transfiguring]] the MacBoons into dangerous creatures known as [[Quintaped]]s.<ref>{{FB|R}}</ref>
