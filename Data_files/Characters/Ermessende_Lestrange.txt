'''Ermessende Lestrange''' ([[1679]] – [[1685]]) was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. Ermessende died as a child in [[1685]] and was laid to rest in the [[Lestrange Mausoleum|Lestrange family Mausoleum]] in [[Cimetière du Père-Lachaise]], [[Paris]], France.<ref name="Archive" />
