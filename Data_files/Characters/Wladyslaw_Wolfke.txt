'''Wladyslaw Wolfke''' (pl. Władysław Wroński) was a [[Poland|Polish]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] who played as the [[Seeker]] for the [[2014]] [[Polish National Quidditch team]].<ref name="PM"/>

==Biography==
He played in the seventh round match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], against [[Japanese National Quidditch team|Japan]]. Wolfke was described as "a daring and graceful flier" by [[Ginevra Weasley|Ginny Potter]]. Wolfke was unlucky enough to miss the [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]] early in the game, and was only narrowly beaten to it by [[Japanese National Quidditch team|Japanese]] Seeker [[Noriko Sato]] in the 59th minute.<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[JAPAN VERSUS POLAND|JAPAN_VERSUS_POLAND]] (Archived)</ref>
