'''Cyrille Lestrange''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. He was the son of [[Cyrille Lestrange (I)|Cyrille Lestrange]] and [[Darenne Lestrange|Darenne Moreau]]. He had a brother [[Fulcran Lestrange (I)|Fulcran.]] He married [[Balbine Lestrange (I)|Balbine Leroy]] and had a son with her, [[Cyrille Lestrange (III)|Cyrille]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
