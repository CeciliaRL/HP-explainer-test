'''Roddy Doyle''' (b. [[8 May]], [[1958]]) was an [[Ireland|Irish]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] author who wrote ''[[The Commitments]]'' and ''[[The Van]]''.<ref name="wiki"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Roddy Doyle is an Irish novelist, dramatist, and screenwriter.<ref name="wiki">[[Wikipedia:Roddy Doyle|Roddy Doyle]] at [[Wikipedia:Main Page|Wikipedia]]</ref> [[J. K. Rowling]] has stated that Doyle's ''The Woman Who Walked Into Doors'' is her favourite novel, and that she would like to meet Doyle in person some day.<ref>[http://www.accio-quote.org/articles/2001/0101-oprah.html "J.K. Rowling's Books That Made a Difference." O, The Oprah Magazine January 2001.]</ref>
