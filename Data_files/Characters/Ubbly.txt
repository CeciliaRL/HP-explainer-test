'''Dr Ubbly''' was a [[potioneer]] that created the medicinal [[Dr Ubbly's Oblivious Unction|Oblivious Unction]] potion. The potion was applied by [[Poppy Pomfrey]] to help [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] heal after he was attacked by brains during the [[Battle of the Department of Mysteries]] in [[1996]].<ref>{{OOTP|B|38}}</ref>
