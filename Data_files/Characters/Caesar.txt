'''Caesar''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who had a [[portrait]] of himself hung somewhere in [[Hogwarts Castle]] by the [[1991-1992 school year]].<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}} {{C|PC version}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This portrait was meant to appear in the PC version of the [[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game)|video game adaptation]] of {{PS}}. Though the portrait cannot be found in the game, its texture, identified as KB_Caesar_128.bmp, can be found in the game's files.
