This '''male Slytherin student''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]]. He was among the students who attended the first [[Care of Magical Creatures]] class taught by [[Rubeus Hagrid]].<ref name="POA">{{POA|F}}</ref>

==Biography==
This boy started his [[magic]]al education at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1991]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]]. He attended the first [[Care of Magical Creatures]] class taught by [[Rubeus Hagrid]]. Once [[Harry Potter]] returned from his flight upon [[Buckbeak]], the boy was pushed out of the way by the jealous and bitter [[Draco Malfoy]] as he approached the [[Hippogriff]].<ref name="POA">{{POA|F}}</ref>
