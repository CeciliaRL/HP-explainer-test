'''Rebecca Ramsea''' (c. [[1994]]) was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] woman who was buried in the [[Little Hangleton]] [[Little Hangleton graveyard|graveyard]].<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}} (''Chapter One - The Riddle House'') {{see image|File:Rebecca Ramsea.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Rebecca" comes from the Hebrew name רִבְקָה (Rivqah), possibly meaning "a snare" in Hebrew, or perhaps derived from an Aramaic name.
