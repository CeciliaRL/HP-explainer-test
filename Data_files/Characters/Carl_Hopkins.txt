'''Carl Hopkins''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student who most likely began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HBPG"/><ref name="age"/>

==Biography==
During the [[1996â€“1997 school year]] at Hogwarts, he took [[Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test|N.E.W.T.-level]] [[Potions (class)|Potions]], as he someday hoped to be an [[Auror]]. He confessed to [[Harry Potter]] in [[1997]] that he would have liked a new [[Broomstick|broom]]. He also admitted that he was a "disaster" in Potions. At the end of the school year, he could be found in the [[Viaduct Courtyard|Entrance Courtyard]], near [[Scott Ancrum]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Carl must have achieved a high grade at [[Ordinary Wizarding Level]] in order to qualify for [[Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test|N.E.W.T.]] level [[Potions (class)|Potions]], although he commented that he largely struggled with the subject at such an advanced level, labelling himself a "disaster".<ref name="HBPG"/>

==Etymology==
The name "Carl" is the German form of Charles.

==Behind the scenes==
*If he survived the [[Battle of Hogwarts]], it is likely that he would have gone on to join the [[British Ministry of Magic]] alongside his peers, Harry Potter, [[Ronald Weasley]], [[Neville Longbottom]] and - possibly - also [[Fay Dunbar]], seen [[Kingsley Shacklebolt]] offered a place at the [[Auror Office]] for students that survived the final battle of the [[Second Wizarding War]].
