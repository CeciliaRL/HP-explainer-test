}}'''Professor Vulpus'''<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE}} (''The Paintings of Hogwarts'')</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]]. It is unknown if he was a [[Muggle-born]], [[half-blood]] or [[Pure-blood|pure blood]].

==Biography==
===Lifetime===
Professor Vulpus was appointed as [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] sometime before [[Armando Dippet]].

===Post-mortem===
After his death, his [[Headmaster portraits|portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]] in order to give advice to the current Head.<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}} DVD (Disc 2 - ''Dumbledore's Office'')</ref>

==Etymology==
*The name ''Vulpus'' may come from ''[[wikipedia:Vulpes|Vulpes]]'', the Latin word for "fox".
