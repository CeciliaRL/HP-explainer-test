==Biography==
In or around [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Fieldwake, stating that she was wanted for [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] & [[House-elf|Elf]] murder and "bearing wand, most menacing and extremely dangerous."<ref name="cob" /> A reward of 2500 [[Dragot]]s was offered for information leading to her capture.<ref name="cob" />
