'''Blythe Parkin''' was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as [[Seeker]] on the [[English National Quidditch team]] in [[1994]]. Whilst playing [[Quidditch]], she [[Flight|flew]] on a Silver White [[Nimbus 2000]] [[broomstick]]. She may be a relative to [[Walter Parkin]] and his [[Walter Parkin's children|children]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

By [[1996]], she was included on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] for her achievements in the sport.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Blythe" comes from a surname which meant "cheerful" in Old English.
