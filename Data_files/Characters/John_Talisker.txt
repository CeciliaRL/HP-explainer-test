'''John Talisker''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was a [[Captain]] for the [[Scottish National Quidditch team]] and a [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force|SOS Task Force]] member in the [[2010s]].<ref name="WU"/>

During an international Quidditch friendly against the [[Irish National Quidditch team]], he learned that the entire Irish team were left-handed, which inspired a tactical change for Scotland.<ref name="WU"/>
