This headless '''[[ghost]]''' resided at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. In [[1991]], it was seen in the [[Great Hall]], just standing. He visited the [[Helena Ravenclaw|Grey Lady]] in the school's [[Hogwarts Library|library]], riding around on a horse.<ref name="LEGOY1">{{LEGO|Y1}} - "The Restricted Section"</ref> In [[1993]], he was seen chasing [[Ghost of a highwayman|another ghost]] to get his head back.<ref name="POAF">{{POA|F}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*It's possible this ghost was [[Patrick Delaney-Podmore]] or another member of the [[Headless Hunt]].
