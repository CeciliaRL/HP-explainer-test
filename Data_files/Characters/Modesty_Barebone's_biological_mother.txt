The '''biological mother''' of [[Modesty Barebone]] lived with her [[Modesty Barebone's biological father|husband]] and [[Modesty Barebone's biological siblings|ten children]] in a [[Tenement in The Bronx|tenement]] in [[the Bronx]] in the [[1910s]] and/or [[1920s]].<ref name="fb96">{{FB|S|96}}</ref> However, Modesty was adopted by [[Mary Lou Barebone]], a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] anti-[[magic|witchcraft]] activist, possibly because the family had more children than they could care for or were magical.<ref name="fb96" />
