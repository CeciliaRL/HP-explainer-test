This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] whose portrait hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] in the [[1990s]], opposite the [[Fat Lady]] and next to a [[portrait of a group of journeying wizards]]. He was depicted wearing green robes, a ponted hat, a ruff around his neck, and a broomstick on his left hand.<ref name="PA13">{{POA|F}} - Chapter 13 (''Flight of the Fat Lady'')</ref>
