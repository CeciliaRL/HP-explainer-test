This '''individual''' was a [[Dark Wizard]] or Witch who attacked [[Jacob's sibling]] and [[Ben Copper]] at the start of the [[1988–1989 school year]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="HM51"/>

==Biography==
At the start of the [[1988–1989 school year]], when [[Jacob's sibling]] and [[Ben Copper]] were talking in the [[Courtyard]], they subdued both students in the Hogwarts [[courtyard]] with a [[Full Body-Bind Curse]] before telling Jacob's sibling that they warned them that "[[death]]" was coming to Hogwarts, and that they needed them alive, but that before the school term ended, one of their friends would have to die.<ref name="HM51"/>

==Physical appearance==
This individual's physical features were completely unidentifiable, due to being hidden by a red cloak.<ref name="HM51"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Dark Arts]]''': This individual appeared to be an accomplished [[Dark Wizard]], as they were capable of performing the [[Full Body-Bind Curse]] proficiently.<ref name="HM51"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Unlike with the first messenger, the identity of his individual is not revealed in the game. It is unknown if they will be indentified in future game information.
