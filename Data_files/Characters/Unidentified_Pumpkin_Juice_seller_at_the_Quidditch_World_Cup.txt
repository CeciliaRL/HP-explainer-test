This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' sold [[Pumpkin Juice|pumpkin juice]] at the [[1994]] [[Quidditch World Cup]].<ref name="LEGOY1">{{LEGO|Y1}}</ref>

He was quite capable of using the [[Levitation Charm]], doing so to move the glasses of pumpkin juice over the counter to his customers.<ref name="LEGOY1"/>

He wore a pointed green wizard's hat and matching green robes. He smiled broadly, chatting happily to his customers as he [[magic]]ally poured the drinks and brought them over the counter to them.
