'''Gerda Curd''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and author who contributed to the revised edition of ''[[Charm Your Own Cheese]]''.<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*[[Wikipedia:Curd|Curd]] is a semisolid substance formed when milk sours. It is used as the base in making some [[cheese]]s.

==Behind the scenes==
*It is possible that "Gerda Curd" is simply a more cheese-related pen name adopted by [[Greta Catchlove]].
