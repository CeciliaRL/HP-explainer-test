'''Kris Kolumbiko''' (b. [[1730s]]/[[1740s]]) was a [[Slytherin]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1750s]]. He was [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Slytherin]] [[Quidditch]] [[Slytherin Quidditch team|team]] from [[1752]] to [[1756]].<ref>{{HPPS}}, p. 349 {{see image|File:Slytherin Captains Plaque 2.jpg}}</ref>[[File:Slytherin Captains Plaque 2.jpg|236x236px|thumb|left|{{PAGENAME}}'s name appearing on a plaque]]

==Behind the scenes==
*The naming seems to be a variant spelling of [[Chris Columbus]], director of the first two [[Harry Potter (film series)|Harry Potter films]] and a producer of the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|third]].
