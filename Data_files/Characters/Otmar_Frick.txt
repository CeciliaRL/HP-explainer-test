'''Otmar Frick''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] from Liechtenstein and a [[Chaser]] for the [[2014]] [[Liechtenstein National Quidditch team]]. During the fifth match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], Liechtenstein versus [[Chadian National Quidditch team|Chad]], was believed to literally have fallen asleep shortly before play was stopped for the second evening.<ref name="lie-v-tcd">{{PM}} - [[LIECHTENSTEIN VERSUS CHAD (II)|LIECHTENSTEIN_VERSUS_CHAD_(II)]] (Archived)</ref>
