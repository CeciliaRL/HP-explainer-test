This '''toddler '''was a [[France|French]] [[Non-magic people|Non-Magique]] resident of [[Paris]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="COGS"/>

==Biography==

[[File:Non-magique toddler playing with it's toys.jpg|250px|thumb|left|The toddler playing with his toys]]
In the [[1920s]], this toddler was born to a [[Unidentified Non-Magique mother|Non-Magique mother]] and her [[Unidentified Non-Magique father|husband]]. 
{{Quote|Mamu.. Mamu?|Toddler's cries out wondering where his mother is|Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald - The Original Screenplay}}
Together with his parents, the baby lived in a [[Unidentified home used by Gellert Grindelwald|house]] somewhere in [[Paris]]. Soon after the [[Gellert Grindelwald]] and his [[Gellert Grindelwald's acolytes|acolyte]]'s inflitrated the home, the toddler cried out for his [[Unidentified Non-Magique mother|Mother]] but was instead heard by [[Gellert Grindelwald]] and his [[Gellert Grindelwald's acolytes|acolyte]]'s and went to it's room and under Grindelwald's order's it was [[Killing Curse|murdered]] by [[Gellert Grindelwald's acolytes|Gellert Grindelwald's acolyte]] [[Carrow (1920s)|Carrow]]. 

They later used the family's home as a temporary headquarters, from where he conducted a search for [[Credence Barebone]].<ref name="COGS">{{COG|S}}</ref>
