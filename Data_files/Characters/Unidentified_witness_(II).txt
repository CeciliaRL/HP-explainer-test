This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was the witness of several cases investigated by the [[British Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="HM75A2"/><ref name="HM76A2">{{HM|7|6|Auror Field Training}}</ref> He lived in a village in [[Northumberland]] with his cousin [[Mildred (1990s)|Mildred]].<ref name="HM714A"/> He could often be seen in the [[Three Broomsticks Inn]].<ref name="HM">{{HM}}</ref>

==Biography==
He once bought the [[rogue alchemist]]'s [[Rogue alchemist's fake panacea|fake panacea]]. During the [[1990â€“1991 school year]], he was interviewed by [[Alastor Moody]] and [[Jacob's sibling]] as a part of their investigations of the rogue alchemist.<ref name="HM75A2"/>

He spotted a [[fugitive Dark Witch]]<ref name="HM76A2"/> and a thief named [[Olivier Kikkert]] who was an unregistered [[frog]] [[Animagus]].<ref name="HM77A1">{{HM|7|7|Assist Madam Bones}}</ref>

He saw some suspicious sweets, including [[Chocolate Frog]]s, [[Fizzing Whizzbees]] and [[Sugar Mice]], in [[Honeydukes]].<ref name="HM77A2">{{HM|7|7|Whispered Rumours}}</ref>

He spotted a [[Muggle Artefact Smuggler]] on multiple occasions, such as outside the [[Leaky Cauldron]] and on the [[Charing Cross Road]].<ref name="HM712A1">{{HM|7|12|Muggle Artefact Smuggler}}</ref>

He witnessed an [[Explosion in Northumberland|explosion]] in his hometown [[Northumberland]] and helped protect the bystanders with [[Mildred (1990s)|Mildred]].<ref name="HM714A"/>

He fell victim to [[Xeep's Luscious Locks Solution]], and drank the [[Counter-serum for Xeep's Luscious Locks Solution|counter-serum]] prepared by [[Severus Snape|Professor Snape]], [[Penny Haywood]] and Jacob's sibling.<ref name="HM715">{{HM|7|15}}</ref>
