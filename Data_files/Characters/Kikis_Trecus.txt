'''Kikis Trecus''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch or wizard]] who worked as a reporter for the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''. Trecus wrote an article about the upcoming final match of the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup]], titled "Thousands of Supporters are Expected to Attend the Final Game", in the summer of [[1994]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:DailyProphet1994BackPage.png|this image]])</ref>
