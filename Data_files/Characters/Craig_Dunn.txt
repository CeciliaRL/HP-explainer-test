'''Craig Dunn''' was a [[Hufflepuff]]<ref name="house" /> student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He competed in the school [[Gobstones]] tournament during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
The name "Craig" is from a Scottish surname which was derived from Gaelic ''creag'' meaning "crag" or "rocks". The surname originally belonged to a person who lived near a crag.
