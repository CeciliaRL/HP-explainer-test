'''Mr Limunus''' was a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] [[astrology|astrologer]]. He was writing the [[horoscope]] column for ''[[Daily Prophet|The Daily Prophet]]'' by [[1997]].<ref>{{DH|F1}} {{see image|File:DailyProphetHoroscopes.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Eduardo Lima]], a graphic artist who has worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' onward.
