'''Owle Bullock'''<ref name="name">[http://www.mugglenet.com/app/news/show/3550 "First look at Deathly Hallows props including 'Life and Lies', 'A History of Magic', more"] at [http://www.mugglenet.com/ MuggleNet]</ref> was a [[Dark Wizard]] or witch who authored ''[[Secrets of the Darkest Art]]'', a book that was "''full of [[Dark Arts|evil magic]]''", including detailed instructions on how to create a [[Horcrux]].<ref name="DH6">{{DH|B|6}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to [[Alastair Bullock]], who worked as an art director on every film from ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' onward, and as an uncredited draughtsman on ''[[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)|Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets]]''.
