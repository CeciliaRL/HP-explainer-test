'''Vincent Davies''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] costume designer in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:Two to Tango.png}}</ref>

He designed the costumes for a production of the musical ''[[Two to Tango]]'' that was staged at [[The Kingly Court]] in [[Times Square]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="poster" />
