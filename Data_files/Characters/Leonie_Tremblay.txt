'''Leonie Tremblay''' (née '''Lestrange''') was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[Tremblay family]]. She was the daughter of [[Falco Lestrange]] and [[Salomé Lestrange]]. She had a sister [[Eglantine Lestrange|Eglantine]]. She married [[Bernard Tremblay]] and had two children with him, daughter [[Clarisse Lestrange|Clarisse]] and son [[Falco Tremblay|Falco]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
