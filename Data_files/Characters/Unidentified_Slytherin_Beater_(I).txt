This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as a [[Beater]] for the [[Slytherin Quidditch team|Slytherin team]] in [[Quidditch]]. In the [[Slytherin]] versus [[Gryffindor Quidditch team|Gryffindor]] Hogwart's [[Inter-House Quidditch Cup|Quidditch Cup]] match in [[1991]], he is shown to have his [[Beater's bat|bat]] taken by [[Marcus Flint]].<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}}</ref>
