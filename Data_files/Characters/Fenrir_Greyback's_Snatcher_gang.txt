[[Fenrir Greyback]]'s '''gang''' of [[Snatchers]] was the most prominent snatcher gang in Britain. There were seven members of this gang, including [[Scabior]].<ref name="DH23">{{DH|B|23}}</ref>

==History==
In [[1998]], this gang captured [[Dean Thomas]] and [[Griphook]] and also killed their companions, [[Gornuk]], [[Dirk Cresswell]] and [[Edward Tonks|Ted Tonks]].<ref name="DH22">{{DH|B|22}}</ref> This group then caught [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] and [[Hermione Granger]]. They almost fell for their lies but then realised who they caught and brought them to [[Malfoy Manor]]. When there, one snatcher got into a disagreement with [[Bellatrix Lestrange]] and all were [[Stunning Spell|stunned]] except for Greyback. Bellatrix then planned to kill them,<ref name="DH23"/> but never did because they escaped when she was punished by [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]].<ref name="DH24">{{DH|B|24}}</ref> Later, Every member of this gang fought in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]]. Six were killed when the [[Wooden Bridge]] collapsed,<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref> and what happened to Greyback is unknown. He was either killed, escaped back to his pack or was caught and sent to [[Azkaban]].
