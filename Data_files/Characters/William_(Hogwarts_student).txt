'''William''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] at a point between the [[1960s]] and [[1980s]]. He was remembered to have been a great troublemaker by caretaker [[Argus Filch]].<ref name="HM">{{HM}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
The name William only appears in the Beta mode of the game. In the final cut of the game, the name William was replaced with the name Jacob in scenes where the name originally appeared. It is likely that William was the original name of the character or another character later cut from the game.
