'''Jackie Jernigan''' was a Welsh witch and a [[Chaser]] for the [[2014]] [[Welsh National Quidditch team]]. On the first match of the quarter-finals of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], [[Brazilian National Quidditch team|Brazil]] versus Wales, on [[4 June]], [[2014]], Jernigan scored one of the tournament's most stunning goals from a distance of fifty yards from the [[goalpost]]s.<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[BRAZIL VERSUS WALES|BRAZIL_VERSUS_WALES]] (Archived)</ref>
