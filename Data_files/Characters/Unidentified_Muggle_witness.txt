This man was a '''[[Non-magic people|Muggle]]''' who witnessed some aspects of the [[wizarding world]] several times and had to be [[Memory Charm|Obliviated]]. He was often interviewed by [[Alastor Moody]] and [[Jacob's sibling]], who worked with Moody during their time with the [[Ministry of Magic student programme]]. After these interviews, he would have his memory wiped.<ref name="HM712A1"/>
