This '''goblin''' was apparently a stockbroker and worked for [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]].<ref name="Rusty Goffe">[http://www.rustygoffe.co.uk/ Rusty Goffe's Official Site]</ref>

==Biography==
===1991===
By the end of the summer of [[1991]], this goblin worked at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]].<ref name="Rusty Goffe" />

===1993===
During the [[Christmas holidays]] in [[1993]], this goblin was among a group of carollers in [[Hogsmeade|Hogsmeade Village]].<ref name="Rusty Goffe" /> He was rudely pushed aside by an angry and [[Cloak of Invisibility|Invisibility Cloaked]]-[[Harry Potter]].<ref name="POA">{{POA|F}}</ref>

===1995===
On [[11 August]] [[1995]], this goblin lost a large amount of gold trading on the [[potions market]]. The [[12 August|following day]], he went to the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] Headquarters, in [[London]], to take care of business, where he told a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] the "terrible day" he had the day before.<ref name="OP">{{OOTP|F}}</ref>

[[File:HBP Narcissa Draco goblin Diagon Alley 1.jpg|thumb|right|This goblin passing by [[Draco Malfoy|Draco]] and [[Narcissa Malfoy]] in [[Diagon Alley]], in [[1996]]]]

===1996===
By [[1996]], this goblin still worked at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank|Gringotts]], on the then-almost deserted [[Diagon Alley]].<ref name="Rusty Goffe"/>

===1998===
It is unknown what happened to him after when [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] took over the [[Ministry of Magic]] and took control over [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank|Gringotts]].

==Behind the scenes==
*The goblin was portrayed by [[Rusty Goffe]] in ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]] and'' ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince]].''
