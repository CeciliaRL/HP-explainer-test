'''Dennis Moon''' was a wizard from New Zealand who played as a [[Chaser]] for the [[2014]] [[New Zealand National Quidditch team]]. During the first round match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], against [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team|Bulgaria]], Moon was sent off by referee [[Georgios Xenakis]]. New Zealand lost the match 410 - 170. His manager [[Charlie Baverstock]] proclaimed himself 'madder than a bloke who's been locked in a box of [[Fwooper]]s' after the sending off.<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[BULGARIA VERSUS NEW ZEALAND]] (Archived)</ref>
