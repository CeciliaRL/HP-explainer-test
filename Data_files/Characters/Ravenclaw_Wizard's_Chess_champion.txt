The '''Ravenclaw Wizard's Chess champion''', as the title suggests, refers to the [[Wizard's Chess]] Captain for [[Ravenclaw|Ravenclaw House]], at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="OOTPG"/>

==Biography==
This girl came to be educated at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw|Ravenclaw House]]. Her [[Head of House]] was [[Professor]] [[Filius Flitwick]], the [[Charms (class)|Charms]] [[Charms Master|Master]], and she shared a dormitory with four other girls in her year in the [[Ravenclaw Tower]]. She was skilled in [[Wizard's Chess]] and could be usually found playing in the [[Hogwarts Library|Library]].<ref name="OOTPG">{{OOTP|G}}</ref>
