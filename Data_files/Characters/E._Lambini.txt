'''E. Lambini''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and sports reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="nyghost-dec6">{{WWMM|1}} {{see image|File:NewYorkGhost-6December1926.jpg}}</ref><ref name="nyghost-dec7">{{CB}} {{see image|File:The New York Ghost - 7 Dec 1926.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
They wrote an article about [[wizardkind|wizarding]] interest in the [[Muggle|No-Maj]] game [[baseball]] that was published in the [[6 December]] [[1926]] sunset issue of the newspaper.<ref name="nyghost-dec6" /> This was followed by an article by them in [[7 December|the next day's]] sunset edition examining whether [[football|soccer]] was the No-Maj equivalent of [[Quidditch]].<ref name="nyghost-dec7" />
