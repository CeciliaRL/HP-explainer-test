'''Ivan Renshaw''' was a [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
He competed in the school [[Gobstones]] tournament during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
The name "Ivan" is the newer form of the old Slavic name Іѡаннъ (Ioannu), which was derived from Greek ''Ioannes''. It refers back to ''John'', which means "God (Herbrew) is gracious".

''Renshaw ''is a surname from Prestbury, Cheshire in England. The suffix ''shaw ''means "wood",so the whole name means "Ren-wood".
