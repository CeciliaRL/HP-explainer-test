'''Barker''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] man who worked as an assistant to [[Henry Shaw Senior]] at the [[Shaw Tower]] in [[New York]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="FB37">{{FB|S|37}}</ref>

==Biography==
When [[Langdon Shaw]] arrived with the [[Barebone family]] on [[6 December]] [[1926]] hoping to see his father, Barker nervously told him that Shaw Snr was in a meeting with [[Henry Shaw Junior|his brother]], but Langdon pushed past him.<ref name="FB37" /> Barker then profusely apologised to his boss for Langdon's interruption.<ref name="FB38">{{FB|S|38}}</ref>
