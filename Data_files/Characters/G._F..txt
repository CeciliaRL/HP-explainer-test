'''G. F.''' was a married [[wizardkind|witch]] from [[Bulwark]], [[South Wales]]. Her testimonial was included in an advertisement for the [[TerrorTours]] travel agency that was printed in the [[1 June]] [[1999]] issue of the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''. In the testimonial, she claimed [[G. F.'s grandfather|her grandfather]] "enjoyed himself right up until the last moment," suggesting he died during a TerrorTours holiday.<ref name="dp3">''[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]'', Issue 3 (see [https://www.hp-lexicon.org/character/mrs-g-f/ this entry] on the Harry Potter Lexicon)</ref>
