The '''Hufflepuff Wizard Duelling Champion''' as the title suggests, refers to the duelling champion for the [[Hufflepuff]] house. He is a selected student from the Hufflepuff house to represent the house in the [[Founder Duels]].

==Biography==
===Early life===
This boy attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Hufflepuff|Hufflepuff House]]. His [[Head of House]] was [[Pomona Sprout]], the [[Herbology]] [[Professor]], and he shared a dormitory with four boys in his year in the [[Hufflepuff Basement]], which is located below ground level and near the [[Hogwarts kitchens]].

===[[1992–1993 school year]]===
He apparently excelled at [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] and became the [[Hufflepuff]] Duelling Champion. When [[Harry Potter]] defeated him, he gave him [[Helga Hufflepuff]]'s [[Chocolate Frog Card|card]].

==Behind the scenes==
*The Hufflepuff Duelling Champion has only appeared in the [[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)|video game adaptation]] of {{COS}}.
