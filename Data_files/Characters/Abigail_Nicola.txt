'''Abigail Nicola''' was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. She once signed up for [[Quidditch]] training at 7pm on Thursday on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in the [[Gryffindor Common Room]].<ref>{{See image|File:QuidditchTrainingSignupSheet.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Abigail" comes from the Hebrew name אֲבִיגָיִל ('Avigayil) meaning "my father is joy".
