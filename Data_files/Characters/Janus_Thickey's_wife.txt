'''Mrs Thickey''' (fl. [[1973]]) was the wife of [[Janus Thickey]]. When, in [[1973]], her husband faked his own [[death]] by pretending that a [[Lethifold]] had eaten him, she and her [[Janus Thickey's children|children]] entered a period of mourning. However, their mourning was cut short when Janus was found to be living five miles away, with the [[Landlady of the Green Dragon|landlady]] of the ''[[Green Dragon]]''.<ref name="FBR">{{FB|R}}, ''Lethifold''</ref>
