These '''children''' were the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] offspring of [[Dougal McGregor]] and [[Dougal McGregor's wife|his wife]].<ref name="shortstories"/>

The McGregors and their children were murdered in a random anti-[[Non-magic people|Muggle]] attack by the Death Eaters during the height of the [[First Wizarding War]] in the [[1970s]].<ref name="shortstories">[[Pottermore Presents]] - ''[[Short Stories from Hogwarts of Heroism, Hardship and Dangerous Hobbies]]'', Chapter 1 (''Minerva McGonagall'')</ref>
