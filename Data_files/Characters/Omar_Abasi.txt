'''Omar Abasi''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked as a [[healer]] at [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries]], working on the first floor and tended to patients suffering from [[creature]]-induced Injuries, an area at which he was an expert.<ref name="FBC">{{FBC}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early career===
At some point in his life, Omar successfully underwent the required tutelage to become a fully-fledged [[Healer]] and were eventually successful at obtaining a position at [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries]] in [[London]], where he worked in the [["Dangerous" Dai Llewellyn Ward for Serious Bites]] on the first floor, specialising in creature-induced injuries.<ref name="FBC1"/>

===Working with the Ministry===
At some point in his career, Omar was drafted in for conjecture by the [[Beast Division]] in the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]] at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]], wherein he would lend its investigators his expertise and give his opinion on cases they were working on. At one point, he attended to [[Chudley Cannons]]' [[keeper]] [[Gordon Horton]] after he was attacked at the [[Chudley Cannons's training ground]] and diagnosed him with a mild concussion. He also noted that Horton's kit had been badly torn, likely by sharp claws.<ref name="FBC1" />

His observation was indeed correct, as the investigators from the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]] discovered Mr Horton had been attacked by a [[Hippogriff]].<ref name="FBC1">{{FBC|1}}</ref>

He later used the [[Memory Charm]] to erase the memories of the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[Laura Thorn]] after she had found about a [[Mountain troll]].<ref name="FBC2"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Healing magic]]''': As a professional [[Healer]], he was highly adept and knowledgeable in the field of healing magic. He was an expert at dealing with [[creature]]-induced injuries.<ref name="FBC1"/> In order to have become a Healer, he would have had at least five [[Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test|N.E.W.T.s]] in the subjects of [[Charms (class)|Charms]], [[Transfiguration (class)|Transfiguration]], [[Herbology]], [[Potions (class)|Potions]] and [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]], all at either "[[Exceeds Expectations]]" or "[[Outstanding]]" level.<ref name="OOTP29">{{OOTP|B|29}}</ref>
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Being a Healer, Abasi was accomplished with charms. In addition for needing both an O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. in the subject at high grades for his career,<ref name="OOTP29"/> he was also capable of using the [[Memory Charm]] to erase the memories of the Muggle [[Laura Thorn]] after she had discovered a [[Mountain troll]],<ref name="FBC2">{{FBC|2}}</ref> showing his proficiency in the field.
