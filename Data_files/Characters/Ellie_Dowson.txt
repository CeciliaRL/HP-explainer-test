'''Ellie Dowson'''<ref>Closed captions on the DVD/Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] reported missing along with her relative, [[Rob Dowson|Rob]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' radio programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of [[Mike Dowson]], a sound mixer who worked on the final three [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0236236 Mike Dowson's profile on IMDb]</ref>
