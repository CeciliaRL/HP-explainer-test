'''Eduardo Lima Filho''' (b. sun in [[Pisces]], [[moon]] in [[Pisces]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked in the [[Runes & Symbols]] department of the [[Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="HPLE" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Eduardo Lima]], a graphic artist who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' onward.
*In accordance with the [[Wikipedia:Portuguese name|Portuguese naming conventions]], the suffix "Filho" is added to the name with initial upper case and without a separating comma, whenever someone is named after their father (in a similar way as "Jnr." is used in the English-speaking world). This practise is most common in Brazil.
