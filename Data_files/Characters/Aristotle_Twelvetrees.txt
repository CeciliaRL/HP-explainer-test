'''Aristotle Twelvetrees''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizard]].<ref name="pottermore-rappaport">"[https://www.pottermore.com/collection-episodic/history-of-magic-in-north-america-en History of Magic in North America: Rappaport's Law]" on {{PM}}</ref> He served as [[Keeper of Treasure and Dragots]] during the [[President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America|presidency]] of [[Emily Rappaport]] in the late [[18th century]], and was apparently good at his job, being described as a "competent man."<ref name="pottermore-rappaport" />

==Biography==
He had a daughter, [[Dorcus Twelvetrees|Dorcus]], who did not take after him.<ref name="pottermore-rappaport" /> She did poorly at [[Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Ilvermorny]] and was still living with him at the time he took office.<ref name="pottermore-rappaport" /> Her fraternisation with the [[Muggle|No-Maj]] [[Bartholomew Barebone]] lead to one of the greatest breaches of the [[International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy|International Statute of Secrecy]] in history and prompted the passage of [[Rappaport's Law]].<ref name="pottermore-rappaport" />
