This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch or wizard]] who became the [[professor]] of [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], for the [[1987–1988 school year]].<ref name="HM44"/> 

Many, among them the famous [[Curse-Breaker]], [[Patricia Rakepick]], considered this individual to be an inept teacher.<ref name="HM44">{{HM|4|4}}</ref>

By the end of the year, this professor needed to be replaced because of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s [[Jinx on the post of Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher|jinx]] on the post.
