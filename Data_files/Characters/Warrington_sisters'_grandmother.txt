This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' was the grandmother of [[Ivy Warrington|Ivy]] and [[Winifred Warrington]].<ref name="MA">{{MA}}</ref>

When her granddaughters were growing up, Winifred accidentaly Vanished herself. In an effort to help Ivy forget what happened, she put a [[Memory Charm]] on her. Unfortunately, it caused Ivy to wholly forget about Winifred.<ref name="MA" />  

==Personality==
She was a caring, [[Love|loving]] woman who couldn't bear to see those she loved in pain.<ref name="MA" />  

==Magical abilities and skills==

*'''[[Charm]]s''': She cast the [[Memory Charm]] on her [[Ivy Warrington|granddaughter]] to make her forget that  Winifred [[Vanishing Spell|vanish]]ed to the point that she made her forget Winifred entirely.<ref name="MA" />
