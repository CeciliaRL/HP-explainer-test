'''Babayaga''' was a [[Middle Ages|medieval]] [[Russia]]n [[hag]] who regularly ate [[human]] children for breakfast, and presumably lunch and tea as well. She lived in [[Babayaga's hut|a hut that stood on giant chicken legs]]. She appeared on a hag [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The name Babayaga is based on the [[Russia]]n folkloric legend of the [[Wikipedia:Baba Yaga|Baba Yaga]].
**In the [[Russia]]n myth of Baba Yaga, she lures children in to her house - which stands on giant [[chicken]] legs - and feeds on them. When they least expect it, she then throws them in the [[oven]] to cook and eat them.
