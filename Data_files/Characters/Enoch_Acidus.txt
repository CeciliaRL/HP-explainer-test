'''Enoch Acidus''' was an [[wizardkind|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].<ref name="vid">"[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v00xz7oB3MY Go Behind the Scenes of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (2016)]" {{See image|File:EnochAcidusPoster.jpg}}</ref>  

In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Acidus, offering a reward of 3695 [[Dragot]]s for information leading to his arrest.<ref name="vid" />
