'''Barnaby Snell''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Quidditch]] fan who supported the [[Chudley Cannons]].<ref name="DP">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 June]], [[1999]], Page 3</ref> He was quoted in the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' as expressing disbelief after the Cannons managed to win a match against the [[Wigtown Wanderers]] after suffering through a seventeen-game losing streak.<ref name="wizards">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/s.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: S]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon</ref>
