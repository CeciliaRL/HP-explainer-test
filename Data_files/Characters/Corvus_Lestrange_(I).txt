'''Corvus Lestrange''' (1729 – 1768) was a [[France|French]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. He married [[Heloise Lestrange|Heloise Beaufort]] and had two sons with her, [[Corvus Lestrange (II)|Corvus]] and [[Falco Lestrange|Falco]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:LestrangeFamilyTree HighRes.png}}</ref>

==Etymology==
His name is derived from the Latin corvus meaning "raven".
