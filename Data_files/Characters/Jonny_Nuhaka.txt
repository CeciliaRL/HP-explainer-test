'''Jonny Nuhaka''' was an Australian [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as the [[Keeper]] for the [[Australian National Quidditch team]] during the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup|four-hundred and twenty-second]] [[Quidditch World Cup]], in [[1994]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Nuhaka's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[David Nolton]] at a [[Quidditch]] challenge.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} {{C|NDS edition}}</ref>

==Etymology==
[[wikipedia:Nuhaka|Nuhaka]] is the [[wikipedia:Māori_language|Māori]] name for a small settlement in New Zealand's Hawke's Bay Region. It is also the name of a [[wikipedia:Nuhaka_River|river]] in the same area.
