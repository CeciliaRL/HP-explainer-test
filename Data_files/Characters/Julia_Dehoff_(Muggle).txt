'''Julia Dehoff''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] fashion designer. Some of her designs were featured in the [[Costume Materialization]] collection opening that was held on [[28 September]], [[1997]], alongside the work of fellow fashion designers [[Emma Vane (Muggle)|Emma Vane]] and [[Mauricio Carneiro]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:CostumeMaterialization.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Julia Dehoff-Bourne]], who worked as a draughtsman on several [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref>{{IMDb name|0215005|Julia Dehoff}}</ref>
