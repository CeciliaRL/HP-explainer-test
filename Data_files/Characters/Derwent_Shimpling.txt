'''Derwent Shimpling''' (b. [[1912]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizarding]] comedian. He once ate an entire [[Venomous Tentacula]] on a bet, and, though he survived, his skin was left purple.<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
Shimpling was born in the year [[1912]].<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

===As a comedian===
Shimpling became a famed comedian, very popular for his daring sense of humour.<ref name="WoM" /> Shimpling was also famous for making bets lightly. Once, he ate an entire [[Venomous Tentacula]] on a bet, and, though he survived, his skin was left permanently purple.<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

===Recognition===
[[File:Derwent Shimpling Portrait.jpg|thumb|Shimpling's portrait]]
He was also featured on a silver [[Chocolate Frog Card]] as part of the [[Famous Witches and Wizards Cards]] series, for his famous achievement. During the [[1993–1994 school year]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], a [[portrait]] of him guarded a secret room, the password to enter was "''Tummy-button''".<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
''Derwent'' means "valley thick with oaks". It was a village in Derbyshire that was drowned by the creation of the Ladybower Reservoir. Oaks usually symbolise strength and endurance, like how Derwent had to endure eating a Venomous Tentacula.

Shimpling is a village and civil parish in south Suffolk, England. Located around seven miles from Bury St Edmunds, it is a part of the Baghbert district.
