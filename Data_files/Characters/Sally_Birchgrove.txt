'''Sally Birchgrove''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="POAG"/>

==Biography==
She played informal [[Quidditch]] matches with other students during the [[1996–1997 school year]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Sally" is a diminutive of the name ''Sarah'' which means "lady" or "princess" in Hebrew.
