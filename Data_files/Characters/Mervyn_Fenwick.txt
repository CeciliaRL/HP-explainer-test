'''Mervyn Fenwick''' was the [[Keeper]] for the [[Tutshill Tornados]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="dp2">''[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]'', Issue 2 (see [https://www.hp-lexicon.org/character/mervyn-fenwick/ this entry] on the Harry Potter Lexicon)</ref>

He fell ill with a bout of sleeping sickness right before a match against the [[Ballycastle Bats]] in [[1999]].<ref name="dp2" /> This may have been caused by him receiving a blow from a [[Bludger]] directed at him by [[Finbar Quigley]].<ref name="dp2" />
