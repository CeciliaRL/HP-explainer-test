'''Harfang Longbottom''' (fl. 20th centuy) was a [[pure-blood]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who married [[Callidora Black]]. The couple had two children, a son and a daughter.<ref>''[[House of Black|Black Family tree]]''</ref> Harfang may be related to [[Frank Longbottom|Frank]] and [[Neville Longbottom]].

==Behind the scenes==
*Harfang was not Featured on the Longbottom family tree shown on Pottermore, but he is Featured on the Black Family tree.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20191002072132/https://www.pottermore.com/about/black-family-tree Pottermore - The Black family tree]</ref>

==Etymology==
The name Harfang may be derived from ''Harfang des neiges'', a French term for "snowy owl."<ref>"[http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/harfang Harfang]" at Wiktionary</ref> In ''The Silver Chair'', the sixth book in the ''The Chronicles of Narnia'' series by [[Wikipedia:C.S. Lewis|C.S. Lewis]], Harfang is a city of giants in the far north.

J.R.R Tolkien, the author of The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings, gave the name Longbottom to one of the Hobbits' villages in the Shire, which is known for its pipe-weed.
