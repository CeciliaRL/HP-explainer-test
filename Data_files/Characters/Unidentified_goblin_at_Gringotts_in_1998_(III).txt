}}This '''[[Goblin]]''' worked at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] during the wizard management in [[1997]]-[[1998]], after the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of magic]] fell to the [[Death Eaters]].<ref name="DHF2"/>

==Biography==
===1998===
He was present at the bank when [[Harry Potter]], [[Hermione Granger]], [[Ronald Weasley]], and [[Griphook]] hosted a break-in in order to retrieve one of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s [[Horcrux]]es, [[Helga Hufflepuff's Cup]] from the [[Lestrange Vault]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

===Later life===
It is unknown what happened to the goblin after the break-in, but he may have been killed.

==Behind the scenes==
*This goblin was portrayed by an unknown actor in {{DH|F2}}. Despite not being credited, the character appears in a Behind the Scenes feature in disc 2.
