This '''individual''' was the older sister of [[Ismelda Murk]]. She attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting Ceremony|sorted]] into [[Gryffindor]].

==Biography==
She was made a [[Prefect]] and, at some point after graduation, joined the [[British Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="HMC"/>

Her parents were very proud of her achievements, causing them to neglect their younger daughter.<ref name="HMC">{{HM|5|Crushed}}</ref>

Sometime during [[1989–1990 school year]], she wanted to visit Ismelda at Hogwarts.<ref>{{HM|6|25}} - Care of Magical Creatures Class "Jackalope"</ref>
