'''Norman Tuttle''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] theatre producer in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:It's Just Heavenly.png}}</ref>

He was the producer of a production of ''[[Tribeca Follies]]'' that had been running at the [[Sovereign Theatre]] on [[Broadway]] for two years when [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] visited [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="poster" />
