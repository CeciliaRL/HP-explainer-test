'''Brandon Angel''' was a [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. He once signed up for [[Quidditch]] training at 7pm on Tuesday on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in the [[Gryffindor Common Room]].<ref>{{See image|File:QuidditchTrainingSignupSheet.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Brandon is from a surname which was derived from a place name meaning "hill covered with broom" in Old English.
