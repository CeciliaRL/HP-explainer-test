'''Albert Belchant''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and member of the up and coming [[Bellchant family]].<ref name="FBC5"/>

==Biography==
His father Astor received [[Hogwarts letter to Astor Bellchant|a letter]] from Hogwarts detailing how Albert was failing [[Transfiguration (class)|Transfiguration]], [[Charms (class)|Charms]], and [[Herbology]]. This motivated Astor to blackmail [[Bilius Finbok]], with whom he had attended Hogwarts, into brewing an illegal mental acuity [[potion]]. [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]] and [[Mathilda Grimblehawk's partner|her partner]] investigated the situation after Finbok was injured and sent to [[St David's Hospital|a Muggle hospital]].<ref name="FBC5">{{FBC|5}}</ref>
