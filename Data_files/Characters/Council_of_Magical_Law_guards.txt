==Biography==
In [[1981]], they brought in [[Igor Karkaroff]] for a [[Trial of Igor Karkaroff|hearing]] about ''[[Death Eaters|names]]''. Later, they also brought in [[Bartemius Crouch Junior|Bartemius Crouch Jnr]] and sent him to [[Azkaban]].<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}} - Chapter 24 (''The Pensieve'')</ref>
