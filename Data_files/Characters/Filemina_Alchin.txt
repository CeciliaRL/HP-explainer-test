'''Filemina Alchin''' (b. [[1670s]]/[[1680s|80s]]) was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1690s]]. She was once [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]] from [[1692]] to [[1696]].<ref>{{HPE}} {{See image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Plaque.jpg}} and {{see image|File:Gryffindor Captains Plaque.jpg}}</ref>
