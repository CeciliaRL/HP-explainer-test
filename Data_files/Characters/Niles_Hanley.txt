'''Niles Hanley''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
During the [[1996–1997 school year]], he frequently stood at one of the [[Viaduct]]s, forcing [[Harry Potter]] to either [[Duelling|duel]] him or drive him away with [[Stink Pellets]] in order to get past.
