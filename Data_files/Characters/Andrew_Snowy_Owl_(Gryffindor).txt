'''Andrew Snowy Owl''' was a [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

Andrew added his name to a sign-up sheet for the [[Hogwarts Gobstone Club]] that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in [[Gryffindor Tower]].<ref>See [[:File:GobstonesClubSignUpSheet.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref> He also registered for a trip to [[Hogsmeade]] on another sign-up sheet posted on the same bulletin board, which was scheduled for Saturday the 15th in an unknown month.<ref name="hogsmeade">See [[:File:HogsmeadeTripSignUpSheet.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is a presumably a tribute to Andrew Ackland-Snow, who worked as an art director on all eight [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]].
