'''Honoria Nutcombe''' (first name alternatively spelt "'''Honouria'''")<ref name="PS">Her [[Chocolate Frog Card|Famous Wizard Card]] in the [[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game)|video game adaptation]] of {{PS}} lists her as "Honouria" Nutcombe.</ref> ([[1665]] – [[1743]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]]<ref>[[Chocolate Frog Card]] found under the "Famous Witches" section, rather than "Hags"</ref> who founded the [[Society for the Reformation of Hags]].

==Behind the scenes==
*Honoria was depicted with green skin in all of her appearances, although she was a human [[Wizardkind|witch]]. It is possible that her skin colour changed due to an illness or a potion, or that she had some hag ancestry.
