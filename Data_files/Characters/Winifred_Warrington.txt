'''Winifred Warrington''' was a witch,  the daughter of a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[Warrington sisters' father|father]]. Her [[Warrington sisters' grandmother|grandmother]] and her sister, [[Ivy Warrington]] were witches.<ref name="MA" />

During her childhood, Winifred accidentally caused herself to vanish, and their grandmother put a [[Memory Charm]] on her so she would forget what happened to her, but it caused her to completely forget Winifred.<ref name="MA" />
