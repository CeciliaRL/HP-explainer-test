'''Jo King''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and a [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="library">See [[:File:LibraryBooksToBeReturned.png|this image]] and [[:File:LibraryBooksToBeReturned2.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref><ref name="gobstones">See [[:File:GobstonesClubSignUpSheet.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

==Biography==
Sometime in the [[1990s]], the [[Hogwarts Library|library]] books Jo took out were overdue, and thus her name was included on a list of students with overdue books that was put up on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in [[Gryffindor Tower]] by [[Irma Pince|Madam Pince]].<ref name="library" /> She personally signed up to join the [[Hogwarts Gobstone Club]] on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the Gryffindor bulletin board.<ref name="gobstones" /> She also registered for a trip to [[Hogsmeade]] on another sign-up sheet posted on the Gryffindor bulletin board, which was scheduled for Saturday the 15th in an unknown month.<ref name="hogsmeade">See [[:File:HogsmeadeTripSignUpSheet.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>
