'''Marina Sanchez''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1980s]].<ref name="HM" />

==Biography==
Marina Sanchez started attending Hogwarts on [[1 September]] [[1984]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into an unknown [[Hogwarts Houses|House]].<ref name="HM" />

During her third year, Marina took [[Care of Magical Creatures]] as one of her electives.<ref name="HM" />

During the [[1989–1990 school year]], her [[sixth year]], Marina proceeded to take [[Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test|N.E.W.T.]] level [[Charms (class)|Charms]], [[Herbology (class)|Herbology]], [[Transfiguration (class)|Transfiguration]], [[History of Magic]] and [[Care of Magical Creatures]].<ref name="HM" />

==Behind the scenes==
*Her House is the same as that of [[Jacob's sibling]], which is chosen by the player in the game.
*Marina replaces [[Rowan Khanna]] on the Leaderboard and classes after Year 6 Chapter 18.
