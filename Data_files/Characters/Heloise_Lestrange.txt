'''Heloise Lestrange''' (née '''Beaufort''') was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She married [[Corvus Lestrange (I)|Corvus Lestrange]] and had two sons with him, [[Corvus Lestrange (II)|Corvus]] and [[Falco Lestrange|Falco]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
