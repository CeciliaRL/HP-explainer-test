'''Music classroom monitor''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] during the [[1990s]].

He attended [[Music]] classes and worked in the capacity of classroom monitor, in order to assist the [[Unidentified 1992–1993 Music teacher|professor]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}} - GBC version</ref>

==Biography==
===1992===
When [[Harry Potter]] gained possession of a harmonica during his second year, he thought he should give it to the [[Music]] [[Unidentified 1992–1993 Music teacher|Professor]]. Finding out he was absent from his [[Music Classroom|classroom]], he gave it to his assistant. In exchange for the harmonica, in return, the classroom monitor gave Harry a [[magic]] [[whistle]].<ref name="COSG" />
