'''Dawn Withey''' was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as a [[Beater]] on the [[English National Quidditch team]].<ref>{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Withey's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Ethan Bexley]] at an [[Exploding Snap]] challenge.<ref>{{HBP|G}} {{c|NDS edition}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Dawn" comes from the English word dawn, ultimately derived from Old English dagung.
