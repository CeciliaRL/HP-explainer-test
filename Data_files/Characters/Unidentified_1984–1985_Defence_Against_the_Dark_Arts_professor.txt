This '''individual''' became the [[professor]] of [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] for the [[1984–1985 school year]].

==Biography==
However, because of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s [[Jinx on the post of Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher|jinx]] on the post, this individual was "lost" by the end of the year and needed to be replaced. According to [[Penny Haywood]], [[Severus Snape]] tried unsuccessfully to get the job.<ref name="professor">{{HM|1|8}}</ref>

During a discussion with Penny Haywood, [[Jacob's sibling]] mentioned how they felt that they did not learn anything in Defence Against the Dark Arts that year, possibly indicating that the professor was not very good.<ref name="professor"/>
