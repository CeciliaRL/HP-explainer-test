This '''goblin''' worked at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] in [[1998]] after the [[Ministry of Magic]]'s fall to the [[Death Eaters]].<ref name="DHF2"/>

==Biography==
===1998===
He is present on [[1 May]], [[1998]] when [[Harry Potter]], [[Hermione Granger]], [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] and [[Griphook]] planned a [[Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)|Break-in]] in order to get one of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort's]] [[Horcrux]]es, [[Helga Hufflepuff's Cup]], from the [[Lestrange Vault]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

===Later life===
It is unknown what happened to the goblin after the break-in but he may have been killed.
