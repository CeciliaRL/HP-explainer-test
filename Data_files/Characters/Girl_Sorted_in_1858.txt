This '''girl''' was a [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] student<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|scottish-rugby}}</ref> from [[1858]] to [[1865]].<ref name="Wikipedia">{{Wikilink|Angus Buchanan (rugby)}}</ref>

During her [[Sorting ceremony]] on [[1 September]], [[1858]], her name was called, but [[Angus Buchanan]], a [[Squib]] pretending to actually be a [[wizardkind|wizard]] who was among the crowd of [[first year|first-years]], threw himself ahead of her in sheer desperation and placed the [[Sorting Hat]] upon his head. The Hat announced kindly that he was "''a good-hearted chap, but no wizard''", and the boy left the hall in tears.<ref name="Pottermore"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Depending on her month of birth, this girl may have been a classmate of [[Phineas Nigellus Black]] at Hogwarts.
