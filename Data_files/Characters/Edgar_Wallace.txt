'''Richard Horatio Edgar Wallace''' ([[1 April]], [[1875]]—[[10 February]], [[1932]]) was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[England|English]] crime writer, journalist, novelist, screenwriter, and playwright, who wrote 175 novels, 24 plays, and numerous articles in newspapers and journals.

Edgar Wallace was also the author of ''The Flying Squad''. [[Albus Dumbledore]] kept a copy of the Swedish translation of this book (''[[Kokain Nästet]]'') in his [[Headmaster's office|office]] at [[Hogwarts Castle]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="chicagotribune">[http://www.chicagotribune.com/sns-harry-potter-tour-pg,0,1261345.photogallery "Sneak peek inside the "Making of Harry Potter" UK studio tour"] on ''[http://www.chicagotribune.com/ The Chicago Tribune]''</ref>
