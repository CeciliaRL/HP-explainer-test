'''Isaac''' was the second of the three [[Wikipedia:Patriarchs (Bible)|Patriarchs]] of the [[Bible]].<ref name="wikipedia">"[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isaac Isaac] on Wikipedia</ref> He was the son of [[Abraham]], who, in a well-known Biblical account, was called upon by [[God]] to offer him as sacrifice in order to test his faith.<ref name="PSf22">{{PS|F}} - Chapter 22 (''Cloaked in Darkness'') {{see image|File:ScreamingFaceBook.jpg}}</ref>

Isaac was mentioned in a [[shrieking book|book]] that was kept in the [[Restricted Section]] of the [[Hogwarts Library|library]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] by the [[1990s]].<ref name="PSf22" />

==Behind the scenes==
*In {{PS|FA}}, the [[shrieking book]], in which Isaac is mentioned, appears to be a copy of ''[http://books.google.ca/books?id=tSs3AAAAMAAJ&printsec=frontcover#v=onepage&q=&f=false The Works of the Most Reverend Dr John Tillotson]''.
