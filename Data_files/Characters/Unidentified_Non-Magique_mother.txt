This '''woman''' was a [[France|French]] [[Non-magic people|Non-Magique]] resident of [[Paris]] in the [[1920s]].

==Biography==
Together with her [[Unidentified Non-Magique father|husband]] and their [[Unidentified Non-Magique toddler|child]], she lived with them in a [[Unidentified home used by Gellert Grindelwald|apartment]] on [[Boulevard Haussmann]] but was murdered by [[Carrow (1920s)|Carrow]] under the orders of Grindelwald himself. 

They later used the [[Non-Magique family|family]]'s [[Unidentified home used by Gellert Grindelwald|home]] as a temporary headquarters, from where he conducted a search for [[Credence Barebone]].<ref name="COGS">{{COG|S}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The mother was portrayed by [[Kayt Webster-Brown]] in {{COG}}.

==Etymology==
The name "Chérie" originates from [[France|French]] background, the name means "darling".
