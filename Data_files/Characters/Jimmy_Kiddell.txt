'''Jimmy Kiddell''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[wandmaker]] who owned the shop [[Jimmy Kiddell's Wonderful Wands]] in [[Diagon Alley]]. He is presumably an inferior wandmaker to [[Garrick Ollivander]] as most wizard children get their wands at [[Ollivanders]].<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Jimmy Kiddell might be a reference to [[Wikipedia:Jimmy Kimmel|Jimmy Kimmel]], an American television host and comedian, currently hosting ''Jimmy Kimmel Live!'', a late-night talk show that airs on ABC.
