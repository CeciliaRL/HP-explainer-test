'''Omar Shaw''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref><ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
He was fond of [[Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes]], and, while at school during the [[1996-1997 school year]], he traded [[Stink Pellets]], [[Fanged Frisbee]]s, and [[Ever-Bashing Boomerang]]s he had purchased at the store for various objects ([[Gobstones]], [[Chocolate Frog Card]]s, [[Wizard's Chess|chess]] pieces, and [[Butterbeer]]). He could usually be seen spending time in the [[The Quad|Paved Courtyard]].<ref name="HBPG"/>

==Etymology==
The name "Omar" means "eloquent speaker" in Hebrew and "long-lived" in Arabic.

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console version]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
