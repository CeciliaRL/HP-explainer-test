}}This '''singer''' was an [[United States of America|American]] female [[goblin]] who sung jazz at the wizarding speakeasy [[The Blind Pig]] during the [[1920s]]. She would use her [[wandless magic]] to create smoky images to accompany her singing.<ref name="FBS82">{{FB|S|82}}</ref>

==Biography==
She started working at the [[The Blind Pig]], sometime before [[1926]]. She was performing with a band of goblin musicians on [[6 December]] [[1926]] when [[Newton Scamander]] and his companions came to obtain information from [[Gnarlak]] regarding Newt's escaped [[beast]]s.<ref name="FBS82" />

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Wandless magic]]''': Being a [[Goblin]], she was shown to be proficient in wandless magic, being able to [[magic]]ally create smoky images to visually assist her musical performances, to a very precise and beautiful effect.

==Behind the scenes==
*The goblin was portrayed by [[Aretha Ayeh]] through motion reference and her singing voice was performed by [[Emmi]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
*She is the first female goblin featured in any [[Harry Potter universe|Harry Potter media]].
