'''Fractcumulus''' was an employee of [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]].<ref name="hypeorlando">"[http://www.hypeorlando.com/in-the-shadow-of-the-mouse/2014/06/20/diagon-alley-features-two-new-rides-in-richly-themed-environment/ Diagon Alley features two new rides in richly themed environment]" from Hype Orlando {{see image|File:GringottsVisitorPass.jpg}}</ref> This individual was responsible for issuing visitor passes allowing people "limited, escorted" access to the bank.<ref name="hypeorlando" /> They also apparently handled transactions at the bank.<ref>{{see image|File:GringottsForm.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*"Fractcumulus" is presumably an altered spelling of "fractocumulus." This term is used to describe low, wispy, somewhat bulging clouds which often appear under [[wikipedia:Nimbostratus cloud|nimbostratus clouds]] when it is raining.<ref>"[http://www.thefreedictionary.com/fractocumulus Fractocumulous]" on The Free Dictionary</ref>
*The species of this individual is unknown but presumed to be a [[Goblin]] or [[human]] [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] by some.
