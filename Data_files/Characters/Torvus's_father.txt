'''Torvus's father''' was a [[centaur]] who lived in the [[Forbidden Forest]].<ref name="HM46"/>

==Biography==
When his son [[Torvus]] was young, he told him about a [[Cursed Vaults|Cursed Vault]] located in the forest, and also told him not to approach it.<ref name="HM46"/>

He inherited [[Torvus's arrow|an arrow]] that had been in his family for a long time.<ref name="HM46">{{HM|4|6}}</ref>
