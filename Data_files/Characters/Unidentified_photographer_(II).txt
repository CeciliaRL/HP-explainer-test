==Biography==
He interviewed a [[Unidentified witness|witness]] who saw a building get destroyed by an [[Obscurus]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]]. After interviewing, he was asked by a [[Unidentified reporter|reporter colleague]] if he felt like getting a drink, to which the photographer replied that he had promised [[Martha (American Muggle)|Martha]] (presumably his wife or girlfriend) that he would abstain from consuming [[alcohol]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>
