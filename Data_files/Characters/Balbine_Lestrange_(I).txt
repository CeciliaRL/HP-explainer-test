'''Balbine Lestrange''' (née '''Leroy''') was a [[France|French]] [[wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She married [[Cyrille Lestrange (II)|Cyrille Lestrange]] and had one son with him, [[Cyrille Lestrange (III)|Cyrille]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref> She might have been the sister of [[Pucine Lestrange (I)|Pucine]].
