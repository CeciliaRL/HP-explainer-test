'''Jacturus Cloke III''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|wizard]], and the last surviving member of the old wizarding [[Cloke family]].<ref name="FBC9-1">{{FBC|9|1}}</ref>

==Biography==
He lived in the [[Cloke Mansion]] near [[Upper Flagley]] along with the [[ghost]] of his ancestor [[Horatio Cloke]].<ref name="FBC9-1"/>

He attacked [[Megan Scott]] and [[Incarcerous Spell|tied her up]] when she was wandering in a nearby graveyard, but was driven off by a protective [[unicorn]]. He had to be [[Stunning Spell|Stupified]] by [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]] when she and [[Mathilda Grimblehawk's partner|her partner]] came to investigate.<ref>{{FBC|9|3}}</ref> He later revealed that he was trying to gather valuable [[unicorn hair]], [[Unicorn horn|horn]], and [[Unicorn blood|blood]] to restore the lost family fortune.<ref>{{FBC|9|4}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Transfiguration]]''': Cloke was capable of performing [[conjuration]], a highly advanced and difficult branch of transfiguration. He effectively conjured ropes with the [[Incarcerous Spell]] to bind [[Megan Scott]] in an [[Upper Flagley]] graveyard.<ref name="FBC9-1"/>
