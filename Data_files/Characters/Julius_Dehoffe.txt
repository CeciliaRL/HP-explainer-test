'''Julius Dehoffe''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the author of ''[[Flying with the Cannons]]'', a book about the history of the [[Chudley Cannons]] [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|team]].<ref>"[http://www.radiotimes.com/news/2013-06-20/from-quidditch-flyers-to-bellatrixs-wanted-poster---see-artwork-from-the-harry-potter-films From Quidditch flyers to Bellatrix's wanted poster - see artwork from the Harry Potter films]" from the Radio Times {{see image|File:FlyingWithTheCannons.png}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Julia Dehoff (real-world)|Julia Dehoff]], who worked as a draughtsman on several [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0215005/ Julia Dehoff]" on IMDb</ref>
