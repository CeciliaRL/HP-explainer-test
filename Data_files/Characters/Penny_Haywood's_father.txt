'''Mr Haywood''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] man from the [[Great Britain|United Kingdom]]. He married a [[muggle-born]] [[Penny Haywood's mother|witch]] and had two [[half-blood]] [[wizardkind|witch]] daughters, [[Penny Haywood|Penny]] and [[Beatrice Haywood|Beatrice]].<ref name="HM5">{{HM|5}} [[History of Magic]]</ref>

His wife took him flying on a [[broomstick]] once, which scared him too much to try again.<ref>{{HM|1}} onward, Flying Lessons in general</ref>

He started playing [[Christmas]] music in [[October]], which drove his wife mad.<ref>{{HM}}, Christmas decorating activity</ref>
