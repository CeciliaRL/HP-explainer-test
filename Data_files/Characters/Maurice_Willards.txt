'''Maurice Willards''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] theatre producer in the early [[20th century]]. He co-produced a production of ''[[Oh, Rosana!]]'' with [[Bernard Lockwood]] that was slated to open at the [[Brockman Theatre]] in [[New York]] on [[4 January]] [[1927]].<ref name="rosana">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:Oh, Rosana!.png}}</ref>
