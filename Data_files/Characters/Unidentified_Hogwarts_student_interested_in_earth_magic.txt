This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into either [[Ravenclaw]] or [[Slytherin]], and was very keen on the subject of [[earth magic]] which he expected to study in the [[1991–1992 school year]].<ref name="PSgbc"/>

==Biography==
===Early life===
{{Quote|Chugga chugga, chugga chugga, woo woo!|The boy, while [[Hogwarts Express|en route]] to [[Hogwarts Castle]], on [[1 September]], [[1991]]|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game)}}
This individual was born no later than [[1980]].<ref name="PSgbc">{{PS|G}} - Game Boy Colour</ref>

===Hogwarts years===
{{Quote|Good for you, Harry. He had it coming.|The boy after [[Harry Potter]] beat [[Draco Malfoy]] in a duel|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game)}}
He started at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in or before [[1991]] and was to be [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into either [[Ravenclaw]] or [[Slytherin]]. He was not very fond of [[Draco Malfoy]], congratulating [[Harry Potter]] after the latter beat him in a [[Duelling|duel]].<ref name="PSgbc"/>

He was expecting to join the [[earth magic]] classes in the start of the [[1991–1992 school year]]. While en route to Hogwarts on [[1 September]], [[1991]], he sat on a compartment while picking his nose and eating his bogeys.<ref name="PSgbc"/>
