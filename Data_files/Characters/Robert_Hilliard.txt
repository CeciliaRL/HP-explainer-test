[[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]], he was made a [[Prefect]] by [[1991]], and welcomed [[First year|first-year]]s when they initially arrived in his [[Hogwarts Houses|House]].<ref name="pottermore">[[Pottermore Introductions to Hogwarts Houses]] (Archived from {{PM}})</ref>
