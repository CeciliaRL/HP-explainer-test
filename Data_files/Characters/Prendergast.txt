'''Prendergast '''was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in around the [[1910s]] in an unknown capacity.

==Biograhy==
He once gave [[Newton Scamander]] a month's worth of detention for arguing that there are no such thing as strange [[creature]]s, just blinkered people. [[Leta Lestrange]] later set off a [[dungbomb]] under Prendergast's desk so she could join Newt in detention.<ref name="COG18">{{COG|S|18}}</ref>
