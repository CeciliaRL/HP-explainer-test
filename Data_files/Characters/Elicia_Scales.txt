'''Elicia Scales''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] who the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] kept tabs on while it was under [[Fall of the Ministry of Magic|Death Eater control]] in [[1997]]. Her file was signed by Chief Registration Authority [[F. L. Wakefield]].<ref>{{HPLE|G}} {{See image|File:EliciaScalesFile.jpg}}</ref>
