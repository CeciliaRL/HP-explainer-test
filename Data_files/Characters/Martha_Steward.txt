'''Martha Steward '''was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]]. She was the mother of [[James Steward]], who co-founded [[Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] with his wife, [[Isolt Sayre]].

==Biography==
Martha and her husband had at least one child, [[James Steward]]. They came from [[Great Britain|Britain]]. James left and went with the Plymouth settlement to the New World where he married a witch, [[Isolt Sayre]] and had two daughters, [[Martha Steward II|Martha Steward]] and [[Rionach Steward]]. Martha died sometime before 1632, since her granddaughter was said to have been named after her late grandmother.<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|ilvermorny}}</ref>
