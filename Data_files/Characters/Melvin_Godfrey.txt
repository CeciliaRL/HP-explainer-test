'''Melvin Godfrey''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] orchestra conductor in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:WhenBroadwaySleeps.jpg}}</ref>

He served as the musical director of a production of the comedy play ''[[When Broadway Sleeps]]'' that ran at the [[Glovers Comedy Theatre]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="poster" />
