'''Richard Griffiths''' (fl. [[1997]]) was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] actor who had a large part in ''[[Equus]]'', a play that was staged sometime in the late [[1990s]]. There was a poster mentioning him at [[Luchino Caffe]] in the summer of [[1997]].<ref name="DHpt1">{{DH|F1}} {{see image|File:Deathly Hallows Equus poster.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This mention is an obvious reference to [[Richard Griffiths]], the actor who played [[Vernon Dursley]] in the [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]] and who also had a part in ''[[Wikipedia:Equus (play)|Equus]]'' in the 2007 revival.

==Etymology==
The name "Richard" means "brave power", derived from the Germanic elements ric "power, rule" and hard "brave, hardy".
