'''Ashley Sanders''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and a former [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] for the [[Gryffindor]] [[Gryffindor Quidditch team|Quidditch team]].<ref name="PMPSH">{{PM}} for Playstation Home {{see image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Pottermore.png}}</ref>

By the early [[1990s]], there was a plaque on the mantlepiece at [[Gryffindor Tower]] listing her as a former Gryffindor Quidditch Captain.<ref name="PMPSH"/>

==Behind the scenes==
