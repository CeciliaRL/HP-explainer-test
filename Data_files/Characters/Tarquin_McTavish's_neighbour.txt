[[Tarquin McTavish]] had a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] '''neighbour'''. For unknown reasons, Tarquin [[magic]]ally trapped this neighbour inside a [[Tarquin McTavish's teakettle|teakettle]], which led to the [[Wizardkind|wizard]]'s imprisonment.<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref><ref name="PM">"Timeline of the Wizarding World (infographic)" at {{PM}} {{c|Archived; view [https://www.reddit.com/r/PottermoreWritings/comments/4woua4/timeline_of_the_wizarding_world/ transcript on Reddit]}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Tarquin" comes from the Roman name Tarquinius which is of unknown meaning, possibly Etruscan in origin. This was the name of two early kings of Rome.
