==Biography==

He interviewed [[Unidentified housewife|two]] [[Unidentified hobo|witnesses]] when some of [[Newton Scamander]]'s creatures were accidentally released from his [[Newton Scamander's suitcase|suitcase]], where one believed it was a gas explosion and the other was going to tell what he saw before he got [[Memory Charm|obliviated]] to make him agree with the other witnesses belief causing some arguments and paranoia in the crowd.<ref>{{FB|S|31}}</ref>
