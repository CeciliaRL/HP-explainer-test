'''Alannis Shepley''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Ravenclaw]]<ref name="house" /> student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She played casual [[Quidditch]] matches with other students during the [[1996–1997 school year]].<ref>{{HBP|G}} {{c|NDS edition}}</ref>
