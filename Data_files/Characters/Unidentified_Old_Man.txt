The '''old man''' was a character in the tale ''[[The Wizard and the Hopping Pot]]'' by [[Beedle the Bard]]. His [[Old man's donkey (The Wizard and the Hopping Pot)|donkey]] either ran away or was stolen, and he asked the [[Wizard (The Wizard and the Hopping Pot)|Wizard]] to help him, for without the donkey to carry his wares to the market, his family would starve. The Wizard rudely refused to help him.<ref name="TBB">{{TBB|R}} - Chapter 1 (''The Wizard and the Hopping Pot'')</ref>
