'''Rosana Amorim''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] who wrote the book ''[[Spellman's Syllabary]]''.<ref>"[http://www.mugglenet.com/2016/01/harry-potter-celebration-day-one-recap/ A Celebration of Harry Potter Day 1 Highlights!]" from [[MuggleNet]] {{see image|File:SpellmansSyllabary.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*Rosana Amorim sounds like a Brazilian given name as this name and surname are very common in [[Brazil]], which may indicate the nationality of the author. Rosana is the agglutination of names Rosa (means "rose") and Ana (variant of "Anna"). Amorim is a surname with Latin origin and means "a place of lovers".
