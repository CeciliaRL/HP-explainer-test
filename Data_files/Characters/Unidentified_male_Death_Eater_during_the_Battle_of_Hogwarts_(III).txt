This '''individual''' was a [[Dark Wizard]] and a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] who served [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]].

==Biography==
In [[1997]], he was present during the [[Meeting at Malfoy Manor|Meeting]] at [[Malfoy Manor]].<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref> In [[1998]], he participated in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]], and was present in the [[Forbidden Forest]] when [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]] ''killed'' [[Harry Potter]]. He was later seen when the [[Death Eaters]] were fleeing from the battle.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref> After Voldemort's defeat he was possibly killed or imprisoned in [[Azkaban]] afterwards.

==Physical Appearance==
This Death Eater seemed to have fairly large ears, fairly short brown hair, and sunken eyes. He also had a large "Adam's Apple" around his neck area.

==Behind the scenes==
*This [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] was portrayed by [[Tony Kirwood]] in {{DH|F2}}.
