==Biography==
During the first match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]] against [[Ivorian National Quidditch team|the Ivory Coast]], Kristoffersen beat her opposite [[Sylvian Boigny]] to the [[Golden Snitch]] after 128 minutes of play, securing a win for Norway.<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[NORWAY VERSUS IVORY COAST]] (Archived)</ref>
