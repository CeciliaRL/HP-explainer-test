'''Angelus Moriattis''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who lived during the [[20th century]].<ref name="trophy">See [[:File:UnidentifiedHogwartsTrophy.jpg|this image]] of a prop trophy used in the [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]] on display at {{WWHP}}.</ref>

==Biography==
In his youth, he attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], where he received an unknown award in [[1942]].<ref name="trophy" />

His name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of [[Sybill Trelawney|Professor Trelawney's]] [[Divination (class)|Divination]] [[Divination Classroom|classroom]] by [[1993]].<ref>{{POA|F}} - Chapter 21 (''Professor Trelawney's Prediction'') {{see image|File:Angelus Moriattis.jpg}}</ref><ref>[http://www.the-leaky-cauldron.org/2004/5/15/los-angeles-motion-picture-arts-sciences-contemporary-set-exhibit-report ''Prisoner of Azkaban'' set report] from the Leaky Cauldron</ref><ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:DivinationGraffitiWall.jpg}}</ref>
