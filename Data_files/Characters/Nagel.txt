'''Nagel''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who lived during the early [[20th century]]. He was a member of [[Gellert Grindelwald]]'s [[Alliance]], and was among the dark wizard's foremost acolytes.<ref name="TLC">[http://www.the-leaky-cauldron.org/2018/08/24/fantastic-beasts-the-crimes-of-grindelwald-full-character-profiles-reveal-trouble-in-paris/ Full Character Profiles] from [https://www.the-leaky-cauldron.org The Leaky Cauldron]</ref>

==Biography==
[[File:Nagel talking with Grindelwald.jpg|140px|thumb|left|Nagel talking with Grindelwald]]
Nagel joined [[Gellert Grindelwald]] at some point prior to [[1927]], and became one of Grindelwald's seven trusted [[Gellert Grindelwald's acolytes|acolytes]]. Under Grindelwald's orders, Nagel was told to visit the [[Circus Arcanus]]' performance, so as to keep an eye on the [[Obscurial]] [[Credence Barebone]].<ref name="COGS">{{COG|S}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*In Swedish, Norwegian, German and Dutch, "nagel" means "nail".

==Behind the scenes==
*Nagel is portrayed by [[Claudius Peters]] in the [[Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald|second instalment]] of the [[Fantastic Beasts (film series)|''Fantastic Beasts'' film series]].
*In ''[[The Art of Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald]]'', Nagel's early portrayal resembled [[Percival Graves]].
*In an early draft of the the storyboard, Nagel was meant to meet up with Credence in the bird market.
