'''Katie Rayknolls''' (b. [[1710s]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1720s]]. She was [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Slytherin]] [[Quidditch]] [[Slytherin Quidditch team|team]] from [[1724]] to [[1728]].<ref>{{HPPS}}, p. 349 {{see image|File:Slytherin Captains Plaque 2.jpg}}</ref>
