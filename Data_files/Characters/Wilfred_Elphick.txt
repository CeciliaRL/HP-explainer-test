'''Wilfred Elphick''' ([[1112]]-[[1199]]) was the first [[Wizardkind|wizard]] to be gored by an [[Africa]]n [[Erumpent]], which is an extremely unlikely event, given the fact that the [[Erumpent horn|horn]] of an Erumpent almost always explodes on contact, due to the [[Exploding Fluid|fluid]] it contains.<ref name="COSG"/> On his [[Chocolate Frog Card]], he was shown in bandages, showing he survived this attack.<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Wilfred means "wanting peace," from the Old English ''wil'', "desire," and ''fred'', "peace."<ref>"[http://www.behindthename.com/name/wilfred Wilfred]" on Behind the Name</ref> The surname Elphick derived from the Old English given name Aelfheah, which meant "high elf," from ''aelf'', "elf," and ''heah'', "high."<ref>"[http://www.surnamedb.com/surname.aspx?name=Elphick Elphick]" on The Internet Surname Database</ref>
