This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' (born c. [[1976]]-[[1978]])<ref name="age"/> attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], beginning in, at latest, [[1989]].<ref name="POAG"/>

==Biography==
He was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin]] House, his [[Head of House]] being [[Severus Snape]], and was made a [[Prefect]] in his [[fifth year]].

During the [[1993–1994 school year]], he patrolled the [[third-floor corridor]], while his Head of House patrolled the [[Gunhilda of Gorsemoor Corridor]] at least one night. [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley]] and [[Hermione Granger]] had to sneak past him multiple times, most notably after secretly obtaining [[Fairy wings]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}} - Console versions</ref>
