'''Lorcan d'Eath''' (b. [[1964]]) was a [[part-vampire]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] singer that was widely fancied by witches. He spent nineteen weeks at number one with his song "[[Necks to You]]."<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}} {{see image|File:Lorcand'eath.jpeg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*﻿Lorcan means "fierce one" in Gaelic, a common name for fictional vampires. The name d'Eath is an obvious reference to death.

==Behind the scenes==
*Lorcan shares his name with the [[Lorcan|son]] of [[Luna Lovegood]].
*If Lorcan attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]], he may have been in the same year as [[Glenda Chittock]], during the [[Marauders]] fifth year.
