'''Dietrich Frenzen''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was hired as a [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch referee|referee]] in or prior to [[1994]].

==Physical appearance==
Frenzen had short white hair and silvery-white stubble covering his rather tan, round face. His prominent cheekbones and dark, bushy eyebrows gave an impression of pride, and his dark eyes shone.

==Etymology==
''Dietrich'' is the [[Germany|German]] form of Theodoric, which in turn means "ruler of the people"<ref name="DIETRICH">[http://www.behindthename.com/name/dietrich Dietrich] on [http://www.behindthename.com/ Behind the Name]</ref><ref name="THEODORIC">[http://www.behindthename.com/name/theodoric Theodoric] on [http://www.behindthename.com/ Behind the Name]</ref>.
