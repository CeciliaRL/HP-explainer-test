'''Timothy Morcott''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
During the [[1996–1997 school year|1996-1997 school year]], he stood in front of a door at one end of the [[Stone Bridge]], forcing [[Harry Potter]] to beat him in a [[Duelling|duel]] in order to get past. He might be the same student that stood in front of Harry in the [[Training Grounds]] to get past.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Timothy" comes from the Greek name Τιμοθεος (Timotheos) meaning "honouring God", derived from τιμαω (timao) "to honour" and θεος (theos) "god".
