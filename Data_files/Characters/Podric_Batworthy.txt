'''Podric Batworthy''', {{OM|First}}, {{c|[[Grand Sorcerer|Grand Sorc.]]}}, was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] whose [[portrait]] hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|PA}}</ref> Batworthy lived in or after the [[15th century]], seeing as that was when the [[Wizengamot]] created the [[Order of Merlin]] award.<ref name="pottermore-ordermerlin"/>

Podric Batworthy is, in all likelihood, an ancestor of [[Podric Batworthy XXIII]], who ran [[Beard Trimming]] in [[Diagon Alley]] in the [[1990s]].

==Behind the scenes==
*The portrait of Podric Batworthy appears to be based on [https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Charles_Dickens_by_Daniel_Maclise.jpg an 1839 portrait of Charles Dickens by Daniel Maclise].
