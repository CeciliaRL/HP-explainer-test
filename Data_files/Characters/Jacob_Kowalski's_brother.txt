'''Mr Kowalski''' (died [[1914]]-[[1918]]) was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Muggle|No-Maj]] man who fought in the [[First World War]] and was ultimately killed during it.

He had a brother, [[Jacob Kowalski|Jacob]], who also served in the war but survived. He may have been born in [[Poland]] like his brother was.<ref name="poland">''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: Magical Movie Handbook]]'' {{see image|File:Jacob Kowalski background - movie handbook.png}}</ref>
