'''A. Osborn''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] and the author of ''[[Shrubs & Trees for the Garden]]'', a book published by [[Ward Lock & Co.]], about floriculture and gardening.<ref name="DHpromo">{{DH|F1}} - see [http://galeria.potterish.com/displayimage.php?album=4714&pid=211699#top_display_media this image].</ref><ref name="AbeBooks">[http://www.abebooks.co.uk/servlet/BookDetailsPL?bi=4719561891&searchurl=an%3Da%2Bosborn%26fe%3Don%26sortby%3D3%26tn%3Dshrubs%2Band%2Btrees%2Bfor%2Bthe%2Bgarden "Shrubs and Trees for the Garden"] at [http://www.abebooks.co.uk/ AbeBooks.co.uk]</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
In real life, Arthur Osborn (fl. [[1933]]) was also head of the arboretum at the [[Wikipedia:Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew|Royal Botanic Gardens at Kew]].<ref name="AbeBooks" />
