'''Tamsin Applebee''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], and was a member of [[Hufflepuff]] house. She played as a  [[Chaser]] on the [[Hufflepuff Quidditch team]] when it was captained by [[Cedric Diggory]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Tamsin is a contracted form of Thomasina. It was traditionally used in [[Cornwall]].
