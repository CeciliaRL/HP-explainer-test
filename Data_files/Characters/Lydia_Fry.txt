'''Lydia Fry''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] media personality. She gave an interview in the [[28 February|28th February]]-[[26 March|26th March]] [[1997]] issue of ''[[My Teeth]]'', in which she shared her latest personal tips on life and maintaining dental health.<ref>{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:MyTeeth.png}}</ref> In the summer of that same year, she gave an interview in ''[[Radio Times]]'', in which she discussed what motivated her to take up [[fishing]].<ref>{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:RadioTimes.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after [[Lydia Fry (real-world)|Lydia Fry]], who worked as an uncredited temporary graphic design assistant on {{HBP}},  and as an art department assistant on {{DH|F1}} and {{DH|F2}}.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2490919/ Lydia Fry]" on IMDb.com</ref>
