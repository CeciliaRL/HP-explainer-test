This '''Hornbill''' was a pet male<ref name="WU"/> [[bird]] owned by [[Minerva McGonagall]] from [[1992]] or earlier.<ref name="COSF"/>

==History==
[[File:COS_Vera_Verto_demo.gif|thumb|left|McGonagall using her bird to demonstrate ''Vera Verto'']]
In [[1992]], [[Minerva McGonagall|Professor McGonagall]] used the bird to demonstrate the [[Transforming Spells|transforming spell]], ''[[Vera Verto]],'' in front of her [[Second year|second-years']] [[Transfiguration (class)|Transfiguration]] class.<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}} - Chapter 14 (''About the Chamber'')</ref>

During the [[2020s]], this hornbill appeared as a [[Foundable]] during the [[Calamity]].<ref name="WU"/>
