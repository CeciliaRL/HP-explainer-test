'''Bethany Harrison''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] child, the daughter of [[Don Harrison]], a construction foreman.

==Biography==
Bethany made a [[Bethany Harrison's sketch|sketch]] of a [[Winifred Whittle|witch]] that provided a clue for [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]] and her partner investigating the [[magic]]al [[beast]] attack at the construction site.

Soon after, Bethany went missing, although the flowers in her [[Bethany Harrison's toybox|toybox]] provided further clues as to her possible whereabouts. Her [[Bethany Harrison's fairy necklace|fairy necklace]] was later found in [[Winifred Whittle]]'s [[Winifred Whittle's cottage|cottage]] and she was ultimately escorted out of the forest by [[Magnus]], a [[centaur]].<ref>{{FBC|3}}</ref>

Her memory was later [[Memory Charm|Obliviated]].<ref>{{FBC|3|4}}</ref>
