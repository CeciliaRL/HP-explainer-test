'''Oliver Wiseman''' (b. [[1954]]) was a [[Detective Inspector]] for the [[Westminster]] [[Anti-Terrorism Unit]]. He investigated the [[Death Eaters|collapse]] of the [[Millennium Bridge]] in [[1996]], but refused to give ''[[The Guardian]]'' any statement about it.<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:TheGuardian.png|this image]])</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The naming is a tribute to the real-life [[Oliver Wiseman (real-world)|Oliver Wiseman]], who served as the assistant to director [[David Heyman]] in the final three films of the ''[[Harry Potter (film series)|Harry Potter]]'' series.<ref name="IMDb">[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2567472/?ref_=fn_al_nm_1 IMDb entry on Oliver Wiseman]</ref>
