This '''goblin''' was a [[spokesgoblin]] employed by [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] in the early [[1990s]]. After the [[Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1991)|break-in]] at the bank on [[31 July]], [[1991]], this spokesgoblin gave an interview to the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'', advising wizards to keep their noses out of [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank|Gringotts]] business.<ref name="PS8">{{PS|B|8}}</ref>
