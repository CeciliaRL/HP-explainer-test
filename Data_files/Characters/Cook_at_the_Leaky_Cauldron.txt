This '''cook''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] who worked at the [[Leaky Cauldron]] in [[1993]]. For unknown reasons, [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] showed this cook the [[Daily Prophet articles|Daily Prophet article]] about his family's trip to [[Egypt]].<ref name="POAF">{{POA|F}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This cook seems to be a skilled and perhaps somewhat morbid witch or wizard, as the pea soup at the Leaky Cauldron, presumably made by him or her, is noted to be able to eat the person who orders it.
