'''Mrs Warbeck''' was the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] wife of [[Celestina Warbeck's father|Mr Warbeck]], a [[wizardkind|wizard]], and the mother of internationally famous singer [[Celestina Warbeck]].

==Biography==
Mrs Warbeck, a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]], lived in [[Wales]] in the early [[20th century]]. Before [[marriage|marrying]], she pursued a career in acting, but was not successful. She met [[Celestina Warbeck's father|Mr Warbeck]], a [[wizardkind|wizard]] who worked in the [[Muggle Liaison Office]], after she was attacked (presumably during a performance) by a [[Lethifold]] that had hidden itself by taking the form of a stage curtain.<ref name="Pottermore" /> The couple married on an unknown date and had at least one child, [[Celestina Warbeck|Celestina]], who was born [[18 August]]<ref name="Pottermore" /> [[1917]].<ref name="chocolatefrog">[[Chocolate Frog Card|Famous Wizard Card]] ([[Celestina Warbeck]])</ref>

Celestina proved to be an exceptionally gifted singer at a young age.<ref name="Pottermore" /> Mrs Warbeck wished to enrol Celestina in wizarding stage school, but upon learning there was no such thing, she agreed to allow her to attend [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="Pottermore" /> Fiercely supportive of her daughter, Mrs Warbeck sent many letters to the school, requesting the creation of a [[Frog Choir|choir]], drama club, and dancing class to allow young Celestina to showcase her talents.<ref name="Pottermore" />
