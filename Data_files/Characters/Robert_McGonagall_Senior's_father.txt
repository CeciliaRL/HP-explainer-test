'''Mr McGonagall''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who lived in a village in [[Scotland]]. With [[Robert McGonagall's mother|his wife]], he had at least one child, [[Robert McGonagall Senior|Robert]]. When the couple's son eloped with [[Isobel Ross]], the daughter of their neighbours, the [[Ross family|Rosses]], it made them extremely angry.<ref name="pottermore">{{WW|professor-mcgonagall}}</ref>
