}}'''Mrs Boot '''was a [[Scotland|Scottish]] [[wizardkind|witch]] who worked for the [[Ministry of Magic]] during the [[17th century]]. She was the wife of [[Webster Boot]], one of the founders of [[Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].

==Biography==
Mrs Boot was born in [[Scotland]] sometime in the [[17th century]]. She was likely educated at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="Pottermore" />

She eventually landed an unspecified job with the [[Ministry of Magic]]. When [[Webster Boot]], as an [[Auror]] for hire, travelled to [[London]] to repatriate a fugitive British [[Dark Wizard|dark wizard]], they both fell in love. Webster then relocated to [[Great Britain|Britain]] to be with her. Their descendants, likely including [[Terry Boot]], would go on to be educated at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]].<ref name="Pottermore" />
