}}'''Vincent Duc de Trefle-Picques''' (fl. [[1794]]) was a [[France|French]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and nobleman, active around the time of the [[Wikipedia:French Revolution|French Revolution]]. In his youth, he attended [[Beauxbatons Academy of Magic]]. He managed to escape the [[Terror|Reign of Terror]] of [[1793]]-[[1794]] by casting a [[Concealment Charm]] on his neck and pretending that his head had already been cut off.<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|beauxbatons-academy-of-magic}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Vincent was able to cast a [[Concealment Charm]] on his neck to pretend that his had already been cut off to escape the [[Terror|Reign of Terror]].<ref name="Pottermore"/>

==Etymology==
''Trèfle'' and ''pique'' are the French names for the [[Wikipedia:Suit (cards)|card suits]] known in English as clubs and spades, respectively.
