'''This individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Merula Snyde]]'s aunt. She became her legal guardian after [[Merula Snyde]]'s [[Merula Snyde's mother|mother]] and [[Merula Snyde's father|father]] were both sent to [[Azkaban]] for being [[Death Eaters]] after the [[First Wizarding War]], although rather than living with her, she would let Merula live at the empty house of her parents, although she checked in regularly.<ref name="HM528">{{HM|5|28}}</ref>
