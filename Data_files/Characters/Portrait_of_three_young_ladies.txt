These '''young ladies''' were [[Wizardkind|witches]] whose portrait hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] in the [[1990s]], between the [[Fat Lady]] and a [[portrait of two lovers in a graveyard]]. The three young ladies were all dressed in white, one of them usually blowing bubbles.<ref name="PA13">{{POA|F}} - Chapter 13 (''Flight of the Fat Lady'')</ref>
