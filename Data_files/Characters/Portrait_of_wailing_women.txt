This '''portrait''' depicted a group of three [[wikipedia:Middle Eastern|Middle Eastern]] women loudly wailing.

==History==
This portrait hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] in the [[1990s]], on the seventh-floor landing of the [[Grand Staircase]], between a [[portrait of a witch flying to the moon]] and a [[portrait of two lovers in a graveyard]].<ref name="PA13">{{POA|F}} - Chapter 13 (''Flight of the Fat Lady'')</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*One of the women in the portrait was portrayed by [[Sally Samad]].<ref name="sallysamad">[http://www.castingcallpro.com/uk/actor/profile/sally-samad Sally Samad's profile on Casting Call Pro]</ref> Her casting agency profile lists her role as "Burbur [sic] Lady."<ref name="sallysamad" /> The [[wikipedia:Berbers|Berbers]] are an ethnic group indigenous to [[Africa|North Africa]], including [[Egypt]], and their traditional dress is consistent with costumes worn by the actresses in the portrait.<ref>"[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berbers Berbers]" on Wikipedia</ref>
