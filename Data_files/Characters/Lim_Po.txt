'''Lim Po''' was a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].<ref name="fb-wanted">{{FB|F}} {{see image|File:LimPoWanted.jpg}}</ref>

==Biography==
In or around [[1926]], the [[International Wizarding Police]] issued a wanted poster for Po, which stated that he was wanted for "[[Muggle|No-Maj]] and [[House-elf|elf]] murder[s]" committed in [[Asia]] and the [[United States of America|United States]], as well as for "counterfeiting [[wand]]s."<ref name="fb-wanted" /> He was described as "powerful" and "extremely dangerous."<ref name="fb-wanted" /> A reward of 5000 [[dragot]]s was offered for information leading to his capture.<ref name="fb-wanted" />
