}}'''Deliverance Dane''' (died [[15 June]] [[1735]]) was an [[United States of America|American]] woman accused of [[wizardkind|witchcraft]] during the [[Salem Witch Trials]] of [[1692]] and [[1693]].<ref name="wiki" />

==History==
In [[1926]], [[Porpentina Goldstein|Tina Goldstein]] used her name as an exclamation of surprise, asking [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] "Why in the name of Deliverance Dane did you let [[Newton Scamander's niffler|that thing]] loose?"<ref name="FB17">{{FB|S|17}}</ref>
