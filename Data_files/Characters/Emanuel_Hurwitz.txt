'''Emanuel Hurwitz''' ([[7 May]], [[1919]] â€” [[19 November]], [[2006]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[violin]]ist who lead several orchestras and ensembles.<ref name="wikipedia" /><ref>[http://www.independent.co.uk/news/obituaries/emanuel-hurwitz-425144.html Emanuel Hurwitz's obituary] by Margaret Campbell in ''The Independent''</ref>

On Sunday, 7 May in a year prior to [[1997]], at 7:30 in the evening, Emanuel and his wife, [[Kay Hurwitz|Kay]], a [[viola|violist]], gave a performance of [[Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart|Mozart]]'s ''[[Mozart's Sinfonia Concertanti|Sinfonia Concertanti]]'' at [[St. Mary's Church Hall]].<ref name="hple">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:Emanuel&KayHurwitzConcertPoster.jpg}}</ref> The performance was directed by [[Alec Webb]], and the [[Dollis Chamber Ensemble]] accompanied the couple for the first time ever.<ref name="hple" /> [[Hermione Granger]] had a poster for this concert stuck up on the bulletin board in [[Hermione Granger's bedroom|her bedroom]] in the [[Granger family]]'s [[Grangers' house|house]].<ref name="hple" /><ref name="dhp1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*In real life, Emanuel and Kay Hurwitz are, respectively, the stepgrandfather and grandmother of [[Miraphora Mina]], a graphic artist who worked on all eight [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref>See [[Talk:Emanuel Hurwitz]].</ref> Mina likely created the prop concert poster featured in {{DH|F1}} as a tribute to them.
