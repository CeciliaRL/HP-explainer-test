'''Cecil Edwards''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] playwright in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:Two to Tango.png}}</ref><ref name="neonsign">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:OrpheumNewYork.jpg}}</ref> 

One of Edwards' works was a musical called ''[[Two to Tango]]'', which featured music and lyrics by [[Nelson Selgado]], and was staged at [[The Kingly Court]] in [[New York]] in late [[1926]].<ref name="poster" />
