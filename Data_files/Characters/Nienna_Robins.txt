'''Nienna Robins''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who worked [[Auror]] at the [[British Ministry of Magic]] in the [[2010s]].<ref name="WU">{{WU}}</ref>

==Biography==
Nienna was a daughter of [[Demelza Robins]]. In the early [[2020s]], during the [[Calamity]], she invited [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force|SOS agents]] to spar.<ref name="WU"/>
