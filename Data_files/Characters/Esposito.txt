'''Mrs Esposito''' was the landlady of a [[brownstone]] house located at [[679 West 24th Street|679]] [[West 24th Street]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="FB40">{{FB|S|40}}</ref><ref name="macusaid">See [[:File:PorpentinaGoldsteinsID.jpg|this image]] of Porpentina Goldstein's [[Magical Congress of the United States of America|MACUSA]] identification card on display as part of a prop exhibition in [[Japan]].</ref> She heavily disproved of single women bringing home strange men.

==Biography==
{{Dialogue a-b|Mrs Esposito|Are you alone?|Porpentina Goldstein|I'm always alone, Mrs Esposito!|Esposito on not having male guests over|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: The Original Screenplay}}
Mrs Esposito lived on the main floor of the [[679 West 24th Street|679]] [[West 24th Street]] brownstone house. She rented out an apartment on the second floor to two sisters, [[Porpentina Goldstein|Tina]] and [[Queenie Goldstein]].<ref name="FB40" /> She did not allow them to bring home male guests. However, on the night of [[6 December]] Tina sneaked [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] and [[Jacob Kowalski]] upstairs. Newt and Jacob needed a place to hide from the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America|MACUSA]] authorities pursuing them.<ref name="FB40" /><ref name="FB39">{{FB|S|39}}</ref> Hearing noise on the stairs, Mrs Esposito called out, asking if Tina was alone. Tina untruthfully replied that she was always alone.<ref name="FB40" />
