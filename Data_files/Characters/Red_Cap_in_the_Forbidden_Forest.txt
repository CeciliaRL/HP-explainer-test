This male '''[[Red Cap]]''' lived in [[Red Cap's Hole|his hole]] in the [[Forbidden Forest]].<ref name="HM410"/>

==Biography==
===1987–1988 school year===
During the [[1987–1988 school year]], this [[Red Cap]] encountered [[Charles Weasley]], [[Jacob's sibling]], and another student in [[Red Cap's Hole|his hole]] using [[Sickleworth]], a [[Niffler]], to try and search for the jewelled head of [[Torvus' arrow]].<ref name="HM410"/>

[[File:Red Cap in the Forbidden Forest after Beautification Potion HM.png|200px|thumb|right|The Red Cap after having been doused with Beautification Potion]]
He drove them out of his hole,<ref name="HM410"/> but they came back later with some [[Beautification Potion]] and used it on him. He was alarmed by his slightly more handsome look and climbed away, allowing Sickleworth to find the arrowhead.<ref name="HM413">{{HM|4|13}}</ref>

At the end of the school year, due to an [[Forest Vault Acromantula|Acromantula]] in his hole, the Red Cap was afraid of going back in there. He was seen batting [[Fairy|fairies]] in the [[Forest Grove]]. Jacob's sibling and [[Barnaby Lee]] distracted him using a piece of jewellery.<ref name="TTF">{{HM|4|TORVUS AND THE TROUBLED FOREST}}</ref>

===1988–1989 school year===
In [[1988–1989 school year|the next school year]], this Red Cap met Jacob's sibling and [[Ben Copper]] who came back to find Sickleworth. Ben struck it down with the [[Knockback Jinx]].<ref name="HM532">{{HM|5|32}}</ref>

===1989–1990 school year===
During the [[1989–1990 school year]], this Red Cap found Jacob's sibling, [[Fred Weasley|Fred]] and [[George Weasley]], and another student from the [[Weasley family]] in his hole. The Weasleys threw a [[Dungbomb]] at him.<ref name="HM65">{{HM|6|5}}</ref>
