'''Trupti Kadam''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She was seen walking through the school corridors during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Trupti is a female given name derived from a Sanskrit word meaning "satisfaction, satiety or contentment."<ref>"[http://www.naamkaran.com/babynames/meaning-of-trupti.html Trupti]" on Naamkaran.com</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}}.
*Given her name and place at Hogwarts, it is likely that although she must've born in the UK she was of Indian descent.
