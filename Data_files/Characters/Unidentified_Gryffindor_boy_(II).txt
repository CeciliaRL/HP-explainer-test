This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was sorted into [[Gryffindor]].<ref name="OOTPF">{{OOTP|F}}</ref>

==Biography==

===Early years===
He was born during the [[1980s]] and attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], where he was sorted into [[Gryffindor]].<ref name="OOTPF"/>

===Fifth year===
In [[1995]], he and his friend were walking in the corridors when [[Dolores Umbridge]] came by. She used a [[Dolores Umbridge's neatness charm|spell]] to tidy their ties and pull their shirts into their trousers.<ref name="OOTPF"/>

===Later life===
It is unknown what became of him next during the [[Second Wizarding War]] or during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]].
