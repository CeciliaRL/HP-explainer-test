'''Robert Hawking''' was a [[Wizardkind|student]] who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] between [[1987]] and [[1989]].

==Biography==
By the [[1993-1994 school year]], he had been made a [[Prefect]], and was tasked with patrolling areas of the school off limits to students.

==Etymology==
''Robert ''traces beck to Germanic name ''Hrodebert'',which means 'bright fame'.This could relate to him being a Prefect.

''Hawking ''could mean 'person with a hawk-like manner', which he displays, as his job is to partol areas.
