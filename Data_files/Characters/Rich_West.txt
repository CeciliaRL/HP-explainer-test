'''Rich West''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] musician. He released at least one album, ''[[Driving It Home]]'', which was available for sale at [[Record Shop]] on [[Charing Cross Road]] in [[London]].<ref>{{WWHP}} {{see image|File:DrivingItHome1.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The name of this character is apparently a tribute to Rich West, who worked as the art director/show set designer for {{WWHP}} theme park in [[wikipedia:Orlando, Florida|Orlando]], [[wikipedia:Florida|Florida]] from 2012 to 2014.<ref>[https://www.linkedin.com/pub/rich-west/8/946/883 LinkedIn profile of Rich West]</ref>
