This '''individual''' served as the President of a "far-distant" [[Foreign country|country]] in [[1996]]. In the summer of that year, the President was to telephone the [[Prime Minister]] of the [[Great Britain|United Kingdom]] but due to the visit of the recently sacked [[Minister for Magic]] [[Cornelius Fudge]] and his successor [[Rufus Scrimgeour]] he was made to forget to call, and telephone the next night instead.<ref name="HBP1">{{HBP|B|1}}</ref>
