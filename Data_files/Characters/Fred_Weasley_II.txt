'''Fred Weasley II''' (b. [[2000s]]) was the oldest child of [[George Weasley|George]] and [[Angelina Johnson|Angelina Weasley]] {{Comment|née Johnson}}. He was named in memory of his late uncle, [[Fred Weasley]], who was killed during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]]. He had a younger sister named [[Roxanne Weasley|Roxanne]].<ref name="story">[http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/books/article3105517.ece Times Online - The Unwritten Story of Harry's Friends and Their Children]</ref>

Fred attended the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]] with his family.<ref name="PM">{{PM}}</ref>
