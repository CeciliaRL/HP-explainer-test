This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' was appointed [[Headmaster|Headmistress]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] likely sometime before [[Armando Dippet|Professor Dippet]]. After her death, her [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head. Her portrait is commonly found between the [[Pink-faced Hogwarts Headmaster]] and [[Fytherley Undercliffe]] and depicts her reclining on an upholstered chair under a crescent moon.<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}}</ref><ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}}</ref><ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref><ref name="MHP">{{MHP}}</ref>
