'''Maurice Willard''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] playwright in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:TheNewYorkAffair.jpg}}</ref>

He adapted a book by [[Maxwell Lima]] into the musical ''[[The New York Affair]]''.<ref name="poster" /> Lima's book was in turn based on a story idea by [[Franklin Greenwood]] and [[Mattie Lovett]].<ref name="poster" />
