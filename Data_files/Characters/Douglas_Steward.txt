'''Douglas Steward''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] composer and lyricist in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:It's Just Heavenly.png}}</ref>

He composed the music and lyrics for the play ''[[It's Just Heavenly]]''.<ref name="poster" /> This play was staged at the [[Pegasi Theatre]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="poster" />
