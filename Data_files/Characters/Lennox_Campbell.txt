'''Lennox Campbell''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the [[Seeker]] for the [[Montrose Magpies]].<ref name="wizardsc">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/c.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: C]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon.</ref><ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 June|June 1st]], [[1999]], Page 4</ref>He once failed to catch the [[Golden Snitch]] when it was kicked at him by a [[Chaser]] on his team.<ref name="wizardsc" /> The Chaser who did this was presumably [[Alasdair Maddock]], who had an obsession with [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] sports, and was known to have tried football techniques during at least two matches.<ref>"[http://www.hplex.info/about/sources/source_dp.html Daily Prophet newsletters]" on The Harry Potter Lexicon</ref>

==Etymology==
"Lennox" is from a Scottish surname which was derived from the name of a district in Scotland, the Levenach.
