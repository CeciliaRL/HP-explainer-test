'''Valmai Morgan''' was a Welsh [[wizardkind|witch]] who played as the Chaser for the [[Wales|Welsh]] all-female [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|team]], the [[Holyhead Harpies]]<ref name=":0">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 June|June 1st]], [[1999]], Page 4</ref>.

She was a [[Chaser]] for the team who replaced [[Wilda Griffiths]] on the team, after the latter left for a 1000 [[Galleon]] fee to join [[Puddlemere United]]<ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[8 February|February 8th]], [[1999]], Page 3</ref>.

Valmai started on a high note, scoring ten goals on her first game<ref name=":0" />.
