'''Oraclitus Spheer''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]]. He had a [[portrait]] of himself hung in the [[Gryffindor Tower reading room]], hiding [[Fred Weasley|Fred]] and [[George Weasley]]'s [[Disused Bathroom (Fred and George's Shop)|convenience shop]]. His password was "''Flibbertigibbet''".<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}} - Console versions</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Spheer may have passed away sometime prior to [[1992]], as many portraits hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] depict deceased individuals.
*In {{POA}}, [[Cadogan|Sir Cadogan]] uses ''Flibbertigibbet'' as the password to [[Gryffindor Tower]].
