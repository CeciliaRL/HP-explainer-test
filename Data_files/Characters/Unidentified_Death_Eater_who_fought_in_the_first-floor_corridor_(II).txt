This [[Dark Wizard]] was a '''[[Death Eaters|Death Eater]]''' who fought at the [[Battle of Hogwarts]].<ref name="DHF2"/>

==Biography==
On [[2 May]] [[1998]], this Death Eater joined his comrades in the assault of [[Hogwarts Castle]], firstly by attempting to break down the [[protective enchantment]]s around the castle with [[spell]]s. He later fought near the [[Marble Staircase]]. Later, during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]], he was cornered while fighting in a [[first-floor corridor]] while being hit with the [[Incarcerous Spell]] cast by [[Harry Potter]] at the [[Marble Staircase]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

He was later seems laying down next to the staircase,<ref name="DHF2"/> implying he had [[Death|died]] from his injuries, or was simply [[Stunning Spell|unconscious]].
