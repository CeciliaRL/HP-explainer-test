This '''unidentified individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] sometime before [[Armando Dippet]].<ref name="COSF"/>

After his death, his [[portrait]] hung in the [[Headmaster's office]] in order to give advice to the current Head.<ref name="interview">In an [http://www.the-leaky-cauldron.org/2007/10/20/j-k-rowling-at-carnegie-hall-reveals-dumbledore-is-gay-neville-marries-hannah-abbott-and-scores-more interview], J.K. Rowling stated, "all the portraits you see in the Headmaster's study are all Headmasters and mistresses who died."</ref><ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}} DVD (Disc 2 - ''Dumbledore's Office'')</ref>

An inscription on his portrait read "Atatis XXXV",<ref name="Inscription">See [[:File:Unidentified Headmaster1.jpg|this image]].</ref> indicating that he was aged thirty-five when the portrait was painted, as ''atatis'' is a Latin form of "aetatis", meaning "at the age of".<ref name="Wikipedia">[[Wikipedia:List of Latin phrases:A|List of Latin phrases:A]] at [[Wikipedia:Main Page|Wikipedia]]</ref>

The inscription on his frame read "''Suprem Aeris regio Aquce Salsa Regio Vegetabis Pura''".<ref name="MHP">{{MHP}} - {{See image|File:Pink-faced_headmaster-frame.png}}</ref>

A portrait of him was hung in the [[Gryffindor Common Room]].<ref name="WU">{{WU}} - {{See image|File:Gryffindor Common Room WU.jpg}}</ref>
