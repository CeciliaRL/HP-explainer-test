'''Sinistra Lowe''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and an advocate of the [[Pure-blood supremacy|supremacy]] of [[pure-blood]] wizards and witches, having authored at least one book on the subject, ''[[The Muggle Conspiracy]]''.<ref name="dhscreen">{{DH|F1}} (see [[:File:SinistraLoweBook.jpg|this image]])</ref> [[Dolores Umbridge]] had a copy of this work in her desk at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]], while on the post of [[Senior Undersecretary to the Minister for Magic]].<ref name="dhscreen" />
