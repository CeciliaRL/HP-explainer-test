This '''Minister of Magical Transportation''' (born c. [[1949]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who headed the [[Department of Magical Transportation]] by [[1996]]. During his school years at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]], he was a member of the [[Slug Club]].<ref name="script">Mentioned on the [https://www.scriptslug.com/assets/uploads/scripts/harry-potter-and-the-half-blood-prince-2009.pdf ''Half-Blood Prince'' script] released on the [http://warnerbros2009.warnerbros.com/#/movies/harrypotter/foryourconsideration/ Warner Bros. Awards website]</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
This individual was born in around [[1949]].<ref name="graduation"/> He attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], where he was made a member of the [[Slug Club]] by his [[Potions Master]], [[Horace Slughorn]]. He graduated in [[1967]].

===Adulthood===
====Professional life====
This individual grew up to work at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] and by [[December]] of [[1996]] was considered the "new" Minister of Magical Transportation.<ref name="script"/>

====Slug Club Christmas party====
On [[20 December]], [[1996]], this individual attended a [[Slug Club Christmas party|Christmas party]] at [[Hogwarts Castle]], held by his former professor, [[Horace Slughorn]]. Slughorn was delighted to see this individual and quickly dismissed [[Harry Potter]] upon seeing him.<ref name="script"/>
