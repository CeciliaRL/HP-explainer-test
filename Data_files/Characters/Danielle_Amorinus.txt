'''Danielle Amorinus''' was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] working for ''[[Le Cri de la Gargouille]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="dec6sunrise">{{COG}} {{See image|File:Le Cri de la Gargouille - Recruitment Campaign.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1927]], Danielle Amorinus was in charge to receive all applications from people trying to be recruited as ''[[Le Cri de la Gargouille]]'''s new reporters.<ref name="dec6sunrise" />
