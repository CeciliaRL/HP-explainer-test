'''Troy "Tank" Duvall''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as a [[Beater]] on the [[American National Quidditch team]], and also served as the team's [[Captain]] at the [[1994]] [[Quidditch World Cup]]. He wore a helmet with a brown [[pumpkin]] design on it.<ref>{{QWC}}</ref>

By [[1996]], Troy was included on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] for his achievements in [[Quidditch]].<ref>{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Troy" is from a surname that originally denoted a person from the city of Troyes in [[France]]. This was also the name of the ancient city that was besieged by the Greeks in Homer's ''Iliad''.
