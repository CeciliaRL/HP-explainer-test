'''Yuri Blishenov''' was a reporter for the ''[[The Wizard's Voice]]'' in [[1926]].<ref name="newspaper">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:The Wizard's Voice - 6 Dec 1926.png}}</ref> Reporting from [[Moscow]], he wrote an article about how the [[Dark Wizard|Dark wizard]] [[Gellert Grindelwald]] was still at large, which was published in the [[6 December]] 1926 issue of the newspaper.<ref name="newspaper" />
