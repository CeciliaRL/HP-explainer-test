This '''individual''' (fl. [[1996]]) was a [[wizardkind|wizard]]. He may have been a former member of the [[Slug Club]], as in [[20 December]], [[1996]], he attended [[Horace Slughorn|Professor Slughorn]]'s [[Slug Club Christmas party|Christmas party]]. He was most likely acquainted with a [[Unidentified female Hogwarts teacher|female Hogwarts teacher]], as he stood with her at the party.<ref name="HBPf">{{HBP|F}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This individual was played by an unknown actor in [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|film adaptation]] of {{HBP}}.
*Given that the [[Unidentified female Hogwarts teacher|female Hogwarts teacher]], whom he stayed with during the [[Slug Club Christmas party]], is depicted as wearing a wedding ring, this individual may be her husband. This is, of course, backed by no canon information, and should not be treated as fact.
