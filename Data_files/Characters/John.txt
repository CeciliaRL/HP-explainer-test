'''John''', also known as '''John the Evangelist''', was traditionally regarded as the author of the [[wikipedia:Gospel of John|Gospel of John]], one of the books of the [[Bible]].<ref name="wikipedia">"[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_the_Evangelist John the Evangelist]"</ref> He was one of the twelve [[wikipedia:Apostle (Christian)|Apostles]] of [[Jesus]] and one of the four Evangelists.<ref name="wikipedia" />

[[File:Stained Glass Godric's Hollow.jpg|200px|thumb|left|John, along with [[Matthew]], [[Mark]] and [[Luke]] flanking [[Jesus]]]]
There was a stained glass window in the [[St Jerome's Church|Parish Church of St. Clementine]] in [[Godric's Hollow]] which depicted Jesus and the Four Evangelists — [[Matthew]], [[Mark]], [[Luke]] and John.<ref>{{HPFW}} {{see image|File:Stained Glass Godric's Hollow.jpg }}</ref>
