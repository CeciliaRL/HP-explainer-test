'''Ruth Mina''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and a [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="list">See [[:File:LibraryBooksToBeReturned.png|this image]] and [[:File:LibraryBooksToBeReturned2.jpg|this image]]  of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

Sometime in the [[1990s]], the [[Hogwarts Library|library]] books she took out were overdue, and thus her name was included on a list of students with overdue books that was put up on the bulletin board in [[Gryffindor Tower]] by [[Irma Pince|Madam Pince]].<ref name="list" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This individual's name is most likely a combined reference to [http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0935101/ Ruth Winick], who worked as a graphic artist on ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Philosopher's Stone]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)|Chamber of Secrets]]'' and as a draughtsman on ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]'', and [[Miraphora Mina]], another graphic artist who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]].
