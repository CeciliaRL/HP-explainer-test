'''M. Mina''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard or witch]] who competed in [[Duelling|duels]]. The ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' published a special report in [[1996]] about a duel between M. Mina and [[E. Lima]] that was the final match of a duelling competition.<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:DailyProphetWizardingDuellingFinal.png}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Miraphora Mina]], a graphic artist who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]].
