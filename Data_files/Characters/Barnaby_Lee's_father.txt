'''Barnaby Lee's father''' was a [[Dark Wizard]] who was a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] during the [[First Wizarding War]].<ref>{{HM|3|8}}</ref>

==Biography==
He once owned a pet [[Kneazle]], which [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] drowned.<ref name="HM4">{{HM|4|13|cmc}}</ref> One time he and his son were exterminating [[Pixie]]s at their house, and he yelled at Barnaby for crying.<ref>{{HM|5|7|d}}</ref> Mr Lee also always used to say he ought to get his son some brains for his birthday, clearly showing him as a rather cruel and abusive father.<ref name="task">{{HM|1|Party Planner|1}}</ref>

Mr. Lee was also acquainted with [[Horace Slughorn]].<ref name="quiz">{{HM}} Slughorn is a correct answer to the question of who his dad knew during friendship quizzes</ref>
