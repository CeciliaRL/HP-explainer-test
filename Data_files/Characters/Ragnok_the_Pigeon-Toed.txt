'''Ragnok the Pigeon-Toed''' was a [[Goblin]]. He was an activist for goblin rights and the author of ''[[Little People, Big Plans]]''. He was a member of the [[Brotherhood of Goblins]], and gave statements to the Daily Prophet after the [[Chipping Clodbury riot]].<ref name="DP">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 June]], [[1999]], page 1</ref>
