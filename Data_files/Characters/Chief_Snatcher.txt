The '''Chief Snatcher''' was the second in command of the [[Snatchers]] during the [[Second Wizarding War]]. He was second to [[Fenrir Greyback]] who was the leader of the group.<ref name="DHF2"/>

==Biography==
During the [[Battle of Hogwarts]], the Chief Snatcher participated the [[Skirmish at the Wooden Bridge|Skirmish at the Covered Bridge]] along with [[Scabior]] and many others and possibly fell to his death when the bridge collapsed.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The Chief Snatcher was portrayed by [[David Decio]] in {{DH|F2}}.<ref>{{IMDb name|1595786|David Decio}}</ref>
*[[Matthew Bancroft]] was a photo-double for the Chief Snatcher in the [[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)|film adaptations]] of {{DH}}.
