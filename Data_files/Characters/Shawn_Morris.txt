'''Shawn Morris''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. 

==Biography==
During the [[1996-1997 school year]], he stood in front of one of the exits in the [[Herbology Store]], forcing [[Harry Potter]] to beat him in a [[Duelling|duel]] in order to get past.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>
