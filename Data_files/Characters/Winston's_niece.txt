'''Winston's niece''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] student during the [[1990s]]. She was usually seen spending her free time at the [[Fourth-floor corridor|fourth floor main hall]]. When questioned whether she knew how to remove a [[curse]] from [[Harry Potter]], she remarked that [[Winston|her uncle]] once had a curse and he never knew how to get rid of it.<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}} - GBC version</ref>
