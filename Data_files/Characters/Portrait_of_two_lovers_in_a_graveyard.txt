These '''two individuals''' were [[Wizardkind|wizard]]s whose portrait hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] in the [[1990s]], next to a [[portrait of three young ladies]]. They were depicted in a graveyard, the woman in a swing, being pushed by the man.<ref name="PA13">{{POA|F}} - Chapter 13 (''Flight of the Fat Lady'')</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This portrait seems to be a reference to [[Wikipedia:Jean-Honoré_Fragonard|Jean-Honoré Fragonard]]'s ca. 1767 rococo masterpiece [[Wikipedia:The Swing (painting)|''The Swing'']].
