'''J. E. Prewitt''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard or witch]] and a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the late [[1970s]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
Prewitt played [[Seeker]] for their house [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|team]], and won an award for it in [[1978]]. A fellow Seeker's name, [[D. L. Boot]] was placed alongside theirs.<ref name="HPLE"/>
