'''Theo Strongarm''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] wizard and a member of the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]].<ref name="WU"/>

==Biography==
At some point during the late [[2010s]] or [[2020]], Strongarm joined the Statue of Secrecy Task Force. As part of the task force, he developed a vigorous stirring method that increased the effectiveness of the [[Tonic for Trace Detection]]. This tactic was soon adopted by other members of the task force in order to improve their own potions.<ref name="WU">{{WU}}</ref>
