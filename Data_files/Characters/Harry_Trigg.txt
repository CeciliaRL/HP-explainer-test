'''Harry Trigg''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] reported missing along with his relative, [[Bronwyn Trigg|Bronwyn]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' radio programme in [[1997]]. His disappearance was linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Derek Trigg, a foley editor who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]], excepting ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire]]''.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0872740 Derek Trigg's profile on IMDb]</ref>
