'''Josephine''' was a baby [[Zouwu]] in the care of the [[London Zoo]]'s [[London Zoo's Magizoology wing|Magizoology wing]].<ref name="WUSOS"/>

==Biography==
In [[2021]], Josephine escaped the Magizoology wing and ran outside the Zoo into the [[Marylebone]] district of [[London]], where she was fully visible to the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] population. After a chase, and one of the largest mass [[Memory Charm]]s in history, she was safely returned to the zoo.<ref name="WUSOS">{{WU}}, Calamity Essentials III, Petting and Scratching Zoo</ref>
