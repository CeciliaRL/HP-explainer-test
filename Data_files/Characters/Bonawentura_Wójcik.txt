'''Bonawentura Wójcik''' is a [[Poland|Polish]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and a professional [[Seeker]] for the [[Polish National Quidditch team]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[Quidditch World Cup 1990 - 2014]] (Archived)</ref>

==Biography==
In anticipation for the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]], accusations emerged that Wójcik was actually the famous Italian [[Seeker]] [[Luciano Volpi]] Transfigured. This was only disproven when both attended a press conference together.<ref name="PM"/>
