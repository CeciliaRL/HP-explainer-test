'''Mr Goldstein''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] who lived in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="fb44" /> He was the father of [[Porpentina Goldstein|Porpentina]] and [[Queenie Goldstein]].<ref name="fb44" />

==Biography==
[[File:Goldstein family dancing.gif|250px|left|thumb|Mr Goldstein, dancing with her wife and daugthers]]
Sometime in his daughters' childhood, the [[Goldstein family]] danced together while dressed up.<ref name="FBS69">{{FB|S|69}}</ref><ref name="FBF">{{FB|F}}</ref>

He and [[Goldstein sisters' mother|his wife]] died of [[Dragon Pox]] when their daughters were young.<ref name="fb44" /> This left the girls to "raise each other," which lead them to forge a life-long bond.<ref name="fb44" /><ref name="collider">"[http://collider.com/fantastic-beasts-and-where-to-find-them-things-to-know/?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=collidersocial ‘Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them’: 43 Things to Know about the New Wizarding World Story]" from Collider</ref><ref name="pottermorevideo">[https://twitter.com/pottermore/status/693594721097101312 Behind-the-scenes video] from the official {{PM}} account on Twitter</ref>

After being sentenced to [[death]] in [[1926]], Tina Goldstein had her happy [[memory|memories]] extracted from her head by [[Bernadette]], who then cast them into the [[death potion]] in the [[Death Cell]].<ref name="fb67">{{FB|S|67}}</ref> To keep Tina sedated, the potion replayed the memories for her, which included "happy images" of her mother and father.<ref name="fb67" /><ref name="fb69">{{FB|S|69}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Mr Goldstein was portrayed by [[Alan Mandel]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]'', but his role was not listed in the film's end credits.
