'''Mr Bones''' was the father of [[Amelia Bones|Amelia]] and [[Edgar Bones]]. He and his [[Amelia Bones' mother|wife]] were killed by [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]] during the [[First Wizarding War]].<ref>[http://www.accio-quote.org/articles/2000/1000-livechat-barnesnoble.html 20 October, 2000 chat with J.K. Rowling] at [http://www.accio-quote.org Accio Quote!]</ref>
==Behind the scenes==
*Mr Bones must have had a third child, as his granddaughter, [[Susan Bones]], was niece to both his daughter Amelia and his son Edgar.
