'''Ruby''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who lived in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="fb68">{{FB|S|68}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1926]], Ruby was in a relationship with [[Sam (Obliviator)|Sam]], an [[Obliviator]] who worked for the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]].<ref name="fb68" /> Unbeknownst to her, however, Sam was also romantically involved with his another woman [[Cecily]].<ref name="fb68" /> [[Queenie Goldstein]], a [[Legilimency|Legilimens]], uncovered Sam's infidelity by reading his mind, and convinced him not to [[Memory Charm|wipe the memory]] of the [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] [[Jacob Kowalski]] by threatening to expose his secret to Ruby as she appeared smiling at him.<ref name="fb68" />

==Behind the scenes==
*Ruby was portrayed by [[Stephanie Aspin]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]'', but was not listed in the film's end credits.
