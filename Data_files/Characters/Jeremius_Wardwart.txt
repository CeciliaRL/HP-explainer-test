'''Jeremius Wardwart''' was an American [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[United States of America]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="cob">{{CB|119}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Wardwart, which stated that he was wanted for [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] kidnap and murder and "bearing wand, most menacing & extremely dangerous."<ref name="cob" /> A reward of 4500 [[Dragot]]s was offered for assistance in his capture.<ref name="cob" />
