This '''Wizard''' attended the [[1994]] [[Quidditch World Cup]] and sat in the stands across from the [[Top Box]] of the [[Quidditch Trillenium Stadium|Quidditch World Cup Stadium]]. [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] looked at this wizard through a pair of [[Omnioculars]] while he picked his nose and used the replay function on them to make him do the same thing numerous times.<ref>{{GOF}}</ref>
