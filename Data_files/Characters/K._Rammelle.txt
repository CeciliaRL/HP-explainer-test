'''K. Rammelle''' (fl. [[1927]]) was a [[France|French]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and the proprietor of [[Confiserie Enchantée de K. Rammelle]], in [[Place Cachée]], [[Paris]].

In [[1927]], K. Rammelle revealed the secret ingredient of his [[caramel]]s in an interview for the newspaper ''[[Le Cri de la Gargouille]]''.<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Le Cri De La Gargouille.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*K. Rammelle is a pun on the word "[[Wikipedia:Caramel|caramel]]", a dark-orange confectionery product made by heating sugar.
