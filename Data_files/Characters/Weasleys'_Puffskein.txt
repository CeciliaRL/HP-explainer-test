This''' [[Puffskein]]''' belonged to the [[Weasley family]]<ref name="FB|R|An A-Z of Fantastic Beasts|Puffskein">{{FB|R}}, ''(An A-Z of Fantastic Beasts), Puffskein''</ref> in the early [[1990s]]. He was kept in the family's [[Weasleys' Puffskein patch|Puffskein patch]], where his favourite delicacies included wild bushes.<ref name="CSps1">{{COS|G}} - PS1 version</ref> Sometime before [[1992]], [[Fred Weasley]], along with his twin brother [[George Weasley|George]],<ref name="CSps1" /> used the family's Puffskein as a [[Bludger]] during [[Quidditch]] practice.<ref name="FB|R|An A-Z of Fantastic Beasts|Puffskein">{{FB|R}}, ''(An A-Z of Fantastic Beasts), Puffskein''</ref> [[Ginevra Weasley]] would in her youth look after the creature in its [[Weasleys' Puffskein patch|patch]] to make sure the twins did not do it again.<ref name="CSps1" />
In [[August]], [[1992]], the Puffskein swallowed Ginny's [[Remembrall]], and escaped to a [[Ottery St Catchpole forest|small forest area]]. Ginny followed it and got lost, only to be found along with the Puffskein by [[Harry Potter]].<ref name="CSps1" />
