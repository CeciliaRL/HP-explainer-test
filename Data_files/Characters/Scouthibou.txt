'''Scouthibou'''<ref>{{GOF|B|26}}; French translation </ref> was a [[brown owl|brown]] [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts school]] [[owl]] that inhabited the [[Owlery]] in the mid-[[1990s]]. 

==History==
In early [[1995]], [[Harry Potter]] sent [[Sirius Black]] a letter on the odd events at Hogwarts surrounding [[Severus Snape]] and [[Alastor Moody]] via this owl.<ref>{{GOF|B|26}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This owl is only named in the French translation of {{GOF}}, by [[Jean-François Ménard]]. The owl remains unidentified in the original English version.
*The suffix "''hibou''" is French for "owl".
