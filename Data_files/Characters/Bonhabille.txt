'''Madame Bonhabille''' (fl. [[1876]]) was an established [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and hat-maker, based in [[Paris]] in the late [[19th century]].<ref name="PM"/>

==Biography==
In [[1876]], [[Eupraxia Mole|Professor Eupraxia Mole]], [[Headmaster|Headmistress]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], commissioned Madame Bonhabille to make a new custom-made hat for [[Peeves]], the resident [[Poltergeist]]. This was in an attempt to calm Peeves down after then-[[caretaker]] [[Rancorous Carpe]] devised an "[[Rancorous Carpe's trap|elaborate trap]]" to banish him from the school premises.<ref name="PM">{{WW|peeves}}</ref>

==Etymology==
In French, her name translates to well-dressed.
