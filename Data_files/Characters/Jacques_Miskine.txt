During the second day of the fifth match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], [[Liechtenstein National Quidditch team|Liechtenstein]] versus Chad, Miskine was so tired he did not notice a [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]] hovering over his left eyebrow for five minutes, and failed to capture it when he did notice as his reaction was too slow.<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[LIECHTENSTEIN VERSUS CHAD (II)|LIECHTENSTEIN_VERSUS_CHAD_(II)]] (Archived)</ref>
