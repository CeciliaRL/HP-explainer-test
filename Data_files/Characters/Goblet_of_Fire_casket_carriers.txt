This was a '''group''' of four [[Wizardkind|wizards]] that worked for the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]]. In [[1994]] they delivered the [[Goblet of Fire]] to [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] during the [[Triwizard Tournament]], and were overseen by [[Argus Filch]], [[Hogwarts Caretaker]].<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}} - Chapter 6 (''The Goblet of Fire'')</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
{{Quote|The casket, then, if you please, Mr Filch.|[[Albus Dumbledore]] asks [[Argus Filch]] alone to bring the [[Goblet of Fire casket|casket]].|Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire}}
*In [[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire|the fourth novel]], the [[Goblet of Fire casket]] is small enough for a man to carry, so [[Argus Filch]] carries it by himself.<ref name="GOF12">{{GOF|B|12}}</ref> As the film version contradicts the literary canon, these men are non-canon.
