{{Quote|Well, well, well... So, you have made it this far? I am the Slytherin Wizard Duelling Champion.|The champion|Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)}}
The '''Slytherin Wizard Duelling Champion''' as the title suggests, refers to the duelling champion for the [[Slytherin]] house. He is a selected [[Wizardkind|student]] from the Slytherin house to represent the house in the [[Founder Duels]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
This boy attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]]. His [[Head of House]] was [[Severus Snape]], the [[Potions Master]], and he shared a dormitory with four boys in his year in the [[Slytherin Dungeon]], which is located below ground level.<ref name="COSG"/>

===[[1992–1993 school year]]===
He apparently excelled at [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] and became the [[Slytherin]] Duelling Champion sometime in or prior to [[1992]]. When [[Harry Potter]] defeated him, he gave him [[Salazar Slytherin]]'s [[Chocolate Frog Card|card]].<ref name="COSG"/>
