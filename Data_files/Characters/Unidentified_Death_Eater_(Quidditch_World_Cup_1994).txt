==Biography==

===Early life===
He eventually joined the [[Death Eaters]] and probably fought in the [[First Wizarding War]]. He successfully managed to avoid imprisonment in [[Azkaban]].

===1994===
In [[1994]], he attacked the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup|Quidditch World Cup]] alongside other Death Eaters, however fled the scene when the [[Dark Mark]] was [[Morsmordre|conjured]] in the sky by [[Bartemius Crouch Junior]].<ref name="LEGOY1"/>
