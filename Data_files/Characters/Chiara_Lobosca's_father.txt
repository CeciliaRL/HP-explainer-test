This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the father of [[Chiara Lobosca]]. He worked as an [[Arithmancer]].

==Biography==
After seeing Chiara with a wizard in the night, [[Jacob's sibling]] thought that he may have been Chiara's father. He turned out to have been [[Remus Lupin]] instead.
