'''Leticia Somnolens''' was a [[Middle Ages|Medieval]] [[hag]] known for conspiring against a [[Unidentified Princess|princess]],<ref name="POAG"/> and apparently served as a source of inspiration for the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] fairy tale, [[Sleeping Beauty]].

==Biography==
A spiteful and vicious creature, Somnolens was jealous of [[Unidentified Princess|the daughter]] of [[Unidentified King|the local King]] and tainted a spindle with the [[Draught of Living Death]]. She then tricked the young princess into pricking her finger on it, sending her into a deep sleep. Her plan was foiled, however, by a [[Unidentified young wizard|young wizard]] who smeared his lips with [[Wiggenweld Potion]], and then kissed the princess, curing her and bringing her out of the sleep.<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Legacy==
Somnolens’s story, despite being a fact to the [[Wizarding world]], appears to be the source of inspiration for the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] fairy tale, [[Sleeping Beauty]].

==Etymology==
*Somnolens is a derivation of "somnolence", which means "sleepiness".
