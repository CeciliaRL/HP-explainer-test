'''Gordon Horton''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] who played as the [[Keeper]] for the [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|team]] the [[Chudley Cannons]].<ref name="FBC1"/>

==Biography==
He was attacked and injured by a [[magic]]al [[beast]] at the [[Chudley Cannons training grounds|Chudley Cannons' training grounds]]. He was sent to [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries]] to be treated by a [[Healer]]. [[Omar Abasi]] concluded that he had a mild concussion from his fall.<ref name="FBC1"/><ref name="FBC video">[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-LCMPyqiQI8&feature=youtu.be Youtube - FANTASTIC BEASTS: CASES Android Gameplay]</ref>

It was later discovered that he had been attacked by a [[Hippogriff]] nesting with its young in the nearby [[Old Bell Tower]].<ref name="FBC1">{{FBC|1}}</ref>
