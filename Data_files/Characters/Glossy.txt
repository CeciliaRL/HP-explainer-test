'''Madame Glossy''' was presumably the [[Wizardkind|witch]] who gave her name to [[Madame Glossy's Silver Polish]] and [[Madame Glossy's Self-Polishing Shoe Polish]], presumably being the owner or operator of [[Madame Glossy's Finest Cleaning Products Ltd.]].<ref name="POAF">{{POA|F}}</ref><ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref> It is unknown whether she was the inventor or simply the manufacturer of these [[Potion|products]].

==Etymology==
The name Glossy comes from the adjective glossy, meaning "having a smooth, silklike, reflective surface".<ref>''[http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/glossy Glossy on Wiktionary]''</ref> This suggests a rather effective silver polish.
