This unidentified [[magic]]al '''[[creature]]''' was owned by [[Rubeus Hagrid]] and lived in his [[Rubeus Hagrid's cabin|hut]]. It was a plant-like creature with an appearance similar to the [[Mandrake]] plant.<ref name="POAF">{{POA|F}} - Chapter 22 (''Witnesses to an Execution'')</ref>

==History==
[[File:Hagrid Hut's hiding creature POA.gif|200px|thumb|left|Hagrid covering up the creature]]
It was implied to be an illegal creature, when Hagrid hid it under a tea towel when the [[Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures]], including [[Albus Dumbledore]], [[Cornelius Fudge]] and [[Walden Macnair]] the Executioner, arrived for the disposal of [[Buckbeak]] the [[Hippogriff]] some days after Hagrid's hearing with the [[Hogwarts Board of Governors]], in [[1994]].<ref name="POAF"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*The creature has a somewhat similar resemblance to the [https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Sarlacc Sarlaac] in ''{{W|Return of the Jedi}}''.
