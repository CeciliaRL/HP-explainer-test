'''Alice Tolipan''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] musician. Along with [[Luca Caruso (Muggle)|Luca Caruso]], she provided the music for the [[Regency Playhouse]]'s adapted production of ''[[Romeo and Juliet]]'', which was staged during the summer of [[1997]].<ref>{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:Romeo&JulietDetail.png}}</ref>
