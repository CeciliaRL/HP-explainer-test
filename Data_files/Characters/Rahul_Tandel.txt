'''Rahul Tandel''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
Rahul is an Indian name, meaning "capable". Rahul was the name of the son of Buddha. Tandel is a surname of Indian origin, widely found in the Gujarat state.

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}}.
*Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.
