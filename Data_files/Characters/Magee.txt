'''Madame Magee''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] entertainer in the early [[20th century]]. In [[1926]], posters were put up in an alleyway in [[New York]] to promote a one-night-only performance by Madam Magee at the [[Brobeck Theatre]], which was to be held on Saturday [[4 December]].<ref>{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:BrobeckTheatrePosters.jpg}}</ref>
