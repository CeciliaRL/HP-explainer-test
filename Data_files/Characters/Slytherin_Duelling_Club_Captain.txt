The '''Slytherin Duelling Club Captain''' as the title suggests, refers to the [[Duelling Club (1990s)|Duelling Club]] Captain for the [[Slytherin]] house.<ref name="HBPG"/>

==Biography==
===Early life===
The boy came to be educated at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]]. His [[Head of House]] was [[Professor]] [[Severus Snape]], the [[Potions Master]] and later [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] teacher, and he shared a dormitory with four other boys in his year in the [[Slytherin Dungeon]]. He apparently excelled at [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] and/or [[Duelling]].<ref name="HBPG"/>

===[[1996–1997 school year]]===
The boy became the Slytherin Duelling Club Captain in [[1996]], when the [[Duelling Club]] was revived by [[Albus Dumbledore]] to prepare the students in case the school was attacked by [[Death Eaters]]. He challenged [[Harry Potter]] to a [[Duelling|Duel]] and taunted him by saying that he did not know the [[Full Body-Bind Curse]] (''Petrificus Totalus''), which Harry proceeded to use to defeat him.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Duelling]]''': As the head of the [[Slytherin]] [[Duelling Club]], this student was a highly talented duellist, who was skilled with the casting of the [[Stunning Spell]], [[Disarming Charm]], [[Shield Charm]], ''[[Levicorpus]]'' and the [[Full Body-Bind Curse]] in combat, meaning he was very proficient with [[martial magic]].<ref name="HBPG"/>
