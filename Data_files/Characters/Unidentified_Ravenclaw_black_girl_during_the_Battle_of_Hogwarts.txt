This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' was a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]]. She participated during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] and was seen running out of the [[Great Hall]] and into the [[Viaduct Courtyard]] while the [[Protective enchantment|Protective Enchantments]] were set up.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>
