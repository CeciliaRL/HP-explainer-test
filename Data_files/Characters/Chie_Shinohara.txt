'''Chie Shinohara''' was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Chie is a Japanese female given name, which means "wisdom". Shinohara is Japanese surname, which means "plain of bamboo grass".

==Behind the scenes==
*Given her name, she is most likely of [[Japan]]ese descent.
*She shares name with the award-winning Japanese manga artist and author, {{w|Chie Shinohara}}. It is unknown whether or not this is coincidental.
