==Biography==
In the [[1980s]], [[Jacob's sibling]] would often bond with and feed this [[Chimaera]].<ref name="ITHLD"/>

During the [[1986â€“1987 school year]], [[Rubeus Hagrid]] and Jacob's sibling lured it to the [[Training Grounds]], but was spotted by [[Argus Filch|Mr Filch]]. He reported them to [[Albus Dumbledore|Professor Dumbledore]], who asked them to talk about the incident. They later went to the dark forest in the [[Magical Creatures Reserve]] to observe and evaluate this Chimaera. [[Mrs Norris]] developed a bond with it, to Filch's surprise. Dumbledore then decided that the Chimaera was no harm and could be kept in the Reserve.<ref name="ITHLD"/>
