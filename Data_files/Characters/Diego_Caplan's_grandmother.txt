This '''individual''' was the grandmother of [[Diego Caplan]].

==Biography==
In the [[1990–1991 school year]], as her grandson was writing a letter to her in the [[Hogwarts Library]] — with the attachment of a recipe she had asked for — he spilled ink on the piece of parchment.<ref name="HM7.9">{{HM|7|9}}</ref>

It is possible she was married to [[Diego Caplan's grandfather|Diego's duellist grandfather]].
