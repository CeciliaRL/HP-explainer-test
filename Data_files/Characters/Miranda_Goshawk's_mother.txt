'''Mrs Goshawk''' (fl. [[1915]]-[[1921]]) was the wife of [[Miranda Goshawk's father|Mr Goshawk]], and the mother of the [[Goshawk sisters|nine Goshawk sisters]], including [[Diadema Goshawk|Diadema]], [[Romilda Goshawk|Romilda]], [[Tangwystl Goshawk|Tangwystl]] and [[Miranda Goshawk]].<ref name="WBoS"/>

This individual had several [[Miranda Goshawk's aunts and uncles|siblings-in-law]] and, through their marriages, many [[Miranda Goshawk's cousins|nieces and nephews]].<ref name="WBoS">{{WBS}}</ref>
