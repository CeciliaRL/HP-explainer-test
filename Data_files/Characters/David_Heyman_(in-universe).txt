}}'''David Heyman''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] and the commissioner of the [[New York Police Department]] in [[1926]].<ref name="itmfb">''[[Inside the Magic: The Making of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]'', page 47</ref><ref name="poster2">[https://www.flickr.com/photos/hummelissa/31348693721/ Prop photo poster] from hummelissa on Flickr</ref>

==Biography==

He signed a wanted poster issued for [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] and [[Jacob Kowalski]] on [[6 December]] 1926. It advised that, in the event of the pair's capture, he should be immediately contacted by [[telephone]] at 3100 Heyman.<ref name="itmfb" /><ref name="poster2"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[David Heyman]], the producer of all eight [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]], as well as ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
