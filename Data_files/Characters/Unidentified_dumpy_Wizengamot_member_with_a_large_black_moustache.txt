This '''individual''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] and member of the [[Wizengamot]] in the mid-[[1990s]]. He was dumpy and heavily-moustached. On [[12 August]], he was present at [[Harry Potter]]'s [[Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter|disciplinary hearing]], appearing highly sceptical of [[Arabella Figg]]'s testimony, whispering about it to [[Unidentified frizzy-haired Wizengamot member|the witch sitting next to him]]. He and his neighbour later voted in favour of [[Harry Potter|Harry]]'s conviction.<ref name="OP8">{{OOTP|B|8}}</ref>
