This '''wizard''' was appointed [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] at some unknown time. After his death,<ref name="DOD" /> his [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head. He was depicted sleeping in an chair with an [[wikipedia:Astrolabe|astrolabe]] nearby.
