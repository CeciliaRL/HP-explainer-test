In [[1995]], she, along with [[Cho Chang]] and [[Fred Weasley|Fred]] and [[George Weasley]], watched [[Argus Filch]] hammer notices into a wall, displaying [[Dolores Umbridge]]'s [[Educational Decree]]s.<ref name="OOTPF">{{OOTP|F}}</ref>
