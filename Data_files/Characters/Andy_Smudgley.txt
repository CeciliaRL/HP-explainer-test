'''Andy Smudgley''' was a reporter who worked for the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''.

In [[1991]], he wrote an article covering a [[Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1991)|break-in]] at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]],<ref name="image"/> which was titled "[[GRINGOTTS BREAK-IN LATEST]]".<ref name="PS8">{{PS|B|8}}</ref>

The [[1992|following year]], he wrote an article covering a major breach of the [[International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy]], which occurred when [[Harry Potter]] and [[Ronald Weasley]] flew a [[Flying Ford Anglia]] to [[Hogwarts Castle]].<ref name="cosanglia">{{COS|F}} {{see image|File:Flying Ford Anglia Mystifies Muggles.png}}</ref>

In [[1993]], he wrote an article titled "'It's Sirius!' say the Ministry of Magic," which concerned the escape of [[Sirius Black]] from [[Azkaban]].<ref name"poasirius">{{POA|F}} {{see image|File:EscapeFromAzkabanProphetLarge.jpg}}</ref>
