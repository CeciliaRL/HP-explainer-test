'''Addison Fawley''' was the son of [[Grim Fawley]] and [[Penelope Fawley]], who were [[Auror]]s. He had a sister, [[Melody Fawley]].

==Biography==
In 2015,<ref name="date">{{WU}} was set in 2018. It states Penelope has been missing for three years.</ref>, Penelope went missing and a subsequent investigation overseen by the council of which [[Gareth Greengrass]] was a member, was called off. Grim began searching for her, leading [[Constance Pickering]] to suspect he had unleashed the [[Calamity]] through the use of the [[Foundables Spell]].<ref name="WU">{{WU}} ([https://youtu.be/bW3-J9k0pp4?t=629 see video])</ref>

==Personality==
Addison was said to have inherited his mother Penelope's stubbornness, but she thought he was like Grim.<ref>[https://wizardsunite.gamepress.gg/foundable/grims-grief A section of Grim's journal]</ref>
