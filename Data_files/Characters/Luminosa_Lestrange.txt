'''Luminosa Lestrange''' (née '''Trouche''') was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She married [[Cyrille Lestrange (III)|Cyrille Lestrange]] and had a two sons with him, [[Cyrille Lestrange (IV)|Cyrille]] and [[Floxel Lestrange|Floxel]].<ref name="COG">{{COG}} {{see image|File:The art of CoG lestrange.jpeg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Her name, ''Luminosa'', means "bright" or "shiny" in Spanish and Italian. It may be possible that Luminosa's family had some connection with those countries.
