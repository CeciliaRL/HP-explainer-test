'''Tarquin McTavish''' (b. [[1955]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was imprisoned in [[Azkaban]] for trapping his [[Tarquin McTavish's neighbour|Muggle neighbour]] in a [[Tarquin McTavish's teakettle|teakettle]].<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref><ref name="PM">"Timeline of the Wizarding World (infographic)" at {{PM}} {{c|Archived; view [https://www.reddit.com/r/PottermoreWritings/comments/4woua4/timeline_of_the_wizarding_world/ transcript on Reddit]}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Tarquin" is from the Roman name Tarquinius, which is of unknown meaning, possibly Etruscan in origin. This was the name of two early kings of [[Rome]].{{Fact}}

==Behind the scenes==
*If he attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], he may have been in the same year as [[Narcissa Malfoy]], and five years above the [[Marauders]].
