'''Ethan Bexley''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
He came into possession of a rare [[Chudley Cannons]] card during the [[1996–1997 school year]]. However, when [[Harry Potter]] wanted a Chudley Cannons card to give to [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]], Ethan agreed to give his to Harry in exchange for an equally uncommon [[Viktor Krum]] [[Miniature figurine of Viktor Krum|figurine]]. He also competed in the school [[Exploding Snap]] tournament during that term.

==Etymology==
"Ethan" means "solid, enduring" in Hebrew.
