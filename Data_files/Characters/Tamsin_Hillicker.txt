'''Tamsin Hillicker'''<ref>Closed captions on the DVD/Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who was reported missing along with her relative, [[Iola Hillicker|Iola]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' wireless programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance is most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after Tamsin Hilliker, the niece of Stuart Hilliker,<ref>"[http://www.wiltsglosstandard.co.uk/news/4493810.Tetbury_teenager_meets_the_stars_at_Harry_Potter_premiere/ Tetbury teenager meets the stars at Harry Potter premiere]" from the ''Wilts and Gloucestershire Standard''</ref> a sound mixer who worked on the final three [[Harry Potter]]'' films.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0385014/ Stuart Hilliker's profile on IMDb]</ref>
