'''Agnes Monkleigh''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. During the [[1996–1997 school year]], she blocked one of the exits in the [[Herbology Store]], forcing [[Harry Potter]] to beat her in a [[Duelling|duel]] in order to get past.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Agnes" is the Latinized form of the Greek name ‘Αγνη (Hagne), derived from Greek ‘αγνος (hagnos) meaning "chaste".
