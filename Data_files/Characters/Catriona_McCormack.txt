'''Catriona McCormack''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who was a [[Chaser]] for the [[Pride of Portree]] in the [[1960s]].<ref name="QTA7"/>

==Biography==
She captained the team to two [[League Cup]]s. She played for the [[Scottish National Quidditch team]] thirty-six times.<ref name="QTA7">{{QTA|R|7}}</ref>

Catriona had two children, [[Meaghan McCormack]] (current [[Keeper]] for the Pride of Portree), and [[Kirley Duke]] (lead guitarist of the [[Weird Sisters|The Weird Sisters]]).<ref name="QTA7"/>

Catriona McCormack was on the cover of ''[[Quidditch Times]]'' on [[29 February]] [[1964]], the magazine's 3000th issue, having been interviewed about her League wins and on "how to wear purple with flair".<ref name="QTAIE">{{QTA|IE}}; retrieved from {{WW}}, see [https://www.wizardingworld.com/news/bloomsbury-reveal-cover-for-new-illustrated-edition-of-quidditch-through-the-ages here]</ref>
