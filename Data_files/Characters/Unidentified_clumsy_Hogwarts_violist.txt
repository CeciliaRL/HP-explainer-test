This '''unidentified individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was also a member of the [[Hogwarts orchestra]] as a violist but was said to be clumsy. In [[1994]], he was part of the orchestra during the [[Yule Ball]].<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref>
