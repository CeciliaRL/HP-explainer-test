'''Steve Laughalot''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1960s]] and [[1970s]]. He was [[Quidditch Captain|Captain]] of the [[Slytherin Quidditch team]] from [[1968]] to [[1972]].<ref>See [[:File:Slytherin House Captain's Plaque.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref><ref>[http://ab.fanrus.com/SMI/ViP/september/51/213/ ''Harry Potter: The Exhibition'' report] {{Comment|in Russian}}</ref>
