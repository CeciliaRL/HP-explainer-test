This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' was appointed [[Headmaster|Headmistress]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]]. After her death, her [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head. Her portrait was commonly found left of the headmaster's desk, in the top row between [[Edessa Skandenberg|Edessa Sakndenberg]] and [[Everard]].<ref name="MHP">{{MHP}}</ref>
