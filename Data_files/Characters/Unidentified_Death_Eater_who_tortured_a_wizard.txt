==Biography==
He was one of the Death Eaters who participated and fought in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] with his fellow Death Eaters and Voldemort's army, on the [[2 May]] [[1998]]. During the battle of Hogwarts, he was seen [[Cruciatus Curse|torturing]] an unknown [[Wizardkind|wizard]], before Voldemort called of the battle and commanded all his Death Eaters to return to him in the [[Forbidden Forest]], which he did accordingly.<ref name="LEGOHP5-7">{{LEGO|Y2}}</ref>
