This '''individual''' (fl. ''circa'' [[First millennium A.D.|993]]) was a wizard who lived during the [[Middle Ages]] and planned, designed and oversaw the construction of buildings of [[Hogwarts Castle]],<ref>[http://www.the-leaky-cauldron.org/2010/3/25/wizarding-world-of-harry-potter-to-open-june-18-details-about-hogwarts-emerge Wizarding World of Harry Potter Grand Opening June 18: Details About Hogwarts Emerge] at [http://www.the-leaky-cauldron.org/ The Leaky Cauldron]</ref> along with [[Hogwarts founders|Founder]] [[Rowena Ravenclaw]], who devised the ever-changing floorplan. 

There was a golden statue of this individual at Hogwarts [[Entrance Hall]], in which he was shown among the four house mascots holding a model of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] on one of his hands, and charts and a set square in the other.<ref name="CSf20">{{COS|F}} - Chapter 20 (''Polyjuice Potion'')</ref> The statue guarded a hidden passageway to the [[Founders' Tower]].

==Behind the scenes==
*In the [[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)|PS1 version]] of {{COS}}, the statue of the Architect hides a passageway to the Founder Duels and the [[Founders' Tower]].
