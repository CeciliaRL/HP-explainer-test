'''Jo King''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1950s]]. She was [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Slytherin]] [[Quidditch]] [[Slytherin Quidditch team|team]] from [[1952]] to [[1956]].<ref>See [[:File:Slytherin House Captain's Plaque.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref><ref>[http://ab.fanrus.com/SMI/ViP/september/51/213/ ''Harry Potter: The Exhibition'' report] {{Comment|in Russian}}</ref>
