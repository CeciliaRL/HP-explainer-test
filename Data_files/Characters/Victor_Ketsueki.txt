'''Victor Ketsueki''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] or [[Ireland|Irish]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] from [[1984]] to [[1991]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]].<ref name="HM716"/>

==Biography==
===Hogwarts years {{C|1984–1991}}===
Victor began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1984]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]].<ref name="HM716"/>

[[File:Victor talking about his experience in the Great Hall.jpg|250px|thumb|left|Victor talking about his experience]]
During the summer of [[1990]], he was allegedly turned into a [[vampire]], and so gained some fame in the [[1990–1991 school year|next school year]]. According to himself, he attended all-night parties in the catacombs beneath [[Paris]] and danced with [[Veela]] until dawn. At some point during the [[1990–1991 school year]], he was seen talking about his experience with a bunch of students in the [[Great Hall]].<ref name="HM716"/>

Victor helped [[Jacob's sibling]] and [[Chiara Lobosca]] clear out the [[doxy]] infestation in [[Hogwarts Castle]], in particular in the [[Hogwarts kitchens]].<ref name="HM718">{{HM|7|18}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Victor was proficient with the [[Knockback Jinx]] and [[Shield Charm]], which he used to help deal with an infestation of doxies.<ref name="HM718"/>

==Etymology==
"Ketsueki" (written in kanji as 血液) is the Japanese word for [[blood]], which is most likely referential of his supposed vampirism.

==Behind the scenes==
*Based on his surname, it is possible that he was of Japanese descent.
*It is unknown if he ever did become an actual vampire, or whether or not he fabricated the tale for attention. It is also unknown if, or how, humans can be turned into vampires in the [[Harry Potter universe]].
