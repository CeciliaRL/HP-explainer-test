This '''[[Dark Wizard|Dark witch]]''' was a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]], who served as one [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s servants during the [[Second Wizarding War]].<ref name="DH1"/>

==Biography==
In late [[July]] [[1997]], This Death Eater present at the [[Meeting at Malfoy Manor|meeting]] at [[Malfoy Manor]]. During the meeting she sat next to [[Thorfinn Rowle]], during which Voldemort discussed his plan to attempt to kill [[Harry Potter]]. She later witnessed [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] [[Killing Curse|murder]] his airborne hostage [[Charity Burbage]], with her corpse being then fed to his pet [[snake]] [[Nagini]].<ref name="DH1">{{DH|B|1}}</ref>

It is unknown whether she participated in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] as she was not seen though she most likely was present, assuming that she survived up to that point. Following Voldemort's defeat and death she was presumably killed, sent to [[Azkaban]], or fled from the battle.

==Behind the scenes==
*This Death Eater was played by [[Ashley McGuire]] in {{DH|F1}}.
