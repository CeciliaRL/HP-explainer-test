This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' was the grandmother of [[Constance Pickering]].<ref name="text"/>

==Biography==
She attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in her youth and was a member of [[Hufflepuff]] house.<ref name="text"/>

When Constance was ten years old, her grandmother came over for [[Christmas]]. She had a [[badger]] brooch, that Constance pilfered to play with. In punishment, Constance spent the rest of Christmas locked in the attic.<ref name="text">{{WU}} {{see image|File:Christmas Calamity - pt2 - Grandma Pickering - WU.png}}</ref>

==Possessions==
*'''Badger brooch''': She owned a brooch in the shape of a [[badger]], the mascot of [[Hufflepuff|Hufflepuff House]].
