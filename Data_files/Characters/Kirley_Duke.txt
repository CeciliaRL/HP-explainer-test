'''Kirley Duke McCormack'''<ref name="BAHR"/> (b. [[1971]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the lead guitarist for the popular band [[Weird Sisters|The Weird Sisters]].

==Biography== 
Kirley was the son of [[Quidditch]] player [[Catriona McCormack]] and the brother of [[Meaghan McCormack]]. Both women played for the [[Pride of Portree]].<ref name="QTA7"/> Kirley attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]<ref name="BAHR"/> from [[1983]].<ref name="HM634">{{HM|6|24}}</ref> He later joined the [[Weird Sisters]], and was one of its first three members.<ref name="BAHR">{{HM|2|Become an Honorary Rocker}}</ref> 

==Personality and traits==
Kirley appeared to be a man of few words.<ref name="HM638">{{HM|6|38}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Kirley was capable of producing a non-corporeal [[Patronus Charm|Patronus]],<ref name="LEGOHP"/> which is a highly impressive [[magic]]al achievement, given the difficulty of casting the charm.

==Possessions==
*'''[[Kirley Duke's guitar|Guitar]]''': Kirley owned a guitar, which was confiscated by [[Argus Filch]] in the [[1989–1990 school year]], which was retrieved by [[Jacob's sibling]] and returned to him.<ref name="HM638"/> He used this guitar to play at numerous events, such as the [[Weird Sisters concert at the Great Lake|concert]] at the [[Great Lake]],<ref name="HM638"/> the [[Party at the Three Broomsticks Inn|party]] at the [[Three Broomsticks Inn]]<ref name="HM644">{{HM|6|44}}</ref> and the [[Yule Ball]].<ref name="GOFF19">{{GOF|F}} - Chapter 19 (''The Yule Ball'')</ref>

==Etymology==
A "Duke" is a nobleman with the highest hereditary rank, especially a man of the highest grade of the peerage in [[Great Britain]].

==Behind the scenes==
*Kirley Duke was portrayed by [[Jonny Greenwood]] of {{W|Radiohead}} in {{GOF|FA}}.
*Kirley's appearance as depicted in the video games differs. In his [[Famous Witches and Wizards Cards|Famous Wizard Card]] appearance from the first two video games, he is depicted as blond, but in {{HM}}, he is depicted as dark-haired (which is based on his depiction in the [[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|fourth film]]).
