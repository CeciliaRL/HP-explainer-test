**[[Department of International Magical Co-operation]]
}}This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked for [[Bartemius Crouch Senior|Bartemius Crouch Snr]] until Crouch's death in [[1995]].<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1981]] after [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s downfall he attended the trial of [[Igor Karkaroff]] in which Karkaroff named [[Death Eaters]].<ref name="GOFF"/>

In [[1994]] he attended the [[Quidditch World Cup]] between [[Ireland]] and [[Bulgaria]]. When the [[Dark Mark]] appeared in the sky he helped [[Bartemius Crouch Senior|Bartemius Crouch Snr]] and some other wizards and witches to find the "[[Bartemius Crouch Junior|man]]" in the ruins of the [[Roberts's campsite|campsite]] owned by [[Roberts]].<ref name="GOFF"/>
