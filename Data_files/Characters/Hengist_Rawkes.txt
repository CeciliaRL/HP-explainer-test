'''Hengist Rawkes'''<ref name="HPLE"/> was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] depicted in a [[Portrait]] hanging near the first-floor landing of the [[Grand Staircase]] of [[Hogwarts Castle]], near the portrait of a [[Count]]. His name could be read on a plaque hanging under his image.

==Behind the scenes==
*The portrait of Hengist Rawkes was based on a [http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/Sargent_-_Lord_Ribblesdale.jpg 1902 portrait of Thomas Lister, 4th Baron Ribblesdale], by [[Wikipedia:John Singer Sargent|John Singer Sargent (1856â€“1925)]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|PA}}</ref>
*This individual may have died sometime prior to [[1991]], as many portraits hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] depict deceased individuals.

==Etymology==
The name "Hengist" means "stallion" in Germanic. Hengist and his brother Horsa were the leaders of the first Germanic settlers in Britain. Hengist established a kingdom in Kent in the 5th century.
