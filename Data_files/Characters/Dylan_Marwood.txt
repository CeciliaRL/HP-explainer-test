'''Dylan Marwood''' was a celebrated [[Wizarding world|wizarding]] author and linguist.<ref name="PM"/>

==Biography==
Fluent in [[Mermish]], he wrote a book on the language and culture of [[Merpeople]], ''[[Merpeople: A Comprehensive Guide to Their Language and Customs]]''.

==Possessions==
*'''[[Dylan Marwood's wand|Wand]]''': His [[wand]] was made out of [[Apple (tree)|applewood]], a fact which may have been connected to his skill with language, as apple wands were reputed to be common among [[Wizardkind|witches and wizards]] with a special talent for speaking to magical [[being]]s in their own tongues.<ref name="PM">{{WW|wand-woods}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*It is possible that Marwood studied at [[Merge School of Under-Water Spellage]] in the Red Sea in order to prepare for underwater expeditions to study the ways of the merpeople.
