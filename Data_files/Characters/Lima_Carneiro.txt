'''Lima Carneiro'''<ref>[https://www.flickr.com/photos/papanotzzi/14609764781 Flickr account of PAPANOTZZI], picture taken at {{WWHP}}</ref> was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] perfumer who made the fragrance [[Divine Magic]].

==Behind the scenes==
*This character's name first appears on a billboard in {{HBP|FA}}.
*This character's name is tribute to graphic artist [[Eduardo Lima]], who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban|Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' onward, as well as [[Mauricio Carneiro (real-world)|Mauricio Carneiro]], costume illustrator from ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]'' onward.
