'''Mrs Tuttle''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a [[Seer]]. She had at least two daughters, [[Lana Tuttle|Lana]] and [[Liz Tuttle|Liz]], who did not inherit her gift, to her disappointment.<ref name="HM51">{{HM|5|1}}</ref>

==Biography==
At some point in her early life, she lived in [[Somalia]] and was home-schooled in [[magic]] by her parents.<ref name="HM515">{{HM|5|15}}</ref>

In her youth, Liz was taught [[astrology]] by her mother; after this, Liz "[would] never trust [[Mercury]] again".<ref name="HM716a">{{HM|7|16|a}}</ref> When Liz started her [[third year]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], her mother forced her to take [[Divination (class)|Divination]] as an elective.<ref name="HM62">{{HM|6|2|d}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Divination]]''': Liz Tuttle's mother was a [[Seer]], meaning she was naturally gifted with the [[Inner Eye]] and could receive genuine [[vision]]s of the future.<ref name="HM51"/>
*'''[[Wandless magic]]''': Accoding to Liz, her mother was capable of performing wandless magic, which her parents taught her how to do in Somalia.<ref name="HM72">{{HM|7|2}}</ref>
