}}'''Josefina Calderon''' was a [[17th century|17th-century]] [[Mexico|Mexican]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Healer]] who became the wife of [[Chadwick Boot]], one of the founders of [[Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|ilvermorny}}</ref>

The couple established the [[Calderon-Boot family]], which remains one of America's most prominent wizarding families today.<ref name="Pottermore"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Healing magic]]''': As a professional [[Healer]], Calderon would have been very accomplished with medical [[magic]], such as with [[healing spell]]s, [[potion]]s and other magical remedies.<ref name="Pottermore"/>

==Etymology==
Josefina is a Spanish name meaning "made bigger by god".

While Calderón is a real surname, it was probably picked because of its similarity with "caldero", meaning "[[cauldron]]".
