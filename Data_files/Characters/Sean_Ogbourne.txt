'''Sean Ogbourne''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was usually seen spending time by the entrance to the [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch pitch|pitch]] during the [[1996–1997 school year]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console versions]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
