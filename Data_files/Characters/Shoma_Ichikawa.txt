'''Shoma Ichikawa''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref> He was a member of the [[Duelling Club]] run by [[Helen Dawlish]] in the [[Middle Courtyard|Transfiguration Courtyard]]. He gave [[Harry Potter]] some [[Honeywater]] so that he could make [[Wiggenweld Potion]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console version]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
