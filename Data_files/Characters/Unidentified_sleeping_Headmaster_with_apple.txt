This '''wizard''' was appointed [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] before [[Armando Dippet|Professor Dippet]]. After his death, his [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head. He was portrayed to have been sleeping on an chair, and there is an [[Apple (fruit)|apple]] and a teapot on the table beside it.<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}}</ref><ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}}</ref><ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref><ref name="MHP">{{MHP}}</ref>
