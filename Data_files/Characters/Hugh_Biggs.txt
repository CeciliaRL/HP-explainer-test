'''Hugh Biggs''' (b. [[1690s]]/[[1700s]]) was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1710s]]. He was once [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]] from [[1712]] to [[1716]].<ref>{{HPE}} {{See image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Plaque.jpg}}</ref>
