The '''Vibes Twins''' were [[wizardkind|wizarding]] twin sisters and musicians<ref name="MoHP" />, active in the [[1990s]].

In [[August]] of [[1993]], the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' reported that the upcoming issue of the ''[[Sunday Prophet]]'' would include an exposé of their past of fame, money, and shame.<ref name="LeakyCon2013">"''[[MinaLima|Making the Magic Real]]''" presentation by [[Miraphora Mina]] and [[Eduardo Lima]], at LeakyCon 2013 (29 June, 2013) {{see image|File:DailyProphet1993BackReference.png}}</ref> In [[September]] of [[1993]], the Vibes Twins were scheduled to feature in the [[Spellbound]] "[[Witch Way?]]" musical tour, along with [[DJ Martha]].<ref name="MoHP" />
