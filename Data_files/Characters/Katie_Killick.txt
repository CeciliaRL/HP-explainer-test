'''Katie Killick''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] reported missing along with her relative, [[James Killick|James]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' radio programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Rob Killick, who worked as a sound mix technician on ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'', and as a dialogue editor on ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince]]'', {{DH|F1}}, and {{DH|F2}}.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1877574/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cr269r Rob Killick's profile on IMDb]</ref>
