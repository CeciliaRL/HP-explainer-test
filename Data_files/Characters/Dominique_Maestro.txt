'''Dominique Maestro''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] whose name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of [[Sybill Trelawney|Professor Trelawney]]'s [[Divination Classroom]].<ref name="POAF">{{POA|F}} - Chapter 21 (''Professor Trelawney's Prediction'') {{see image|File:Dominique Maestro.jpg}}</ref><ref>[http://www.the-leaky-cauldron.org/2004/5/15/los-angeles-motion-picture-arts-sciences-contemporary-set-exhibit-report ''Prisoner of Azkaban'' set report] from the Leaky Cauldron</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The name of this character is a tribute to Dominic Masters, who worked as an assistant art director on the first three [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]].<ref name="IMDB">[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0557666/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cr120 Dominic Master's profile on IMDb]</ref>
