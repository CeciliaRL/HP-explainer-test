The '''Hogwarts Express driver''' was the individual who drove the [[Hogwarts Express]] from [[Platform Nine and Three-Quarters]] to the [[Hogsmeade station]].<ref name="PS6"/><ref name="POA5"/>

==History==
On [[1 September]] [[1991]], [[Hermione Granger]] once asked the Hogwarts Express driver when they would be arriving at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]]. The driver claimed that they were nearly there.<ref name="PS6">{{PS|B|6}}</ref>

On [[1 September]] [[1993]], the Hogwarts Express [[trolley witch]] assured the [[trio]] that she would be up with the Hogwarts Express driver if [[Remus Lupin]] woke and was hungry. On the same day, Hermione went to ask the driver why the train had stopped when [[dementor]]s were searching the train for [[Sirius Black]], and Remus Lupin also went to speak to the same individual.<ref name="POA5">{{POA|B|5}}</ref>
