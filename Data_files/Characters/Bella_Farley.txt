'''Bella Farley'''<ref>Closed captions on the DVD/Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] reported missing along with her relatives [[Jake Farley|Jake]], [[Charlie Farley|Charlie]], and [[Madge Farley|Madge]] on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' radio programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.

==Etymology==
"Bella" is the short form of Isabella and other names ending in bella. It is also associated with the Italian word meaning "beautiful".
