'''M. Carneiro''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[wikipedia:theatre director|stage director]] and [[wikipedia:scenic design|production designer]]. Carneiro directed and designed the adapted production of ''[[Romeo and Juliet]]'' that was staged at the [[Regency Playhouse]] in the summer of [[1997]].<ref>{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:Romeo&JulietDetail.png}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after Mauricio Carneiro, who worked as a costume illustrator for every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]'' onward, and as a costumer for ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Order of the Phoenix]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Half-Blood Prince]]''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1769188/ Mauricio Carneiro]" on IMDb</ref>
