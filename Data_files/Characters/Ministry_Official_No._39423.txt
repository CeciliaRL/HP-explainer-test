'''Ministry Official No. 39423''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] who worked at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]]. A Ministry Official, this individual was in charge of validating Ministry of Magic Identity Cards, having validated [[Mafalda Hopkirk]]'s.<ref>{{HPFW}} (see [[:File:MafaldaHopkirkMinistryID.jpg|this image]])</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character's signature seems to say "Wakefield", but that is not entirely legible. If that is correct, this character was presumably named after [[Lauren Wakefield]], a graphic artist who worked on ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince]]'', and both parts of ''[[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)|Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows]]''.
