==Biography==
This individual was one of the attendees of [[Henry Shaw Junior]]'s [[Fundraising Dinner for the Re-election of Senator Henry Shaw|Fundraising Dinner]] on the [[6 December]], [[1926]]. During the dinner, he witnessed the sudden and horrific death of Henry Shaw Junior at the hands of [[Credence Barebone]]'s [[Obscurial]] form, in which he and all of the No-Majs present witnessed [[magic]] with their own eyes.<ref name="FBF">{{FB|F}}</ref>
