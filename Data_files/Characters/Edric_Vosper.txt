'''Edric Vosper''' was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as a [[Chaser]] on the [[English National Quidditch team]] during the four-hundred and twenty-second [[Quidditch World Cup]], in [[1994]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref> He was immortalised on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] by [[1996]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} - NDS version</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Edric" comes from Old English ''ead'', meaning "rich", "blessed" and ''ric,'' "ruler". After the Norman conquest this Old English name was not commonly used. It has occasionally been revived in modern times.
