'''Rachael Codnor''' was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
During the [[1996–1997 school year]], she was usually seen in the [[The Quad|Paved Courtyard]], challenging other students to games of [[Wizard Skittles]].

==Etymology==
"Rachael" is from the Hebrew name רָחֵל (''Rachel'') meaning "ewe".

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
