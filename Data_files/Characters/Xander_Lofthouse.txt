'''Xander Lofthouse''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HBPG"/>

==Biography==
When [[Harry Potter]] and [[Katie Bell]] were on their way to the [[Training Grounds|Training Ground]] for the [[Gryffindor]] [[Quidditch]] tryouts in [[1996]], he and three other Slytherins intercepted them at the [[Stone Circle|Sundial Garden]], threatening to steal the [[broomstick]] that Harry had borrowed from [[Lina Tande]]. Harry had to [[Duelling|duel]] him in order to prevent the theft and drive off all three Slytherins.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>
