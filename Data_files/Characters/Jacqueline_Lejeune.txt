}}'''Jacqueline Lejeune'''<ref>[http://www.nedergamen.nl/games/hpwkz ''Harry Potter: WK Zwerkbal'' credits list] (in Dutch)</ref> was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Quidditch Commentator]] during the [[Quidditch World Cup]] matches.<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

==Physical appearance==
Jacqueline had fair, smooth-looking skin and wide brown eyes. She wore a brown lace hat atop her brown hair, and had very white teeth.<ref name="QWC"/>

==Etymology==
"''Lejeune''" is most likely derived from French "''le''", meaning "''the''" and "''jeune''", meaning "''young''" - essentially, her name is "Jacqueline the Young".
