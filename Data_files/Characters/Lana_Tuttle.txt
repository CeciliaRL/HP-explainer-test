'''Lana'''<ref name="HM722">{{HM|7|22}}</ref> '''Tuttle''' was the older<ref name="age"/> sister of [[Liz Tuttle]]. The two were very close, with Liz saying she would do anything for her sister.<ref name="HM722"/>

Lana owned a [[crup]] that had cruppies in the early [[1980s]]; she wrote to her sister at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] to let her know.<ref name="HM">{{HM|1|Magical Pets}}</ref>
