'''Mr Trelawney''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]].<ref name="PM">{{WW|sybill-trelawney}}</ref> He was the great-grandson of the famous [[Seer]] [[Cassandra Trelawney]].<ref name="PM" /> However, along with the two generations of [[Trelawney family|Trelawneys]] preceding him, he did not possess the gift of [[Divination|Second Sight]].<ref name="ootp15">{{OOTP|B|15}}</ref> He married a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[Sybill Trelawney's mother|woman]], and together they had a daughter, [[Sybill Trelawney|Sybill]], who turned out to have limited Sight.<ref name="PM" />
