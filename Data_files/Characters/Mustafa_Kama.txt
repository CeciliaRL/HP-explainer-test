'''Mustafa Kama''' was a [[France|French]] [[pure-blood]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] of [[Senegal]]ese descent, the husband of [[Laurena Kama]] and the father of [[Yusuf Kama]].<ref name="COG100">{{COG|S|100}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1896]], Mustafa's wife, Laurena, was [[Imperius Curse|Imperiused]] by [[Corvus Lestrange (IV)|Corvus Lestrange]] and left him.<ref>{{COG|S|101}}</ref> When he learned of Laurena's death, he was driven insane out of grief. Shortly before his death, Mustafa made his son swear an [[Unbreakable Vow]] to kill the person whom Corvus loved the most.<ref>{{COG|S|102}}</ref>

==Personality and traits==
According to his son [[Yusuf Kama|Yusuf]], Mustafa was very much in [[love]] with his wife, indicating that he was a devoted husband, but he went insane with grief after learning of her death. Indeed, Mustafa became so overwhelmed by his grief that he forced his son to give an [[Unbreakable Vow]] to avenge his family' ruin by killing the one Corvus IV loved the most.

==Etymology==
His name means "the chosen one" in Arabic, an epithet of Muhammad.
