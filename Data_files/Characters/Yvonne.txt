'''Yvonne''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] woman who was a friend of [[Petunia Dursley]].<ref name="PS2"/>

==Biography==
On [[23 June]] [[1991]], [[Vernon Dursley]] considered her as a replacement babysitter for [[Harry Potter]] when [[Arabella Figg]] broke her leg. Unfortunately for the [[Dursley family|Dursleys]], she was on holiday in [[Majorca]] at the time (which Petunia bluntly reminded Vernon of), and they were forced to take Harry to the [[Zoo]] on [[Dudley Dursley]]'s [[Dudley Dursley's eleventh birthday|eleventh birthday]].<ref name="PS2">{{PS|B|2}}</ref>
