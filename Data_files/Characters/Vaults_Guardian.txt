The '''Vaults Guardian''' was a [[Giant Fire Crab]] placed in [[Spellbook vault|one of the vaults]] of [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] to guard the [[Incendio spellbook|spellbook]] for the [[Fire-Making Spell]] contained within. Gaining access to the vault required a [[Spellbook vault key|special key made of five different coloured gemstones]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}} {{C|GBA version}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*In the GBA version of [[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)|the video game adaptation]] of {{COS}}, [[Harry Potter]] had to defeat this creature to learn the Fire-Making Spell and clear the [[cobweb]]s blocking the entrance to [[Vault 687]].
