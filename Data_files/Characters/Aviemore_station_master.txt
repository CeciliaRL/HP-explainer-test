This '''individual''' worked as the [[Wikipedia:Station master|station master]] at [[Aviemore train station]] in [[1981]]. He speaks in very strong [[Wikipedia:Scots language|Scots]].<ref name="Act4Scene2">{{CC|B|4|2}}</ref>

When they inadvertently were brought back in time to [[1981]] by [[Delphini]], wandless and broomless [[Albus Potter]] and [[Scorpius Malfoy]] walked from the Hogwarts grounds to Aviemore train station. There, they learned from the oblivious station master that it was the day before [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] [[Attack at Godric's Hollow (1981)|killed]] [[James Potter I|James]] and [[Lily J. Potter|Lily Potter]], and the two boys journeyed to [[Godric's Hollow]] in the [[West Country]] to prevent Delphini from changing history.<ref name="Act4Scene2" />
