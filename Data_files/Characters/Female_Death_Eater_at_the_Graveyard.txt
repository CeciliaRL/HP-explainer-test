This '''[[Dark Wizard|Dark witch]]''' was a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]], who was summoned to the [[Little Hangleton graveyard|graveyard]] in [[Little Hangleton]] on the night of [[24 June]], [[1995]], after the newly resurrected [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] summoned his followers to his side.<ref name="GOFF"/>

==Biography==
===First Wizarding War===
In order to have answered Voldemort's call on the night of his return, this witch must have been a Death Eater during the [[First Wizarding War]], and had to have somehow avoided [[Azkaban|imprisonment]].<ref name="GOFF"/>

===Voldemort's return===
[[File:Malfoysvoldemort.PNG|150px|thumb|left|[[Lucius Malfoy]] standing with her whilst watching [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]] torture [[Harry Potter]]]]
This witch arrived at the [[Little Hangleton graveyard|graveyard]] in [[Little Hangleton]] after [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] summoned his [[Death Eaters]] there on the night of [[24 June]], [[1995]]. She [[Apparition|Apparated]] into the area, wearing a Death Eater mask and hood.<ref name="GOFF"/>

She witnessed [[Harry Potter]] being put under the [[Cruciatus Curse]] by Voldemort. When Harry made to escape, she stood by, as Voldemort had ordered the Death Eaters not to do anything.<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}} - Chapter 28 (''Voldemort'')</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Dark Arts]]''': Being a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]], this witch would have been accomplished with dark magic, most likely including the [[Unforgivable Curses]] and other [[dark charm]]s.<ref name="GOFF"/>
*'''[[Apparition]]''': This witch was capable of apparition, as she was capable of apparating to her [[Tom Riddle|master]]'s side in the [[Little Hangleton graveyard]].<ref name="GOFF"/>
