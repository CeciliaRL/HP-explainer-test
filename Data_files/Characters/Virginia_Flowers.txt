'''Virginia Flowers''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] actor in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:TheNewYorkAffair.jpg}}</ref>

Flowers starred with [[Edward Fountain]] in a production of ''[[The New York Affair]]'' staged at the [[Brockman Theatre]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="poster" />
