'''Céléase Lestrange''' (née '''Volant''') was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] born in [[1610s|1611]] who married into the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She died in [[1630s|1635]] and was laid to rest in the [[Lestrange Mausoleum|Lestrange family Mausoleum]] in [[Cimetière du Père-Lachaise]], [[Paris]], France.<ref name="Archive" />

==Behind the scenes==
*Céléase may be related to [[Salomé Lestrange|Salomé Volant]] and [[Jodelle Lestrange|Jodelle Volant]], both of whom also married into the Lestrange family.<ref name="Tree">{{COG}} {{see image|File:LestrangeFamilyTree HighRes.png}}</ref>
