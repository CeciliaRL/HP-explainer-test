This '''student''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the late [[20th century]]. She was appointed the [[Head Boy or Girl|Head Girl]].<ref name="HM33"/>

==Biography==
This witch began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1980]]. In her [[seventh year]], she was appointed the [[Head Boy or Girl|Head Girl]]. She dated the [[Unidentified Head Boy|Head Boy]], which might have been an unusual event.<ref name="HM33">{{HM|3|3}}</ref>

==Possessions==
*'''[[Head Boy or Girl badge|Head Girl badge]]''': She was awarded one of these special badges when she was made [[Head Boy or Girl|Head Girl]] at Hogwarts, for which she would have worn during her formal duties.<ref name="HM33"/>
