'''Mr Plunkett''' was a man who lived during the [[19th century]].<ref name="HPPAVG">{{POA|G}} - [[Chocolate Frog Card|Famous Wizard Card]] ([[Mirabella Plunkett]])</ref>

He had at least one child, a [[wizardkind|witch]] daughter, [[Mirabella Plunkett|Mirabella]].<ref name="HPPAVG" /> During a holiday to [[Loch Lomond]], his daughter fell in love with a [[Unidentified Merman|merman]], and wished to [[marriage|marry]] him.<ref name="HPPAVG" /> However, Mr Plunkett and [[Mirabella Plunkett's mother|his wife]] were deeply opposed, and forbade Mirabella to marry her merman lover.<ref name="HPPAVG" /> Mirabella [[transfiguration|transformed]] herself into a [[haddock]] and was never seen again.<ref name="HPPAVG" />
