This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was sorted into [[Hufflepuff|Hufflepuff House]]. During his school years, he performed in the [[Frog Choir]] in the 1990s.<ref name="POAF">{{POA|F}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1993]], after the sorting, he appeared with other members singing [[Double Trouble|Something Wicked This Way Comes]] before Dumbledore spoke.<ref name="POAF"/>

In [[1997]], he was part of the group who sang [[In Noctem]] before Headmaster [[Albus Dumbledore]] was [[Battle of the Astronomy Tower|killed]] by Professor [[Severus Snape]].<ref name="HBPF">{{HBP|F}}</ref>

It is unknown if he survived the [[Second Wizarding War]].
