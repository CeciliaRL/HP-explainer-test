During the [[1984]] [[Christmas holidays]], [[Jacob's mother]] visited these relatives. For this reason, [[Jacob's sibling]] stayed at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] during the holidays.<ref name="HM1C">{{HM|1|Christmas at Hogwarts|1}}</ref>
