'''Ernestina Abutor''' (also known as '''Red Panther''' or '''Tina Estora''') was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[United States of America]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="CinemaBlend">"[http://www.cinemablend.com/news/1554929/10-fantastic-things-we-learned-on-the-fantastic-beasts-set?story_page=4 10 Fantastic Things We Learned On The Fantastic Beasts Set]" from CinemaBlend</ref>

In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Abutor, which stated that she was a [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] kidnapper and murderer and offered a reward of 4850 [[dragot]]s.<ref>{{FB|F}} {{see image|File:Ernestina Abutor - wanted poster.png}}</ref>

==Etymology==
''Abutor'' is a Latin verb meaning "to consume," "to waste," or "to abuse."<ref>"[https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/abutor abutor]" on Wiktionary</ref>
