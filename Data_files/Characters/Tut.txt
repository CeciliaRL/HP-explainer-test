'''Tut''' was a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] [[greengrocer]] who owned the [[Tut's Nuts]]<ref name="PSIE">{{PS|IE}} {{see image|File:Tut's Nuts.png}}</ref> produce shop in [[Diagon Alley]].<ref name="full">{{PS|IE}} {{see image|File:Diagon Alley - Jim Kay.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
"Tut" is the nickname for the [[Egypt|Egyptian]] {{w|pharaoh}} Tutankhamun.<ref>{{wtlink|Tut}}</ref><ref>{{wikilink|Tutankhamun}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Illustrator [[Jim Kay]] used to work at the {{w|Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew}}; at the time, they had a collection of seeds from {{w|Tutankhamun}}'s {{w|Tomb of Tutankhamun|tomb}}, which were known as "Tut's Nuts", which was how Kay came up with the idea for the shop.<ref name="behind TN">[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PDPcGdt40RU Jim Kay Illustrator - Create Expo 2016] {{c|from 26:13 to 26:28}}</ref>
