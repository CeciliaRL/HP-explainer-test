This blonde-haired '''actress''' starred on a [[Great Britain|British]] [[soap (programme)|soap]] during the [[1990s]]. 

==History==
She wrote an article, entitled "[[MY SECRET SHAME]]", which was published on page 5 of the [[2 August]] [[1995]] issue of the ''[[Daily Express]]''. This article was teased on the front page of the newspaper with a photo by [[Chris Lunney]] of the actress wearing dark sunglasses and fending off [[camera]]s.<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:DailyExpress.png}}</ref>
