'''Diadema Goshawk''' was one of [[Miranda Goshawk]]'s eight sisters.<ref name="WBoS"/>

When she was growing up, she once borrowed clothing from her youngest sister, [[Miranda Goshawk|Miranda]], without permission. Miranda cast a [[Bat-Bogey Hex]] on her to motivate her to give it back.<ref name="WBoS"/>
