'''Irving Cram''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He stood guard over places that were off limits during the [[1996-1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
The name "Irving" is from a Scottish surname which was in turn derived from a Scottish place name.
