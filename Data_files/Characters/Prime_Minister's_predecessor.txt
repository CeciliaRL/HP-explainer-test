This '''man''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Prime Minister (title)|Prime Minister]] who left office sometime before [[1992]]. He met with [[Cornelius Fudge]], who revealed the existence of the [[wizarding world]] to him. Believing the meeting to be a hoax perpetrated by the [[Opposition|opposing political party]], he tried to throw Fudge out of the window of his [[Prime Minister's office|office]].<ref name="HBP1">{{HBP|B|1}}</ref>
