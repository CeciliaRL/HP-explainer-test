This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Durmstrang Institute]], and came to [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] for the [[Triwizard Tournament]] in [[1994]]. While he and his classmates were coming in down the [[Great Hall]], he blew out a fiery bird-symbol that represented the school. He put his name into the [[Goblet of Fire]], but [[Viktor Krum]] was chosen as his school's [[Triwizard Tournament|Triwizard Champion]]. He appeared, watching Krum, in every task, and danced at the [[Yule Ball]]. At the end of the year after [[Cedric Diggory]] had been murdered, he attended the [[Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory]].<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref>
