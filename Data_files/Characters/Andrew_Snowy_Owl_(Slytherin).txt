'''Andrew Snowy Owl''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1940s]] and [[1950s]]. He was [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Slytherin Quidditch team]] from [[1948]] to [[1952]].<ref>See [[:File:Slytherin House Captain's Plaque.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref><ref>[http://ab.fanrus.com/SMI/ViP/september/51/213/ ''Harry Potter: The Exhibition'' report] {{Comment|in Russian}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Andrew" comes from the Greek name Ανδρεας (Andreas), which was derived from ανηρ (aner) "man" (genitive ανδρος (andros) "of a man"). "Snowy Owl" refers to the [[Snowy owl|species of owl]].

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is a presumably a tribute to Andrew Ackland-Snow, who worked as an art director on all eight [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]].
