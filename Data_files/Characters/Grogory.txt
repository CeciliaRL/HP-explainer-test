'''Saint Grogory''' was a [[saint]] after whom [[St. Grogory's Primary School]] was named.<ref name="hppts">{{HPPS}}, p. 403 {{see image|File:DudleyAward1.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*St. Grogory is possibly a reference to the real St Gregory: Pope Gregory I. He is considered the patron saint of, among other things, students and teachers, making him a prime candidate to name a school after.<ref>"[[Wikipedia:Pope Gregory I|Pope Gregory I]]" on [[Wikipedia:Main Page|Wikipedia]]</ref>
