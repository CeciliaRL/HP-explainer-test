'''Lara Von Sigg''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and reporter for the ''[[The Wizard's Voice]]'' in [[1926]].<ref name="newspaper">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:The Wizard's Voice - 6 Dec 1926.png}}</ref> Reporting from [[Houston]], she wrote an article revealing the secret [[wand core]] used by [[New Orleans]] [[wandmaker]] [[Violetta Beauvais]] to be [[rougarou]] [[Rougarou hair|hair]], which was published in the [[6 December]] 1926 issue of the newspaper.<ref name="newspaper" />
