This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who wore a purple cloak with a goatee, that operated in [[Great Britain]] during the late [[20th century]].<ref name="HM75A2"/>

==Biography==
At one point, he began scamming other wizards by alleging to be an [[Alchemy|alchemist]] that had cracked the code of [[panacea]]; a remedy that was supposed to [[Healing magic|cure]] any ailment. Rather than doing what it was supposed to, however, all his [[Rogue alchemist's fake panacea|fake panacea]] did was give several patients boils.<ref name="HM75A2"/>

He was tracked down by [[Alastor Moody]], [[Jacob's sibling]] and [[Nymphadora Tonks]] during the [[1990–1991 school year]].<ref name="HM75A2"/>
