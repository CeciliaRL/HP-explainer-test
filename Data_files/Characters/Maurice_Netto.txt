'''Maurice Netto''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who lived during the [[20th century]].<ref name="trophy">See [[:File:UnidentifiedHogwartsTrophy.jpg|this image]] of a prop trophy used in the [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]] on display at {{WWHP}}.</ref>

Netto attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1940s]].<ref name="trophy" /> He received an award at the school in [[1945]].<ref name="trophy" />

His name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of [[Sybill Trelawney|Professor Trelawney's]] [[Divination (class)|Divination]] [[Divination Classroom|classroom]].<ref>{{POA|F}} {{See image|File:DivinationGraffitiWall.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*[[Wikipedia:Netto (store)|Netto]] is a Danish-based supermarket which has stores in several European countries, including the U.K.
