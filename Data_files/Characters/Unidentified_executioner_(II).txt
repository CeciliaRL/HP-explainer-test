==Biography==
Along with [[Bernadette]] and [[Unidentified executioner (I)|another witch]], he assisted in the execution of [[Newton Scamander]] and [[Porpentina Goldstein]] after being sentenced to [[Death]] by [[Gellert Grindelwald]] (disguised as [[Percival Graves]]) for violating the [[International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy]]. The execution was stopped when Newt and Tina were rescued by a [[Swooping Evil]].<ref name="FBS"/>

During the fight, Newt knocked him out by whacking him in the face with his (Newt's) handcuffs.<ref name="FBS"/>

==Behind the scenes==
