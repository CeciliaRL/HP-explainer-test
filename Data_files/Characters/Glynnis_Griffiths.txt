'''Glynnis Griffiths''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|witch]] who was the [[Seeker]] for the [[Holyhead Harpies]]. She caught the [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]] after a seven-day match against the [[Heidelberg Harriers]] in [[1953]].<ref>{{QTA|R|7}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Griffiths has the same surname as the late British actor [[Richard Griffiths]], who portrayed [[Vernon Dursley]] in the [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' film series]].
