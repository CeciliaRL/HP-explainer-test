'''Tina Lundstrom''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as [[Seeker]] on the [[Nordic National Quidditch team]]. She was also the team's [[Captain]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Lundstrom's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Craig Dunn]] at a [[Gobstones]] challenge.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} {{c|NDS edition}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*Lundstrøm/Lundström is a Danish and Swedish surname, possibly indicating that Tina was either [[Denmark|Danish]] or [[Sweden|Swedish]].
