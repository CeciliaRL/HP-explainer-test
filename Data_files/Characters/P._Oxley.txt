'''P. Oxley''' was an American [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] who worked as a clerk for the [[Division of Vital Statistics]] in the [[New York State Department of Health]]. On the [[25 October]], [[1905]], Oxley attested to and signed off on the certificate for the adoption of [[Credence Barebone]] by [[Mary Lou Barebone]] of the [[New Salem Philanthropic Society]], which had taken place [[21 October|four days earlier]].<ref name="Archive" />
