'''Phillip Montgomery''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="POAG"/>

==Biography==
At some point in the [[1990s]], Phillip began attending Hogwarts, where he was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]] house. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Phillip" is derived from Latin Philippus, from Greek Philippos "fond of horses," from philos "beloved, loving" plus hippos "horse". In the 16th century, Philip and Cheyney was a way to say "any two common men."
