'''Sylvian Boigny''' was an [[Ivory Coast|Ivorian]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as the [[Seeker]] for the [[2014]] [[Ivorian National Quidditch team]]. During the first match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]] against [[Norwegian National Quidditch team|Norway]], Boigny was beaten by her opposite [[Sigrid Kristoffersen]] to the [[Golden Snitch]] after 128 minutes of play, securing a win for Norway.<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[NORWAY VERSUS IVORY COAST]] (Archived)</ref>
