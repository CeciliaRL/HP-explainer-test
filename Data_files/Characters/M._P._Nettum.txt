'''M. P. Nettum''' was a reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]]. They wrote an article, published in the [[6 December]] [[1926]] issue of the newspaper, on the claims of the [[New Salem Philanthropic Society]] and the potential threat said claims posed to [[wizardkind|wizarding]] [[International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy|secrecy]].<ref name="nyghost">{{WWMM|1}} {{see image|File:NewYorkGhost-6December1926.jpg}}</ref>
