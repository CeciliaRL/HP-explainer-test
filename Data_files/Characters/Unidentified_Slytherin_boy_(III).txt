This '''male Slytherin student''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]].

==Biography==
This boy started his [[magic]]al education at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1991]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Slytherin|Slytherin House]]. He confronted Harry and Ron, and told them that Gryffindor was going to lose the match to Slytherin and that "Slytherin is Supreme". He managed to stun Ron, but he lost his duel to Harry.
