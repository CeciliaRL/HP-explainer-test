'''Balbine Lestrange''' was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She was the daughter of [[Fulcran Lestrange (II)|Fulcran Lestrange]] and [[Sotère Lestrange|Sotére Lestrange]] and sister of [[Pucine Lestrange (II)|Pucine Lestrange]] and [[Fulcran Lestrange (III)|Fulcran Lestrange]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref> She shared the same first name with her great-aunt, [[Balbine Lestrange (I)|Balbine Leroy]].
