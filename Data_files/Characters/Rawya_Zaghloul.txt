'''Rawya Zaghloul''' was an [[Egypt]]ian [[wizardkind|witch]] who played as [[Seeker]] for the [[Egyptian National Quidditch team]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[Quidditch World Cup 1990 - 2014]] (Archived)</ref>

==Biography==
She played in the [[2002 Quidditch World Cup]] where she famously beat Bulgarian [[Seeker]] [[Viktor Krum]] to the [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]], leading to the [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team]]'s defeat. This was a "''crushing disappointment''" for Bulgaria and, coupled with the team's loss of the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup]] final, it led to [[Viktor Krum]]'s retirement.<ref name="PM"/>
