'''Eldon Pembroke''' was a [[Hufflepuff]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. During the [[1996-1997 school year]], he could usually be found in the [[Charms Classroom]], and enjoyed playing [[Exploding Snap]] with other students.

==Etymology==
The name "Eldon" comes from a surname which was from a place name meaning "Ella's hill" in Old English.
