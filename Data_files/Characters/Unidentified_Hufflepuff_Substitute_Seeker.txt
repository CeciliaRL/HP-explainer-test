This '''individual''' was a [[Hufflepuff]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and a substitute [[Seeker]] on their [[Quidditch]] [[Hufflepuff Quidditch team|team]] in [[1991]] to [[1992]]. He was filling in when [[Unidentified Hufflepuff Seeker|the actual Seeker]] was unable to play.

==Behind the scenes==
*In the PC versions this Seeker substitute doesn't appear, having instead been replaced by [[Unidentified Hufflepuff Seeker|another student]].
