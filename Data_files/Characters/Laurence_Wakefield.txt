'''Laurence Wakefield ''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] documentary filmmaker. He made several documentary [[television]] series, including ''[[The Australia Series]]'', which was shown on British television in [[1997]].<ref>{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:RadioTimes.jpg}}</ref>
