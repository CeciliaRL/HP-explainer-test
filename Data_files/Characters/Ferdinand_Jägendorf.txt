'''Ferdinand Jägendorf''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] from [[Liechtenstein]], who, in the [[2010s]], worked as coach and manager of the [[Liechtenstein National Quidditch team]].<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20180314234522/http://www.pottermore.com/writing-by-jk-rowling/quidditch-world-cup-2014-daily-prophet-reports Quidditch World Cup 2014: Daily Prophet reports] at {{PM}} {{c|via The Internet Archive}}</ref><ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20180315092254/https://www.pottermore.com/quidditch-world-cup/daily-prophet-reports-2014 Quidditch World Cup enhanced reading experience] at {{PM}} {{c|via The Internet Archive}}</ref>

==Biography==
During the celebrations following the quarter-final match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]], in which the [[American National Quidditch team|USA]] defeated [[Liechtenstein National Quidditch team|Liechtenstein]] on [[8 June]], [[2014]], [[Hans]] was reported to have been kidnapped by celebrating American fans. Jägendorf issued the following statement: "''Das finden wir nicht lustig''" ("we don't find that funny").<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[LATE BREAKING NEWS]]</ref>
