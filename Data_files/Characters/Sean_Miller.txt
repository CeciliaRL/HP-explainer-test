'''Sean Miller''' (born c. [[1974]]-[[1980]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. His quill was accidentally eaten by a female [[Ravenclaw]] student during the [[1991–1992 school year]].<sup><ref name="PSgba">{{PS|G}} - GBA version (see [[:File:Sean Miller ref.jpg|this image]])</ref></sup>
