}}'''Georgi Zdravko'''<ref>{{PM}} - [[2014 Quidditch World Cup final]] (Archived)</ref> was a Bulgarian [[wizardkind|wizard]] and the [[Keeper]] on the [[2014]] [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team]].

==Etymology==
*Georgi (hard G on both counts) is the Bulgarian version of George.
*Zdravko is a male given name, from ''здрав ['zdrav]'' ("strong", "healthy").

==Behind the scenes==
*Zdravko is a male given name. The derivative surname is Zdravkov.
