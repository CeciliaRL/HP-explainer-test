}}'''Arnold Vogler'''<ref>[http://www.nedergamen.nl/games/hpwkz ''Harry Potter: WK Zwerkbal'' credits list] (in Dutch)</ref> was a [[Germany|German]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Quidditch]] commentator, who might have worked for the [[German Ministry of Magic]]. He was a former player of the [[Heidelberg Harriers]], for which he played in an unknown position.<ref>{{QWC}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Arnold" comes from a Germanic name meaning "eagle power", derived from the elements arn "eagle" and wald "power". It was brought by the Normans to England, where it replaced the Old English cognate Earnweald.
