The '''Fates''' (also known as '''Moirai''') was a group of three weaving [[Greece|Greek]] goddesses who assign [[Life path|individual destinies]] to mortals at birth. Their names were Clotho (the Spinner), Lachesis (the Alloter) and Atropos (the Inflexible).<ref name="wiki">{{Wikilink|The Fates}}</ref> Professor Trelawney claimed have gotten information about the future [[Divination (class)|Divination]] examination from them in [[June]] [[1994]].<ref name="POA15">{{POA|B|15}}</ref>
