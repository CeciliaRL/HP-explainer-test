'''Alberto Macellarius''', also known by the nickname '''The Rat''', was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].<ref name="newspaper">[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ViuDsy7yb8M Second teaser trailer for ''Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them''] {{see image|File:TNYG.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
''[[The New York Ghost]]'' published a wanted poster from [[Magical Congress of the United States of America|MACUSA]] stating that Macellarius was wanted for "counterfeiting [[wand]]s" and murder, and that they were offering a reward of 3,500 [[dragot]]s for information leading to his capture.<ref name="newspaper" />

==Etymology==
''Macellarius'' is a Latin word meaning "butcher" or "meat seller," from the Latin word ''macellum'', meaning "provision market."<ref>"[https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/macellarius macellarius]" on Wiktionary</ref>
