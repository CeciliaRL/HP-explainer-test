'''Fulcran Lestrange''' was a wizard and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. He was the son of [[Cyrille Lestrange (I)|Cyrille Lestrange]] and [[Darenne Lestrange|Darenne Moreau]]. He had a brother [[Cyrille Lestrange (II)|Cyrille]]. He married [[Pucine Lestrange (I)|Pucine Leroy]] and had a son with her, [[Fulcran Lestrange (II)|Fulcran]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
