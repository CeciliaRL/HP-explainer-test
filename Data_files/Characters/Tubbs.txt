'''Mrs Tubbs''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] who owned the [[Tuck Shop]]. This store sold wizarding snacks like [[Cauldron Cake]]s and [[Pumpkin Pasty|Pumpkin Pasties]].<ref name="poster">See [[:File:TuckShopPoster.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*A tuck shop is a term used to describe a store that is very near to, or on the grounds of, a school. In the UK they are usually associated with selling sweets or other snacks and may also sell school supplies. This indicates that the store may be on [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] grounds.
