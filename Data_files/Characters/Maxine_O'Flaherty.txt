'''Maxine O'Flaherty''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] that attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

==Biography==
She was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] sometime before [[1994]] into [[Hufflepuff]]. She was one of the [[Beater]]s for the [[Hufflepuff Quidditch team]] at the time that it was captained by [[Cedric Diggory]].<ref name="QWC"/>
