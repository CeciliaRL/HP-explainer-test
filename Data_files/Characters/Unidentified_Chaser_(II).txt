This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1980s]].<ref name="QC"/>

==Biography==
During his time at Hogwarts, he was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into an unknown house. At some point during his time at school, he became a reserve [[Chaser]] of an unknown Quidditch team.<ref name="QC">{{HM|4|Q3|3}}</ref>

===1987–1988 school year===
{{Quote|Glad [[Ethan Parkin]] came to train with us! I feel like part of the team!|This boy before a Quidditch match|Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery}}
In the [[1987–1988 school year]], after [[Erika Rath]] injured [[Orion Amari]] in a [[Quidditch friendly]], he became a regular team's member.  As a result, he played on the team during the first Quidditch [[Inter-House Quidditch Cup|match]].<ref name="QC"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*His house is the same as that of [[Jacob's sibling]], which is chosen by the player.
*This Chaser's appearance was initially used for [[Unidentified Chaser|another Chaser]] on the same Quidditch team.<ref>{{HM|2|Q1|12}}</ref>
