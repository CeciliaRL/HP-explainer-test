These unnamed '''[[Wampanoag]] children''' were two [[Native Americans|Native American]] [[Wizardkind|wizards]] who became one of the first students of [[Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[17th century]]. They were part of the Wampanoag tribe.<ref name="Pottermore" />

==Biography==
Along with a [[Unidentified Narragansett mother|mother]] and her [[Unidentified Narragansett children|two daughters]] of the [[Narragansett]] tribe, they were given [[wand]]s made by [[Isolt Sayre]] and [[James Steward]], and instructed in the use of them and in return they shared their own [[magic]]al knowledge.<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|ilvermorny}}</ref>
