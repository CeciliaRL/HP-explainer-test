'''Nikola Vassileva''' was a Bulgarian witch and a [[Chaser]] for the [[2014]] [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team]]. During the second quarter-final match of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], against [[Norwegian National Quidditch team|Norway]], Nikola Vassileva scored both of Bulgaria's goals, which Norwegian [[Chaser]] [[Lars Lundekvam]] soon equalised. Seeker [[Viktor Krum]], soon after, caught the [[Golden Snitch|Snitch]], making Bulgaria the victor.<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[BULGARIA VERSUS NORWAY]] (Archived)</ref>
