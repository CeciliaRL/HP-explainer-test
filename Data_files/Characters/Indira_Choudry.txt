'''Indira Choudry''' was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as a [[Beater]] for the [[English National Quidditch team]].

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Choudry's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Gareth Pewsey]] in a [[Gobstones]] challenge.<ref>{{HBP|G}} {{c|NDS edition}}</ref>

==Etymology==
"Indira" means "beauty" and "splendid" in Sanskrit. This is another name of Lakshmi, the wife of the Hindu god Vishnu. A notable bearer was Indira's first female prime minister, Indira Gandhi (1917-1984).
