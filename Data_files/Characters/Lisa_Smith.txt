'''Lisa Smith''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who was resident of [[6 Wisteria Walk|Number 6]], [[Wisteria Walk]], [[Little Whinging]].<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The fictitious Lisa Smith is only mentioned on the F.A.Q. section of {{JKRS}}, where a letter addressed to [[J. K. Rowling]] is stated to have been sent by her.
*Lisa was aged 12 at the time that she sent her letter.
