'''Marcus Fenwick''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
During his [[fifth year]], he was apparently the head of the [[Hogwarts Gobstone Club]], as students interested in joining the club were instructed to see him on a sign-up sheet posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in the [[Gryffindor]] [[Gryffindor Tower|common room]].<ref name="HPE">See [[:File:GobstonesClubSignUpSheet.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref>
