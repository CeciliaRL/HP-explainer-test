'''Fischer Frey''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who started attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[2008]]{{MATimeRef}} and was a member of [[Slytherin]] house. He was the older<ref name="MA138">{{MA|1|38}}</ref> [[Twins|twin]] brother of [[Colby Frey]].<ref name="MA">{{MA}}</ref>

==Biography== 
At the age of eleven, he and Colby began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[Slytherin]] house. They befriended [[Cassandra Vole]], and she led their group in bullying their fellow students including [[Daniel Page]].<ref name="MA"/>

They went to the [[Hogwarts Library|Library]] one night to find a newspaper article about Daniel. Daniel, [[Ivy Warrington]] and their [[Unidentified 2000s Hogwarts student|friend]] found out that they had broken curfew and went to find out why. They watched the group at a distance, until Colby noticed and told Cassandra.<ref name="MA"/>

==Personality and traits==
Fischer had a mean-spirited personality and enjoyed bullying others with this friends.<ref name="MA"/> He was known to like [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] and [[Quidditch]], and dislike [[History of Magic]] at Hogwarts.<ref name="info"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Fischer was able to produce a corporeal [[Patronus Charm|Patronus]] in the form of a [[crocodile]], an exceptional [[magic]]al feat for a Hogwarts student.<ref name="info"/>
*'''[[Defence Against the Dark Arts]]''': Fischer was known to enjoy the subject of Defence Against the Dark Arts at Hogwarts.<ref name="info"/>

==Possessions==
*'''[[Fischer Frey's wand|Wand]]''': Fischer's [[wand]] was 12¼ inches, made of [[ash]] with a [[unicorn hair]] [[Wand core|core]].<ref name="info"/>
