This '''student''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the late [[20th century]]. He was appointed the [[Head Boy or Girl|Head Boy]].<ref name="HM33"/>

==Biography==
This wizard began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1980]]. In his [[seventh year]], he was appointed the [[Head Boy or Girl|Head Boy]]. He dated the [[Unidentified Head Girl|Head Girl]], which might have been an unusual event.<ref name="HM33">{{HM|3|3}}</ref>

==Possessions==
*'''[[Head Boy or Girl badge|Head Boy badge]]''': He was awarded one of these special badges when he was made [[Head Boy or Girl|Head Boy]] at Hogwarts, for which he would have worn during his formal duties.<ref name="HM33"/>
