Sergeant '''Fisher''' (fl. [[1977]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[police]]man who was partnered with Police Constable [[Anderson]].<ref name=":0">[[Untitled charity auction story|Harry Potter Prequel]]</ref>

==Biography==
A large man, Fisher at some point in his life joined the police force and had a [[Fisher's daughter|daughter]] who dated a [[Unidentified guitarist|guitarist]]. In [[1977]], he was partnered with PC Anderson, and followed a [[Sirius Black's motorcycle|motorbike]] that was speeding through the streets of an unnamed city and finally cornered it in a dead-end alley. As the two attempted to arrest the pair of youths on the bike — who identified themselves as [[Sirius Black]] and [[James Potter I|James Potter]] — they witnessed an attack on the pair by three men flying on [[broomstick]]s.<ref name=":0" />
