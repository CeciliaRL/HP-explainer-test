'''Josette Gage''' (née '''Lestrange''') was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Gage family]]. She was the daughter of [[Corvus Lestrange (II)|Corvus Lestrange]] and [[Minette Lestrange]]. She had two siblings, sister [[Manon Perrot|Manon]] and brother [[Corvus Lestrange (III)|Corvus]]. She married [[Reynard Gage]] and had two sons with him, [[Louis Gage|Louis]] and [[Mathis Gage|Mathis]].<ref name="COG">{{COG}} {{see image|File:The art of CoG lestrange.jpeg}}</ref>
