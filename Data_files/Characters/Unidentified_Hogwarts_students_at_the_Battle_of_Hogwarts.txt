==Slytherin girl (II)==
This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. It is unknown if she herself fought in the Battle of Hogwarts, since many Slytherins departed the school before the battle started, but it is possible she among the Slytherin students recruited by [[Horace Slughorn]] to fight for Hogwarts.

;Behind the scenes
*This student was portrayed by [[Holly Murdoch]]; on the actress' Mandy page, her role was credited as "Slytherin school girl" with the production year stated as 2010-2011, meaning she was present during the filming of both [[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1|Part 1]] and [[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2|2]] of the film adaptations of {{DH}}.<ref>[https://www.mandy.com/dancer/profile/holly-murdoch Holly Murdoch's Mandy profile]</ref>
