They arrived and tried to convince the new [[Gurg]] [[Golgomath]] to join them. He eventually agreed and then told this Death Eater and Macnair how [[Rubeus Hagrid]] and [[Olympe Maxime]] had tried to convince the old Gurg [[Karkus]] to join the [[Order of the Phoenix]] led by [[Albus Dumbledore]].
