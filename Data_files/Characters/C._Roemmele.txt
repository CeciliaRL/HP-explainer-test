'''C. Roemmele'''<ref name="hppts">{{HPPS}}, p. 403 {{see image|File:DudleyAward1.jpg}}</ref> was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] woman who was headmistress of [[St. Grogory's Primary School]] in the early [[1990s]].<ref name="PS2"/>

==Biography==
She once sent a letter to [[Vernon Dursley|Vernon]] and [[Petunia Dursley]] informing them that their nephew and ward, [[Harry Potter]], had been "climbing school buildings," when he had in fact [[underage magic|unintentionally]] [[Flight|flown]] onto the school roof while trying to hide from his cousin [[Dudley Dursley|Dudley]] and his [[Dudley Dursley's gang|gang]].<ref name="PS2">{{PS|B|2}}</ref>

On [[22 June]], [[1991]], Roemmele presented Dudley with [[Headteacher's Award|an award]] for "always eating up his lunch."<ref name="hppts">{{HPPS}}, p. 403 {{see image|File:DudleyAward1.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is referred to simply as "Harry's headmistress" in {{PS}}. Her name was first revealed on a prop award certificate featured in this novel's [[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|film adaptation]].
