'''Laurentia Fletwock''' (b. [[1947]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who was a celebrated breeder and [[Winged horse racing|racer]] of [[winged horse]]s. Presumably because of her involvement in the world of winged horses, she had subsequently campaigned for [[broomstick]] usage to come under stricter regulations.<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>
