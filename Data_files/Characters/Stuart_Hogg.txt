'''Stuart Hogg''' (b. [[24 June]], [[1992]]), a professional rugby union player who plays as a [[Wikipedia:Rugby union positions#Full-back|full-back]] for the [[Scottish rugby team]] since [[2012]],<ref name="Wikipedia">{{Wikilink|Stuart Hogg (rugby player)}}</ref> was rumoured to be a very talented [[Squib]].<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|scottish-rugby}}</ref> He was, however, a [[wizardkind|wizard]].<ref name="wizard">[https://twitter.com/jk_rowling J.K. Rowling (@jk_rowling)] on Twitter: [https://twitter.com/jk_rowling/status/711242899279781888 "That thing where I said Stuart Hogg might be a Squib? He's a wizard."]</ref>

In [[2014]], it came under suspect among [[wizarding world|wizarding circles]], that Stuart Hogg, along with [[Kelly Brown]] and [[Jim Hamilton]], were smuggled into the team by the [[Wizarding Supporters of Scottish Rugby Union|WSSRU]], in violation of the [[International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy]].<ref name="Pottermore"/>
