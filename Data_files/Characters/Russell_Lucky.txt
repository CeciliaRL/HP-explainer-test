'''Russell Lucky''' (b. [[1720s]]/[[1730s]]) was a [[Slytherin]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1740s]]. He was [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Slytherin]] [[Quidditch]] [[Slytherin Quidditch team|team]] from [[1740]] to [[1744]].<ref>{{HPPS}}, p. 349 {{see image|File:Slytherin Captains Plaque 2.jpg}}</ref>[[File:Slytherin Captains Plaque 2.jpg|236x236px|thumb|left|{{PAGENAME}}'s name appearing on a plaque]]
