'''Idris Oakby''' ([[1872]]—[[1985]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]], philanthropist and the founder of the [[Society for the Support of Squibs]].<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>

==Biography==

===Early Life===
Idris Oakby was born in [[1872]] to parents of unknown [[blood status]], in an unknown country, therefore, nothing is known of her upbringing or education. In her adult life, she set up a charitable organisation called [[Society for the Support of Squibs]] which supported Squibs and helped them live in the [[Wizarding world]].<ref name="JKRS"/> She may have been frowned upon by other wizards and witches for her charitable work, considering how poorly Squibs were generally viewed in the magical world.

===Death and Post-Mortem===
Oakby [[Death|died]] in [[1985]] at the age of 113.<ref name="JKRS"/>

==Etymology==
The name "Idris" means "ardent lord" from Welsh ''udd'', "lord, prince" combined with ''ris'', "ardent", "enthusiastic", "impulsive".
