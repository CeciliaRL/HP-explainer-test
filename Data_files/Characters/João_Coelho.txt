|nationality=Peruvian or Brazilian (Possibly)}}'''João Coelho''' was a [[South America]]n [[wizardkind|wizard]] who, as of [[2016]], was the Captain of the [[Tarapoto Tree-Skimmers]], a world-renowned [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch#Known Quidditch teams|team]] from [[Peru]]. He received his magical education at [[Castelobruxo]] in [[Brazil]].<ref name="WW">{{WW|castelobruxo}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*"''João''" (pronounced [ʒwɐ̃u̯]) is the Portuguese form of the name [[John (disambiguation)|John]]. "''[[Wikipedia:Coelho (surname)|Coelho]]''" (pronounced [kwˈe.ʎu]) is a Brazilian and Portuguese surname, and it translates as "rabbit".

==Behind the scenes==
*While his name is of Portuguese origin and [[Brazil]] is the only Portuguese-speaking country in South America, he captained a [[Peru]]vian Quidditch team. His attendance of Castelobruxo gives no indication of his nationality, as the school accepts students from all over South America.
