'''Luc Millefeuille''' was a [[France|French]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and pâtissier who became famous for deliberately [[poison]]ing [[Non-magic people|Muggle]]s, as a form of [[Muggle-baiting]]. In his youth, he attended [[Beauxbatons Academy of Magic]].<ref name="Pottermore">{{WW|beauxbatons-academy-of-magic}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*A [[Wikipedia:Mille-feuille|mille-feuille]] (more commonly called a vanilla slice, a cream slice, or a Napoleon, in the United Kingdom) is a pastry comprised of puff pastry, crème pâtissière, and glazed icing, in layers.
