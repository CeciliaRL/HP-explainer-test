This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was appointed [[Headmaster]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] at some unknown time. After his death, his [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head.<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref><ref name="MHP">{{MHP}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*In {{GOF|F}}, this headmaster's portrait is found nearly at ground level to the left of the Headmaster's desk. At {{MHP}} his portrait is near the top of the wall above that of [[Fytherley Undercliffe]].
