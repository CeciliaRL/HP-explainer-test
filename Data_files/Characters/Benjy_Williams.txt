}}'''Benjy Wiliams''' was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as the [[Seeker]] for [[Puddlemere United]].<ref name="wizardsw">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/w.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: W]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon</ref> His catch of the Snitch, amidst the chaos of the riot during a 1999 match against the [[Holyhead Harpies]], won the game and brought it to a close<ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]], [[1 October]], [[1999]], Page 2</ref>. He and other team members had to be escorted to safety by [[Hit Wizard|Hit-Wizards]] from the Magical Law Enforcement Squad<ref>https://www.hp-lexicon.org/character/benjy-williams/</ref>.
