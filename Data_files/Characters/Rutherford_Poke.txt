'''Rutherford Poke''' was a [[Healer]] at and a spokesperson for [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries]].<ref name="POT"/>

==Biography==
In [[2010]], he lamented the incidence of injuries caused by people attempting to use [[Faux Floo|homemade substitutes]] for [[Floo Powder]], reminding people that the authentic article only cost two [[Sickle]]s per scoop, and swearing he would refuse to treat the next [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who came into St Mungo's with a burned bottom.<ref name="POT">{{WW|floo-powder}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Healing magic]]''': As a professional [[Healer]], Rutherhood would have been highly adept and accomplished with medical [[magic]], such as with [[healing spell]]s, [[potion]]s and other magical remedies.<ref name="POT"/>
