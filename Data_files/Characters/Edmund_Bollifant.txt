'''Edmund Bollifant''' was a publisher based in [[London]] in the late [[16th century]].<ref name="hple">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:A Perambulation of Kent.png}}</ref> His imprint published ''[[A Perambulation of Kent]]'' in [[1596]].<ref name="hple" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This publisher was mentioned on a prop book spine label featured in the [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] [[Hogwarts Library|library]] scenes throughout the [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]]. This label was based on the [https://books.google.pt/books?id=FwY6AAAAcAAJ&printsec=frontcover&hl=pt-PT#v=onepage&q&f=false title page] of an actual [[1596]] edition of ''[[A Perambulation of Kent]]'' by [[wikipedia:William Lambarde|William Lambarde]].
