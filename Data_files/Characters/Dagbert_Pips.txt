'''Dagbert Pips''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]], who was the owner of [[Pumpkins R Us]]. He stated to a ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' reporter that the latest [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry]]-imposed restrictions on [[Hallowe'en]] celebrations would affect pumpkin-growers  like himself extremely harshly.<ref name="DP">{{DP}}: [[1 October]], [[1999]], Page 1</ref>

[[Magic]]al pumpkins cultivated by Pips included [[luminous pumpkin]]s, [[flying pumpkin]]s, [[singing pumpkin]]s and [[exploding pumpkin]]s.<ref name="DP"/>
