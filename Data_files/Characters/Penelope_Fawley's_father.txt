'''Mr Padgett''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]], the husband of [[Penelope Fawley's mother|Mrs Padgett]] and the father of [[Penelope Fawley]].<ref name="WU"/>

==Biography==
During the [[Second Wizarding War]], Mr and Mrs Padgett were outspoken supporters of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] and his [[Magic is Might]] campaign. Following the end of the war and the fall of Voldemort, Mrs Padgett was imprisoned in Azkaban for her role in the conflict, although it is unknown if Mr Padgett was also sent to Azkaban or not.<ref name="WU">{{WU}}</ref>
