'''Joscelind Wadcock''' (b. [[1911]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|witch]] who played as a [[Chaser]] for the [[Puddlemere United]] [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|team]]. She holds the record for highest number of [[Quidditch]] goals in the British League during the [[20th century]], achieved during a [[1931]] match against the [[Ballycastle Bats]].<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref><ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref><ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref><ref name="PM">{{PM}}</ref>
