'''Eustace Fawley''' was a [[Hufflepuff]] student who studied at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].

==Biography==
[[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Hufflepuff|Hufflepuff House]], he was involved with a [[fire]] at the [[Boathouse]] during his [[third year]]. He said he came down to practise [[gobstones]], but ended up losing [[Eustace Fawley's father|his father]]'s best set during the fire.<ref name="FBC7">{{FBC|7}}</ref>

He was known to read [[Martin Miggs]] comics and has [[Eustace Fawley's uncle|an uncle]] that lived in [[Fiji]].<ref name="FBC7" />

==Etymology==
Eustace is the English form of ''Eustathios'', derived from the Greek word ''eustathes ''meaning "well-built, stable".

Fawley is a name derived from the Old English word ''fugelere'' which means "hunter of wild birds, fowler".
