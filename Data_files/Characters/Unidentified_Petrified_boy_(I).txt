This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1980s]].

==Biography==
This individual began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] between [[1 September]] [[1983]] and 1 September [[1989]].<ref name="age">He appeared during the [[1989–1990 school year]], and he could have been in any year.</ref>

During the [[1989–1990 school year]], he was found [[Petrification|Petrified]] in the [[Clock Tower Courtyard]].<ref name="HM62">{{HM|6|2}}</ref> He was later brought to the [[Hospital Wing]] along with [[Unidentified Petrified Gryffindor girl|another Petrified student]].<ref name="HM67">{{HM|6|7}}</ref>

Eventually, he was cured with the other victims.<ref name="HM630">{{HM|6|43}}</ref>
