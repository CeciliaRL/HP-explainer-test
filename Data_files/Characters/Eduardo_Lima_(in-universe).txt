'''Eduardo Lima''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] who the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] kept tabs on while it was under [[Fall of the Ministry of Magic|Death Eater control]] in [[1997]]. His file was signed by Chief Registration Authority [[F. L. Wakefield]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{See image|File:EduardoLimaMinistryPaperwork.JPG}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is tribute to [[Eduardo Lima]], a graphic artist who worked on every film from ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' onward, and whose photo is featured in the prop file.
