This '''secretary''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] woman<ref name="FBF"/> who worked for [[Gilbert Bingley]] at the central [[Steen National Bank]] branch in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="FB8"/>

On [[6 December]] [[1926]], when [[Jacob Kowalski]] came to the bank seeking a business loan, the secretary came to him to remind him that her boss was ready to see him.<ref name="FB8">{{FB|S|8}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The secretary was portrayed by [[Lucie Pohl]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
*This character's gender is not stated in {{FB|S}}, but instead shown in [[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|its film adaptation]].
