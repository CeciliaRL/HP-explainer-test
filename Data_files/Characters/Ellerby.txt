'''Ellerby''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] who worked along with [[Able Spudmore]] and founded the [[Black Forest]] Broomstick Company [[Ellerby and Spudmore]]. They released the [[Tinderblast]] in [[1940]] and then the [[Swiftstick]] in [[1952]].<ref>{{QTA|R|9}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Ellerby's full name and gender are never revealed, and they are only referenced indirectly as the namesake of [[Ellerby and Spudmore]], a broomstick company that is only mentioned in {{QTA}}.
