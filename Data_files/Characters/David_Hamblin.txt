'''David Hamblin''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] and a former [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] for the [[Gryffindor]] [[Gryffindor Quidditch team|Quidditch team]].<ref name="PMPSH">{{PM}} for Playstation Home {{see image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Pottermore.png}}</ref>

By the early [[1990s]], there was a plaque on the mantlepiece at [[Gryffindor Tower]] listing him as a former Gryffindor Quidditch Captain.<ref name="PMPSH"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*David Hamblin is a lead artist for PlayStation Home, so this naming is a tribute to him.<ref name="together">[http://www.familyfriendlyvideogames.com/NewsandNotes/making-games-together---the-family-impact Making Games Together - The Family Impact]</ref>
