'''Lottie Turner''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[2008]].<ref name="MA">{{MA}}</ref>{{MATimeRef}}

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Astronomy]]''': Lottie was known to enjoy the study of Astronomy.<ref name="MA fact file"/>
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Lottie was capable of producing a fully corporeal [[Patronus Charm|Patronus]] in the form of a [[hummingbird]], an exceptional [[magic]]al feat for a Hogwarts student.<ref name="MA fact file"/> She was also proficient with the [[Shrinking Charm]] and [[Doubling Charm]].<ref name="MA"/>
*'''Artist''': Lottie was good at painting and used to paint with [[Lottie Turner's father|her father]].<ref name="MA"/>

==Possessions==
*'''[[Lottie Turner's wand|Wand]]''': Lottie's [[wand]] was 10½ inches, made of [[elder]] [[Wand wood|wood]] with a [[unicorn hair]] [[Wand core|core]].<ref name="MA fact file"/>
