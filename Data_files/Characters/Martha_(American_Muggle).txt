'''Martha''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] woman who lived in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="FB5">{{FB|S|5}}</ref>

==Biography==
While at the scene of a [[Obscurial|mysterious attack]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]], a [[Unidentified reporter|newspaper reporter]] asked his [[Unidentified photographer (II)|photographer colleague]] if he felt like getting a drink, to which the photographer replied that he had promised Martha (presumably his wife or girlfriend) that he would abstain from consuming [[alcohol]].<ref name="FB5" />
