These '''sinister-looking monks''' were depicted in a picture hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]]. Their portrait hung at the top of the [[Divination Stairwell]] in the [[North Tower]], close to the [[Divination Classroom]]. [[Cadogan|Sir Cadogan]] bade farewell to [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley]] and [[Hermione Granger]] through this portrait, after leading the trio along the [[Divination corridor|long corridor]] to the Divination Classroom.<ref name="POA6">{{POA|B|6}}</ref>
