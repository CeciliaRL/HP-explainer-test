This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1980s]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]].

==Biography==
He lived somewhere in [[Great Britain]] or [[Ireland]] and started attending Hogwarts on [[1 September]] [[1984]].<ref name="Transfiguration" />

He had [[Transfiguration (class)|Transfiguration]] class with [[Jacob's sibling]] and didn't like the other students talking during lectures.<ref name="Transfiguration">{{HM}}</ref>

By his [[fifth year]], this student attended [[Minerva McGonagall|Professor McGonagall]]'s lectures on [[Transfiguration|transforming]] [[ferret]]s to feather dusters<ref name="Transfiguration">{{HM}}</ref> and [[Switching Spell|switching]] ferrets with feather dusters.<ref name="HM5ch5">{{HM|5|5|t}}</ref>
