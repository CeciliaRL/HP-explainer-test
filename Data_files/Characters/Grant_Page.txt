'''Grant Page''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Ravenclaw]] student who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was the [[Keeper]] for the [[Ravenclaw Quidditch team]] during the [[1993–1994 school year]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Grant" comes from a Scottish surname which was derived from Norman French grand meaning "great, large".
