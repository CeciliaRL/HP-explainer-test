'''Winkus Oddpick''' was a columnist for the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''<ref name=":0">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 June]], [[1999]], Page 1</ref>. One noteworthy contribution he has made for the paper was an article entitled, ''"Why Can't [[Goblin]]s Be More like [[House-elf|Elves]]?''<ref name=":0" />''"'' after the [[Chipping Clodbury riot]].
