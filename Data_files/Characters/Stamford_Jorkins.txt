'''Stamford Jorkins''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] spokesperson. In [[October]] [[1999]] he was interviewed regularly by the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' regarding Ministry restrictions on [[Hallowe'en]] celebrations.<ref name="DP">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 October]], [[1999]], Page 1</ref>
