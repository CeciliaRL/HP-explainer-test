'''Anna Mirfield''' was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She gave [[Harry Potter]] some ground [[unicorn]] horn in exchange for a bottle of [[butterbeer]] during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (PlayStation 2, Xbox, GameCube)|console versions]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (Nintendo DS)|NDS version]] of {{HBP}}.
