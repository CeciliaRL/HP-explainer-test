This '''[[Dark Wizard]]''' was stolen from by [[Mundungus Fletcher]] and duelled [[Jacob's sibling]] in the [[1988–1989 school year]].<ref name="HM524"/>

==Biography==
During the [[1988–1989 school year]], [[Mundungus Fletcher]] asked [[Jacob's sibling]] to duel this wizard, who, according to him, cheated him, in [[Knockturn Alley]] in exchange for the [[jinx]]ed [[Ever-Bashing Boomerang]]s. Jacob's sibling did so, and after the duel, the wizard threatened to crush Fletcher if he stole from him again.<ref name="HM524"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Dark Arts]]''': This wizard was proficient with dark magic, being a Dark Wizard.<ref name="HM524"/>
*'''[[Duelling]]''': This wizard was an accomplished duellist who was skilled with [[martial magic]].<ref name="HM524"/>
