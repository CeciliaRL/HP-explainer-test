'''Mrs Bingley''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] woman and the wife of [[Gilbert Bingley]], who lived in [[New York]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="FB118"/>

==Biography==
In [[December]] of [[1926]], [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] had [[Frank]] the [[thunderbird]] disperse [[Swooping Evil]] [[Swooping Evil venom|venom]] over [[New York]] by creating a [[Water|rainstorm]], in order to wipe the [[Memory|memories]] of a [[Obscurial|recent magical catastrophe]] from the city's No-Maj population. Mrs Bingley was brushing her teeth in the bathroom while her husband showered when this occurred. Her expression was described as becoming "vacant" and "carefree" as she lost the memories.<ref name="FB118">{{FB|S|118}}</ref>
