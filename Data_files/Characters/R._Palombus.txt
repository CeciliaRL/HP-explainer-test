'''R. Palombus''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] and reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="nyghost-dec6">{{WWMM|1}} {{see image|File:NewYorkGhost-6December1926.jpg}}</ref><ref name="nyghost-dec7">{{CB}} {{see image|File:The New York Ghost - 7 Dec 1926.png}}</ref>

==History==
Palombus wrote an article about the questioning of "suspicious [[wizardkind|wizards]]" by [[Magical Congress of the United States of America|MACUSA]] that was published in the [[6 December]] [[1926]] sunset issue of the newspaper.<ref name="nyghost-dec6" /> This was followed by a report by Palombus in [[7 December|the next day's]] sunset issue about how MACUSA was cooperating with the [[International Confederation of Wizards]] to crack down on "illegal wizards."<ref name="nyghost-dec7" />
