'''Colby Frey''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who started attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[2008]]{{MATimeRef}} and was a member of [[Slytherin]] house. He was the younger<ref name="MA138">{{MA|1|38}}</ref> [[Twins|twin]] brother of [[Fischer Frey]].<ref name="MA">{{MA}}</ref>

==Biography== 
At the age of eleven, he and Fischer began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[Slytherin]] house. They befriended [[Cassandra Vole]], and she led their group in bullying their fellow students including [[Daniel Page]].<ref name="MA"/>

They went to the [[Hogwarts Library|Library]] one night to find a newspaper article about Daniel. Daniel, [[Ivy Warrington]] and their [[Unidentified 2000s Hogwarts student|friend]] found out that they had broken curfew and went to find out why. They watched the group at a distance, until Colby noticed and told Cassandra.<ref name="MA"/>

==Personality and traits==
Colby had a mean-spirited personality and enjoyed bullying others with his friends.<ref name="MA"/> He was known to enjoy [[Herbology]], handcraft and poetry, and he disliked [[History of Magic]] which he was not good at.<ref name="info"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Colby was capable of producing a corporeal [[Patronus Charm|Patronus]] in the form of a [[rhinoceros]], an exceptional [[magic]]al feat for a Hogwarts student.<ref name="info"/>
*'''[[Herbology]]''': Colby was known to enjoy the subject of Herbology at Hogwarts, the study of magical plants and fungi.<ref name="info"/>

==Possessions==
*'''[[Colby Frey's wand|Wand]]''': Colby's [[wand]] was 12 inches, made of [[ash]] with a [[unicorn hair]] [[Wand core|core]].<ref name="info"/>
