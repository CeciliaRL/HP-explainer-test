A '''painting of a {{Wplink|wolfhound}}''' was hung at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], near [[Cadogan|Sir Cadogan]]'s [[portrait]].<ref name="OOTP12"/>

==History==
In [[1995]], when [[Harry Potter]] was on his way to [[Divination (class)|Divination class]], Sir Cadogan tried to follow him by running through a neighbouring picture. However, the large and angry-looking [[dog]] inhabiting the painting chased him back into his own portrait.<ref name="OOTP12">{{OOTP|B|12}}</ref>
