'''Angus''' was a boy around the same age as [[Harry Potter]] who visited [[Diagon Alley]].<ref name="PS5"/>

==Biography==
He was seen outside [[Quality Quidditch Supplies]] in [[Diagon Alley]] on [[31 July]], [[1991]], commenting on a [[Nimbus 2000]] displayed in the window.<ref name="PS5">{{PS|B|5}}</ref> The [[1992|following year]], he was seen at [[Flourish and Blotts]], on the day that [[Gilderoy Lockhart]] appeared there to sign copies of his new book, ''[[Magical Me]]''.<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Angus was portrayed by [[Ben Borowiecki]] in the first two [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]]. He is credited as "Diagon Alley Boy" in ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Philosopher's Stone]]'' and "Angus (Diagon Boy)" in ''[[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)|Chamber of Secrets]]''. Though the character appears in the novel {{PS}}, his name is not given, and he is described as simply "one" of a group of "boys of about Harry's age."
*In {{LEGO|Y1}}, he is absent but due to the character "Boy" appearing Angus may appear generic.
