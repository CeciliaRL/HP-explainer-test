'''Georgia Clark-Day'''<ref>Closed captions on the DVD/Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who was reported missing on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' wireless programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.

==Behind this scenes==
*This character was presumably named after [[Georgia Clarke-Day]], the daughter of [[Mark Day]],<ref name="markdayfather">[https://twitter.com/markdayeditor/status/570604452392144896 Tweet by Mark Day (@markdayeditor) on Twitter]</ref> an editor who worked on the final four [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]], including {{DH|F1}}.<ref name="markdayimdb">[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0206504/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cr10 Mark Day's IMDB profile]</ref> Clarke-Day is an actress and played a [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] student in the film adaptations of ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince]]''.<ref name="ccp">[http://www.castingcallpro.com/uk/actor/profile/georgia-clarke-day Georgia Clark-Day's profile on Casting Call Pro]</ref>
