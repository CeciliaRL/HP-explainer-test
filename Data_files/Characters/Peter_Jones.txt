'''Peter Jones''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. He once posted a notice asking if anyone had found his lost [[cauldron]], which he described as "sort of dented on the top and burned on the bottom," on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in the [[Gryffindor Tower|Gryffindor common room]].<ref name="peter jones">See [[:File:PeterJonesLostCauldronNotice.jpg|this image]].</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Peter" is derived from the Greek Πετρος (Petros) meaning "stone".
