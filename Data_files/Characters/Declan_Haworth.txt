'''Declan Haworth''' was a [[Ravenclaw]]<ref name="house" /> [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
He competed in the school [[Wizard Skittles]] tournament during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
The name "Declan" is an anglicised form of Irish ''Deaglán'', which is of unknown meaning. Saint Declan was a 5th-century missionary to Ireland.
