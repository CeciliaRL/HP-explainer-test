'''Lucretia Fawley''' (died c. [[1997]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|witch]], the wife of [[Marshall Fawley]], and the mother of [[Grim Fawley]].

Mrs Fawley and her husband were both killed during the height of the [[Second Wizarding War]], while their son Grim was a student at Hogwarts.<ref name="WU">{{WU}} (see [https://wizardsunite.gamepress.gg/guide/mysteries-chapter-2-grim-fawley-background this])</ref>
