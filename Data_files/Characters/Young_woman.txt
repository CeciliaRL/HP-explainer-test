The '''young woman''' was a character in the tale ''[[The Wizard and the Hopping Pot]]'' by [[Beedle the Bard]]. Her [[Young woman's child (The Wizard and the Hopping Pot)|baby]] was very ill, and when she asked the [[Wizard (The Wizard and the Hopping Pot)|Wizard]] for help, he slammed the door on her.<ref name="TBB">{{TBB|W}}</ref>
