'''Fulcran Lestrange''' was a French wizard and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. He was the son of [[Fulcran Lestrange (II)|Fulcran Lestrange]] and [[Sotère Lestrange|Sotère Petit]]. He married his second cousin [[Quintilla Lestrange]] and had a daughter with her, [[Nozéa Lestrange]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
