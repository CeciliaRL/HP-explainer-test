'''Astor Bellchant''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and member of the [[Bellchant family|Bellchants]], a prominent wizarding family.<ref name="FBC5" />

==Biography==
Astor was born at some point into the respectable wizarding [[Bellchant family]]. In his adult life, he had at least one son, [[Albert Bellchant|Albert]], who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="FBC5" />

In an investigation concerning the disappearance of a wizard named [[Bilius Finbok]], Astor Bellchant was suspected to have blackmailed Finbok into brewing an illegal [[potion]].<ref name="FBC5" />

==Possessions==
*'''[[Astor Bellchant's owl|Owl]]''': Astor owned an owl to deliver his mail. Astor Bellchant used this owl to send a [[Threatening note|blackmail note]] to [[Bilius Finbok]], who was hospitalised at the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[St David's Hospital]]. [[Michael Babatola]], who was a nurse there, noted that Finbok had run away from the hospital shortly after an owl landed on his window in the middle of the day.<ref name="FBC5.2">{{FBC|5|2}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*Astor, rarely used as a given name, derives from a surname derived from Occitan ''astur'', meaning "hawk". The [[Wikipedia:Astor family|Astor family]] was a very prominent family in business and politics in the United States and the United Kingdom during the 19th and 20th centuries; famous members include [[Wikipedia:John Jacob Astor|John Jacob Astor I]], the first multi-millionaire in the United States; [[Wikipedia:William Waldorf Astor, 1st Viscount Astor|William Waldorf Astor]], the first Viscount Astor; and [[Wikipedia:John Jacob Astor IV|John Jacob Astor IV]], the richest passenger aboard the [[Wikipedia:RMS Titanic|RMS ''Titanic'']].

==Behind the scenes==
*Before he introduces himself to the player, Bellchant's lines are identified as those of "Impatient Wizard". On the Task List, however, he is referred to as "Rude Wizard".
