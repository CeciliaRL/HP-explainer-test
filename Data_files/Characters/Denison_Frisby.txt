'''Denison Frisby''' was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as [[Keeper]] on the [[English National Quidditch team]] during the four-hundred and twenty-second [[Quidditch World Cup]], in [[1994]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

By [[1996]], Frisby was included on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] for his achievements in the sport of [[Quidditch]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>
