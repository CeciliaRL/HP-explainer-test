}}'''Basil Horton''' (fl. 1929) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Quidditch]] player for the [[Falmouth Falcons]].

==Biography==
In [[1929]], he established the [[Comet Trading Company]] with fellow player [[Randolph Keitch]]. Together they also invented the [[Horton-Keitch Braking Charm]].<ref>{{QTA|E}} - Chapter Nine (The Development of the Racing Broom)</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Flight|Flying]]''': Horton was an accomplished and talented [[broomstick]] flyer, being a professional [[Quidditch]] player for the [[Falmouth Falcons]]
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Horton was highly proficient in charm-work, given how he invented the [[Horton-Keitch Braking Charm]].
*'''[[Spell creation]]''': Horton succeeded in the immensely complex and difficult [[magic]]al art of inventing new [[spell]]s, as he successfully created the [[Horton-Keitch Braking Charm]].

==Etymology==
The name "Basil" comes from the Greek name Βασιλειος (Basileios) which was derived from βασιλευς (basileus) meaning "king". Saint Basil the Great was a 4th-century bishop of Caesarea and one of the fathers of the early Christian church.
