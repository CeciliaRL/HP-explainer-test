This '''unidentified Non-Magique father''' was a [[France|French]] [[Non-magic people|Non-Magique]] resident of [[Paris]] in the [[1920s]].

==Biography==
Together with his [[Unidentified Non-Magique mother|wife]] and [[Unidentified Non-Magique toddler|child]], he lived in an apartment block somewhere in Paris and was murdered by [[Gellert Grindelwald's acolytes]] under the orders of Grindelwald himself. They later used the family's home as a temporary headquarters, from where he conducted a search for [[Credence Barebone]].<ref name="COGS">{{COG|S}}</ref>
