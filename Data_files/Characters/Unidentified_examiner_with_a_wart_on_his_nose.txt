This '''individual''' was an ugly [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was a member of the [[Wizarding Examinations Authority]].

==History==
In [[1996]], he administered [[Ronald Weasley]]'s practical [[Divination]] [[Ordinary Wizarding Level|O.W.L.]]. Ron inadvertently insulted him when he seeing "an ugly man with a wart on his nose" in the [[crystal ball]], only to realise he was describing the examiner's reflection.<ref>{{OOTP|B|31}}</ref>
