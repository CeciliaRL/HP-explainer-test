'''Ivy Warrington''' was a [[half-blood]] or [[muggle-born]]<ref name="blood"/> [[Wizardkind|witch]], who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[2008]].{{MATimeRef}}

==Biography==
===Early life {{C|Before 2008}}===
Ivy was born to a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[Warrington sisters' father|father]]. She had a [[Warrington sisters' grandmother|grandmother]] who was a witch, and one sister, [[Winifred Warrington]].<ref name="MA"/>

At one point during her childhood, Winifred [[Vanishment|vanished]] herself. Their grandmother tried to help her forget what happened by using a [[Memory Charm]]. However, it caused her to wholly forget about Winifred. Ivy had an ability to vanish, and would do so even when she didn't intend to.<ref name="MA"/>

===Hogwarts years {{C|2008–}}===
At the age of eleven, Ivy began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. During the [[Sorting Ceremony]], Ivy was at first missing, having been distracted by bushes outside the castle, but was later found by [[Rubeus Hagrid]].<ref name="MA"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Ivy was proficient in the casting of charms. She could successfully cast the [[Levitation Charm]], the [[Disarming Charm]] and the [[Locking Spell]], for example.<ref name="MA"/> She was also capable of producing a corporeal [[Patronus Charm|Patronus]] in the form of a [[pekingese]], an exceptional [[magic]]al feat for a Hogwarts student.<ref name="info"/> She was known to enjoy [[Charms (class)|Charms class]] at Hogwarts.<ref name="info"/>
*'''[[Transfiguration]]''': She could successfully cast the [[Vanishing Spell]], to the point that she was able to [[Vanishment|vanish]] large objects, like luggage, adult-sized [[Suit of armour|suits of armours]] and even people.<ref name="MA"/>
*'''[[Healing magic]]''': She once successfully used the [[healing spell]] ''[[Episkey]]'' to heal [[Daniel Page]]'s broken nose.<ref name="MA">{{MA}}</ref>

==Possessions==
*'''[[Ivy Warrington's wand|Wand]]''': Ivy's wand was 12½ inches, made of [[Cherry (tree)|cherry]] with a [[dragon heartstring]] [[Wand core|core]].<ref name="info"/>
