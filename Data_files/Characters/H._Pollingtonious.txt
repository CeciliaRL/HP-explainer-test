}}'''H. Pollingtonious''' was a [[Healer]] who compiled ''[[The Healer's Helpmate]]''. This book featured home remedies to common wizarding ailments.<ref name="hppts">''[[Harry Potter Page to Screen: The Complete Filmmaking Journey|Harry Potter Page to Screen]]'', page 504 (see [[:File:TheHealersHelpmate.jpg|this image]])</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to [[Heather Pollington]], a graphic artist who worked on ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire]]'',<ref>[http://www.heatherpollington.com/downloads/heather-pollington-cv-may-2015.pdf Heather Pollington's CV] from her professional website</ref> as well as ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1712260/ Heather Pollington's profile on IMDb]</ref>
