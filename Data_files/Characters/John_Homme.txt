'''John Homme''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and painter. He painted the [[portrait]] of [[Percival Pratt]], which he signed in the lower right corner, opposite the subject's name on the left.<ref>See [[:File:JohnHSignature.jpg|this image]] and [[:File:PercivalPratt.jpg|this image]].</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "John" is the English form of Iohannes, the Latin form of the Greek name Ιωαννης (Ioannes), itself derived from the Hebrew name יוֹחָנָן (Yochanan) meaning "Yahweh is gracious".
