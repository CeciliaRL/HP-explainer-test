The '''Leaky Cauldron bartender''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who worked at the [[Leaky Cauldron]] as a bartender.<ref name="FBC2"/>

==Biography==
She helped during [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]]'s investigation into an escaped [[magic]]al [[beast]]. She lost a golden tooth in the fray and mistakenly hit a portrait of [[Ulick Gamp]] with a [[Confundus Charm]]. She later donated meat from the larder to help lure the beast into a trap.<ref name="FBC2">{{FBC|2}}</ref>
