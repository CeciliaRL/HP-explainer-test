'''Minister Josephina Flint''' was [[Minister for Magic]] from [[1819]] to [[1827]].  She was elected at least twice.<ref name="WW"/>

She revealed an unhealthy anti-[[Non-magic people|Muggle]] bias in office and disliked new Muggle technology such as the [[telegraph]], which she claimed interfered with proper [[wand]] function.<ref name="WW">{{WW|ministers-for-magic}}</ref>
