{{Quote|The members of the Wizengamot were muttering. All eyes were now on Dumbledore. Some looked annoyed, others slightly frightened; two elderly witches in the back row, however, raised their hands and waved in welcome.|Description|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix}}
These '''two elderly women''' were [[wizardkind|witches]] and members of the [[Wizengamot]] in the mid-[[1990s]]. They seemed to be friendly with [[Albus Dumbledore]], and were not influenced by the [[Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter|Ministry's campaign to discredit Dumbledore and Harry Potter]]. On [[12 August]], they were present at [[Harry Potter]]'s [[Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter|disciplinary hearing]]. When [[Albus Dumbledore|Dumbledore]] arrived at [[Courtroom Ten]] as witness for the defence, these two witches waved to him as a welcome.<ref name="OP8">{{OOTP|B|8}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*One of these two witches may be [[Griselda Marchbanks|Professor Griselda Marchbanks]], who knew Dumbledore since his youth and was one of the [[Wizengamot]] elders at the time of the hearing.
