'''Mr Fawley''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a member of the [[Fawley family]].<ref name="status" />

==Biography==
He was part of the [[Fawley family]], who appeared in the ''[[Pure-Blood Directory]]'' as belonging to the [[Sacred Twenty-Eight]].

He had at least one child, a son, [[Eustace Fawley|Eustuce]], to whom he gave his best set of [[gobstones]] to take to [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. Eustace lost the gobstones in a [[fire]] at the [[boathouse]].<ref name="FBC7">{{FBC|7}}</ref>

==Etymology==

Fawley is a name derived from the Old English word fugelere which means "hunter of wild birds, fowler".
