'''Mrs Trelawney''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] woman.<ref name="PM">{{WW|sybill-trelawney}}</ref> She married a [[Trelawney family|wizard]] descended from the famous [[Seer]] [[Cassandra Trelawney]], and had at least one child with him, a daughter, [[Sybill Trelawney|Sybill]].<ref name="PM" /> Sybill, unlike her father, turned out to possess the [[Divination|Second Sight]], albeit in a limited capacity.<ref name="ootp15">{{OOTP|B|15}}</ref>
