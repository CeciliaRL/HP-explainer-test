'''Lord Draben'''<ref name="Draben">[http://www.castingcallpro.com/uk/view.php?uid=90262 Paul Marc Davis's castingcallpro profile]</ref> (fl. [[17th century|1600s]]),<ref name="1600s">The term [[Wikipedia:Cavalier|"Cavalier"]] was used to name supporters of the Royalist cause during the Wars of the Three Kingdoms (1642 – c. 1679).</ref> otherwise known as the '''Ghost of the Cavalier''',<ref name="Cavalier">{{HPPS}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who had some connection with [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="PSF"/>

==Biography==
In his lifetime, Lord Draben was a [[Wikipedia:Cavalier|Cavalier]]: a Royalist supporter of [[Wikipedia:Charles I of England|King Charles I]] and his son [[Wikipedia:Charles II of England|Charles II]] during the English Civil War, the Interregnum, and the Restoration in the mid-[[17th century|seventeenth century]].<ref name="PSF"/>

When Lord Draben died, he became a [[ghost]], taking residence at [[Hogwarts Castle]]. He attended the [[Start-of-Term Feast]] in [[1991]], and later joined a group of [[ghost carollers]] singing ''[[Christmas at Hogwarts]]'' later that year.<ref name="PSF">{{PS|F}}</ref>
