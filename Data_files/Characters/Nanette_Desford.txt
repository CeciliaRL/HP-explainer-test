'''Nanette Desford''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She was usually found spending time by the [[Clock Tower]] during the [[1996–1997 school year]]. She often played [[Exploding Snap]]. She gave [[Harry Potter]] [[Chocolate Frog Card]]s whenever he beat her.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console versions]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
