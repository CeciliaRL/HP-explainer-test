}}'''Erica Stainwright''' ([[1932]]—[[2001]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who had a career as a housekeeping guru in the [[1950s]].<ref name="JKRS"/>

==Biography==
===Early Life===
Erica Stainwright was born in [[1932]] into parents of unknown [[blood status]].<ref name="JKRS"/>

She became rich selling supposed ''"cleaning"'' [[potion]]s, such as [[Everklena]], but fell into disgrace when it was discovered that these products actually spawned more mould and grime, which presumably forced people to buy more of them to try to fix the problem.<ref name="JKRS"/>

===Death and Post-Mortem===
Stainwright [[Death|died]] in [[2001]] at the age of 68 or 69.<ref name="JKRS"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Stainwright was a talented professional [[potioneer]], given how she successfully created her own product range of cleaning potions such as [[Everklena]], on the market and managed to make a good living out of selling them. However, these potions were fraudulent solutions which actually spawned mould and grime as opposed to eradicating it.<ref name="JKRS"/>
