'''Minister Albert Boot''' was [[Minister for Magic]] from [[1747]] to [[1752]]. He was considered a likeable Minister, but also an inept one. After mismanaging a [[18th century goblin rebellion|goblin rebellion]], he resigned from the post.<ref>{{WW|ministers-for-magic}}</ref>  He was first elected Minister by the Wizarding population of [[Great Britain]] and [[Ireland]] after the death of [[Eldritch Diggory]].
