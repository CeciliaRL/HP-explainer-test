'''Astrix Alixan''' (b. [[1670s]]) was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1680s]] and [[1690s]]. He was once [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]] from [[1688]] to [[1692]].<ref>{{HPE}} {{See image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Plaque.jpg}} and {{see image|File:Gryffindor Captains Plaque.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*In {{COS}}, set during the [[1992–1993 school year]], it is mentioned that the Gryffindor team had been one player short during the final match of the previous school year, against [[Ravenclaw Quidditch team|Ravenclaw]] ([[Harry Potter]] had been unconscious in the [[Hospital Wing]]), and had for that reason suffered their worst defeat in three-hundred years.<ref name="COS7">{{COS|B|7}}</ref> That would mean that their previous worst defeat ever had taken place in around the 1691-1692 school year, most likely during Astrix Alixan's captaincy.
