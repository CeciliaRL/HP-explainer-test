'''Merwyn the Malicious''' was a [[Middle Ages|medieval]] [[Dark Wizard]] credited with the [[Spell creation|invention]] of many unpleasant [[jinx]]es and [[hex]]es.<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref><ref name="PM">{{PM}}</ref>

==Biography==
[[File:Merwyn_The_Malicious-12-chocFrogCard.png|thumb|140px|left|Merwyn the Malicious immortalised on a bronze Chocolate Frog Card]]
Merwyn was born sometime during the [[Middle Ages]]. During his lifetime, he was a practitioner of the [[Dark Arts]] and a [[Dark Wizard]], having [[Spell creation|invented]] many unpleasant [[jinx]]es and [[hex]]es. He also possessed a hooked [[wand]]. For his infamy, he was immortalised on a bronze [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref><ref name="PM">{{PM}}</ref> A [[portrait]] of Merwyn hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]], guarding a secret shortcut between the third-floor landing of the [[Grand Staircase]] and the [[Entrance Hall]] side room. The password required to gain access to said shortcut was "''Malevolence''".<ref>{{POA|G}} - PC version</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Dark Arts]]''': Merwyn the Malicious was a notorious [[Dark Wizard]] in the [[Middle Ages]], and thus was highly proficient and well-versed in dark magic and with the casting of [[dark charm]]s. He invented numerous unpleasant [[jinx]]es and [[hex]]es in his lifetime, earning him his sinister reputation.
*'''[[Spell creation]]''': Merwyn mastered the immensely difficult and advanced [[magic]]al art of inventing new [[spell]]s, having successfully created many unpleasant [[jinx]]es and [[hex]]es during his lifetime, for which he was famous for, gaining him recognition on a bronze [[Chocolate Frog Card]] for his magical achievements.

==Etymology==
Merwyn is a Welsh name, said to be a form of the name [[Merlin]].
