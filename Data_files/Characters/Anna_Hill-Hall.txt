'''Anna Hill-Hall''' (fl. [[1996]]) was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who worked as the data retrieval officer for the [[Metropolitan Police]] of [[London]]. Her name appeared in a July [[1996]] cover story in the ''[[The Guardian]]'', having been quoted as saying that "nothing of value" had been found in all of the retrieved [[wikipedia:closed-circuit television|CCTV]] footage of the [[Death Eaters|collapse]] of the [[Millennium Bridge]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:TheGuardian.png|this image]])</ref>
