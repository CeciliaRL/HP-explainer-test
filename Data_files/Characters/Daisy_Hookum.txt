'''Daisy Hookum''' (b. [[1962]]) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]], who wrote a best-selling book, ''[[My Life as a Muggle]]'', after voluntarily giving up using [[magic]] for a year. She was married to celebrity [[Herbologist]], [[Potioneer]] and radio presenter [[Tilden Toots]].<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>
