'''Betty Bickering''' (b. [[1690s]]) was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1700s]] and [[1710s]]. She was once [[Quidditch Captain#Hogwarts Quidditch Captains|Captain]] of the [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]] from [[1708]] to [[1712]].<ref>{{HPE}} {{See image|File:Gryffindor Quidditch Captains Plaque.jpg}}</ref>
