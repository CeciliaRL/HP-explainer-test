This '''individual''' was a [[Dark Wizard]] and [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] who fought during the [[Battle of the Department of Mysteries]] in the [[Second Wizarding War]]. He was about to cast a [[spell]] when [[Alastor Moody]] used his [[Alastor Moody's walking stick|staff]] to [[magic]]ally knock him out, possibly with the [[Knockback Jinx]] or a similar spell.<ref name="OOTPF">{{OOTP|F}}</ref>
