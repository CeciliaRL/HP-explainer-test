'''Rubens Winikus''' was presumably the founder of [[Rubens Winikus and Company Inc.]], a corporation which manufactured and sold [[potion]]s, including [[Skele-Gro]], of which they were the sole producer.<ref name="COSF">{{COS|F}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is mentioned only on the label of a bottle featured in {{COS|FA}}. His name is presumably a tribute to [[Ruth Winick]], who worked as a graphic artist on ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Philosopher's Stone]]'' and ''Chamber of Secrets'', and as a draughtsman on ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|Prisoner of Azkaban]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]''.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0935101/ Ruth Winick]" at IMDb</ref>
