This '''individual''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] and member of the [[Wizengamot]] in the mid-[[1990s]]. She had frizzy-hair. On [[12 August]], she was present at [[Harry Potter]]'s [[Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter|disciplinary hearing]], appearing sceptical of [[Arabella Figg]]'s testimony, smirking and nodding to [[Unidentified dumpy Wizengamot member with a large black moustache|the wizard sitting next to her]]'s whispers. She and her neighbour later voted in favour of [[Harry Potter|Harry]]'s conviction.<ref name="OP8">{{OOTP|B|8}}</ref>
