'''Primrose Jorkins''' was the wife of [[Albert Jorkins]], and the mother of [[Grimwold Jorkins|Grimwold]], [[Granville Jorkins|Granville]], and [[Griselda Jorkins|Griselda Harmonia]]. Along with her husband, she announced the birth of her youngest daughter, Griselda, in the ''[[Daily Prophet]]''.<ref name="DP">[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[8 February]], [[1999]], Page 2</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Primrose" is from the English word for the flower, ultimately deriving from Latin ''prima rosa'' "first rose".
