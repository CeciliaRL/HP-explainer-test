'''Marilyn''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and eccentric [[Hufflepuff]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] during the [[1990s]]. She collected [[beetle]] pincers in her spare time as a hobby.<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}} {{C|PC version}}</ref> She had a younger brother named [[Arthur (Hufflepuff student)|Arthur]], who were both described by other students as "being a bit off", meaning others found them somewhat strange.<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}} {{C|PC version}}</ref>
==Behind the scenes==
*While Marilyn appears to be Caucasian and blonde, her brother Arthur is of black African descent.
