}}'''Oona''' (fl. [[12th century]]) was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] lived in the same village as [[Goodwin Kneen]], and worked in the local inn as a Barmaid. After the ''Kwidditch'' (''[[Quidditch]]'') team won a match against a team from [[Ilkley]], she gave the team free [[mead]].<ref name="QTA3">{{QTA|R|3}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Oona" is possibly derived from Irish uan meaning "lamb".
