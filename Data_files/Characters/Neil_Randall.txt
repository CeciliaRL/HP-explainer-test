'''Neil Randall''' was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was a member of the [[Duelling Club]] run by [[Karl Limpley]] in the [[Clock Tower Courtyard]] during the [[1996â€“1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
The name "Neil" comes from the Gaelic name Niall, which is of disputed origin, possibly meaning "champion" or "cloud". This was the name of a semi-legendary 4th-century Irish king, Niall of the Nine Hostages.
