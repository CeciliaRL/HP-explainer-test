'''Thomas Paley''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was usually seen spending time by the [[Viaduct]] with [[Terrence Fogarty]] during the [[1996–1997 school year]]. He confessed to [[Harry Potter]] that he would like a new [[Broomstick|broom]] and admitted it was a disaster in [[Potions (class)|Potions]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Thomas" is the Greek form of the Aramaic name תָּאוֹמָא (Ta'oma') which meant "twin".

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
