This '''Policeman''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Muggle|No-Maj]] who worked for the [[New York Police Department]] in [[1926]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Biography==

He and [[Unidentified police officer (I)|fellow officer]] caught [[Newton Scamander]] and [[Jacob Kowalski]] believing that they were robbing a jewellery store before they saw a [[lion]] that escaped from the [[Central Park Zoo]], leaving the two suspects to escape.<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
The Policeman was portrayed by [[Bart Edwards]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
