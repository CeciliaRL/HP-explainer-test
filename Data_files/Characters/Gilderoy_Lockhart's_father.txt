'''Mr Lockhart''' was the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] father of [[Gilderoy Lockhart]] and [[Gilderoy Lockhart's sisters|two older Squib daughters]].<ref name="PM">{{WW|gilderoy-lockhart}}</ref>

The [[Healer]] who looked after [[Gilderoy Lockhart|Gilderoy]] at [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries|St Mungo's]], [[Miriam Strout]], stated he never got visitors in [[December]] [[1995]].<ref name="OOTP23">{{OOTP|B|23}}</ref> This meant that either his father was deceased by then, or that he did not wish to visit his son.
