This '''individual''' was a male [[giant]] who fought in the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] for [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] and his [[Death Eaters]]. He was seen in a courtyard close to [[Greenhouse One]] in which he slept. He held a [[Ice pop|blue ice cream]], which [[Ronald Weasley]] and [[Hermione Granger]] took in order to make an [[Exploding Potion]].<ref>{{LEGO|Y2}}</ref>
