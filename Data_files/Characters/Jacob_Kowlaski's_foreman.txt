This '''foreman''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] man who worked at the [[Moreton Dale Canning Factory]] during the [[1920s]] while [[Jacob Kowalski]] was employed there.<ref name="FB82"/>

During a visit to [[The Blind Pig]] [[speakeasy]] in [[1926]], Jacob remarked that [[Gnarlak]] reminded him of his foreman, after [[Muggle duelling|punching]] the disreputable [[Goblin]] in the face.<ref name="FB82">{{FB|S|82}}</ref>
