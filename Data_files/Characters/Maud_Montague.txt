'''Maud Montague''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] whose [[portrait]] hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|PA}}</ref>

It is possible that Maud was a forerunner of the [[Montague family]], which was active in the [[20th century]].
