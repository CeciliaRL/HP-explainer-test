'''Glover Hipworth''' ([[1742]] – [[1805]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who specialised in [[Potion]] development. He became famous for the invention of the [[Pepperup Potion]], the cure for the [[common cold]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
Glover Hipworth was born in [[1742]]. If he enrolled at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], he attended the school from around [[1753]]-[[1760]], under [[Headmaster|Headmistress]] [[Dilys Derwent]].

===Adulthood===
{{Quote|Madam Pomfrey, the nurse, was kept busy by a sudden spate of colds among the staff and students. Her Pepperup potion worked instantly, though it left the drinker smoking at the ears for several hours afterward.|Description|Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets}}It is likely Hipworth studied further into [[Potion|Potion-making]], becoming a fully-fledged [[potioneer]] in his adult years.

Sometime in the late [[18th century|eighteenth]] or early [[19th century|nineteenth]] centuries, Glover Hipworth developed the [[Pepperup Potion]], based on an earlier remedy by [[12th century]] [[potioneer]] [[Linfred of Stinchcombe]], used today as an efficacious [[magic]]al remedy for the common cold.<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

By the [[1990s]], Hipworth was featured on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]], which pictured him symbolically sneezing, in a reference to his achievement.<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref> In the [[1993–1994 school year]] a [[portrait]] of him guarded a secret room in the [[Dungeons]], at [[Hogwarts Castle]]; the password was ''Gesundheit'' (the German word for "health", also used for "Bless you").<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Hipworth was a professional [[Potioneer]] and was highly proficient in the art of brewing potions, having invented the [[Pepperup Potion]], a [[Healing magic|Healing]] potion which remedies the effects of the [[Common cold]]. For his achievement, he was included on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]], showing the great level of talent he possessed in this branch of [[magic]] and [[science]].
