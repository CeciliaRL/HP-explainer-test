'''Alguff the Awful''' was a [[goblin]] who was known for being particularly foul-smelling. He was well-known throughout the goblin community for attempting to sell vials of his own sweat to a [[Dungbomb]] maker. For this, he was featured on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref>{{POA|G}}</ref>
