This '''wizard''' (c. [[1975]]-[[1976]]) attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. He was Sorted into [[Gryffindor|Gryffindor House]], with [[Minerva McGonagall]] as his [[Head of House]], and became a [[prefect]] in his [[fifth year]]. He often stood in the [[Entrance Hall]] near the front door, and told any who came what the house point totals were.<ref>Seen in the ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game)|Philosopher's Stone]]'', ''[[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)|Chamber of Secrets]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|Prisoner of Azkaban'' games]]</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*For him to have been a [[Gryffindor]] [[Prefect]] for Harry's first three years at Hogwarts, this would seem to put him in the same year as [[Percy Weasley]], who was also a Gryffindor Prefect. As only one Prefect is chosen per gender per House per year, this creates a discrepancy. It is possible that he may have been held back a year for some reason, like [[Marcus Flint]].
