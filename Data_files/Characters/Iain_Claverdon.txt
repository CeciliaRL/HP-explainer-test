'''Iain Claverdon''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="HBPG"/>

==Biography==
He owned two sets of [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch uniform|robes]], one of which he agreed to give to [[Harry Potter]] before the final match between [[Gryffindor]] and [[Ravenclaw]] in [[1997]], in exchange for five [[Ever-Bashing Boomerang]]s. Harry gave the robes to [[Dean Thomas]] in order to replace the ones he dirtied in a [[Duelling|duel]] with some [[Slytherin]]s.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} - NDS only</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (PlayStation 2, Xbox, GameCube)|home console versions]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (Nintendo DS)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
