'''Luccas Carusus''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] presumably associated in some way with the sport of [[Quidditch]], possibly a member of the [[Quidditch Union for the Administration and Betterment of the British League and its Endeavours]]. They wrote the foreword for the revised edition of ''[[Quidditch Teams of Britain and Ireland|Quidditch Teams of England and Ireland]]''.<ref>{{MHP}} {{see image|File:QuidditchTeamsofBritainandIreland.jpg}}</ref>
