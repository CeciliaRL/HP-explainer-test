'''Dobbin''' was a [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] [[Wizardkind|student]] in the early [[1990s]]. They played as one of the two [[Beater]]s for one of the School's [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|teams]].<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*In {{HBP}}, [[Sybill Trelawney]] refers to the [[Centaur]] [[Firenze]] using this unflattering nickname.<ref name="HBP15">{{HBP|B|15}}</ref> "Dobbin" is a rural slang term for a common [[horse]].
