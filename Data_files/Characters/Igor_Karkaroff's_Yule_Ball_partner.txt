This '''individual''' (fl. [[1994]] - [[1996]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who was working at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] during the [[1994–1995 school year]]. She attended the [[Yule Ball]] with the [[Headmaster]] of [[Durmstrang Institute]], [[Professor]] [[Igor Karkaroff]], and stepped onto the dance floor after [[Albus Dumbledore]] and [[Minerva McGonagall]].<ref name="GOFF">{{GOF|F}}</ref>

==Biography==
===[[1994–1995 school year]]===
[[File:AuroraGOF.png|thumb|150px|left|This employee at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] in [[1994]]]]
This witch was working at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] by [[1994]] when the [[Triwizard Tournament]] was being held at the school.

She was present at the [[Start-of-Term Feast]]. She was sat next to [[Severus Snape]].<ref>See [[:File:SinistraGOF.png|this image]].</ref>

She attended the [[Yule Ball]] after the First Task and stood with [[Professor]]s [[Igor Karkaroff]], [[Minerva McGonagall]], [[Severus Snape]] and [[Unidentified female Hogwarts teacher|another witch]], watching over the proceedings as a chaperone. She wore turquoise coloured robes and had her dark hair tied up.<ref name="GOFF"/>

After [[Albus Dumbledore]] and [[Minerva McGonagall|McGonagall]] joined in the dancing, the professor joined in with [[Igor Karkaroff|Professor Karkaroff]], who was spending most of the time with [[Professor]] [[Severus Snape|Snape]].<ref name="GOFF"/>

She later watched the Second Task, supporting [[Harry Potter]] and [[Cedric Diggory]].
===[[1995-1996 school year]]===
[[File:YB partner.png|150px|thumb|left|The woman in the [[Great Hall]]]]
This witch continued her career at the school the following year. She attended the [[Start-of-Term Feast]], where she watched over the students in the [[Great Hall]].<ref name="OOTPF">{{OOTP|F}}</ref>

===[[1997-1998 school year]]===
[[File:2011-07-28_17-35-15.png|thumb|This staff member before the [[Battle of Hogwarts]]]]
She was seen meanwhile the [[Order of the Phoenix]] members were arriving at the [[Great Hall]], along with others [[professor]]s such as [[Filius Flitwick]] and [[Pomona Sprout]]. Then she, along with [[Minerva McGonagall]], [[Aurora Sinistra]], [[Unidentified female Hogwarts teacher]], [[Horace Slughorn]], an [[Unidentified Hogwarts employees#Oriental Hogwarts employee|Asian employee]] and members of the [[Order of the Phoenix]] came out of the [[Great Hall]] at the main entrance to the school to protect the school using protective charms to hold back against the attacks from [[Tom Riddle|Voldemort]] and his [[Death Eaters]]. It is unknown whether she survived the battle or not.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

==Physical appearance==
The woman appeared quite solemn and was nearing her elderly years. Her hair was dark black, her eyes were dark brown and she was noticeably shorter than most other professors, especially her date, [[Igor Karkaroff]]. When she attended the [[Yule Ball]], she wore dark robes of turquoise and black.<ref name="GOFF"/>
