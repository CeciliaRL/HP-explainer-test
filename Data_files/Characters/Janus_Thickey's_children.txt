[[Janus Thickey]], a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]], was married to a [[Janus Thickey's wife|woman]] and had some '''children'''. When, in [[1973]], their father faked his own death by pretending that a [[Lethifold]] had eaten him, she and her children entered a period of mourning. However, their mourning was cut short when Janus was found to be living five miles away, with the [[Landlady of the Green Dragon|landlady]] of the [[Green Dragon]].<ref name="FBR">{{FB|R}}, ''Lethifold''</ref>
