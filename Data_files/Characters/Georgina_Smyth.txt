'''Georgina Smyth''' was a [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who lived in [[Nettlebed]], [[Oxfordshire]].<ref name="JKRS"/> 

She wrote a [[Georgina Smyth's letter to Tilden Toots|letter]] to the [[wireless]] programme ''[[Toots, Shoots 'n' Roots]]'' asking how to treat a [[Flitterbloom]] that she had not [[water]]ed in months. The Flitterbloom was needed for a [[potion]] that Smyth planned to brew, but, even after she threatened the plant with [[curse]]s, it would not revive.<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>
