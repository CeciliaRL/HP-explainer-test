'''Milford Vessel''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] actor in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="fbpic">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:HansonTheatre.jpg}}</ref>

He starred in a production of the play ''[[The Hudson River]]'' that was staged at the [[Hanson Theatre]] near [[Times Square]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="fbpic" />
