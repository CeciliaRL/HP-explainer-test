The '''spouse''' of [[Cho Chang]] was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who married Cho sometime after the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] in [[1998]].<ref name="JKR">''[https://web.archive.org/web/20190403083218/http://blog.nola.com/living/2007/10/new_orleans_students_give_rowl.html New Orleans students give Rowling a rousing welcome]'' from ''[https://www.nola.com/ The Times-Picayune]'' (via ''[https://web.archive.org/ The Internet Archive]'')</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*[[J. K. Rowling]] revealed that Cho married a Muggle during a Q&A session following a reading in [[New Orleans]] as part of her tour of U.S. schools. Their name and gender however were not revealed.<ref name="JKR"/>
