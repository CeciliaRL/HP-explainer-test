'''Fred Baille''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] singer in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="poster-lego">{{LEGO|D}} {{See image|File:Fred Baille & Co.png}}</ref> He was the leader of the famous singing troupe [[Fred Baille & Co.]].<ref name="poster-lego" />

In [[1926]], posters were put up in [[New York]] promoting a performance by Fred Baille & Co. at the [[Corbour Theatre]], which was slated for [[15 November]].<ref name="poster-lego"/>
