'''Alice Tolipan''' was a British [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. In [[1995]] she joined [[Dumbledore's Army]], an organisation taught and led by [[Harry Potter]] and was one of the people to sign their name on its [[Dumbledore's Army parchment|parchment]].<ref name="Pensieve">[http://thepensieve.org/HPMuseumEX/HPexhibition1.html ''Harry Potter: The Exhibition'' report] from ThePensieve.org</ref>
