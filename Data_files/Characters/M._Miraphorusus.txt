'''M. Miraphorumus''' was a reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="dec6sunrise">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:The New York Ghost - 6 Dec 1926 - Sunrise Early Edition.png}}</ref>

They wrote an article entitled "[[Muggles|No-Majs]] in Grave Danger Warns [[Seraphina Picquery|Picquery]]" that was published in the paper's [[6 December]] [[1926]] morning edition.<ref name="dec6sunrise" />
