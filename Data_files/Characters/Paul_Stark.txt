'''Paul Stark''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] luggage maker from [[wikipedia:Austria|Austria]].<ref name="trunk">{{POA|F}} {{see image 2|File:Paul stark.jpg|File:PaulStarkLabel.jpg}}</ref> His workshop was located at [[Erdbergstrasse]] [[Erdbergstrasse 35|35]] in [[Vienna]],<ref>The [[:File:Paul stark.jpg|label]] on [[Remus Lupin]]'s trunk bears the text "Paul Stark Wien." ''Wien'' is the official German name of the city of Vienna, Austria.</ref> and his [[telephone]] number was 98316.<ref name="trunk" />

[[Remus Lupin]] owned a trunk produced by Paul Stark.<ref name="trunk" />
