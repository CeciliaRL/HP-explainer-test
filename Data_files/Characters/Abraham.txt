'''Abraham''' was the first of the three {{W|Patriarchs (Bible)|Patriarchs}} of the [[Bible]].<ref name="wikipedia">"{{wikilink|Abraham}}</ref> According to the Biblical account, when Abraham was in [[Mesopotamia]], [[God]] called on him to sacrifice his son, [[Isaac]], in order to test his faith.<ref name="PSf22">{{PS|F}} - Chapter 22 (''Cloaked in Darkness'') {{see image|File:ScreamingFaceBook.jpg}}</ref>

Abraham was mentioned in a [[shrieking book|book]] that was kept in the [[Restricted Section]] of the [[Hogwarts Library|library]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] by the [[1990s]].<ref name="PSf22" />

==Behind the scenes==
*In {{PS|FA}}, the [[shrieking book]], in which Abraham is mentioned, appears to be a copy of ''[http://books.google.ca/books?id=tSs3AAAAMAAJ&printsec=frontcover#v=onepage&q=&f=false The Works of the Most Reverend Dr John Tillotson]''.
*[[Richard Harris]], who portrays [[Albus Dumbledore]] in the film adaptations of ''Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)|Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets]]'', played Abraham in the [[1994]] film ''{{W|Abraham (film)|Abraham}}''.
