'''Elizabeth I''' ([[7 September]] [[1533]] - [[24 March]] [[1603]]) was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who reigned as [[Queen]] of [[England]] and [[Ireland]] from [[1558]] until her [[death]].<ref name="wikipedia">"[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elizabeth_I_of_England Elizabeth I of England]" on Wikipedia</ref>

==Biography==
She was the daughter of [[Henry VIII|King Henry VIII]] by his second wife, [[Anne Boleyn]], and the half-sister of [[Mary I]].<ref name="wikipedia" />

Queen Elizabeth never [[marriage|married]] during her lifetime.<ref name="wikipedia" /> However, there was "ample" evidence suggesting that the first [[Lucius Malfoy I|Lucius Malfoy]] sought her hand in marriage, but was rebuffed by her.<ref name="pm-malfoys">{{WW|the-malfoy-family}}</ref> Some [[wizardkind|wizarding]] historians believed that her later opposition to marriage was the result of a [[Jinx against marriage|jinx]] that Malfoy placed on her in revenge for her spurning him.<ref name="pm-malfoys" /> This account was denied by future generations of the [[Malfoy family]].<ref name="pm-malfoys" />

During her rule, she was seen as the one the greatest ever rulers of England, with her reign being called the "Golden Age".<ref name="wikipedia" />

==Etymology==
The name "Elizabeth" comes from Ελισαβετ (Elisabet), the Greek form of the Hebrew name אֱלִישֶׁבַע ('Elisheva') meaning "my God is an oath" or perhaps "my God is abundance".
