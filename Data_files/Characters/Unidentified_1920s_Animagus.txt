This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and an [[Animagus]] who lived in the early [[20th century]]. In 1927, ''[[Le Cri de la Gargouille]]'' reported that he was somehow implicated in the "''affaire du [[duck|canard]]''".<ref name="COG">{{COG}} {{see image|File:Le Cri De La Gargouille.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The newspaper title that mentions this individual is probably a pun: "[[Wiktionary:canard#French|canard]]", which means "duck" in French, can also refer to an untrustworthy newspaper, or a false or misleading report.
