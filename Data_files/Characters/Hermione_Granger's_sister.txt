'''Miss Granger''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]], second daughter of [[Hermione Granger's father|Mr]] and [[Hermione Granger's mother|Mrs Granger]] and the younger sister of [[Hermione Granger]].

==Behind the scenes==
*[[J. K. Rowling]] stated in an interview that she had initially planned for Hermione to have a younger sister, but by the time {{OOTP}} was released, she still had not made an appearance and Rowling felt it was "too late" by that point.<ref>[http://www.accio-quote.org/articles/2004/0304-wbd.htm JK Rowling's World Book Day Chat, March 4, 2004]</ref>
