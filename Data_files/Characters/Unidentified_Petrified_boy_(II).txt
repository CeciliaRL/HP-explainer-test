This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was a student who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] during [[Jacob's sibling]]'s time at school.

==Biography==
This individual began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] between [[1 September]] [[1983]] and 1 September [[1989]].<ref name="age">He attended Hogwarts in the [[1989–1990 school year]], and he could have been in any year.</ref>

In the [[1989–1990 school year]], he was found [[Petrification|Petrified]]. He was later brought to the [[Hospital Wing]] along with other Petrified students.<ref>{{HM|6|24}}</ref>

Eventually, he was cured with the other victims.<ref name="HM643">{{HM|6|43}}</ref>
