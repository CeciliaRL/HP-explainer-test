'''Merton Graves''' (b. [[1978]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the cellist for the popular [[Wizarding world|wizarding]] band [[Weird Sisters|The Weird Sisters]].<ref>{{PS|G}}</ref><ref>{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*If he attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], he would have been in school from around [[1989]] to [[1996]], making him a [[third year]] during [[Harry Potter]]'s first year there, and a [[sixth year]] during the [[Triwizard Tournament]], when the [[Weird Sisters]] performed at Hogwarts for the [[Yule Ball]].
*Merton Graves and [[Herman Wintringham]] were absent from {{GOF|FA}}, and did not play with the [[Weird Sisters]].
*It's possible he is a relative of the ''[[Fantastic Beasts (film series)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]'' character [[Percival Graves]].

==Etymology==
The name "Merton" is from a surname which was derived from a place name meaning "town on a lake" in Old English.
