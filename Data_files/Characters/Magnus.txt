'''Magnus''' was a male [[Centaur]] that lived in the [[Fairy forest|forest]] near [[Winifred Whittle's cottage]].<ref name="FBC33"/>

==Biography==
While initially argumentative with [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]] and her [[Mathilda Grimblehawk's partner|partner]] during their investigation of the attack on the [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] construction workers, Magnus ultimately escorted the Muggle child [[Bethany Harrison]] from the forest on the condition that no other Muggles were allowed into the forest.<ref name="FBC33">{{FBC|3|3}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Magnus was the first named Centaur who did not live in the [[Forbidden Forest]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]]. The fact that Centaurs live all over [[Europe]] was previously known through [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]]'s book {{FB}}.
