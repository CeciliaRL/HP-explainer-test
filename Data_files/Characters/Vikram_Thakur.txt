'''Vikram Thakur''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a [[Slytherin]]<ref name="house" /> student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He competed in the school [[Gobstones]] tournament during the [[1996–1997 school year]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Vikram" means "stride, pace" or "valour" in Sanskrit. This is another name of the Hindu god Vishnu. This was also the name of a semi-legendary 1st-century BC king (full name Vikramaditya) of Ujjain in India.
The surname "Thakur" is a North Indian surname and a title which means "lord".

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console version]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
*Based on his name, he is most likely of Indian descent.
