'''Amanda Pettet''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] reporter who co-wrote a front-page article with [[Robert Bliss]] about the heat wave that swept over [[Great Britain]] for the ''[[Daily Express]]'' in the summer of [[1995]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:DailyExpress.png|this image]])</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after Amanda Pettett, who worked as a production assistant on {{PS|FA}}, and as a construction coordinator on all seven [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' films]] released thereafter.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1391114/ Amanda Pettett]" on IMDb</ref> Whether the spelling of her surname was altered intentionally or as a result of a typographical error is unknown.
