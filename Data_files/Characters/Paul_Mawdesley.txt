'''Paul Mawdesley''' was a [[Gryffindor]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He competed in the school [[Exploding Snap]] tournament during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
The name "Paul" comes from the Roman family name Paulus, which meant "small" or "humble" in Latin.
