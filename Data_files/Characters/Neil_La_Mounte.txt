'''Neil La Mounte''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] whose name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of [[Sybill Trelawney|Professor Trelawney's]] [[Divination (class)|Divination]] [[Divination Classroom|classroom]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:DivinationGraffitiWall.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named in honour of [[Neil Lamont]], the supervising art director for the first six [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]], from ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Philosopher's Stone]]'' to ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Half-Blood Prince]]''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0483678/ Neil Lamont] on IMDb</ref>
