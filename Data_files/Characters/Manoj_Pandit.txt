'''Manoj Pandit''' was a [[Hufflepuff]]<ref name="house" /> student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He competed in the school [[Exploding Snap]] tournament during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
'Manoj' means 'mental love' in hindi. 'Pandit' means 'wise man' in Hindi as well.

==Behind the scenes==
*This student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|PlayStation 2 version]] of {{POA}} and the NDS version of {{HBP|G}}
*Based on his name, he is most likely of Indian descent.
