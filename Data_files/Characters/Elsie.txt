'''Elsie''' was a female [[creature]] kept by [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] in the menagerie beneath his [[Newton Scamander's apartment|apartment]] in [[1927]].<ref name="COG33" />

==Biography==
In [[1927]] whilst talking to [[Bunty]] in the basement of his [[9 Sherringford Square|house]], Newt asked about Elsie, to which Bunty replied, that her [[Faeces|droppings]] were nearly normal again.<ref name="COG33">{{COG|S|33}}</ref>
