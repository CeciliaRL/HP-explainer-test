These '''individuals''' were family of [[Warlock (The Warlock's Hairy Heart)|The Warlock]] of the popular wizarding tale [[The Warlock's Hairy Heart]].<ref name="TBBH"/>

==Biography==
{{Quote|All will change, when a maid catches his fancy!|The couple wishing their son would change.|The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)}}
The elderly couple would ridicule their son for his aloof nature and inability to [[love]], unaware that he used [[Dark Arts]] to remove [[Hairy Heart|his heart]] from his body. In due time, the couple died, to which their son was happy, as he inherited their castle and would transfer his heart to the depths of the dungeons.<ref name="TBBH">{{TBB|WHH}}</ref>
