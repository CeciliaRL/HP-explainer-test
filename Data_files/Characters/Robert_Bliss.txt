'''Robert Bliss''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] reporter who co-wrote a front-page article with [[Amanda Pettet]] about the heat wave that swept over [[Great Britain]] for the ''[[Daily Express]]'' in the summer of [[1995]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:DailyExpress.png|this image]])</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after [[Rob Bliss]], who worked as a concept artist on the film adaptations of [[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|''Philosopher's Stone'']], [[Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets|''Chamber of Secrets'']], [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)|''Prisoner of Azkaban'']], [[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix|''Order of the Phoenix'']], and [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|''Half-Blood Prince'']].<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0088528/ Rob Bliss]" on IMDb</ref>
