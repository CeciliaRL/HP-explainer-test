This '''individual''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] who worked as reporter in the [[1920s]].

==Biography==
He interviewed a [[Unidentified witness|witness]] who saw a building get destroyed by an [[Obscurus]] in [[New York]] in [[1926]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

After interviewing, he asked a [[Unidentified photographer (II)|photographer colleague]] if he felt like getting a drink, to which the photographer replied that he had promised [[Martha (American Muggle)|Martha]] (presumably his wife or girlfriend) that he would abstain from consuming [[alcohol]].<ref name="FBS">{{FB|S}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The reporter was portrayed by [[Tristan Tait]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
