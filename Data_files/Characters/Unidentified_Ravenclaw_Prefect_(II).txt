This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]] at some point between [[1981]] and [[1985]]<ref name="age"/>

==Biography==
In his [[fifth year]], he was made a [[Prefect]] and between this year and his [[seventh year]], he duelled [[Gabriel Truman]], a [[third year]] at that time, when he claimed that [[Bridget Wenlock]] was [[Hufflepuff|not a Ravenclaw]]. Gabriel felt he deserved a week's worth of detentions for his own actions, but [[Pomona Sprout|Professor Sprout]] went easy on him, sending him off with a warning and a box of [[Pink Coconut Ice|coconut ice]].<ref>{{PM}} - [[Pottermore Introductions to Hogwarts Houses#Hufflepuff|Introduction to Hufflepuff House]] (Archived)</ref>
