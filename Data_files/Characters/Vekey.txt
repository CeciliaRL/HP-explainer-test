'''Vekey''' was a [[house-elf]] that worked at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1980s]]. They worked in the [[Hogwarts kitchens|kitchens]] along with the other [[Hogwarts house-elves]]. Shortly before [[Valentine's Day]], [[Retch]] asked Vekey be their Valentine. Vekey, however, was unaware what a Valentine was.<ref name="HM"/>
