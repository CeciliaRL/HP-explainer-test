This '''[[portrait|painting]] of an {{Wplink|elephant}}''' hung at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1993-1994 school year]], on the seventh-floor level of the [[Grand Staircase]], among paintings of other animals such as a [[Painting of a caged monkey|caged monkey]], a [[Painting of a tiger|tiger]] and [[Painting of a hippopotamus|a hippopotamus]]. When the [[Fat Lady]] was attacked by [[Sirius Black]] this elephant was one of them that was shocked.<ref name="PA13">{{POA|F}} - Chapter 13 (''Flight of the Fat Lady'')</ref>
