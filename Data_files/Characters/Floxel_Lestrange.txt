'''Floxel Lestrange''' was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. He was the son of [[Cyrille Lestrange (III)|Cyrille Lestrange]] and [[Luminosa Lestrange|Luminosa Trouche]]. He had a brother [[Cyrille Lestrange (IV)|Cyrille]]. He married [[Jodelle Lestrange|Jodelle Volant]] and had a daughter with her, [[Quintilla Lestrange|Quintilla]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
