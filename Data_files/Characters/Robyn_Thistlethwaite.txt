'''Robyn Thistlethwaite''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who started attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[2008]] and was sorted into [[Gryffindor]].<ref name="MA"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Robyn was a [[Hufflepuff]] in the first [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_release_life_cycle#Beta beta release] of {{MA}}, but has been changed to [[Gryffindor]] in subsequent releases.
