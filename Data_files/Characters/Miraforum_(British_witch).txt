'''Madame Miraforum''' was a [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] employee, who worked at [[Quill Control & Rune Translation]]<ref name="Twitter">[https://twitter.com/minalima/status/599605713455943683 "Our real jobs! :-)"] posted by MinaLima on Twitter on 16 May 2015</ref> for the [[Minister for Magic and Support Staff|Minister for Magic's Support Staff]].<ref name="SupportStaff">The plaque listing Mdme. Miraforum is identical in style to the other plaques on the doors to the offices of [[Minister for Magic and Support Staff|Level One]], as seen in {{DH|F1}} {{See image 2|File:MinistryLevelOne.png|File:Dolores Umbridge sign.png}}.</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Madame Miraforum is named after [[Miraphora Mina]], one of the graphic artists that produced many of the paper props seen in the [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]].
