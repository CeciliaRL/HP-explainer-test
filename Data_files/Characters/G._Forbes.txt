'''G. Forbes''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who presumably owned and/or founded the production company [[G. Forbes Productions]].<ref name="poster-lego">{{LEGO|D}} {{See image|File:G. Forbes Productions.png}}</ref> 

In [[1926]], posters were put up around [[New York]]'s harbour promoting a series of twelve performances staged by G. Forbes Productions, which were slated to begin on Saturday [[11 December]].<ref name="poster-lego" />
