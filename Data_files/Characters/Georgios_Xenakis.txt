'''Georgios Xenakis''' was a Greek wizard and a [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch referee|referee]]. He officiated in the [[Bulgarian National Quidditch Stadium|Bulgaria]] versus [[New Zealand National Quidditch team|New Zealand]] first round encounter at the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]] in [[Argentina]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[BULGARIA VERSUS NEW ZEALAND]] (Archived)</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*If this individual's name is indication of anything, he is probably [[Greece|Greek]] or of Greek descent — the name "Georgios" is the Greek form of George.<ref>[http://www.behindthename.com/name/georgios "Georgios"] at [http://www.behindthename.com/ Behind the Name]</ref>
*The surname Xenakis is also Greek, from ''ξένος ['ksenos]'' ("stranger", "foreigner", but also "friend from far away"). Greek artist [[Wikipedia:Constantin Xenakis|Constantin Xenakis]] (born 1931), Greek composer and architect [[Wikipedia:Iannis Xenakis|Iannis Xenakis]] (1922–2001), and Greek gymnast [[Wikipedia:Thomas Xenakis|Thomas Xenakis]] (1875–1942) are some notable people who bore this surname.
