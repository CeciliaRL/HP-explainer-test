'''Alison Denbright''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] reported missing along with her relative, [[Jason Denbright|Jason]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' wireless programme in [[1997]]. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Allison" is the Norman French diminutive of ''Aalis''. It was common in England and France in the Middle Ages, and was later revived in the 20th century.
