'''Charlesetta Nagy''', also known by the nickname '''Mother''', was a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].<ref name="wwmm">{{WWMM|1}} {{see image|File:Charlesetta Nagy - wanted poster.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Nagy, which stated that she was wanted for [[spell]] forging and [[house-elf|elf]] trafficking.<ref name="wwmm" /> The poster warned that she was "unpredictable" and would "likely resist arrest."<ref name="wwmm" /> A reward of 4,500 [[Dragot]]s was offered for help that led to her arrest.<ref name="wwmm" />
