'''Daisy Corran''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] student who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1990]] or [[1991]].<ref name="age" /> During the [[1996–1997 school year]], she, [[Banga O'Deluga]], and [[Justin Steele]] gave [[Harry Potter]] two [[Beater's bat]]s in exchange for six [[Gobstones]] while in the [[The Quad|Paved Courtyard]]. Later that same term, she could be found in the [[Seventh-floor corridor|seventh floor corridor]], where she traded [[potion]]s for [[Wizard's Chess|chess]] pieces.

==Etymology==
The name "Daisy" is from the English word for the white flower, ultimately derived from Old English ''dægeseage'' meaning "day eye". It was first used as a given name in the 19th century, at the same time many other plant and flower names were coined.
