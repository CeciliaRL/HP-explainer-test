'''Susie Sooth''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Seer]] who did readings for a living. She was certified by the [[Divinus Haruspex Association of Clairvoyants]]. An advertisement promoting her services appeared inside the [[Knight Bus]] in [[1993]].<ref name="image1">{{POA|F}} {{see image 2|File:SusieSooth1.jpg|File:SusieSooth2.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*The name of this character was presumably intended as a combination of the word ''soothsayer'', meaning "one who predicts the future,"<ref>"[http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/soothsayer Soothsayer]" on Wiktionary</ref> and the name of [[England|English]] musician [[Wikipedia:Siouxsie Sioux|Siouxsie Sioux]] (pronounced "SOO-zee soo"), who is best known as the lead singer of the band [[Wikipedia:Siouxsie and the Banshees|Siouxsie and the Banshees]].
