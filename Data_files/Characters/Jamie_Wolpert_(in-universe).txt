'''Jamie Wolpert''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] journalist who, in the summer of [[1996]], wrote the front-page article about the collapse of the [[Millennium Bridge]] for ''[[The Guardian]]''.<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:TheGuardian.png|this image]])</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Jamie Wolpert]], who worked behind-the-scenes as an assistant to [[David Yates]] on ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince]]'', and as a book consultant on {{DH|F1}} and ''[[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2|Part 2]]''. He also had a cameo as a [[Unidentified newspaper vendor|''Daily Prophet'' newspaper vendor]] at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] in ''Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1660828/ Jamie Wolpert]" on IMDb</ref>
