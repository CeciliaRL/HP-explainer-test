'''Josiah Jackson''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] who served as the first [[President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America|President]] of the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]].<ref name="PottermoreHistory3" />

==Biography==
Described as "warlike," Jackson was elected into office by his fellow representatives in [[1693]], as he was considered tough enough to deal with the troubles [[Wizarding world|wizarding]] America faced in the wake of the [[Salem Witch Trials]]. His top priority after taking office was the recruitment of new [[Auror]]s.<ref name="PottermoreHistory3" />
