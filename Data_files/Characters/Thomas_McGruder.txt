'''Thomas McGruder''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
During the [[1996–1997 school year]], he blocked the door at one end of the [[Stone Bridge]], forcing [[Harry Potter]] to [[Duelling|duel]] with him in order to get past.

==Etymology==
The name "Thomas" is the Greek form of the Aramaic name תָּאוֹמָא (Ta'oma') which meant "twin".
