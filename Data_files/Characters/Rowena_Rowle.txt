'''Rowena Rowle''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who worked at the [[British Ministry of Magic]] in the [[2010s]].<ref name="WU">{{WU}}</ref>

== Biography ==
At some point in the [[Calamity]], she lets a [[Calamity Investigator]] into the [[Department of Mysteries]] to share theories about [[Grim Fawley]]. As she monologues, the Investigator stares into the Tank of Brains - accidentally inspiring one to leap onto them. Rowena saves the Investigator.<ref name="WU"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Unless they are related, it is possible that the name "Rowena Rowle" is a continuity error and is supposed to be Regina.
