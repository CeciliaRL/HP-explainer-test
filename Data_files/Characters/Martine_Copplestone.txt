'''Martine Copplestone''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Hufflepuff]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="POAG"/><ref name="HBPG"/>

==Biography==
She was seen walking around the school in the [[1993–1994 school year]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}} - Console versions</ref>

Some people considered her nosy, as she often used the [[Levitation Charm]] to lift objects, in order to find things hidden underneath. She told [[Harry Potter]] about this trick during the [[1996–1997 school year]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console versions]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
