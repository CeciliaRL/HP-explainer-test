This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was the father of [[Cadogan|Sir Cadogan]], a [[Knights of the Round Table|Knight of the Round Table]] famous for his defeat of the [[Wyvern of Wye]].<ref name="PPG">{{PP|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Cadogan is a family name of Welsh and Irish origin.<ref>[http://www.behindthename.com/name/cadogan "Cadogan"] at [http://www.behindthename.com/ Behind the Name]</ref> This could mean that [[Cadogan|Sir Cadogan]]'s family — at least on the paternal side — hailed from Wales or Ireland. Whether Cadogan's father himself was Welsh or Irish is unconfirmed.
