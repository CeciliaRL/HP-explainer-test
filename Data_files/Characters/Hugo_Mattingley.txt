'''Hugo Mattingley''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="POAG">{{POA|G}}</ref> He was usually seen spending time in the [[Middle Courtyard|Transfiguration Courtyard]] during the [[1996–1997 school year]]. He was a fan of [[Gilderoy Lockhart]]. Unlike most people who thought that the [[Potions Master]] [[Horace Slughorn]] was a stakeholder, Trevor thought that he was a good teacher.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This particular student was only featured in the [[Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)|console versions]] of {{POA}} and the [[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)|Nintendo DS version]] of {{HBP}}.
