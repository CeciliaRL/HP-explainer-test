The '''2-Headed Baby''' was a name given to a set of ischiopagous conjoined twins. They were part of the [[Circus Arcanus]], a travelling [[Wizarding world|wizards']] freak show, and visited the [[United States of America|United States]] with it for the first in [[November]]-[[December]] [[1926]].<ref name="poster">[http://store.minalima.com/shop/fantastic-beasts/limited-edition-prints/no-maj-street-posters/circus-arcanus-museum-of-human-oddities-poster/ MinaLima Store - Circus Arcanus - Museum of Human Oddities Poster] {{see image|File:Circus Arcanus.png}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Their performance in a wizarding circus may suggest that 2-headed wizards had special magical abilities that one-headed wizards did not have, or their inclusion may be based on just their unusual physique.
*The 2-Headed Baby is an example of conjoined twins.
