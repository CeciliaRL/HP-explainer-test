'''Jeremy Clarkson''' is a [[Great Britain|British]] broadcaster and journalist best known as the co-presenter of the [[television]] programme ''[[Top Gear]]''. In [[2015]], the [[wikipedia:BBC|BBC]] announced it was cancelling Clarkson's contract after he verbally and physically attacked one of the show's producers, thus leading to an [[Internet|online]] petition to reinstate him.<ref name="twitter"/>

Among those who signed the petition was [[Vernon Dursley]], who stated that ''Top Gear'' was the only reason he paid his [[wikipedia:television licence|television licence]], which all British households are required to pay yearly in order to legally view television broadcasts.<ref name="twitter">[https://twitter.com/jk_rowling/status/622157481582698496 Tweet from J.K. Rowling]</ref>
