This '''[[Wizardkind|wizard]]''' was a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1980s]] who was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin]]. He had [[Potions (class)|Potions]] class with [[Jacob's sibling]].<ref name="Potions" />

==Biography==
This individual started attending Hogwarts on [[1 September]] [[1984]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin]] [[Hogwarts Houses|House]].<ref name="Potions" />

The individual frequently drank various botched [[potion]]s in [[Potions (class)|Potions]] classes, taught by [[Professor]] [[Severus Snape]].<ref name="Potions" />
