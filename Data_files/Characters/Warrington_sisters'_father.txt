An '''unknown man''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] and the father of two daughters, [[Ivy Warrington|Ivy]] and [[Winifred Warrington]]. Both of his daughters were [[Wizardkind|witches]].<ref name="MA" />

==Biography==
During her childhood,  Winifred accidentally  [[Vanishment|vanished]] herself, and their grandmother put a [[Memory Charm]] on Ivy so she would forget what happened, but it caused her to completely forget Winifred. Ivy later started attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="MA" />
