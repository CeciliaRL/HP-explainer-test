This '''group of women in crinolines''' were depicted in a picture hung at [[Hogwarts Castle]]. Their portrait hung at the bottom of the [[Divination Stairwell]] in the [[North Tower]], at the end of the [[Divination corridor]]. When [[Cadogan|Sir Cadogan]] lead [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley]] and [[Hermione Granger]] to the Divination Classroom, they passed through this portrait, whose occupants were alarmed by Sir Cadogan's sudden appearance.<ref name="POA6">{{POA|B|6}}</ref>
