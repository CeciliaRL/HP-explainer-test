This '''individual''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] who worked as an employee at the [[Steen National Bank]] in the [[20th century]].

==Biography==
On [[6 December]], [[1926]], this individual was working at [[Steen National Bank]] when [[Newton Scamander]] entered the bank to find his escaped [[Newton Scamander's niffler|niffler]]. The employee, suspicious of Newt due his furtive glances around the bank, asked him if he needed any help to which Newt replied that he was only waiting.<ref>{{FB|S|8}}</ref>
