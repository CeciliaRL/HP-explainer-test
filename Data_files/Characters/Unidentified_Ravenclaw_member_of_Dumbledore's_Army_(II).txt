This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] who was a member of [[Ravenclaw]] [[Hogwarts Houses|house]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

==Biography==
He was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw]], possibly in [[1991]] or [[1992]]. In [[1998]], he became a member of [[Dumbledore's Army]] and fought during the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] on the [[2 May]], against [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s [[Death Eaters]]. It is unknown if he survived the battle or not.<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>
