'''Toby Gleaves'''<ref>Closed captions on the DVD/Blu-ray of {{DH|F1}}</ref> was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was reported missing along with his relative, [[Olivia Gleaves|Olivia]], on the ''[[Potterwatch]]'' wireless programme in [[1997]]. His disappearance was most likely linked to a [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] attack.

==Behind the scenes==
*This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of Peter Gleaves, an ADR mixer who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]], excepting ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Philosopher's Stone]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]''.<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0322367 Peter Gleave's profile on IMDb]</ref>
