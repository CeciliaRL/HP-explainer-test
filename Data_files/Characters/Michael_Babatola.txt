'''Michael Babatola''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] nurse who worked at [[St David's Hospital]].<ref name="FBC51">{{FBC|5|1}}</ref>

==Biography==
Babatola was an important eyewitness in an investigation led by [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]] and her [[Mathilda Grimblehawk's partner|partner]], concerning the disappearance of [[Bilius Finbok]], a [[Ministry of Magic]] employee.<ref name="FBC51" />
