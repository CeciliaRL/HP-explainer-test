'''Randolph Pauncefoot''' (b. [[1957]])<ref>https://www.hp-lexicon.org/character/randolph-pauncefoot/</ref> was a [[Quidditch]] fan who supported the [[Montrose Magpies]].<ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[1 June|June 1st]], [[1999]], Page 4</ref> He did not have a problem with the team's [[Chaser]], [[Alasdair Maddock]], wanting to try out [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] sports, but stated that a person must be a little crazy to play golf.<ref>''The Lexicon'' by Steve Vander Ark, page 232</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Randolph" is from the Germanic elements rand meaning "rim (of a shield)" and wulf meaning "wolf".
