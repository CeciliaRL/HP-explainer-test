==Behind the scenes==
*This character may have been named after [http://hadriangardendesign.com/ Hadrian Whittle], a British garden designer who has worked with [http://www.filmscapes.co.uk/ Filmscapes],<ref>[http://hadriangardendesign.com/data/1_1.htm About page on HadrianGardenDesign.com]</ref> a company which did landscaping work for the first five ''Harry Potter'' [[Harry Potter (film series)|films]].<ref>[http://www.filmscapes.co.uk/credits.php Credits list on Filmscapes.co.uk]</ref>
*Whittle possibly wrote [[Magical Mediterranean Water-Plants and Their Properties|Magical Water Plants of the Mediterranean]] after the popular [[Magical Water Plants of the Highland Lochs]].

==Etymology==
The name "Hadrian" comes from the Roman cognomen Hadrianus, which meant "from Hadria" in Latin. Hadria was a town in northern Italy (it gave its name to the Adriatic Sea). A famous bearer of the name was Publius Aelius Hadrianus, better known as Hadrian, a 2nd-century Roman emperor who built a wall across northern Britain.
