This '''boy''' (fl. [[1992]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] who was sorted into [[Gryffindor]].<ref name="PSG"/>

==Biography==
In [[1992]], he fetched [[Harry Potter]], who had just finished a [[Charms (class)|Charms]] class with [[Filius Flitwick]], and told him to go up to [[Gryffindor Tower]], as [[Draco Malfoy]] had placed a [[Leg-Locker Curse]] on [[Neville Longbottom]].<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}} - Console versions</ref>
