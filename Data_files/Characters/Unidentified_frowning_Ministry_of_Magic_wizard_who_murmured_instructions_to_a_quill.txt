This '''individual''' was a [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] official. On [[2 September]], [[1997]], he passed by [[Harry Potter]] ([[Polyjuice Potion|disguised]] as [[Albert Runcorn]]) while under his [[Cloak of Invisibility|Invisibility Cloak]] on Level One of the Ministry of Magic. The wizard was murmuring instructions to a [[Levitation|floating]] [[quill]] scribbling on a trail of [[parchment]].<ref name="DH13">{{DH|B|13}}</ref>
