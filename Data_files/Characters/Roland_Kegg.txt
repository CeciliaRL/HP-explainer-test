'''Roland Kegg''' (b. [[1903]]) was an [[England|English]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who was President of the [[English National Gobstones Team|English Gobstones Team]]. For his achievement he was commemorated on a bronze [[Chocolate Frog Card]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Roland" means "famous land" from the Germanic elements ''hrod'' "fame" and land. Roland was a semi-legendary French hero whose story is told in the medieval epic 'La Chanson de Roland', in which he is a nephew of Charlemagne killed in battle with the Saracens. This name was introduced to England by the Normans.
