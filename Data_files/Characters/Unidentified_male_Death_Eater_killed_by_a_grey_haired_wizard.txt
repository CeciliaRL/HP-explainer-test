This '''individual''' was a [[Dark Wizard]] and [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] who served [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] during the [[Second Wizarding War]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

==Biography==
He became a member of the [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] sometime prior to or during the [[Second Wizarding War]], presumably participating in numerous battles with his fellow Death Eaters, such as the [[Battle of the Department of Mysteries]], [[Battle of the Astronomy Tower]] and [[Battle of the Seven Potters]]. On [[2 May]], [[1998]], he fought at the [[Battle of Hogwarts]] as a member of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]]'s army of Death Eaters, [[Snatchers|Snatcher]]s and [[Dark Arts|Dark]] [[creature]]s. During the final stage of the battle, he was killed during the [[Skirmish at the Great Hall]] by a [[Unidentified grey haired man during the Battle of Hogwarts|grey haired wizard]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>
