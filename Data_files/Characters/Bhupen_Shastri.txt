'''Bhupen Shastri''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Bhupen is an Indian given name, of Sanskrit origin, meaning "king." Shastri is a surname of Indian origin. 

==Behind the scenes==
*Given his name, it can be presumed the he is of [[India]]n descent.
