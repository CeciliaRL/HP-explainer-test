'''Katsuji Yamazaki''' was a [[Hufflepuff]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Katsuji is a Japanese given name, meaning "victorious". Yamazaki is a Japanese surname from a common place name meaning "mountain promontory".

==Behind the scenes==
*Given his name, it can be presumed that he is of [[Japan]]ese descent.
