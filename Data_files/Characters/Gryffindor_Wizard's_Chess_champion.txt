The '''Gryffindor Wizard's Chess champion''', as the title suggests, refers to the [[Wizard's Chess]] Champion for [[Gryffindor|Gryffindor House]], at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="OOTPG"/>

==Biography==
The boy came to be educated at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Gryffindor|Gryffindor House]]. His [[Head of House]] was [[Professor]] [[Minerva McGonagall]], the [[Transfiguration]] teacher, and he shared a dormitory with four other boys in his year in [[Gryffindor Tower]]. He was skilled in [[Wizard's Chess]] and could be usually found playing on a table in a niche in the common room.<ref name="OOTPG">{{OOTP|G}}</ref>
