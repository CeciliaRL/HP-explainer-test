This '''[[Wizardkind|witch]]''' was appointed [[Headmaster|Headmistress]] of [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] before [[Armando Dippet|Professor Dippet]]. After her death, her [[portrait]] was hung in the [[Headmaster's office]], in order to give advice to the current head. Her portrait is hung over the entryway to the Headmaster's Office and depicts her sleeping on a chair holding a transparent sphere.<ref name="MHP">{{MHP}}</ref>
