'''Cormack McLeod''' was the manager of the [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|team]], the [[Montrose Magpies]].<ref name="wizardsm">"[http://www.hplex.info/wizards/a-z/m.html Wizards, Witches and Beings: M]" on the Harry Potter Lexicon</ref><ref>[[The Daily Prophet (real-world)|Daily Prophet Newsletters]]: [[8 February]], [[1999]], Page 3</ref> He fired one of the team's [[Chaser]]s, [[Alasdair Maddock]], prior to a match against the [[Caerphilly Catapults]] because he had been caught playing a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] sport involving the use of "''peculiar metal sticks''" to knock "''non-flying balls into holes in the ground.''"<ref name="wizardsm" /><ref name="dp">"[http://www.hplex.info/about/sources/source_dp.html Daily Prophet newsletters]" on The Harry Potter Lexicon</ref> Maddock had previously upset Quidditch matches by trying out football and basketball tactics.<ref name="wizardsm" />

==Behind the scenes==
McLeod is not only a real Scottish clan, but also the surname of the main character of the [[1986]] fantasy film ''[[wikipedia:Highlander (film)|Highlander]]'': Connor McLeod, played by [[France|French]] actor [[wikipedia:Christopher Lambert|Christopher Lambert]].
