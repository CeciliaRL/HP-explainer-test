'''M. P. Carneirus''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizard or witch]] and the [[Captain of Aurors]] for the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="poster">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:Eleanora Shanks - wanted poster.png}}</ref> Their name appeared as official certification on some wanted posters issued by MACUSA.<ref name="poster" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after [[Mauricio Carneiro (real-world)|Mauricio Carneiro]], who worked as a costume illustrator for every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)|Goblet of Fire]]'' onward, and as a costumer for ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Order of the Phoenix]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Half-Blood Prince]]''.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1769188/ Mauricio Carneiro]" on IMDb</ref>
