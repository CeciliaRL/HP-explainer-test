'''Junpei Suruga''' was a [[Slytherin]]<ref name="house" /> student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].

==Biography==
He competed in the school [[Wizard Skittles]] tournament during the [[1996–1997 school year]].

==Etymology==
Junpei is a male name of Japanese origin, meaning "pure, genuine".

==Behind the scenes==
Given his name, he is most likely of Japanese descent.
