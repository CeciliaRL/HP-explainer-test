'''Sam''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked as an [[Obliviator]] for the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] in the [[1920s]].<ref name="fb68">{{FB|S|68}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1926]], Sam was tasked with [[Memory Charm|wiping the memory]] of the [[Non-magic people|No-Maj]] [[Jacob Kowalski]] after he entered the MACUSA headquarters, but was intercepted by [[Queenie Goldstein]].<ref name="fb68" /> Queenie, a [[Legilimency|Legilimens]], was able to read Sam's mind and see that he was in relationships with two women at the same time, fellow MACUSA employees [[Ruby (witch)|Ruby]] and [[Cecily]].<ref name="fb68" /> Queenie threatened to reveal his infidelity and was thus able to convince him not to Obliviate Jacob.<ref name="fb68" />

==Physical appearance==
Sam had brown hair and wore a navy blue blazer with the MACUSA logo on the right side and a white button-down shirt.

==Behind the scenes==
*Sam was portrayed by [[Matthew Wilson]] in ''[[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)|Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]''.
