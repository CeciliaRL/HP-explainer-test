'''Mentor Metaxas''' was a Greek [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the chairman of the [[International Confederation of Wizards Quidditch Committee]] in 2014.<ref name="the-qwc2014-final">{{PM}} - [[2014 Quidditch World Cup final]] (Archived)</ref>

==Biography==
He attended the final of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], on [[11 July]], [[2014]]. Shortly before the match started, he was spotted by ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' reporter [[Rita Skeeter]] chatting to the President of the [[Argentinian Council of Magic]], [[Valentina Vázquez]].<ref name="the-qwc2014-final" />

==Behind the scenes==
*If this individual's name is indication of anything, he is probably [[Greece|Greek]] or of Greek descent — the name "Mentor" is an Ancient Greek name that features in Greek Mythology,<ref>[http://www.behindthename.com/name/mentor "Mentor"] at [http://www.behindthename.com/ Behind the Name]</ref> and the surname Metaxas is also Greek: Greek archicted and marksman [[Wikipedia:Anastasios Metaxas|Anastasios Metaxas]] (1862-1937), Greek politician [[Wikipedia:Andreas Metaxas|Andreas Metaxas]] (1790-1860), and Greek general and dictator [[Wikipedia:Ioannis Metaxas|Ioannis Metaxas]] (1871–1941) are some notable people who bore this surname.
