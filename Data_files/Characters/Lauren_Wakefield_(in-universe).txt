'''Lauren Wakefield''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] circus performer. She was one of the performers whose talents were featured in [[Le Cirque du Vaga Lume]].<ref name="HPLE">{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:LeCirqueduVagaLumePoster.jpg}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Lauren Wakefield]], a graphic artist who worked on ''[[Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)|Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince]]'', and both parts of ''[[Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)|Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows]]''.
