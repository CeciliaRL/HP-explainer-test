This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] who was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin]] in [[1991]].<ref name="OOTPF"/>

==Biography==

===Fifth year===
During the [[1995–1996 school year]], when [[Fred Weasley|Fred]] and [[George Weasley]] set off fireworks in the castle, she was seen looking at a [[Weasleys' Wildfire Whiz-bangs|Whiz-Bang]] that followed and hit [[Vincent Crabbe]] in the rear end. She later attended her [[Charms (class)|Charms]] [[Ordinary Wizarding Level|O.W.L.]] exam.<ref name="OOTPF">{{OOTP|F}}</ref>

===Seventh year and the Battle of Hogwarts===
[[File:HP_0130.jpg|thumb|140px|left|The girl fleeing the school before the [[Battle of Hogwarts]]]]
During the [[1997–1998 school year]] she was forced to leave the school along with her fellow Slytherins before the start of the [[Battle of Hogwarts]].<ref name="DHF2">{{DH|F2}}</ref>

===Later life===
It is unknown if she survived the [[Second Wizarding War]].
