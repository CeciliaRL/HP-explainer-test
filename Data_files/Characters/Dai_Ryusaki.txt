'''Dai Ryusaki''' was a [[Japan]]ese [[Wizardkind|wizard]] that lived hundreds of years prior to the [[20th century|21st century]], and who was known to have held a teaching post at [[Mahoutokoro]]. At some point in his life, he became a [[Dark Wizard]]. Ryusaki's life was briefly covered in a [[book on the history of Mahoutokoro]] found in the [[Restricted Section]] of the [[Hogwarts Library]].<ref name="HM722">{{HM|7|22}}</ref>

==Biography==
Dai Ryusaki was a [[professor]] at [[Mahoutokoro]] centuries ago.<ref name="HM722"/> At some point, he violated the [[Japanese wizard's code]] by indulging in "[[Dark Arts|forbidden practises]]",<ref name="Pottermore2">{{WW|mahoutokoro}}</ref> becoming a [[Dark Wizard]].<ref name="HM722"/>

Legend had it that Dai Ryusaki created a [[Dai Ryusaki's mind-enhancing potion|mind-enhancing potion]] that highly powerful, but the potion was deemed dangerous and destroyed, with Ryusaki arrested. Rumour had it, was that before he was arrested, he was able to hide a [[Dark Scroll]] with the [[potion]] ingredients. Nobody after his death however knew if the scroll really existed, or what its contents could have been.<ref name="HM723">{{HM|7|23}}</ref>

He created a [[Dai Ryusaki's weapon|powerful weapon]], but was stopped before it could be used. It is unknown what the weapon did or was created for, and the information died with his [[death]].<ref name="HM722"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Dark Arts]]''': Ryusaki was said to be a [[Dark Wizard]], meaning he would have been proficient with Dark Magic. His [[Dai Ryusaki's weapon|weapon]] that he created was presumably of a dark nature.<ref name="HM722"/>
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Legend had it that Ryusaki invented an incredibly powerful [[Dai Ryusaki's mind-enhancing potion|mind-enhancing potion]] that was destroyed due to it being dangerous.<ref name="HM723"/>
