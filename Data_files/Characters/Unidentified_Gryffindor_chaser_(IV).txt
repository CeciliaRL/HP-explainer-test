==Biography==
During her time at Hogwarts, she was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Gryffindor]] [[Hogwarts Houses|House]]. At some point by the [[2019â€“2020 school year]], she became the [[Chaser]] of [[Gryffindor Quidditch team]].<ref name="WU"/>

On the eve of the final [[Gryffindor Quidditch team|Gryffindor]]-[[Slytherin Quidditch team|Slytherin]] matches of the [[Inter-House Quidditch Cup|Hogwarts Inter-House Quidditch Cup]] she feared that her broom would vanish or weaken as a result of becoming a [[Foundable]]. [[Headmaster|Headmistress]] [[Minerva McGonagall]] assured her that the Foundable in question was almost certainly the broom of a former famous [[Harry Potter|student]].<ref name="WU"/>
