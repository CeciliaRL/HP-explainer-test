'''Fulcran Lestrange''' was a [[France|French]] wizard and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. He was the son of [[Fulcran Lestrange (I)|Fulcran Lestrange]] and [[Pucine Lestrange (I)|Pucine Leroy]]. He married [[Sotére Lestrange|Sotére Petit]] and had a three children with her, two daughters [[Balbine Lestrange (II)|Balbine]] and [[Pucine Lestrange (II)|Pucine]], and a son [[Fulcran Lestrange (III)|Fulcran]].<ref>{{COG}} {{see image|File:Lestrange family tree.png}}</ref>
