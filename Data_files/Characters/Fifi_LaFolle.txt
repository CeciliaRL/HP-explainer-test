'''Fifi LaFolle''' ([[1888]]â€“[[1971]]) was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and the author of the ''[[Enchanted Encounters]]'' series.<ref name="JKRS"/>

==Biography==

===Early life===
Fifi LaFolle was born in [[1888]] to parents of unknown blood status. She either was home schooled by her parents or attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], which she would have attended from 1899 or 1900 to 1906 or 1907.

In her adult life, she began writing her famous book collection, titled the ''[[Enchanted Encounters]]''.<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>

===Death and post-mortem===
LaFolle died in [[1971]] at the age of 82 or 83. She was on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] after her death and was awarded 'Wizard of the Month' in [[2005]].<ref name="JKRS"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*Fifi LaFolle was [[Wizard of the Month]] on {{JKRS}} for [[October]] [[2005]].
*The image accompanying her entry on the website appears to be based on the deceased [[Great Britain|British]] romance novelist {{W|Barbara Cartland}}.
*In French, 'La Folle' means 'the mad woman'.
