'''Mrs Warren''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]]<ref name="COS12">{{COS|B|12}}</ref> and the mother of [[Myrtle Warren|Myrtle Elizabeth Warren]], a [[Muggle-born]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], where she was killed by a [[Salazar Slytherin's Basilisk|Basilisk]] on [[13 June]], [[1943]]. She and her [[Myrtle's father|husband]] visited Hogwarts shortly after the tragic event occurred.<ref name="COS13">{{COS|B|13}}</ref>
