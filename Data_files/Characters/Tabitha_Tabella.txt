'''Tabitha Tabella''', also known by the nickname '''La Bella''', was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].<ref name="itm">''[[Inside the Magic: The Making of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them]]'' {{see image|File:TabithaTabellaWanted.jpg}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1926]], the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] issued a wanted poster for Tabella, which stated that she was wanted for "[[Dragot]] laundering," "defenestration of wizards," and "[[Muggle|No-Maj]] murder."<ref name="itm" /> It warned that she was "bearing [a] [[wand]] and extremely dangerous."<ref name="itm" /> The poster promised a reward of 2538 Dragots for information leading to her capture.<ref name="itm" /> This poster was hanging in [[The Blind Pig]] when [[Newton Scamander|Newt Scamander]] visited in December 1926.<ref name="itm" />
