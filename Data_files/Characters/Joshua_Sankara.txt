}}'''Minister Joshua Sankara''' was a [[Burkina Faso|Burkinabé]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as [[Seeker]] for the [[Burkinabé National Quidditch team]] during the [[2006 Quidditch World Cup]]. When the team won the World Cup, Sankara was promptly named [[Minister for Magic]], although he resigned two days later since he would much rather play [[Quidditch]] than hold office at the [[Burkinabé Ministry of Magic]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[Quidditch World Cup 1990 - 2014]] (Archived)</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*He may be named after Thomas Sankara, a former president of Burkina Faso.
