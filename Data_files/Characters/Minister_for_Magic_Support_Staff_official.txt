This '''individual''' was a [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] official after the [[Fall of the Ministry of Magic]].

==Biography==
On [[2 September]], [[1997]], he calmed the Ministry pamphlet makers down after [[Harry Potter]] released a bunch of [[Decoy Detonator]]s in order to distract them and gain entrance to [[Senior Undersecretary to the Minister for Magic's office|Dolores Umbridge's office]].<ref>{{DH|F1}} - Chapter 12 (''Secret Files'')</ref>
