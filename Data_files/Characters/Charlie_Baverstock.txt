He proclaimed himself 'madder then a bloke who's been locked in a box of Fwoopers' after his [[Chaser]] [[Dennis Moon]] was sent off in the 106th minute of New Zealand's first round match against [[Bulgarian National Quidditch Stadium|Bulgaria]] at the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup]].
