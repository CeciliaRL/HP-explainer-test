'''Miraphora Mina''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Gryffindor]] student who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name="gobstones">See [[:File:GobstonesClubSignUpSheet.jpg|this image]] of a prop used in the films on display as part of {{HPE}}.</ref> She signed up to join the [[Hogwarts Gobstone Club]] on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in [[Gryffindor Tower]].<ref name="gobstones" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was named after [[Miraphora Mina]], a graphic artist who worked on every [[Harry Potter films|''Harry Potter'' film]] from ''[[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)|Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone]]'' onward.
