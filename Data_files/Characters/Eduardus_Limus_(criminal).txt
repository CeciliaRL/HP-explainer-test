'''Eduardus Limus''', also known by the nickname '''Pretty Face''', was a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] criminal who operated in the [[1920s]].<ref name="cob">{{CB|119}} {{See image|File:EduardusLimusWanted.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
In [[1926]], the [[International Wizarding Police]] issued a wanted poster for Limus, stating that he was wanted for "counterfeiting [[wand]]s" and for committing "[[muggle|No-Maj]] murder" in [[Asia]] and [[Europe]].<ref name="cob" /> It described him as a "powerful wizard" who was "[[wand]]ed and dangerous."<ref name="cob" /> A reward of 5000 [[Dragot]]s was offered for information leading to his capture.<ref name="cob" />
