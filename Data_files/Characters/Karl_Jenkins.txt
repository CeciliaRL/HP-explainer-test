'''Karl Jenkins''' (b. [[2006|2005]] or [[2007|2006]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and student in [[Albus Potter]]'s year at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].

==Biography==
===Early life===
Karl Jenkins was born somewhere in the [[Great Britain|United Kingdom]] between [[1 September]] [[2006|2005]] and [[31 August]] [[2007|2006]].<ref name="act1scene4">{{CC|B|1|4}}</ref>

===First year===
{{Quote|Albus Potter, the Slytherin Squib.|Jenkins insulting Albus Potter's poor magical abilities|Harry Potter and the Cursed Child}}
Karl began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] on 1 September [[2017]].<ref name="act1scene4" /> As the [[sorting ceremony]] was starting, he expressed wonder at the prospect of [[Albus Potter]], son of the famous [[Harry Potter]], being in his year.<ref name="act1scene4" /> Karl was sorted into [[Gryffindor]] [[Hogwarts Houses|house]].<ref name="House"/>

When Albus Potter proved unable to get his [[broomstick]] to rise off the ground during their first [[flying (class)|flying]] lesson with [[Rolanda Hooch|Madam Hooch]], Karl teased him, calling him the "[[Slytherin]] [[Squib]]."<ref name="act1scene4" />

===Second year===
During a [[second year|second-year]] [[Potions (class)|Potions]] lesson, Albus and [[Scorpius Malfoy]] had difficulty preparing their [[potion]], prompting Karl to tell the other students in the class to "leave [Albus] and Voldemort’s child to it."<ref name="act1scene4" />

===Fourth year===
Upon returning to [[2020]] after [[Time-Turner|altering the past]], [[Scorpius Malfoy]] found himself in an [[alternate reality]] in which [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] won, and was greeted as the "Scorpion King" by Karl Jenkins on the Hogwarts grounds.<ref name="act3scene2">{{CC|B|3|2}}</ref> [[Yann Fredericks]] asked Scorpius if he was still on for an event the following night and Karl enthused they were both ready to "spill some proper [[Muggle-born|Mudblood]] guts."<ref name="act3scene2" />

==Behind the scenes==
