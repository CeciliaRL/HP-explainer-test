'''Shania Shaw''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|witch]], [[Feminism|feminist]] and member of the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]].<ref name="WU" />

==Biography==
In her adult life, she became a women's activist and joined the [[Salem Witches' Institute]]. At some point during [[2020]], she and a cadre of its members joined the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]], bolstering the efforts to contain the [[Calamity]].<ref name="WU"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Charm]]s''': She was an expert on the usage of the [[Hour-Reversal Charm]]. Her knowledge of the [[time]] manipulating [[spell]] allowed her to illuminate her [[Calamity Investigator|colleagues]], and to be seen as a "master" in its use.<ref name="WU">{{WU}} (Calamity Essentials II: The Salem Witches' Institute)</ref>
