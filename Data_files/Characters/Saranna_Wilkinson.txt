'''Saranna Wilkinson''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who was employed as an [[Auror]] by the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]].<ref name="WU">{{WU}} (see [https://wizardsunitehub.info/profession-skill/American-Ingenuity/ this])</ref>

==Biography==
In the [[2010s]], Saranna Wilkinson visited the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] in [[London]] during the [[Calamity]]. She gave spellcasting tips to [[Professor]] members of the [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force]] to aid them in their field work.<ref name="WU" />
