'''Theophilus Abbot''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] and historian.

==Biography==
He studied [[Muggle|No-Maj]] families which descended from [[Scourers]] who escaped justice following the establishment of the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]], and identified in each family a deep belief in the existence of magic and an intense hatred of it, convictions passed down from their vengeful ancestors.<ref name="pottermoreHistory2">{{PM}} - [https://www.pottermore.com/collection-episodic/history-of-magic-in-north-america-en Writing by J.K. Rowling: "History of Magic in North America: Seventeenth Century and Beyond"]</ref> He authored the book ''[[Scourers and the Creation of MACUSA]]'' sometime prior to [[1926]].<ref name="cb">{{CB}}, page 20</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[History of Magic]]''': As a magical historian, Theophilius had extensive knowledge in American wizarding history, particularly [[Scourers]].
*'''[[:Category:Authors|Author]] skills''': Theophilius was an accomplished author and wrote [[Scourers and the Creation of MACUSA]].

==Behind the scenes==

*He may be related to [[Hannah Abbott]]. His [[Abbott family|last name]] may be misspelled, and may actually be the same as Hannah's. This opens up the possibility that there is a branch of the Abbott family in the [[United States of America|United States]].
