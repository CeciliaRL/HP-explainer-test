This '''individual''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard or witch]] attended a [[Quidditch]] match in [[Norfolk]] in [[1357]], most likely as a spectator. For reasons unknown, they killed the match's [[Quidditch referee|referee]], [[Cyprian Youdle]], with a [[curse]] and fled, thus becoming the only known person in history to kill a Quidditch referee during the course of a match. The individual was never caught for their crime or identified.<ref name="QTA6">{{QTA|R|6}}</ref>
