'''Gulliver Pokeby''' ([[1750]] - [[1839]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]], [[Magizoologist]], expert on [[magic]]al [[bird]]s and the author of ''[[Why I Didn't Die When the Augurey Cried]]''. He was the first to identify that the [[Augurey]]'s cries meant [[Water|rain]] was coming, rather than impending [[death]].<ref name="FBR"/> He was immortalised on a [[Chocolate Frog Card]],<ref name="PM">{{PM}}</ref> which featured him wearing his [[Gulliver Pokeby's pocket watch|golden pocket watch]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Magizoology]]''': Pokeby was an expert of [[magic]]al ornithology, the study of magical [[bird]]s, having famously discovered the true explanation of the [[Augurey]]'s cries meaning impending [[Water|rain]] rather than [[death]], a very important [[Science|scientific]] discovery in this field. He wrote the book on the subject ''[[Why I Didn't Die When the Augurey Cried]]'', showing his experience and knowledge of his research.<ref name="FBR"/>

==Etymology==
Gulliver is French for glutton.
