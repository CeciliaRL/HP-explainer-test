'''Luxo Karuzos II''' was the editor-in-chief of the ''[[The Wizard's Voice]]'' in [[1926]].<ref name="newspaper">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:The Wizard's Voice - 6 Dec 1926.png}}</ref> He wrote an editorial entitled "Rare Spells Exercised to Control the Truth" in the [[6 December]] 1926 issue of the newspaper.<ref name="newspaper" />

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after Luca Caruso, the son of [[Miraphora Mina]], a graphic artist who worked on all eight [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]] and the [[Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film series)|''Fantastic Beasts'' films]].<ref name="talkswith">Rick Gerardy, ''Talks with Emanuel Hurwitz: 82 Years with the Violi'', Page 109</ref>
