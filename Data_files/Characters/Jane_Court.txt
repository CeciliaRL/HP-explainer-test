'''Jane Court''' was a [[wizardkind|witch]] who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1980]]<ref name="age" /><ref name="graduation">[https://twitter.com/themattlondon/status/1005427171459026945 For wiki clarity: Angelica, Chester, Jane, and Felix all entered Hogwarts Sept 1st 1980 and graduated Spring 1987.] by [https://twitter.com/themattlondon Matt London on Twitter]</ref> and was Sorted into [[Hufflepuff]]. In [[1984]], she had been made a [[Prefect]], and was responsible for watching over the [[first year]]s.<ref name="HM13">{{HM|1|3}} {{c|info revealed to [[Hufflepuff]] players}}</ref> At some point in her life, she served time in [[Azkaban]] for an unknown crime, and was released by [[September]] [[1988]].<ref name="Y5SQ">{{HM|5|Yer a Prefect}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Hogwarts years {{c|1980-1987}}===
She started going to Hogwarts in 1980, where she became a good [[Duelling|duellist]] and met [[Jacob's sibling]] in 1984.

During the [[Start-of-Term Feast]] of the [[1985–1986 school year]], Jane told [[Jacob's sibling]] she thought she saw the missing [[Ben Copper]] studying [[Transfiguration]] by the [[Clock Tower]].<ref name="HM21">{{HM|2|1}}</ref>

Jane was still at Hogwarts the following two years and graduated in [[1987]].<ref name="graduation" />

===Later life {{c|1987-}}===
After Hogwarts, Jane was sentenced to serve time in [[Azkaban]] for an unknown crime. She was released by September [[1988]].<ref name="Y5SQ"/>

In September of 1988 she joined other previous prefects to meet with new prefects in the [[Three Broomsticks Inn]].<ref name="Y5SQ"/>

Later this year, she met [[Jacob's sibling]] in [[Knockturn Alley]] and informed them that [[Mundungus Fletcher]] had been heading to [[Ollivanders]].<ref name="HM510">{{HM|5|10}}</ref>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Duelling]]''': Jane became a proficient duellist during her years at Hogwarts.
*'''[[Charm]]s''': Jane was adept with the casting of charms, such as the [[Freezing Charm]].<ref name="farewell">{{HM|3|Farewell My Prefect}}</ref>
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Jane was skilled with the brewing of potions, such as [[Babbling Beverage]].<ref name="farewell"/>
*'''[[Herbology]]''': Jane was skilled with the handling of [[magic]]al plants, such as [[Snargaluff]].<ref name="farewell"/>
