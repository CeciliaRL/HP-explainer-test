'''Pedrus Almeidus''' was a reporter for ''[[The New York Ghost]]'' in the [[1920s]].<ref name="dec6sunrise">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:The New York Ghost - 6 Dec 1926 - Sunrise Early Edition.png}}</ref>

==Biography==
He wrote an article about a call by [[Magical Congress of the United States of America|MACUSA]] officials for more witches in the workforce that was published in the paper's [[6 December]] [[1926]] morning edition.<ref name="dec6sunrise" />
