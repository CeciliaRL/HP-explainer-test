'''Tiago Montoya''' was a [[Spain|Spanish]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as a [[Beater]] on the [[Spanish National Quidditch team]] in [[1994]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

During the [[1996–1997 school year]], Montoya's [[Chocolate Frog Card]] could be won by beating [[Gladys Prescott]] at a [[Quidditch]] challenge.<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}} {{C|NDS edition}}</ref>

==Etymology==
''Tiago'' is Portuguese for "James". ''Montoya'' is a common Spanish surname derived from "monte", the Spanish word for hill.<ref>[http://heraldicahispana.com/ApA/monedero-montoya.htm Heraldic Hispana]</ref>

Together with teammate [[Inigo Fuente Marrero|Iñigo Fuente]], this character's name may be a reference to Íñigo Montoya from the fantasy comedy romance novel (and [[wikipedia:The Princess Bride (film)|film]]) ''[[wikipedia:The Princess Bride|The Princess Bride]]''.
