'''Raymond''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Ravenclaw]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="PSG"/>

==Biography==
===Early life===
Raymond was born between [[1974]] and [[1980]], if not earlier. He was accepted into [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw|Ravenclaw House]].<ref name="PSG"/>

===Cat in the bottom of the well===
[[Harry Potter]] first met Raymond in the Hogwarts Grounds, in [[1991]]. The boy was sitting next to a well and when Harry went closer, he told him that [[Raymond's kitten|his kitten]] fell into the well. Later, Harry was in a [[Potions (class)|potions]] side-room and a cloaked [[Quirinus Quirrell]] blasted him in a potions storeroom with the ''[[Deprimo]]'' [[charm]]. After defeating some [[Troll]]s Harry went into the bottom of the well and saw Raymond on top. That was the first time he mentioned his name to Harry. After giving back [[Raymond's kitten|his kitten]], Raymond threw a [[Chocolate Frog Card]] to Harry.<ref name="PSG">{{PS|G}} (PS1 version)</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Raymond" comes From the Germanic name Raginmund, composed of the elements ragin "advice" and mund "protector".
