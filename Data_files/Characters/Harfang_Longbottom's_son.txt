The '''only son''' of [[Harfang Longbottom]] and [[Callidora Black]] was a [[pure-blood]] wizard.<ref name="BFT">[[House of Black|Black family tree]]</ref> He had a [[Harfang Longbottom's daughter|sister]].<ref>[[House of Black#Black family tree|Black family tree]]</ref> He was a first cousin of [[Arthur Weasley]] and possibly [[Bartemius Crouch Senior|Bartemius Crouch Snr]] through his mother. He was also possibly related to [[Frank Longbottom]] and his [[Neville Longbottom|son]] through his father.
