'''Erica J.''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. She once signed up for [[Quidditch]] training at seven, Tuesday evening, on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the [[Gryffindor Notice Board|bulletin board]] in the [[Gryffindor Common Room]].<ref>{{See image|File:QuidditchTrainingSignupSheet.jpg}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*Her name; ''Erica'' means several things. In Latin it means Heather. In the ancient language of Norse it means Eternal Ruler. In old German it means Honourable Ruler. The Latin, ''Erica'', is used in gardening terms to mean Heather even in the modern day.
*Her possible surname, ''Jerankordr'', may have come from Indian or Moroccan roots where Jerank is used to describe a type of cuisine.
