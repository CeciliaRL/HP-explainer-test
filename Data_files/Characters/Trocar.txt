'''Professor Trocar''' was a [[vampire]], and a [[Professor|teacher]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="PM">{{WW|vampires}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Trocar made an appearance only on [[J. K. Rowling]]'s earliest planning notes for the books, and disappeared from her plans fairly early on, since she "''did not think much of him as a character''".<ref name="PM"/>
*A [[Wikipedia:Trocar|trocar]] is a medical device inserted into arteries or cavities to extract bodily fluids.
