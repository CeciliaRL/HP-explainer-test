'''Professor'''<ref name="MHP"/> '''Podric Piles'''<ref name="name">{{MHP}} {{see image|File:ChessPlayersNames.jpg}}</ref> was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] that was depicted in a large painting that hung in [[Gryffindor Tower]], at [[Hogwarts Castle]]. In his lifetime, he was [[Head of Gryffindor House]].<ref name="MHP" />

In the painting, Piles is depicted as playing a game of [[Wizard's Chess]] with [[Uric Oapley]], with [[Tobias Stump]], standing, watching over the game, and [[Muldoon Cragg]], in the background, absorbed in reading. Piles is depicted as sitting on a chair, wearing a long curly white powdered wig with a stripey pointy hat, a large red coat, a cravat, blue breeches and white stockings.
