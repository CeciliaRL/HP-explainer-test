'''Herman Wintringham''' (c. [[1974]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and the [[lute]]nist for the popular band the [[Weird Sisters]].<ref name="COSG">{{COS|G}}</ref> He was extremely hairy and joined his fellow band members to play at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] at the [[Yule Ball]] in [[1994]].<ref name="GOF23">{{GOF|B|23}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Herman Wintringham and [[Merton Graves]] were absent from {{GOF|FA}}, and did not play with the [[Weird Sisters]].
*If Herman attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], he would have attended from [[1985]] to [[1992]], being a seventh year when [[Harry Potter]] was a first year.

==Etymology==
The name "Herman" means "army man", derived from the Germanic elements hari "army" and man "man".
