'''Lauren''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who wrote for ''[[My Teeth]]'' magazine. She contributed an article urging mothers to take better care of their gums to the [[28 February|28th February]]-[[26 March|26th March]] [[1997]] issue. The ad for the article on the issue's front cover promised to reveal Lauren's "gum treat."<ref>{{HPLE|G}} {{see image|File:MyTeeth.png}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*This character was presumably named after [[Lauren Wakefield]], who worked as a graphic artist on {{HBP}}, {{DH|F1}}, and {{DH|F2}}.<ref>"[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm3204651/ Lauren Wakefield]" on IMDb.com</ref>
