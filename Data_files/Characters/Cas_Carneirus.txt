'''Cas Carneirus''' was an [[United States of America|American]] [[wizardkind|witch]] and the [[Captain of Aurors]] for the [[Magical Congress of the United States of America]] in the [[2020s]].

==Biography==
In the early 2020s, during the [[Calamity]], she hosted a bootcamp to better prepare [[Statute of Secrecy Task Force|SOS agents]] for duels against Adversaries.<ref name="WU">{{WU}}</ref>
