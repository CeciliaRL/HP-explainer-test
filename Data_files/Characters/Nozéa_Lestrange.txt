'''Nozéa Lestrange''' was a [[pure-blood]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] and a member of the [[pure-blood]] [[Lestrange family]]. She was the only daughter of [[Quintilla Lestrange]] and [[Fulcran Lestrange (III)|Fulcran Lestrange]]. By [[1927]], she had not been married or had any children, and represented the end of her branch of the Lestrange family.<ref name="Tree" />
