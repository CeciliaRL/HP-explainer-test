'''Tomoko Kajiwara''' was a [[Ravenclaw]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She was seen walking around the school during the [[1993–1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
"Tomoko" is a Japanese name, ''智 ''(tomo) means "wisdom, intellect" or ''朋 ''(tomo) "friend", combined with the popular suffix ''子 ''(ko), which means "child".

==Behind the scenes==
*Based on her name, she is most likely of [[Japan]]ese descent.
