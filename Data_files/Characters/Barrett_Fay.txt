'''Barrett Fay''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a strong advocate of the [[Pure-blood supremacy|supremacy]] of [[pure-blood]] wizards, having authored at least two books on the subject: ''[[Mudbloods and How to Spot Them]]''.<ref>{{DH|F1}} {{see image|File:92O mBJQ7Dw.jpg}}</ref> and ''[[When Muggles Attack]]''<ref>{{DH|F1}} {{see image|File:WhenMugglesAttack.jpg}}</ref>. [[Dolores Umbridge]] had copies of his works in her desk at the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]], while on the post of [[Senior Undersecretary to the Minister for Magic]].<ref name="DHF1">{{DH|F1}}</ref>
