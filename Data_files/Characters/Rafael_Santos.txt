==Biography==
He was the [[Beater]] for the [[2014]] [[Brazilian National Quidditch team]]. According to [[Ludovic Bagman]] in a report to the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'', American Beaters [[Lucas Picquery|Picquery]] and [[Pringle]] would have to "''find better form''" if they were to best the Brazilians Santos and [[Carlos Clodoaldo|Clodoaldo]] in their semi-final match on [[4 July]], [[2014]].<ref name="PM">{{PM}} - [[PLACE YOUR BETS WITH LUDO BAGMAN]] (Archived)</ref>

On the final, Brazil versus [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team|Bulgaria]], Santos hit opposing [[Seeker]] [[Viktor Krum]] hard over the head with his [[Beater's bat|bat]] on the 123rd minute. Referee [[Herman Junker]] paused the game and examined [[Omnioculars|Omniocular]] footage to determine if a foul had been committed.<ref name="the-qwc2014-final">{{PM}} - [[2014 Quidditch World Cup final]] (Archived)</ref>
