'''Molly Weasley''' (b. [[2000s]]){{fact}} was the elder daughter of [[Percy Weasley|Percy]] and [[Audrey Weasley]]. She had a younger sister, [[Lucy Weasley|Lucy]]. She was named after her [[Molly Weasley|paternal grandmother]].<ref name="WFT">[http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/books/article3105517.ece Times Online - The Unwritten Story of Harry's Friends and Their Children] ((dead link}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*Her cousin, [[Lily L. Potter|Lily Potter]] is also named after her paternal grandmother, [[Lily J. Potter|Lily Evans]].
*She is named after her grandmother, but Molly is also the middle name of her aunt (Percy's sister) [[Ginevra Weasley|Ginevra Molly Potter (née Weasley)]].

==Etymology==
Molly is an English name of Irish origin, meaning "sea of bitterness," and is a diminutive form of Mary, with the Latin meaning "star of the sea".
