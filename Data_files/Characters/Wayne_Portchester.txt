'''Wayne Portchester''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[Gryffindor]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He began school between [[1990]] and [[1993]] and studied [[Herbology]] in the [[Hogwarts greenhouses|greenhouses]] during the [[1996–1997 school year]].<ref name="HBPG">{{HBP|G}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Wayne" is from an occupational surname meaning "wagon maker", derived from Old English wægn "wagon".
