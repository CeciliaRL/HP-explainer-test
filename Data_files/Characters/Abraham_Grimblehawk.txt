'''Abraham Grimblehawk''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[wizardkind|wizard]] who worked for the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]]. He was the uncle of [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]].

==Biography==
Abraham attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was placed in [[Hufflepuff|Hufflepuff House]]. He most likely studied [[Herbology]] and [[Care of Magical Creatures]] at Hogwarts.<ref name="FBC" />

Abraham worked in the [[British Ministry of Magic]], but it is unknown in which department. It is most likely that he worked in the [[Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures]], as he had extensive knowledge on the subject of [[Magizoology]].<ref name="FBC" />

Abraham and Mathilda were very good friends of [[Gethsemane Prickle]]<ref name="FBC" />, a [[Herbologist]] and [[Potioneer]] who worked alongside the Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures.

Abraham would often read [[Newton Scamander]]'s {{FB}} to Mathilda when she was younger.<ref name="FBC" />

Abraham retired to [[St Oswald's Home for Old Witches and Wizards]] in his later years.<ref name="FBC" />
