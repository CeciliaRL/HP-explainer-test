'''Robert Louis Balfour Stevenson''' ([[13 November]] [[1850]] - [[3 December]] [[1894]]) was a [[Scotland|Scottish]] writer who wrote thirteen novels, as well as a number of collections of poems, essays, and short stories. Stevenson was a literary celebrity during his lifetime.<ref name="wikipedia">{{Wikilink|Robert Louis Stevenson}}</ref>

[[Albus Dumbledore]] kept a copy of ''[[Tales and Fantasies]]'', a small collection of short stories by this author, on his desk in the [[Headmaster's office]] at [[Hogwarts Castle]].<ref name="MHP">{{MHP}} {{see image|File:Tales and Fantasies by Robert Louis Stevenson.jpg}}</ref>
