These [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] '''[[Wizardkind|students]]''' started at Hogwarts presumably in [[1992]], who were members of [[Ravenclaw]] house.

==Biography==
In [[1995]], they appeared on the first day of classes at Hogwarts and appeared to be frightened by [[Harry Potter]]. Depending on their gender, some of them may have shared a dormitory with [[Luna Lovegood]].<ref name="OOTP12">{{OOTP|B|12}}</ref>
