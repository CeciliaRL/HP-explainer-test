'''Stephen "Steve" McClaren''' was the manager of the [[England national football team]] by [[July]] of [[1996]]. When the team's captain [[John Terry]] could not complete a practise due to a reoccurrence of a knee injury, McClaren made the decision to replace him with [[Sol Campbell]] for the upcoming group E fixture against the [[Russia national football team]] in [[Moscow]].<ref>{{HPLE|G}} (see [[:File:TheGuardian.png|this image]])</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*In the real world, Steve McClaren managed the England national football team from [[4 May]], [[Between 2010 and 2017|2006]] to [[22 November]], 2007. In 1996, McClaren was working as the assistant manager of {{wplink|Derby County F.C.}}
