'''Amrish Gupta''' was a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. He was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==
Amrish is a male given name, meaning "Lord Indra". Gupta is a North India surname, most likely derived from the Sanskrit "goptri" meaning "military governor". Another more direct translation from Sanskrit would be "secret" or "hidden".

==Behind the scenes==
*Based on his name, he is most likely of [[India]]n descent.
