'''Violetta Black''' (née '''Bulstrode''') (fl. [[1912]]-[[1920]]) was a [[pure-blood]] [[Wizardkind|witch]], the wife of [[Cygnus Black II]], and the mother of [[Pollux Black|Pollux]], [[Cassiopeia Black|Cassiopeia]], [[Marius Black|Marius]], and [[Dorea Black|Dorea]].<ref name="BFT" /> She possibly may be related to [[Millicent Bulstrode]].

==Etymology==
''[[wikipedia:Violetta|Violetta]]'' is a version of the name Violet, derived from the flower. It was the stage name of a German-born circus performer born without legs or arms who was well-known during the 1920s. The name Violetta could also be meant to mean violent or violence.

''Bulstrode ''is an English surname that can refer to the the medival village of Bulstrode in Berkshire. It means either "the bull's marsh", or "the fort on the marsh". It could refer to a violent attitude.

==Behind the scenes==
*Violetta is the great-grandmother of [[Narcissa Malfoy]], [[Bellatrix Lestrange]], [[Sirius Black]], [[Andromeda Tonks]], and [[Regulus Black]], the great-great-grandmother of [[Nymphadora Tonks|Nymphadora Lupin]] and [[Draco Malfoy]], and the great-great-great-grandmother of [[Edward Lupin|Teddy Lupin]] and [[Scorpius Malfoy]].
*Before the reveal that [[Fleamont Potter|Fleamont]] and [[Euphemia Potter]] were the parents of [[James Potter I|James]], it was speculated that Violetta's daughter [[Dorea Black|Dorea]] and her husband [[Charlus Potter|Charlus]] were his parents and thus the grandparents of [[Harry Potter]]. Therefore, Violetta was the presumed grandmother and great-grandmother of James and Harry, respectively.
