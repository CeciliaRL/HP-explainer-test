'''Cadium Featherstone''' was an individual who lived at 6a Pelican Crescent in the borough of Chelsea, [[London]], in [[1927]]. In that year, a letter addressed to Featherstone was written by the [[British Ministry of Magic]]'s [[Wizengamot Administration Services|Wizengamot Administrative Department]] and approved to be sent off.<ref name="Archive">{{AM}}</ref>
