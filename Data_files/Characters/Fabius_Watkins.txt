'''Fabius Watkins''' ([[1940]]–[[1975]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and legendary [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch Captain|Captain]] and [[Chaser]] for the [[Montrose Magpies]]. In [[1975]], he was involved in a freak collision with a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[helicopter]] and [[Death|died]] almost instantly.<ref name="JKRS">{{JKRS}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The name "Fabius" was a Roman family name which was derived from Latin faba "bean". Quintus Fabius Maximus was the Roman general who used delaying tactics to halt the invasion of Hannibal in the 3rd century BC.
