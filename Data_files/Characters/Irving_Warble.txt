'''Irving Warble''' was a [[wizardkind|wizarding]] composer. He married [[Celestina Warbeck]] after she left her [[Celestina Warbeck's second husband|second husband]] to be with him.<ref name="Pottermore" />

==Etymology==
*Irving is a [[Scotland|Scottish]] surname, though it is sometimes used as a first name as well. The name is derived from the {{wplink|River Irvine}}. [[Wikipedia:Irving Berlin|Irving Berlin]] is considered one of the greatest American songwriters; his most popular popular songs include such musical standards as ''[[Wikipedia:Alexander's Ragtime Band|Alexander's Ragtime Band]]'', ''[[Wikipedia:White Christmas (song)|White Christmas]]'', ''[[Wikipedia:Happy Holiday (song)|Happy Holidays]]'', ''[[Wikipedia:Cheek to Cheek|Cheek to Cheek]]'', ''[[Wikipedia:Let's Face the Music and Dance|Let's Face the Music and Dance]]'' and ''[[Wikipedia:Oh! How I Hate to Get Up in the Morning|Oh! How I Hate to Get Up in the Morning]]''.
*Warble is an English verb meaning "to sing in a high or shaky voice" and is a word often associated with the singing of birds.

==Behind the scenes==
*The [[warble flute]] may be attributed to Irving Warble. The [[flute]] was regarded to be developmental to the [[jazz]] scene in [[New Orleans]], which Warble was likely alive to see.
