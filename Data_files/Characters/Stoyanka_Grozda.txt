==Etymology==
*Stoyanka is a name derived from Old Bulgarian ''стоити [stoiti]'', "to stand" or "to stay", equivalent and cognate to Constantine, given to a child as a blessing for a long life.
*Grozda is a female given name derived from ''грозд [grozd]'', "grape cluster".
