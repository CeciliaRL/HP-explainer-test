'''Manami Ichijoh''' was a [[Slytherin]] [[Wizardkind|student]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the [[1990s]]. She was seen walking around the school during the [[1993-1994 school year]].

==Etymology==

Manami is a female Japanese given name, ''愛 ''(mana) means love, combined with either ''美 ''(mi) "beautiful" or ''海 ''(mi) "sea, ocean".

==Behind the scenes==

*Based on her name, she is presumably of Japanese descent.
