This '''individual''' was an [[Africa]]n [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Uagadou]]. He was a skilled [[potioneer]] and represented his school at the [[Wizarding Schools Potions Championship]].<ref name="WBP">{{WBP}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Uagadou===
Like other students, the African wizard was most likely given a token by a [[Dream Messenger]] in his sleep for entrance into [[Uagadou]].<ref name="PM">{{WW|uagadou}}</ref>

===Wizarding Schools Potions Championship===
[[File:Uagadoubanshee.png|250px|thumb|right|The wizard driven to madness by a Banshee]]
The wizard became a skilled potioneer, and at age 17 or above participated in the [[Wizarding Schools Potions Championship]], where he represented Uagadou, his school. In the championship, he came upon a [[Banshee]], who screamed and drove him to madness. Later, the [[Hogwarts potions champion]] used a [[Laughing Potion]] on the Banshee to incapacitate it.<ref name="WBP"/>

==Physical appearance==
The wizard was dark-skinned with black hair and brown eyes, and wore a white shirt.<ref name="WBP"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': As a skilled [[potioneer]], this potioneer was skilled at making potions.<ref name="WBP"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*This is the first student from Uagadou introduced throughout the Harry Potter series.
