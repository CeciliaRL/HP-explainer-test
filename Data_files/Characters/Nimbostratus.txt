'''Nimbostratus''' was an employee of [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] who was apparently responsible for handling transactions.<ref name="form">See [[:File:GringottsForm.jpg|this image]].</ref> This individual's entry on one particular form was crossed out for an unknown reason.<ref name="form" />

==Etymology==
*"Nimbostratus" is a term used to refer to low, grey, usually dark clouds that produce rain, snow, and other forms of precipitation.<ref>"[http://www.thefreedictionary.com/nimbostratus Nimbostratus]" on The Free Dictionary</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
*As Gringotts hired both [[Goblin]]s and [[Wizardkind|wizards]] as staff, it is unknown what species this individual was.
