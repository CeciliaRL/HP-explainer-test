The '''kinsfolk''' or family of a [[Maiden (The Warlock's Hairy Heart)|Maiden]] were minor protagonists of the children's tale, ''[[The Warlock's Hairy Heart]]'' in {{TBB}}.<ref name="TBB">{{TBB|H}}</ref>

==History==
{{Quote|...a maiden answering his every wish arrived in the neighbourhood to visit her kinsfolk.|The Maiden |The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)}}
The Maiden met the [[Warlock (The Warlock's Hairy Heart)|Warlock]] while visiting her family.<ref name="TBB"/> Though she regarded him with a kind of grotesque fascination, her family deemed them a suitable match and accepted the Warlock's invitation to a great feast in the maiden's honour.<ref name="TBB"/>
