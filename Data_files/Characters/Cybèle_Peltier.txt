'''Cybèle Peltier'''<ref>''[http://www.nedergamen.nl/games/hpwkz ''Harry Potter: WK Zwerkbal'' credits list]'' (in Dutch)</ref> was a [[France|French]] [[Wizardkind|witch]] who played as [[Seeker]] on the [[French National Quidditch team]].<ref name="QWC">{{QWC}}</ref>

==Etymology==
The meaning of the name "Cybèle" is unknown. This was the name of the Phrygian goddess of fertility and nature, later worshipped by the Greeks and Romans.
