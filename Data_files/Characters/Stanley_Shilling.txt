'''Stanley Shilling''' was a [[Muggle|No-Maj]] actor in the early [[20th century]].<ref name="fbpic">{{FB|F}} {{See image|File:ComingSoonSovereignTheatre.jpg}}</ref>

In [[December]] [[1926]], there was a sign on the side of the [[Sovereign Theatre]] in [[Times Square]] in [[New York]], announcing that a production of ''[[A Man Called Clark]]'' starring Shilling would begin there soon.<ref name="fbpic" />
