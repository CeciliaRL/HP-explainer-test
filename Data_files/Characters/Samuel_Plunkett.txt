{{Quote|There was also the famous case of wizard Samuel Plunkett, who poured Shrinking Solution into the well of a village that had persecuted him, then spent his birthday chasing the shrunken villagers in hobnail boots.|[[Zygmunt Budge]], ''[[Book of Potions]]''|Wonderbook: Book of Potions}}
'''Samuel Plunkett''' was a [[wizardkind|wizard]] who was famously persecuted by a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] village at some point in history.<ref name="well">{{WBP}} - see [https://archive.is/20131112204506/www.sony.com/pottermore/gb/book-of-potions/view-image/assets/img/carousel-bop/BoP-ShrinkStory.jpg this]</ref> To avoid persecution he poured [[Shrinking Solution]] into the village well<ref name="well"/> and spent his entire birthday chasing the shrunken villagers around in hobnail boots.<ref name="WP"/> Author [[Zygmunt Budge]] wrote about this incident in his ''[[Book of Potions]]''.<ref name="WP"/>

==Magical abilities and skills==
*'''[[Potion]]s''': Plunkett was evidently skilled in the field of potion-making, as he was able to successfully brew the [[Shrinking Solution]] and use it to take revenge on his Muggle persectuors.<ref name="WP"/>
